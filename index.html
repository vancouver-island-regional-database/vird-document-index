Online Search Engine Regional Public Document Index

This database provides access to authoritative regional open data by linking directly to pdf files across multiple platforms and jurisdictions. It is currently capable of searching the text contents of 15,000 documents across the official websites of the Town of Ladysmith, the Stz'uminus First Nation, and School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith). Please check back later for additional jurisdictions and content as this database continues to grow.

To lookup information, type your search terms below and documents with matching content will appear. Filter by jurisdiction on the left to narrow down your results, and click on the document title to view the file on its official source website. You can also export all of the search results to a CSV file by clicking the "Download All Matches (CSV)" button that appears after you start typing in the search bar.

Please note that none of these documents are hosted on the Vancouver Island Regional Database website. Clicking a link will open the document on its original website in a new tab. We have no control over whether external websites are configured to open the pdf in your browser or automatically download to your device.
