Regional Document Search Engine

An interjurisdictional directory of available online public documents, directly linking to their official source url. This database is currently capable of searching the text contents of 15,000 pdf files across the official websites of the Town of Ladysmith, the Stz'uminus First Nation, and School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith). Please check back later for additional jurisdictions and content as this database continues to grow.

Use the search bar to enter your search terms, and the documents with matching content will appear. Filter by jurisdiction on the left to narrow down your results, and click on the document title to be taken directly to the file's original url.

Please note that all files are hosted externally and you will leave the VIRD website when you click on a link.
