Rong Hu Natural Massage Spa 220 Coronation Ave Duncan, BC V9K 2B5 Email: February 5, 2025 Dear Rong Hu: RE: Council Consideration to Cancel Business Licence 1891, Unit 1, 16 High Street This letter is to inform you, pursuant to section 60(3) of the Community Charter , that Council, at its February 4, 2025 meeting, considered the cancellation of the above-noted business licence. Council will make a final decision on the matter at its February 18th meeting to be held at the Ladysmith Seniors Centre at 630 2nd Avenue at 7pm. A copy of the staff report outlining the circumstances that the Town considers may warrant cancellation of the licence is included with this letter. You are entitled to attend the Council meeting, with legal counsel if you choose, and will be given an opportunity to ask questions and to present your own evidence. If you fail to attend the meeting, Council will be at liberty to proceed with the hearing and to render its decision based upon the evidence presented by Town staff. Should you have any questions please contact the undersigned. Yours truly, Sue Bouma Corporate Officer/License Inspector Encl: February 4, 2025 staff report
