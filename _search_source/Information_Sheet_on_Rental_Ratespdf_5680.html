THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY-TREASURER INFORMATION SHEET TO: Business Committee FROM: J. David Green/Pete Sabo DATE: October 14, 2010 SUBJECT: Update on Rental Rates Previous to the 2010-2011 fiscal year the district included the GST in the base published rental rate. The amount of revenue actually recorded was therefore net of the GST. When the HST came into effect staff added the 12% to the published rental rates so that the revenue recorded was in fact the published rate. In addition all rental rates were increased by 10%. The result of this is that all contracts received at least an increase of 10% and some contracts up to 22%. The variance has to do with the item charged on the rental agreement and the applicability of tax on those specific items. .
