Portability: Items that would have to be moved for each meeting: - Laptop - Cameras (x2) - Microphone (x1) - Various cables Non-portable: Items that cannot be brought offsite as they are hardwired: - Microphones (x6) - Sound mixing board - Microphone receivers (x6) - Audio amplifier - eScribe live stream hub - Telephone conference phone Site requisites: What would be needed to run a live stream offsite: - Dedicated ethernet port - Phone line in the event a Trustee has to call in Page 39 of 40 Costs: There should not be any costs incurred for livestreaming offsite. Only potential cost would be staff time to set up the room, electronic set up and test internet connection (for Communications, Sr. Executive Assistant, and IT staff). Conclusion: - Video will not be affected, unless the internet connection is slow. Audio will be affected. Without the full audio setup, it will be difficult to hear each Trustee or staff member when they are speaking. Effective Fall 2019, livestreaming will be transitioned to eScribe’s service rather than YouTube. This new service requires a large piece of electronic hardware that is permanently installed in the Boardroom. Recommendation: Taking into account the equipment needed, livestreaming a Committee meeting with the current specifications is not a viable option outside of the Boardroom. Page 40 of 40
