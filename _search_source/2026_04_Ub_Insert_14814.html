NEW RATES EFFECTIVE 2026 The Utility Bill enclosed with this newsletter covers the period January to March 2026. You will notice your bill is calculated based on the new water rates that came into effect January 1, 2026. The water rates increased by 3% to the base rate and consumption steps. This equates to a $1.92 increase to the base rate for a single family dwelling and $2.88 increase to the base rate for a single-family dwelling with a suite for the quarter. The sewer rates also increased by 3% for all of the classes. This equates to a $2.94 increase for each dwelling unit for the quarter. The solid waste fees increased by $10.50 for the quarter per unit. The rate increase helps to cover the rising costs of providing safe, reliable drinking water and treating our waste water, and contractor rates for solid waste services. The funds also go towards building reserves for future improvements to our sewer and water supply systems. STAGE 1 WATER RESTRICTIONS Non-contract garbage such as yard waste, rocks, commercial trade waste, lumber, household construction, renovation or demolition waste, highly flammable or hazardous waste, along with banned materials will not be collected. Stage 1 Water Restrictions will come into effect for Town of Ladysmith residents as of May 1, 2026. These annual restrictions are intended to help manage water usage during the warmer, drier months of the year. The Cowichan Valley Regional District (CVRD) Peerless Road Facility has expanded its list of acceptable items.
