Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: December 9, 2015 TO: Business Committee FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Dave Prevost, Transportation Supervisor SUBJECT: Z Pass + Implementation Update At the update given to the Business Committee on October 14th, bus registrations were reported at 900. Bus registrations to date are at 1,723 students. It is estimated that no remaining riders require registration. Only new bus students should now require registration. After the last report, staff communicated a deadline for registration of November 15th, which assisted in encouraging the remaining riders to register. The issue of establishing operating guidelines for this new system is now being discussed by staff as there have been ongoing issues of training, the registration process including deadlines, lost cards, forgotten cards, damaged cards, replacement cards, process for recording of transporting friends of registered riders, cost of replacement cards, scanning cards, etc. are becoming apparent. Staff are also considering a fee for the replacement of repeatedly lost cards. With the largest implementation issues now behind the Transportation Department, it would be important to note that school staff, bus drivers and the dispatch coordinator have been instrumental in the success of the new program, software and associated processes. Transportation Department staff continues to:      Register students on-line for 2015-16. Provide bus driving staff with ongoing training on using Z Pass + Work with staff, schools, students and families to ensure the implementation of Z Pass + is successful. Monitor implementation and make improvements and recommendations as required. Prepare to report out in January on; student ridership (numbers), status (eligible and noneligible), route type (regular, special needs, out of policy, etc.), ridership by schools and ridership eligibility by route.
