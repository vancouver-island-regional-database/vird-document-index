The City is planning to decommission the dams because they are at risk of failure in an earthquake or extreme rainfall event. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Green made a presentation to the Board on the topic of childhood poverty and its relation to mental illness in children. Mr. Green said that people with mental illness often live in chronic poverty and, conversely, poverty can be a significant risk factor for poor physical and mental health. He said that a multifaceted approach is required that includes universal programs to promote health for all children and talked about the role of elementary counselors in empowering students toward positive change. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Hutchinson presented the Draft Strategic Plan to the Board and said that the primary purpose of the strategic plan is to create a blueprint for the district that helps maintain its exemplary qualities, while supporting the continuous improvement of student learning. “A good strategic plan helps organize a system around a few significant goals, and ensures that the resources are in place to successfully implement the goals in a manner that is sustainable over time,” he said. He said that the work done over the past year by the Board on its governance structure, followed by community consultations, has led to the point where the Board is now ready to put into place a strategic plan.
