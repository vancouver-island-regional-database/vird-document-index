Sproston, DASG Rep Meeting No. SB8 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio SB14/04/09-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS 2014-15 Draft Budget Budget binders were distributed to Committee members. Mr. Turin said that the proposed budget reduction strategies total $6.8 million, Draft Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 9, 2014 which is $1.4 million more than the required amount of reductions. He said the Board has the option of selecting $5.4 million of reductions from those proposed by management, and may also want to suggest other strategies that have not yet been identified. There are 32 budget proposals listed under tab 9. The Committee reviewed each proposal.
