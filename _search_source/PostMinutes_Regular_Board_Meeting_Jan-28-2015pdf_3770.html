The brochure will be distributed to secondary schools in February and elementary schools in March. Ms. McKay thanked the Board and district staff for their ongoing support in developing this initiative. Learning Services Presentation - Bob Esliger RE: John Barsby Secondary Health Centre Dr. Wilma Arruda presented to the Board and update on the John Barsby Secondary Heath Centre project. There were other representatives in the health field in attendance. Dr. Arruda shared that there is evidence linking access to school based health centres and improvement in academic performance. As well as fewer absences, higher educational aspirations and higher graduation rates. Dr. Arruda thanked the Board for their ongoing support and partnership in the development, implementation and evaluation of The Barsby Wellness Centre.
