December 12, 2013 Ref: 169969 Jamie Brennan, Chair Board of Education School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 395 Wakesiah Ave Nanaimo BC V9R 3K6 Email: jbrennan@sd68.bc.ca Dear Mr. Brennan: Thank you for your letter dated September 12, 2013, requesting approval for the NanaimoLadysmith Board of Education to underspend its targeted Aboriginal education amount for the 2012/13 school year. Pursuant to section 106.4(2) of the School Act, approval is granted for the Board to underspend its 2012/13 school year Aboriginal Education target in an amount up to $153,889. This variation of direction is granted on the condition that these funds will be brought forward and added to the 2013/14 school year Aboriginal Education targeted amount to be expended on Aboriginal education programs. Sincerely, Peter Fassbender Minister pc: Dave Hutchinson, Superintendent of Schools Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer Laura Tait, District Principal, Aboriginal Education Ministry of Education Office of the Minister Mailing Address: PO Box 9045 Stn Prov Govt Victoria BC V8W 9E2 Location: Parliament Buildings Victoria
