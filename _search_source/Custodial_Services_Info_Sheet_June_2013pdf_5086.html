THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: May 15, 2013 TO: Business Committee FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Diane Bunnah, Operations Supervisor SUBJECT: Final Update on SD68 Custodial Review Staff have been working on the Custodial Services Review Action Plan since June 2012 and present the status of actions to date (attached). All the recommendations have now been addressed. A few of the recommendations will require continued attention or ongoing review:  Custodial Staffing Formula as a management tool; and  Custodial Training. Some recommendations will require consultation with other District departments:  Exception based timesheets with the Payroll Department;  Absent Management Program with the Human Resources Department; and  Custodial training with the OH&S Department. Since the hiring of the General Foreman, the Operations Supervisor has had more time to spend on staffing issues, as well as HR and CUPE concerns. The Assistant Supervisor has been able to provide 50% more site inspections which have assisted in rectifying cleaning deficiencies and has resulted in proactive training of employees. The District will also have an ergonomic and efficient alternative to traditional floor cleaning methods with the upcoming purchase of the auto scrubbers. Staff are pleased to provide this report to the Board on the status of the custodial review and to have been able to incorporate all of the recommendations to the best extent possible.
