Reimer, Director of Communications CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:03 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Saunders IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio S13/07/03-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Achievement Contract Staff reviewed the draft district Achievement Contract for 2013-14 with Trustees and responded to questions. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Neary Folio S13/07/03-02 Superintendent’s Report Educational Updates That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves the 2012-13 District Achievement Contract and (allowing for minor editorial revisions) forwards it to the Minister of Education by July 15, 2013. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Hutchinson provided Trustees with an update on several educational initiatives. • This year, the district received about $270,000 from the government for the B.C. Education Plan Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held July 3, 2013 grant. Mr. Hutchinson reported on the use of the funds this past school year and plans for next year. • The district assessment project is underway. The draft policy on assessment was sent out as a notice of motion this spring and will come back to the Board for a decision at the August Board meeting. • The Board received a report on the selfregulation project, which included a demonstration classroom which was visited by more than 80 percent of the district’s schools, presentations at the February 1 Success for All Day; consultations with schools; and the development of a district resource guide. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Saunders IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio S13/07/03-03 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receives the information provided with respect to the BC Education Plan Grant, the Learning Services Support Teams, the District Assessment Project and the SelfRegulation Initiative. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Standing Committee Meetings Calendar Change Folio S13/07/03-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Bard Conversion of Cedar Secondary School to an Elementary School Folio S13/07/03-05 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Saunders That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) amends Policy 2.6 – Meetings and Board Operations and sends it out as a Notice of Motion. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the immediate hiring of KMBR Architects Planners Inc. to complete the planning and design work required to convert Cedar Secondary to Cedar Elementary for school opening in September 2015. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held July 3, 2013 QUESTION PERIOD Andrea Bonkowski Ms. Bonkowski asked several questions on process, data, conclusions reached, and bids regarding Cedar High School contract with KMBR. ADJOURNMENT IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Saunders Folio R13/07/03-06 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 8:00 pm.
