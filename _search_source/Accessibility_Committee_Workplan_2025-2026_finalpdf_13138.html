Appendix A Accessibility Committee Workplan 2025-2026 1. Develop an Accessibility Plan for 2026-2029 Utilizing feedback from students, staff and community members, the committee will develop a renewed three-year accessibility plan. 2. Continued work on the 2022-2025 Accessibility Plan • Priority #1 Employment and Service Delivery Practices o Engage in dialogue with students, specialist staff and school leaders to explore potential gaps in accessibility, in support of the goals in the new plan. • Priority #2: The Built Environment o o o Refine district guidelines for the built environment by: Fully implement the Accessibility Matrix as a tool for the prioritization and completion of accessibility requests Enhancing school team knowledge of strategies for increasing playground accessibility that do not require infrastructure changes • Priority #3: Inclusion, Mental Health and Peer Connections o Enhance classroom teacher understanding of ways to reduce the impact of accessibility barriers on inclusion, mental health, and peer connections for their students by building classroom teacher awareness of the Classroom Accessibility Matrix. • Priority #4: Information and Communication o Create guidelines for the system to increase the accessibility of the district’s print and digital communication.
