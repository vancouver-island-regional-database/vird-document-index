THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: January 21, 2011 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: 2010-2011 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw BACKGROUND: Section 113 of the School Act, “Adoption of the Budget” states: 113. (1) A board, by bylaw, (a) must adopt an annual budget on or before June 30 of each year for the next fiscal year, and (b) may amend the annual budget adopted under paragraph (a) (2) (3) If an Annual grant to a board is amended under section 106.3(6) or a grant is withheld or reduced under section 117(1), the minister may order that (a) the board, by bylaw, must amend its annual budget, and (b) the board must send a certified copy of the amended annual budget to the minister within 60 days of the order of the minister At the request of the minister, the board must send to the minister a certified copy of the annual budget. The School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Amended Annual Budget Bylaw for fiscal year 2010-2011 is attached. FIRST READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: SECOND READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: UNANIMOUS CONSENT ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the 2010-2011 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the 2010-2011 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw and that it be given first reading. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the 2010-2011 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the 2010-2011 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw and that it be given second reading. In order for the third and final reading of the 2010-2011 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw to take place at the same meeting as the first and second readings, unanimous approval of the board is required. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read the 2010-2011 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw for a third and final time. THIRD & FINAL READING ACTION REQUIRED: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the 2009-2010 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the 2010-2011 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw and that it be given a third and final reading. 2010-2011 AMENDED ANNUAL BUDGET BYLAW A Bylaw of THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 (NANAIMOLADYSMITH) (called the “Board”) to adopt the Amended Annual Budget of the Board for the fiscal year 2010-2011 pursuant to section 113 of the School Act, R.S.B.C., 1996, c.412 as amended from time to time (called the “Act”). 1. The Board has complied with the provisions of the Act respecting the Amended Annual Budget adopted by this bylaw. 2. This bylaw may be cited as School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Amended Annual Budget Bylaw for fiscal year 2010-2011. 3. The attached Schedule “A” showing the estimated revenues and the estimated expenditures for the 2010-2011 fiscal year and the total budget amount of $125,235,954 for the 2010-2011 fiscal year was prepared in accordance with the Act. 4. Schedule “A” as amended at the Regular Board meeting of January 26, 2011 is adopted as the Amended Annual Budget for the Board for the fiscal year 20102011.
