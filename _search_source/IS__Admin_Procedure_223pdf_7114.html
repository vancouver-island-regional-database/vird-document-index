NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: INFORMATION SHEET April 22, 2020 Board of Education Tim Davie, Deputy Superintendent Administrative Procedure 223 – Selection of Learning Resources Background The original Administrative Procedure 223 was adopted in 1978 and amended in 1993 and 1996, this revision modernizes the NLPS Selection of Learning Resources Administrative Procedure to meet current 2020 requirements. Information The Board of Education of Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools (NLPS) is committed to the Board’s goal of continuous improvement of instruction and assessment. The amended Selection of Learning Resources Administrative Procedure is designed to support student learning and increase student success. Selection of Learning Resources AP 223 has been updated to include:     Redefining “Learning Resource” to include digital and media Outlining general guidelines for selecting resources – in that they must be from either the Ministry of Education’s list of resources, from the ERAC list of approved resources, or need to comply with the criteria as set out in AP 223 Addition of the criteria of the Privacy and Copyright Legislation, especially for media and digital resources Guidelines for streaming commercial films, videos and audio files, and an in-depth explanation of the BC Rating System and NLPS Requirements The rationale for the changes were to update the district’s AP 223 to:      Reflect current standards in general terminology, inclusiveness, social considerations (eg. Indigenous peoples’ perspective), sustainability, and privacy and copyright legislation Include digital and media formats in “Learning Resources” as these formats have become more and more prevalent in classrooms Follow a standardized set of criteria for the selection of all Learning Resources, as used by ERAC, with additional considerations for digital, media and audio resources Align with Privacy and Copyright Legislation Align NLPS practices with regards to the Selection of Learning Resources with other districts across the province Appendix A: Administrative Procedure 223 – Selection of Learning Resources (April 2020) Appendix B: Administrative Procedure 223 – Selection of Learning Resources (1996)
