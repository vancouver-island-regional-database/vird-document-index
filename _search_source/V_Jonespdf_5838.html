April 22nd2016 To the Nanaimo School Board Chair, Superintendent and Trustee's: On behalfof the Pleasant ValleySchool PAC, the parents at our school and myself we are voicing our concernsabout losing our school bus service. We are not a school that this can happen to for the following reasons: SAFETYfor our children- there are three areas in particular that are of great concern if our bus service was taken away. The north Iingle pot Rd. location is not safe for our kids to be walking to and from school. They would have to cross the very busy Hwy.19 or be walking on a wooded trail. The next is the Aulds Rd. location again children will have to cross the busy Hwy.19 and be walking on the wooded trail. The third location is the Iordan/Wellington Rd., our children would have to walk along and cross the very busy Mostar Rd and Metral Dr. The bus service was expanded back when Mt. Benson school was closed because the catchment was expanded for Pleasant Valley schoolto take on the childrenfrom Mt. Benson catchment. This meansthat the children are coming from a farther distance than normal and again having to cross busy hwy'S and streets. Thisneeds to be taken into consideration! I for our children's safety if our school bus service We are very concerned was taken away. The school bus is a vital service to get our children safely to and from school! Thank you for your time. Vanessa Jones Pleasant Valley School PAC chair
