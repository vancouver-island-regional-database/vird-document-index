BOARD OF SCHOOL TRUSTEES SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) INFORMATION SHEET TO: Business Committee FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer DATE: January 10, 2012 SUBJECT: Kilometric Reimbursement Rates BACKGROUND: BCSTA has recently updated their travel allowance/expense form. Currently, BCSTA provides the same meal allowances as the School District. Breakfast Lunch Dinner Full Day Per Diem $10 $17 $23 $50 Effective January 1, 2012 BCSTA has increased their kilometric rate from $0.52 to $0.53. RECOMMENDATION: The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that it increase its kilometric rate from $0.52 to $0.53 effective February 1st, 2012 and further that it continue to monitor BCSTA’s kilometric rate and per diems and match them accordingly.
