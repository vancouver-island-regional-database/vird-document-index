January 29th Curriculum In-Service Day at Dover Bay Dear Educators, the following communication is information regarding the January 29th, 2016 Curriculum In-service Day. In-Service The BC Ministry of Education has supported the use of 10 hours of instruction time to allow Educators to explore the newly, redesigned curriculum.  Our first opportunity to come together as a district around this redesign is January 29th  The second opportunity, is May 16, 2016 What Will January 29th Look Like?  The learning focus of the day will be to explore the underpinnings and rationale for the shifts in the curriculum redesign which will include a focus on the Core Competencies for K-12  Break-out sessions are organized into K-12 learning groups, as the learning focus for this day is relevant across all grade and subject areas  The day will be highly interactive, with sessions led by your colleagues and fellow educators  You will have an opportunity to explore the new curriculum on-line during your learning sessions: o If you have a device (laptop, tablet etc.) please bring with you o The plan is to have 1 device per 2 people o If you don’t have a device, that’s ok as there will be devices to share.  Room assignments will be forthcoming in an email from our NDTA colleagues, along with other logistical items What’s Next on May 16th, 2016?
