Budget Process Debrief/Review The Committee reviewed the budget process and discussed whether there should be any changes to the process. FOR INFORMATION School District Property Loss Changes - Correspondence from Ministry of Education Mr. Turin told the Committee that the Ministry has informed districts that there will be a new property loss charge billed to school districts. For School District 68, this will be an added cost of $77,000. Holdback Grant Mr. Turin told the Committee that the Ministry has announced that it will distribute the 2010-11 holdback funds to school districts. School District 68 will receive $206,186 in holdback funds, minus the $77,000 in the additional property loss charge. 2010-2011 Forecast as at June 30, 2011 Mr. Turin said the district is currently projecting about a $981,000 surplus to carry forward into next year’s budget of which $701,000 will be restricted which includes the amount of $575,000 required to balance the 2011/12 budget. Asset Management Plan The Facilities Planning Committee has requested staff to seek a "Request for Expression of Interest" (RFEI) for the purpose of providing recommendations for the development of a comprehensive Asset Management Plan and that specific criteria including educational use, ownership, community use and others as determined necessary for the plan be incorporated for the purpose of determining the next steps on how to proceed.
