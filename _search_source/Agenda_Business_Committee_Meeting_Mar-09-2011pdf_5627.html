Staff recommended that catchments be adjusted at the following elementary schools in accordance with the attached “rationalization”:  Coal Tyee  Pleasant Valley  Forest Park  Quarterway  Chase River The changes at Coal Tyee and Pleasant Valley are due to the closure of Mt Benson and housekeeping. Forest Park, Quarterway and Chase River adjustments are housekeeping items and mainly due to the Parkway cutting off a portion of the schools catchment area. This recommendation is made in accordance with Policy 3022. The recommended adjustments reflect realist attendance patterns, support existing choices by families, clean up issues from the Parkway construction and affect few students. Staff expect to grandfather existing student affected by the changes. Consultation would proceed in the fall of 2011 and it is expect that new boundaries would take affect for September of 2012. School boundaries shall be determined after consultation with the affected communities and due consideration of all relevant factors, including:  the present school enrolments and future population trends in the District  the accommodation available in the schools  the accessibility of the school to the pupils  the safety of the students in going to and from the school, and  the availability of transportation. Boundary decisions will normally be made no later than the January prior to the commencement of instruction in a new school or a school undergoing school boundary/catchment area revisions. Although all reasonable attempts are made to provide students with a placement in their catchment-area school, subject to timelines set each year students may need to be accommodated in a school other than their catchment-area school, particularly when students do not register within the established timelines, subject to Policy and Procedure 3022. The Board supports the provision of Choice for attending schools other than the catchment-area school and for attending District programs where space and facilities permit. Refer to Policy and Procedure 3022 related to guidelines for student admission to catchment-area school, schools of choice and programs of choice.
