Thanks, From: To: Subject: Date: Consultation Consultation process (Naming of facilities or parts of facilities) April 29, 2021 6:41:35 PM CAUTION: External Message Dear Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools, Just something to consider that came up after a Zoom meeting I participated with this evening: I believe that Part 2 of the Naming of facilities or parts of facilities could be amended to reflect the dynamically-changing culture and society we live in with respect to historical figures or noteworthy members of the community who have acted or behaved contrary to contemporary societal acceptance. This could be accomplished for example by being pro-active adding a Part 2(g) with wording to include consultation with submissions from the general public for input into specific name changes. The submissions should be confidential in nature. The goal would be to protect Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools from not only making a significant error but also protect students, staff, and the public from any emotional trauma due to events not public. A specific example of this came up during conversation. Naming a school after an abuser whose victim or victims have been reluctant or afraid to come forward, or who are not ready to come forward at this point in their lives-- this would be unfortunate. To name a facility after one's abuser would be traumatic for an individual, and to a lesser extent put the school district on the spot for having to re-name a facility. Just a thought or two to stay pro-active. Thank you for your consideration.
