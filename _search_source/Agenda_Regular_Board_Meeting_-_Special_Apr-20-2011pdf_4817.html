THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 SPECIAL BOARD MEETING TO BE HELD WEDNESDAY, APRIL 20, 2011 BOARD ROOM AGENDA Page 1. CALL TO ORDER The Chair will call the meeting to order and recognizes that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. 2. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Excerpt from Board Procedure Procedure No. 1001(B)P Special meetings may be called in accordance with Section 88 of the School Act. Notice of such meetings shall be given in writing specifying the business to be transacted, and shall be given 48 hours in advance except in the case of meetings called in emergencies. An emergency shall be defined as a matter requiring immediate attention. 3. UNFINISHED BUSINESS 3.1 Local School Calendar - 2011-12 2 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the attached Local School Calendar for all of its schools for the 2011-12 school year. 4. QUESTION PERIOD Questions from the audience must be submitted in writing and given to the Board’s Executive Assistant (Cathy Kelt) for submission to the Chair. The question period is intended to enable the public to obtain clarifying information regarding a current agenda item.
