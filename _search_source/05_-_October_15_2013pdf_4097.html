CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Cedar School Conversion Visioning Workshop Report The Board received a report from KMBR Architects Planners Inc. to use as a guide for the revisioning of Cedar Secondary. On September 13th a workshop was held by KMBR Architects. There were approximately 30 people in attendance including trustees, school district officials and PAC representatives. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Bard IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson Folio S13/10/15-02 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receives the September 13, 2013 Cedar School Conversion Visioning Workshop Report prepared by KMBR Architects Planners Inc. and use the report as a guide for planning purposes to repurpose Cedar Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held October 15, 2013 Secondary School to Cedar Elementary School. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Allen Folio S13/10/15-03 Cooperative Gains Mandate Savings Plan for CUPE That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) follow its Policy/Procedure No. 4005 for appointing a school construction committee for the new Cedar Elementary School. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Turin said that School Boards have been mandated by the Minister of Education to develop savings plans from within their existing budgets to pay for the wage increases. The wage increases are 1 percent on July 1, 2013; 2 percent on February 1, 2014; and 0.5 percent on May 1, 2014. He said that the cost of the wage increases to our district will be $523,454 this school year and $969,951 in subsequent school years. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Robinson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Saunders Folio S13/10/15-04 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves the Cooperative Gains Mandate Savings Plan for CUPE for School District 68 as presented. Opposed by Trustee Allen CARRIED IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio S13/10/15-05 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) directs the Board Chair to write to the Minister of Education expressing the Board's dismay and frustration at the negative impact that a cooperative gains mandate savings plan for CUPE will have on the Board's Strategic plan for the improvement of student learning. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held October 15, 2013 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio S13/10/09-06 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) write to BCSTA Executive to express our grave concerns regarding the Ministry of Education's directive to fund the provincially bargained CUPE salary increases using the Cooperative Gains Savings Plan; and further, that the BCSTA, representing all the BC School Districts, work diligently to achieve a co-governance model for BC School Districts and the BC Ministry of Education. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY QUESTION PERIOD ADJOURNMENT Folio S13/10/15-07 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the meeting be adjourned.
