SD 68 Chairperson’s Report Sharon Welch January, 2011 I would like to start by wishing everyone a Happy New Year, and thanking you again for your commitment to SD68. The Trustees started 2011 by approving the new committee appointments. The Trustees and Senior Staff will be working together at our Board Retreat next week to determine the direction of our committees and to build stronger communication between our sub-committees, committees, staff, and the Board. Here’s a special ‘thank you’ to everyone who has served and will serve on our District committees. Today, most of our schools and administrative office participated in the Great BC Shak Out earthquake preparation exercise. Emergency preparedness and heightened awareness are so important for student and staff safety. The French Immersion review will be under way soon. The Board is looking forward to hearing the public feedback and recommendations from the consultants. This will be an integral part of moving forward in our new facilities plan. I would like to say congratulations and a sincere ‘thank you’ to Darlene Creighton, our Manager HR Secondary Education, and Sandy Doyle, Benefits Officer, who are retiring from SD68. They are both longterm employees who worked in key middle management roles. You will both be missed very much! This has been a busy end-of-semester time for our secondary students and staff. We commend all of you for your hard work, and look forward to a great second semester. Finally, I would like to recognize the very hard work done by our Senior Staff at a time when the District Office is under-staffed and so very busy. The Superintendent and his staff are doing amazing work under great pressure. We appreciate your dedication and effort! Thank you.
