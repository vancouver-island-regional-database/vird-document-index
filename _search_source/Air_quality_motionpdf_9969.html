Rationale Provide a succinct description of why this motion is needed, plus any relevant background information. The creation of healthy learning environments is important for all students and staff. The acceleration of funding for capital projects to improve indoor air quality should be considered an effort to mitigate the impact of extreme weather events, such as wildfires, which increase the exposure to smoke & high concentrations particulate matter. Air quality is a key component of tackling the adverse effects of climate change.
