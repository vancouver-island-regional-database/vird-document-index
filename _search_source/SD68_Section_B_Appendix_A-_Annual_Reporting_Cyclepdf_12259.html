SD68- Section B – Appendix A Board Strategic Plan ESL Annual Reporting Cycle NLPS Strategic Plan is closely aligned with the Ministry requirements prescribed in the Framework for Enhancing Student Learning (F/ESL) and the ESL Report Order • The ESL will be used as the annual reporting mechanism for the Strategic Plan o ESL Report can be nimble and responsive, whereas the Strategic Plan is a longer-term planning document Ministry of Education and Childcare Data • Grad rates, transition rates, graduation assessments, FSA, Student Learning Survey • MECC Data is released at various times throughout the year o This data release schedule is shared in the Annual Board Work Plan o This data is reported to the Board in the ESL Report  This data will be one year behind the year that is being reported on • 23/24 student data used to inform planning for 25/26 year SD68- Section B – Appendix A Data Informed System • There are plans in place to establish infrastructure to enable easy access to local student data, in order to triangulate data to inform decisions (school/district) o IT Infrastructure o District-wide Student Assessments Horizontal & Vertical Alignment • Horizontal Alignment – All NLPS Departments are now required to submit Department Operational Plans o Organizational Alignment - roles and responsibilities across teams and functions • Vertical Alignment – School Plans continue to align with the Strategic Plan • Strategic Plan has three goals and seven objectives, o School plans will reﬂect one or two areas of focus in the Strategic Plan o Departments may reﬂect indirect connections to the Goals of the Strategic Plan o Context and data will inform these focus areas
