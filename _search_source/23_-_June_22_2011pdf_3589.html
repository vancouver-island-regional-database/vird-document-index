CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY ANNOUNCEMENTS AND REMINDERS Thursday, June 30, 2011 Wednesday, July 13, 2011 Wednesday, August 31, 2011 Graduation Day/Dry Grad Closed Board Meeting Regular Board Meeting Closed Board Meeting 4:30 pm 6:00 pm 4:30 pm PRESENTATIONS SOGI Art Contest Trustee Donna Allen, Chairperson of the Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity Committee presented awards to the student winners of the art contest sponsored by the committee. The goals of the contest were to raise awareness of the Board’s new policy on sexual orientation and gender identity and to obtain artwork that could be used in classrooms. There were 39 entries from five secondary schools and one elementary school. The artwork will be displayed on the school district website in the near future. Contest winners are Amber Bussiere, first place; Isobel Potter, second place; Dalton Gilbert, third place; Abigail Gilchrist, fourth place; Marla Ebell, fifth place; and Kaylie Russell, sixth place. Seven honourable mention awards were also presented. Ms. Nicole Cederberg Hammond Bay Gym Ms. Cederberg, representing the Hammond Bay Parent Advisory Council, spoke to the Board about the frustration Hammond Bay students, staff and parents feel with the lack of progress towards getting a full-sized gym for the school. She asked the Board to use local funding to provide the school with a full-sized gymnasium. Dr. Liz DeMattia Nanaimo Science and Sustainability Society (NS3) Dr. Liz DeMattia made a presentation to the Board about a proposal by the Nanaimo Science and Sustainability Society to establish a science centre in the central Vancouver Island region. The society is in the process of developing a concept plan to locate such a centre in Bowen Park.
