A SPECIAL MEETING OF THE TOWN OF LADYSMITH COUNCIL AGENDA 5:00 P.M. Tuesday, June 16, 2020 This meeting will be held electronically 1. CALL TO ORDER Call to Order 5:00 p.m. in Open Session, in order to retire immediately into Closed Session. Members of the public are welcome to attend all Open Meetings of Council, but may not attend Closed Meetings. 2.
