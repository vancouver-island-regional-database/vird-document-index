NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: September 28, 2022 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Revised Draft Policy 2.5 Board Committees Representation and Annual Workplan and Revised Draft Policy 2.6 Board Meeting Procedures Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopt the revised Draft Policy 2.5 - Board Committees Representation and Annual Workplan and revised Draft Policy 2.6 – Board Meeting Procedures. Background: On June 22, 2022, the Board passed the following motion: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive the revised Draft Policy 2.5 - Board Committees Representation and Annual Workplan and revised Draft Policy 2.6 – Board Meeting Procedures and circulate as a notice of motion for 30 days, utilizing the consultation process as per Board Governance, Section 1, 2.7 – Policy Development. Community engagement plan The consultation was posted on the district’s community engagement webpage and included background information with instructions on how to participate during the consultation period: https://www.sd68.bc.ca/board/community-engagement/ Information on the community engagement period was: • Emailed to all district staff via district email • Emailed to all families via School Messenger platform • Posted on district news • Shared on social media (Facebook, Twitter and Instagram) The 30-day notice of motion consultation period began on June 27, 2022. The community had until July 31, 2022 to provide feedback by emailing consultation@sd68.bc.ca.
