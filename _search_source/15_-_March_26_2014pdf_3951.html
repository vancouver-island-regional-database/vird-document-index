Phyllis Argue, Manager, Government Relations Canadian Red Cross Western Zone The Board received a certificate from the Canadian Red Cross Western Zone in appreciation of the significant commitment the Board has made to the Red Cross Society. Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools was the first district in the province to deliver training for the RespectED program to all secondary students. The Red Cross will be using the district as a model in B.C., the Yukon and across the country.
