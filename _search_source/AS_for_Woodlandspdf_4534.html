THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: December 2, 2015 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Woodlands Secondary School Closure FIRST READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-05 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Woodlands Secondary School, 1270 Strathmore Street, Nanaimo, BC, V9S 2L9; Facility No. 068013; on June 30, 2016, and that it be given first reading. SECOND READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-05 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Woodlands Secondary School, 1270 Strathmore Street, Nanaimo, BC, V9S 2L9; Facility No. 068013; on June 30, 2016, and that it be given second reading. UNANIMOUS CONSENT REQUIRED TO READ THIRD AND FINAL BYLAW RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Woodlands Secondary School Closure Bylaw No. 2015-05 at its Special Board Meeting of December 2, 2015. THIRD AND FINAL READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-05 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Woodlands Secondary School, 1270 Strathmore Street, Nanaimo, BC, V9S 2L9; Facility No. 068013; on June 30, 2016, and that it be given third and final reading. RECOMMENDED MOTION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) relocate the Hockey, Soccer, and Beach Volleyball Academies to Nanaimo District Secondary effective September 2016. RECOMMENDED MOTION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) changes Wellington Secondary catchment area to include Cilaire as a feeder school. RECOMMENDED MOTION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) changes Nanaimo District Secondary catchment area to included Forest Park and Brechin as feeder schools.
