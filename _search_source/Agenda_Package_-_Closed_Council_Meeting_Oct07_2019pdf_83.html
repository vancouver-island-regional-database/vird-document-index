Rob Johnson By Hand Dear Councillor Johnson: RE: NOTICE OF HEARING – CONSIDERATION OF SANCTIONS Please be advised that Council is concerned that you may have acted in a manner that is unbecoming a member of Town Council by disclosing confidential information contrary to the requirements of s. 117 of the Community Charter. Specifically, it is alleged that you may have disclosed confidential information regarding Council’s intention with respect to a new library in Ladysmith to the Executive Director of the Vancouver Island Regional Library. I am placing this matter on the agenda of a Special Council meeting in closed session. The reason for the meeting is to follow fair process in considering the matter, together with a resolution in relation to your conduct. Procedural fairness calls for providing you with two weeks’ notice of this special meeting. I am therefore proposing a meeting date of September 30, 2019 at 5:00 p.m. Should you wish to have this hearing held at an earlier date, even as early as during the week of September 16 to 20, please advise the Corporate Officer in writing. You are welcome to request a specific date that we would try and accommodate. Enclosed is a copy of the draft Resolution that will be considered at the meeting for discussion, debate and a vote. Note that one of the possible ultimate outcomes of the process described in the Resolution is that Council may be considering imposing sanctions flowing from your conduct, including rescinding your appointment to the Board of the Vancouver Island Regional Library, censuring you and releasing a media release. Council may also subsequently consider progressive sanctioning, including in relation to committee appointments. I wish to expressly notify you that you may retain legal counsel to represent your interests in this matter. Prior to Council voting on the resolution, you will be provided with the opportunity to address Council regarding the contents of the draft form of resolution and draft media release. Following any submissions that you (or your legal counsel) make at the meeting, Council will consider this resolution and media release. Enclosed you will find as supporting material a transcript of a telephone message left for the Chief Administrative Officer by the Executive Director of the Vancouver Island Regional Library. We have requested a written statement from the Executive Director of the Vancouver Island Regional Library and will provide it to you as soon as we receive it. In regard to any Council decisions, we will provide you with oral reasons for our decision(s).
