NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: February 10, 2021 Business Committee Secretary-Treasurer, Mark Walsh Executive Director Planning Operations, Pete Sabo Forest Park Elementary School Waterworks Rights-of-Way, Bylaw 2021 Recommendation The Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) pass all three readings of the Bylaw, cited as “Forest Park Elementary School Rights-of-Way Bylaw, 2021” in one meeting, and that the Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No.68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopt the Bylaw cited as "Forest Park Elementary School Rights-of-Way Bylaw, 2021, during the February 24, 2021, Open Board meeting. Background The City of Nanaimo (“CoN”) has requested access to a portion of the Forest Park Elementary School, located at 2040 & 2050 Latimer Road, for the purpose of installing a water line to support the Lady Rose Area Watermain and Stormsewer Upgrade Project (the “Works”). Discussion A Statutory Right-of-Way (“SRW”) is required for the CoN to access, install and maintain a water line on Forest Park Elementary property. The location proposed is along a property line and within building setbacks, meaning it will not be encumbered by future expansion. Further the area affected is devoid of surface features with minimal subsurface infrastructure affected (one catch basin will be relocated to accommodate the Works). Once the Bylaw has been passed by the Board, the Secretary-Treasurer can sign both of the CoN and SRW documents: Appendix A: Appendix B: Appendix C: Appendix D: Appendix E: Schedule “A” Key Plan of the Right-of-Way, provided to depict where the SRW resides on the Forest Park Elementary site. Schedule “B” Illustrates the location of area in the SRW. Allows CoN general access using Standard Charge Terms ST100092. Allows CoN to perform works within area specified on Schedule A, using Standard Charge Terms ST100090. Forest Park Elementary School Rights-of-Way Bylaw, 2021
