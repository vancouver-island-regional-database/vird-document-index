Minutes of the Parks, Recreation & Culture Advisory Committee Wednesday, April 18, 2018 at 7pm (Ladysmith Little Theatre) COMMITTEE MEMBERS PRESENT: Tim Richards, Chair Roger Kimmerly Bruce Mason Tara Pollock Councillor Duck Paterson REGRETS: Tim Godau STAFF PRESENT: Clayton Postings Julie Tierney Gord Horth AGENDA Moved and seconded: 2018-06: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the agenda for the meeting as presented. Motion carried. MINUTES Moved and seconded: 2018-07: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the minutes of the March 21, 2018 meeting as presented. Motion carried. TOUR B. Mason hosted the meeting at the Ladysmith Little Theatre and provided a tour of the facility. REFERRED BY COUNCIL N/A UNFINISHED BUSINESS Aggie Sign Station – Committee Members reviewed the proposed sign design and location. Concerns were expressed regarding traffic sightlines and depending on nearby street lights, additional lighting may be required. Moved and seconded: 2018-08: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve in principle the sign design and the location providing sightlines for traffic will not be affected. Motion carried. NEW BUSINESS Ladysmith Youth Plan – C. Postings reviewed the emerging trends and the corresponding feedback resulting from the feedback received at the open house as well as the planned scheduled for completion. The Committee provided feedback on the process, themes and options to methods to achieve deliverables once the plan has been approved by Council.
