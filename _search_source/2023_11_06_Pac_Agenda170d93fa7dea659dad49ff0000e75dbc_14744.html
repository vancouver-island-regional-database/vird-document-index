Public Art Committee Agenda for Meeting to be held on Monday, November 6th, 2023 at 4:00pm Frank Jameson Community Centre at 810 6th Avenue The purpose of the Public Art Committee is to assist the Town with providing advice and recommendations to Council on specific Public Art projects, including selection processes, acceptance of grants, gifts, donations, bequests and deaccession as referred by Council. The Public Art Committee has two functions: (a) To advise the Town on public art issues and trends relevant to public art initiative in Town. (b) To advise on specific issues, such as donations, deaccession, and commissions of work. 1. Call to Order and Acknowledgement The Town of Ladysmith acknowledges with gratitude that this meeting takes place on the traditional, unceded territory of the Stz’uminus First Nation. 2.
