April 29, 2016 VIA EMAIL Ref: 187456 To: All Secretary-Treasurers All School Districts Re: 2015/16 Recalculated Funding Allocations Following the February 2016 Enrolment Count The adjusted funding allocations have been determined based on actual enrolment as at February 12, 2016 for distributed learning, continuing education, special needs enrolment growth, new ACE-IT enrolment, and newcomer refugees. The operating grant block has been increased by $8.96 million for 2015/16 to provide funding to all eligible students reported as at February 12, 2016 at the rates published in March 2015. The complete package is located on our website at: http://www2.gov.bc.ca/gov/content/educationtraining/administration/resource-management/k-12-funding-and-allocation/operatinggrants/k12funding-15-16. Where indicated, the tables have been updated since the autumn recalculation. Adjustments will be applied equally to the four payment dates in May and June 2016. In addition, a total of $3.98 million is being allocated to all districts to assist with the costs of salary increases resulting from the Economic Stability Dividend that takes effect May 1, 2016. The allocation is based on total full-year school-age FTE enrolment and will also be paid in four equal instalments in May and June 2016. The table attached provides the district allocations. If you have any questions about the schedule, please feel free to contact me at 778-676-4471 or by email at Rebecca.John@gov.bc.ca. Yours sincerely, Rebecca John Director, Funding and Allocation Branch Attachment pc Superintendents, all school districts.
