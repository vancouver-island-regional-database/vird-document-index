THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: October 15, 2010 TO: The Board of Education FROM: J. David Green, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: DISTRICT FACILITIES PLAN CHANGE PROCESS BYLAW BACKGROUND: At the September 28, 2010 Special Board Meeting, the Board adopted the following motion: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) directs the Secretary-Treasurer to work with the Policy Committee to draft a bylaw related to the District Facilities Plan that will outline the steps required to adopt policy changes relating to modifications to district programs and facilities that will necessitate changes to the District Facilities Plan and to bring the bylaw to the October 19, 2010 Special Board Meeting. FIRST READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: SECOND READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: UNANIMOUS CONSENT ACTION REQUIRED: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw and that it is given first reading. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw and that it is given second reading. Unanimous consent of the Board must be given to authorize the SecretaryTreasurer to read the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw a third time at the same Board Meeting. RECOMMENDATION: THIRD READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw a third time at the current Board meeting. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw and that it is given third and final reading.
