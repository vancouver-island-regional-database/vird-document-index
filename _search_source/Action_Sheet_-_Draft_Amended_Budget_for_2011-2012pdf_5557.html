THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: January 18, 2011 TO: Business Committee FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Amended Budget 2011-2012 BACKGROUND: At the November 16, 2011 Business Committee Meeting, the Secretary-Treasurer provided a financial update for the current school year, based on the September 30th enrolment information and summarized the significant financial changes since the district’s preliminary budget was approved last spring: Enrolment has increased by 117 full-time equivalent students over what was projected in the budget, but the enrolment is still 74 students below the 2010/11 school year. This year’s September 30 enrolment is 13,540 full-time equivalent students. The number of students designated in a funded special needs category has decreased by 118 students from the projections for this year’s budget and by 258 students from last year. Staffing levels in the district are close to the levels projected in the budget, with the exception of education assistant positions. The number of education assistants is 22 FTE below the budget projections, as a result of the decrease in the number of funded designated students. The district’s funding protection grant has decreased from about $5.4 million that was projected in the preliminary budget to $3.9 million due to the receipt of holdback funds of $1.6 million. At the December Board Meeting, Mr. Turin provided an update on Ministry of Education funding. He said that the Ministry has announced changes to the funding formula for 2012-13. The changes include a vulnerable student supplement; a student location factor supplement; a small community supplement; a low enrolment factor supplement; and a reduction in funding protection. Mr. Turin said that changes to the funding protection policy will have a significant impact on the district in the future commencing with the 2012-13 budget where the district will begin to see a $1.7 million decrease in its funding protection allocation. Attached to the Action Sheet is the draft amended budget for 20112012 in the amount of $124,211,864. MOTION: The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the Board of Education adopt the amended budget for 2011-12 in the amount of $124,211,864.
