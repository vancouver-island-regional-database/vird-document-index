TOWN OF LADYSMITH PROCLAMATION BC Heritage Week WHEREAS: Heritage forms the foundation of the Town of Ladysmith’s identity, connecting citizens to the stories and traditions that inspire pride, shared values, and a sense of belonging; and WHEREAS: The Town of Ladysmith recognizes and celebrates the rich and diverse cultural heritage created by all members of our community; and WHEREAS: Heritage places and spaces enrich our community, support economic activity and tourism, contribute to social well-being, and play a vital role in building a sustainable future; and WHEREAS: The Town of Ladysmith supports initiatives that celebrate heritage, recognize cultural contributions, and encourage active community participation; THEREFORE, I, Deena Beeston, Mayor of the Town of Ladysmith, do hereby proclaim February 16–22, 2026 as BC Heritage Week in the Town of Ladysmith. __________________________ Mayor D. Beeston February 3, 2026
