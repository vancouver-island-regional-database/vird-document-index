Appendix B Administrative Procedure 501 – Selection of Architect AP 501 – Selection of Architect Purpose The board shall ensure that an architect, or an architectural firm, which has demonstrated the skill and ability required to perform the work, for a particular program, is appointed, for major construction projects. Procedure For additions to and/or alterations of an existing facility, with a projected cost of $500,000.00 or less, the architect employed to design the original facility will normally be recommended to the Board for appointment to design the addition and/or alterations. For projects expected to cost more than $500,000.00, the District Building Committee will evaluate potential architects, and will recommend, to the Board, the appointment of a specific architect or firm. Evaluation Process The Facilities Department will: 1. Compile a list of potential architects for review by the District Building Committee; and 2. Prepare a "Request for Proposal" specifying: the scope of work, required completion dates, construction policies and standards, hiring of consultants, method of payments, and an interview process. The Building Committee will evaluate potential Architects based upon their past work performance with the school district or, in the event that they have not previously worked for the school district, based upon a "Proposal to Provide Consulting Services", prepared in accordance with the provisions listed below: Proposal to Provide Consulting Services: Proposal to Provide Consulting Services shall include: 1. Offer of Services An offer to provide consulting services as specified in the scope of work. AP 501 – Selection of Architect Page 1 of 4 AP 501 – Selection of Architect 2.
