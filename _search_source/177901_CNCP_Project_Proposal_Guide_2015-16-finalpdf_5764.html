Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) Project Proposal Guide 2015/16 Call for Projects INTRODUCTION This guide has been written to assist school districts and their consultants with the preparation of project proposals for the Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) - 2015/16 Call for Projects. A maximum of three separate project proposals will be accepted from each school district. School districts are responsible for the accuracy of data submitted in the 2015/16 Call for Projects. For a proposed project to be considered for CNCP funding support, the completed Project Data Sheet and all supporting documentation must be received by the Ministry before 4:30 p.m. on Friday, January 9, 2015. All submission material should be emailed to Craig Harris, Planning Officer, Capital Management Branch, at: Craig.Harris@gov.bc.ca ENERGY STUDY AND MECHANICAL STUDY REPORTS Reports should be completed either by a school district Energy Specialist/Manager or a qualified independent consultant. The Ministry may require the author(s) to validate or clarify the content of submitted reports.
