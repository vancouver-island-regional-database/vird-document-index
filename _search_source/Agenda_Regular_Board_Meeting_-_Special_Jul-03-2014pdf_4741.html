ADJOURNMENT Page 1 of 2 3.1 THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET TO: The Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Brent Spohn, Facilities Planning Supervisor DATE: July 3, 2014 SUBJECT: Cedar Civil Works – Tender 2445 Results BACKGROUND:  On May 30, 2014 a public tender was issued for the civil works in support of the Cedar Elementary conversion project. The civil tender included site works, playground works and playfield construction.  Included in the $2.0M AFG project envelope are the site works and playground works.  At the Business Committee Meeting on June 11, 2014 staff recommended that the Board consider local capital funds for playfield works.  The public tender closed on June 13, 2014 and four responses were received as per the summary attached.  Our consultant, Newcastle Engineering Ltd., has analyzed the four tender submissions and recommends the low bidder Superior Excavating Ltd. be awarded the civil works at a cost of $629,684 inclusive of GST.  Newcastle Engineering Ltd.’s tender breakdown is as follows: Tender 2445 (Incl. in Budget) Playground (Not incl. in Budget) Playfield Totals $312,988 $316,696 $629,684 GST Rebate Fees/Contingencies (10,255) 55,159 Totals $361,600 RECOMMENDATIONS: 1. “That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) directs staff to award Tender 2445 to the low bidder Superior Excavating Ltd. in the amount of $629,684.” 2. “That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves the allocation of $361,600 from the local capital reserve for the playfield portion of Tender 2445 inclusive of fees/contingencies.” Page 2 of 2
