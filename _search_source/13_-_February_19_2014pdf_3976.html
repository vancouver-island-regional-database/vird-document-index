Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No. S13 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Welch Folio S14/02/19-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Transportation Proposal from Senior Staff for EDR French Immersion Students Folio S14/02/19-02 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Welch IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Bard That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith), utilizing existing transportation routes, provides school bus transportation services from stops at Davis Road Elementary, Ladysmith Primary and Ladysmith Intermediate to North Oyster Elementary for students currently enrolled in French Immersion at Davis Road Elementary. The service will be offered on a transition basis until June 2021, with parents receiving annual reminders that the service will be discontinued after that date. Courtesy riders will be permitted, if space permits, with first priority given to siblings of Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held February 19, 2014 French Immersion students.
