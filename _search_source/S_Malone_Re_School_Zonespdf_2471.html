To: School District 68 Board From: Sharen Malone Date: November 14, 2017 Re: School Zones I am writing to request your consideration of the removal ofa School Zone speed limit and the installation of another. The zone to be removed is on Uplands Drive and is for the benefit of the students of Uplands Park Elementary School. I have been driving this stretch of road for 10 years and I can honestly say that, in that time, I have probably seen no more than a dozen students on this section of road. Yesterday, I was there at 2:45 p.m. and the only pedestrian was a man waiting at the bus stop. This School Zone is approximately 700 meters long on a road that is not adjacent to the school.
