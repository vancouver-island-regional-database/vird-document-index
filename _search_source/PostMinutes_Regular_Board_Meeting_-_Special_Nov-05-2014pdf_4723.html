THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 688 MINUTES OF SPECIAL BOARD MEETING HELD NOVEMBER 5, 2014 Trustees D. Neary, in the Chair D. Allen B. Bard J. Brennan N. Curley K. Howland B. Robinson T. Saunders S. Welch CALL TO ORDER Staff P. Turin, Secretary-Treasurer J. Blain, Deputy Superintendent D. Reimer, Director of Communications C. Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No. S07 The Chair called the meeting to order at 5:30 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Saunders IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan Folio S14/11/05-01 APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio S14/11/05-02 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) waives the forty-eight hours notice for tonight's Special Board Meeting. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Five Acres Junior Alternatives School - Bylaw Mr. Turin reviewed the sale of the Five Acres Property and outlined the requirement to pass a bylaw to dispose of the property. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio S14/11/05-03 That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Disposal of Real Property Bylaw No. 2014-02 for the Five Acres Junior Alternatives School and that it is Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held November 5, 2014 given first reading. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Welch IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan Folio S14/11/05-04 That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Disposal of Real Property Bylaw No. 2014-02 for the Five Acres Junior Alternatives School and that it is given second reading. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Welch Folio S14/11/05-05 That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Disposal of Real Property Bylaw No. 2014-02 for the Five Acres Junior Alternatives School at its Special Board Meeting of November 5, 2014. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Welch Folio S14/11/05-05 Local Capital Funds Folio S14/11/05-06 That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Disposal of Real Property Bylaw No. 2014-02 for the Five Acres Junior Alternatives School and that it is given third and final reading. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) allocates the net proceeds from the sale of the Five Acres Junior Alternatives School to the local capital funds account for the rebuild of the Nanaimo District Secondary School. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held November 5, 2014 ADJOURNMENT Folio S14/11/05-07 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 5:25 pm. Dot Neary, Board Chair Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer
