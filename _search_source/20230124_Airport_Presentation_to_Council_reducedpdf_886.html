Nanaimo Airport (YCD) Your Island Gateway to the World Ladysmith Council Meeting Tuesday, January 24, 2023 The Airport Lands ✈522 acres located in Cassidy BC ✈Located on the traditional lands of: ✈Stz’uminus First Nation ✈Snuneymuxw First Nation ✈Vancouver Island’s most centrally located airport Governance of YCD Incorporated as a not-for-profit authority under the Canada Corporations Act Part II – August 1990 Mission Statement To provide safe, financially sustainable, friendly, high quality airport services and facilities that enhance economic prosperity and meet the needs of the communities we serve.
