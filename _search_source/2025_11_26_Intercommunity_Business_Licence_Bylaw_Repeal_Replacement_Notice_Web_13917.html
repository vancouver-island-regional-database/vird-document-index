NOTICE INTER-COMMUNITY BUSINESS LICENCE BYLAW REPEAL AND REPLACEMENT Repeal and replacement of “Town of Ladysmith Inter-Community Business Licence Bylaw 2013, No. 1839” In accordance with Section 59 of the Community Charter, notice is given that the Council of the Town of Ladysmith intends to consider “Town of Ladysmith InterCommunity Business Licence Bylaw 2025, No. 2230” at its Regular meeting on December 2, 2025. In general terms, the proposed bylaw will: 1. 2. Restrict access to an Inter-Community Business Licence to resident businesses; and Repeal “Town of Ladysmith Inter-Community Business Licence Bylaw 2013, No. 1839” A copy of “Town of Ladysmith Inter-Community Business Licence Bylaw 2025, No. 2230” is available for viewing on the Town’s website at https://www.ladysmith.ca/city-hall/news-media/publicnotices or in person Monday through Friday, except Statutory Holidays, between the hours of 8:30am and 4:00pm at Town of Ladysmith City Hall, 410 Esplanade, Ladysmith, BC V9G 1A2 (250.245.6400).
