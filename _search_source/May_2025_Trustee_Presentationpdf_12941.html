Together…Making a Difference Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools Presentation May 28, 2025 Kathy Berg, NLFS Board Chair Crystal Dennison, NLSF Exec Director Nanaimo-Ladysmith Schools Foundation Through partnerships and collaboration, we continue to provide resources, programs and support to NLPS students  Raise awareness to increase support from Community  Develop & implement efficient, direct-to-student supports  Quick and efficient response to requests  Allocate funds to meet student needs  Provide confidential support for students  Encourage graduating students to pursue post-secondary education and training  Provide supports to schools where funds are not otherwise available Diverse Programs -
