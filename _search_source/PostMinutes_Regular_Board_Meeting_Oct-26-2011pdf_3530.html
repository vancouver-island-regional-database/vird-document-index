Mr. Hutchinson gave an update on the BCTF job action. He said that starting on Monday, exempt staff will be deployed to provide before and after school supervision in support of the decision to move recess to the end of the school day. Current plans are for principals to issue report cards in November; however because the reports will likely include very little student achievement information the district will be sending a letter to parents in advance to prepare parents for this eventuality. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS School Janitorial Services Update Mr. Sabo reviewed the information in the Agenda and responded to questions from Trustees. He said the district will undertake a third-party review of janitorial services which will look at workloads for custodians, comparing SD68 to other districts, as well as reviewing the state of cleanliness in schools. The Board asked for the review after the budget process last spring, when there was a 1.5 FTE reduction in custodian positions.
