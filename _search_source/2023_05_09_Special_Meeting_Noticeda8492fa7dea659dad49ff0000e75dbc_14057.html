NOTICE SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING The Town of Ladysmith Council will meet in person at Ladysmith Seniors Centre 630 2nd Avenue on Tuesday, May 9, 2023at 6:30 PM for a Special Council Meeting, immediately followed by a Regular Committee of the Whole Meeting Pursuant to “Council Procedure Bylaw 2009, No. 1666” notice is provided that Council will hold a Special Meeting on Tuesday, May 9, 2023 at 6:30 p.m. The meeting will include the following business: • Adoption of “Financial Plan Bylaw 2023, No. 2141” • Adoption of “Property Tax Rates Bylaw 2023, No. 2142” • Adoption of "Sewer Parcel Tax Bylaw 2023, No. 2143" • Adoption of "Water Parcel Tax Bylaw 2023, No. 2144” Corporate Officer (M. O’Halloran)
