TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE GOVERNMENT SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, JUNE 21, 2010 – 6:00 P.M. LADYSMITH PRESENT: Councillor Duck Paterson, Chair Councillor Lori Evans Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Scott Bastian Councillor Bruce Whittington Sandy Bowden Joe Friesenhan Joanna Winter ABSENT: Councillor Steve Arnett STAFF PRESENT: Pat Durban Felicity Adams CALL TO ORDER Councillor Paterson called the meeting to order at p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda be adopted as amended / circulated. 2010- 037 MINUTES 2010- 038 It was moved, seconded and carried that Government Services Committee minutes of April 19, 2010 be adopted as circulated. DELEGATION Government Services Committee May 17, 2010 Page 1 REPORTS 2010-03X TOWN OF LADYSMITH ANNUAL WATER REPORT That Council approve the 2009 Annual Water Report for submission and authorize the report to be uploaded to the Town’s web page. PROPOSED SOLAR HOT WATER READY BYLAWS 2010-03X That the Committee recommend to Council that staff prepare the bylaws to require that all new single family residences be made solar hot water ready. SEWER TREATMENT PLANT PHASE TWO – TENDER RESULTS 2010-04X That the Committee recommend to Council that all submissions received for Tender #218.066.0304 Waste Water Treatment Plant Upgrade - Biosolids Phase 2 be rejected, and that a redesign be completed without the construction of Autothermal Thermophilic Aerobic Digesters in this phase of the Waste Water Treatment Plant Upgrade. GOLF COURSE LAKE – IRRIGATION 2010-04X It is recommended that the Committee recommend that Council authorize the construction of a lake on the Ladysmith Golf Course for the purpose of irrigating both the Golf Course and the Holland Creek Ballpark. Bud Bell, Sealegs Kayaking Log Debris at Transfer Beach 2010-04X That the Committee consider if it wishes to recommend to Council to direct staff to clean up log debris at Transfer Beach. Rob Johnson James Dunsmuir’s Rail Car 2010-04X That the Committee consider if it wishes to refer this matter to staff for review, investigation and report back.
