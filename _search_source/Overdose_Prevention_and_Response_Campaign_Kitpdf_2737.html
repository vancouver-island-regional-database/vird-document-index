Overdose Prevention and Response Campaign Kit This kit contains messages and products to make it easy for your organization and partner organizations to support the province-wide overdose prevention and response campaign. If you have any questions or would like to request certain products, please contact healthyfamiliesbc@gov.bc.ca.
