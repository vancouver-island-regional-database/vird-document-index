SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) POLICY NO. 1450 TRUSTEE APPOINTMENT - ASSIGNMENT OF SCHOOLS To ensure familiarization with school programs and school related issues the Chairperson of the Board shall annually appoint each Trustee to a zone/family of schools (containing elementary and secondary schools where possible), excluding the assignment of a zone/family of schools to the Board Chair. To promote Trustee knowledge of the entire district, zone assignments shall normally be ratified by the Board on an annual basis, effective July 1 of each year. Such assignments shall be presented to the Board for ratification at a Regular Meeting prior to July 1 of each year. In the year of an election, incumbent trustees will carry on in their current appointments while newly elected trustees will take on the out-going trustees’ appointments for the balance of the school year.
