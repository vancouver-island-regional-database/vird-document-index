Appendix 1 THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) WELLINGTON SECONDARY SCHOOL UTILITY RIGHTS-OF-WAY BYLAW, 2017 WHEREAS section 65 (5) of the School Act provides that a board of education may exercise a power with respect to the acquisition or disposal of property owned or administered by the board only by bylaw; AND WHEREAS pursuant to Section 96 (1) of the School Act, “land” includes any interest in land, including any right, title or estate in it of any tenure; AND WHEREAS: (i) As a condition of constructing and maintaining works for the supply of utility services to the Wellington Secondary School site (the “Property”), City of Nanaimo (the “City”) has asked The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (the “Board”) to grant to the City two statutory rights-of-way (the “SRW’s”) in the City’s prescribed forms; (ii) the Property is facility number 68015; (iii) the address of the Property is 3135 Mexicana Road, Nanaimo, British Columbia, V9T 2W8, and the legal description of the Property is: Parcel Identifier: 000-446-009 Lot 1 Section 5 Wellington District Plan 16516; (iv) the Board is satisfied that the granting of the SRW’s in favour of the City will not interfere with the Board’s use of the Property for educational purposes. NOW THEREFORE be it resolved as a Bylaw of the Board that the Board grant the SRW’s to the City in the forms prescribed by the City. BE IT FURTHER resolved that the Secretary-Treasurer be and is hereby authorized, on behalf of the Board, to execute and deliver the SRW’s, and all related and ancillary documents required to complete the granting of the SRW’s. This bylaw may be cited as “School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Wellington Secondary School Utility Rights-ofWay Bylaw, 2017”.
