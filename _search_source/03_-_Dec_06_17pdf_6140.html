RECONCILIATION Trustee Responses/Next Steps Folio EC17/12/06-02 All members of the Education Committee were invited to join a book club and select either "Speaking our Truth: A Journey of Reconciliation" or Unsettling the Settler Within" to have completed by January 16, 2018. Board members and Education Committee members were also invited to engage in a workshop on reconciliation on January 24, 2018. Trustee and Education Committee members will report out at the February 7, 2018 Education Committee meeting.
