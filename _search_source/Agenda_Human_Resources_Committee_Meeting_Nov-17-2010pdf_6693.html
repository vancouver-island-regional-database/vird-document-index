Committee meeting June 16, 2010 Minutes Meeting Page 4 of 19 Behavioural Interviewing The Competency-Based Interview Agenda Item Number 10.1 Page 5 of 19 Behavioural Interviewing The Competency-Based Interview • What are competencies? – Competencies are defined behavioral skills combined with knowledge and skills that will serve as indicators of success in a position. Agenda Item Number 10.1 Page 6 of 19 Behavioural Interviewing The Competency-Based Interview • What is a competency-based interview? Page 7 of 19 Agenda Item Number 10.1 – Competency based interviewing has a different focus; the focus shifting to assess the learning agility of applicants, based on identifying competencies, and using the interview process to obtain in-depth information about applicants, and how they perform, and translate learning to new situations. Behavioural Interviewing The Competency-Based Interview Page 8 of 19 Agenda Item Number 10.1 • The competency-based interview is built on the idea that past behaviour/performance is the best predictor of future behaviour/performance. • The interview will focus on the competencies that are most important for the particular job you are applying. • Some of the questions may also include knowledge based essential criteria such as the in depth knowledge of a particular area or expertise in working in a similar role previously. Behavioural Interviewing The Competency-Based Interview Agenda Item Number 10.1 Page 9 of 19 • Questions are likely to start with: "Please give an example of when ." "Please describe an occasion when ." "Tell us about a time when ..." – How did you approach it? – What were the steps you took? – Why did you do it that way? – What did you learn from the experience? – How would you have done it differently? – How have you used what you have learned since then? Behavioural Interviewing The Competency-Based Interview • Multiple sources of information about the capacity of applicants to perform the duties of a posted position. • Clear job description identifying duties and required qualifications.
