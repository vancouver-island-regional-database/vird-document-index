September 23, 2015 Ref 184171 Dear District Superintendents: We would like to provide you with an important update regarding the BC Early Years Centres. The Provincial Office for the Early Years continues to expand this network and there is currently an application process underway to identify up to 16 more Early Years Centres. Health authorities and school districts are key partners in Early Years Centres and it is likely that many of your staff may be involved in leading or supporting applications. The deadline is October 28, 2015 and more information can be found on BC BID www.bcbid.gov.bc.ca. The current 26 Early Years Centres continue to evolve and each site is required to develop a three year strategic plan that will outline key goals, strategies, indicators of success and partnerships over the next three years. Critical stakeholders in BC Early Years Centres, including health authorities and school districts, will be invited to contribute and support this plan. Given the interconnections with health authorities, school districts, and MCFD offices in relation to early years, this information will provide you, as a key partner, greater understanding as to the intention of this work. Please find attached the key service features and strategic plan document that has been completed as an example, as your staff may be involved and consulted as part of this planning. I would like to thank you again for your continued support in facilitating these connections. The Centres have told us that your support fosters important connections in collaboration and provision of services for families raising young children resulting in improved outcomes for young children.
