Debot, DASG Rep Meeting No. B04 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:02 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. ADDITIONS TO THE AGENDA Folio BC18/01/17-01 DELETIONS TO THE AGENDA Annual Transportation Route Review be added as item 8.4 CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held January 17, 2018 There were none. CHANGE IN ORDER There were none. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio BC18/01/17-02 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY APPROVAL OF THE MINUTES Minutes - November 15, 2017 Folio BC18/01/17-03 That the minutes from the Business Committee meeting held November 15, 2017, be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS There were none. SENIOR STAFF REPORTS Superintendent Blain Re: Amended 2017-2018 Budget Superintendent Blain presented the draft Amended 2017-2018 Budget for information purposes. The presentation is attached to the Agenda. Superintendent Blain Re: NLPS 2020 Vision for Learning Progression Calendar Folio BC18/01/17-04 Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh Re: Appointment of Audit Firm IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic That the Business Committee recommend that district staff look at the possibility of an additional Special Business Committee Meeting in April for department presentations and to present a draft 2020 Vision for Learning Progression Calendar to the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) for approval. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held January 17, 2018 Folio BC18/01/17-05 Director of Planning and Operations Sabo Re: Annual Transportation Route Review Folio BC18/01/17/06 Folio BC18/01/17-07 CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM REGULAR BOARD MEETING IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) appoint KPMG as auditors for School District No. 68 for a term of 3 years, with an option for two one-year renewals. The first audit year will be fiscal year ending June 30, 2018. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Sabo and Mr. Prevost presented the 20172018 Bus Route Review to the Business Committee. This presentation is attached to the Agenda. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Solomon That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive, as information, the 2017-2018 Annual Transportation Route Review as recommended by staff. Opposed by Trustee Routley CARRIED IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) rescind Motion S14/02/1902 “That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith), utilizing existing transportation routes, provides school bus transportation services from stops at Davis Road Elementary, Ladysmith Primary and Ladysmith Intermediate to North Oyster Elementary for students currently enrolled in French Immersion at Davis Road Elementary. The service will be offered on a transition basis until June 2021, with parents receiving annual reminders that the service will be discontinued after that date. Courtesy riders will be permitted, if space permits, with first priority given to siblings of French Immersion students.” CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held January 17, 2018 There were none. UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was none. NEW BUSINESS Trustee Kimler Re: BCPSEA Public Administrator Special Resolution S-1 School District No. 42 Ordinary Resolution O-1 Trustee Kimler will attend the BCPSEA AGM to vote on the BCPSEA Public Administrator Special Resolution S-1 and School District No. 42 Ordinary Resolution O-1. Trustees gave no objections to Trustee Kimler voting in support of these resolutions. Trustee Kimler Re: Viability of Learning Studios Folio BC/18/01/17-08 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff, in consultation with CUPE, to investigate the viability of building "Learning Studios" using NLPS staff as well as looking into the possibility of utilizing CTC students to help build them as a part of a broader, hands-on educational experience, and report back to the Board with a recommendation. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY FOR INFORMATION QUESTION PERIOD There were none. ADJOURNMENT That the meeting be adjourned. The meeting adjourned at 7:42 pm.
