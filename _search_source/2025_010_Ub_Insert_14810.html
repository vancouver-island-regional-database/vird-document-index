MACKIE & COLONIA WEIRS REMOVED The Town’s contractors, Ecora and Spider Mountain Excavators, have successfully removed the Colonia and Mackie weirs from Holland Creek, restoring roughly 1 kilometre of the creek to its natural condition. The work was required to be done by September 16 to ensure fish activities within the creek would not be adversely impacted. The Town recieved a $2.75 million provincial grant to support the remidiation of three weirs along Holland Creek. Phase one is now complete, and work on the final weir is forthcoming. In the coming weeks, environmental specialists will carry out plantings and erosion control measures to wrap up this portion of the project. The Holland Creek trail system adjacent to the creek is now fully open to the public. Learn more at https://www.ladysmith.ca/discoverladysmith/civic-recognition-program.
