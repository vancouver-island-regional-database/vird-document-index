LOCAL BURNING & WILDFIRE BANS BC Wildfire Service prohibits campfires throughout the Coastal Fire Centre, except for the Haida Gwaii Forest District. These prohibitions apply to all public and private land and help prevent human-caused wildfires and protect public safety. In addition to open fires being prohibited, the following activities and equipment are also restricted: • Fireworks; • Sky Lanterns; • Burn Barrels or Burn Cages of any size or description; • Binary Exploding Targets; • Air curtain burners; and • Tiki and similar kind of torches. WILDLIFE AWARE Nature is right on our doorstep and this means always being mindful of how human activity impacts wildlife. At this time of year, wildlife can be spotted more frequently. Don’t leave garbage or compost outdoors. Use caution when on the trails. If you see a bear or cougar, please report it immediately. Reports of wildlife in an urban setting or reports of wildlife in conflict should always be reported to the BC Conservation Officer Service Conservation reporting line 1.877.952.7277 (RAPP) or #7277 on the TELUS Mobility Network.
