NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS STRATEGIC DIRECTIONS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: April 8, 2026 Strategic Directions Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Administrative Procedure 501.9AP – Performances, Presentations and Materials by Outside Groups Background This information sheet outlines a new administrative procedure related to risk management for outside performers and presentations within schools that balances access to our community and the protection of our students. Discussion The district is staﬀed by skilled professionals who are entrusted to exercise sound judgment in supporting student learning. At the same time, the district is committed to ensuring that students are not exposed to material that is oﬀensive, discriminatory, or age inappropriate. This administrative procedure is intended to support staﬀ in continuing to access community-based opportunities that enhance the student educational experience, while ensuring appropriate oversight of materials and content presented in schools or at external events that our schools participate in. Similar to existing ﬁeld trip processes, the procedure establishes a consistent framework for review and approval at the school level. To inform the development of this procedure, a working group of principals and vice-principals was convened. The objective was to design an approach that is practical, eﬀective, and does not create an unnecessary administrative burden for school staﬀ. The procedure aims to balance due diligence with ﬂexibility, enabling schools to access valuable external opportunities in a timely manner. The procedure also clariﬁes that principals may engage external performers or presenters, provided that any associated agreements fall within their established signing authority.
