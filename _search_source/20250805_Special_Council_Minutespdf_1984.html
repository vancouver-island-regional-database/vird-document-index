MINUTES OF A SPECIAL MEETING OF COUNCIL Tuesday, August 5, 2025 6:30 P.M. Ladysmith Seniors Centre 630 2nd Avenue Council Members Present: Mayor Deena Beeston Councillor Gourlay Councillor Jacobson Councillor Mayor McKay Councillor Paterson Councillor Stevens Council Members Absent: Councillor Virtanen Staff Present: Allison McCarrick Julia Dewijn Erin Anderson Mark Van Vliet Chris Barfoot Sue Bouma Jake Belobaba Hayley Young _____________________________________________________________________ 1. CALL TO ORDER AND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT Mayor Beeston called this Special Meeting of Council to order at 6:28 p.m., recognizing with gratitude that it was taking place on the unceded territory of the Stz’uminus First Nation. 2. AGENDA APPROVAL CS 2025-195 By unanimous consent, Council approved the agenda for this Special Meeting of Council for August 5, 2025. Town of Ladysmith Special Council Meeting Minutes: August 5, 2025 1 RECESS CS 2025-196 By unanimous consent Council recessed at 6:30 p.m. to allow the delegation to join the meeting via Zoom. Council reconvened the meeting at 6:31 p.m. 3. DELEGATIONS 3.1 Allan Neilson, Local Government Consultant Allan Neilson, Local Government Consultant, presented a summary of the Economic Development Cowichan (EDC) service review, including its purpose, current challenges, and preliminary findings. Council heard that EDC operates with budget and staffing limitations, serves a diverse region, and faces uncertainty about its role and priorities. Key functions such as information gathering, advocacy, coordination, and partnerships were identified as valuable, with sector-specific initiatives receiving stronger support than others. Mr. Neilson sought Council’s input on the value of EDC to Ladysmith and on priorities for future regional economic development activities. He responded to Council's questions. 4. ADJOURNMENT CS 2025-197 That this Special Meeting of Council be adjourned at 7:00 p.m. Motion Carried CERTIFIED CORRECT Mayor (D. Beeston) Corporate Officer (S.
