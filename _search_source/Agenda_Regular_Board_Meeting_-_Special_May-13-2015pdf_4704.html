Bylaw for Fiscal Year 2015-2016 Background: Section 113 of the School Act, “Adoption of the Budget” states: 113. (1) A board, by bylaw, (a) must adopt an annual budget on or before June 30 of each year for the next fiscal year, and (b) may amend the annual budget adopted under paragraph (a) (2) If an operating grant to a board is amended under section 10.63 (6) or a grant is withheld or reduced under section 117(1), the minister may order that (a) the board, by bylaw, must amend its annual budget, and (b) the board must send a certified copy of the amended annual budget to the minister within 60 days of the order of the minister (3) At the request of the minister, the board must send to the minister a certified copy of the annual budget.
