2024-11-04

TOWN OF LADYSMITH PROCLAMATION Louis Riel Day WHEREAS: The Métis people are recognized as one of Canada’s aboriginal peoples; and WHEREAS: the Métis culture, rich in spiritual beliefs and colourful traditions, is an integral part of British Columbia’s multicultural character; and WHEREAS: throughout history, Métis citizens have made significant contributions to the development and success of our province; and WHEREAS: Louis Riel has made an enormous contribution towards the development of this country; and WHEREAS: British Columbia recognizes “Louis Riel Day”, November 16th in commemoration of the anniversary of the death of Louis Riel. THEREFORE, I, Mayor Beeston, Mayor of the Town of Ladysmith, do hereby proclaim November 16, 2025 as “Louis Riel Day” in the Town of Ladysmith, British Columbia. _______________________________ Mayor D. Beeston November 4, 2025
