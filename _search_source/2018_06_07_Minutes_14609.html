Minutes of the Parks, Recreation & Culture Advisory Committee Wednesday, Jun 7, 2018 at 6:15pm COMMITTEE MEMBERS PRESENT: Tim Richards, Chair Roger Kimmerly Bruce Mason Tara Pollock Tim Godau Gord Horth REGRETS: STAFF PRESENT: Clayton Postings Councillor Duck Paterson AGENDA Moved and seconded: 2018-13: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the agenda for the meeting as presented. Motion carried. MINUTES Moved and seconded: 2018-14: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the minutes of the May 16, 2018 meeting as presented. Motion carried. REFERRED BY COUNCIL Ladysmith Youth Plan – Committee members reviewed the draft plan in detail and made the following recommendation to Council: Moved and seconded 2018-2015: That the Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee enthusiastically receive the draft Ladysmith Youth Plan and recommend that Council make it a priority to implement plan including the appointment of a youth representative to the Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee. Meeting adjourned at 6:45PM Next Meeting will be held at 7:00pm, September 19, 2018 at Frank Jameson Community Centre.
