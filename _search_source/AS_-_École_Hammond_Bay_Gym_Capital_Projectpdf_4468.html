Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION ACTION SHEET DATE: February 21, 2018 TO: The Board of Education FROM: Carrie McVeigh, Secretary - Treasurer SUBJECT: Capital Project Development École Hammond Bay Gym Expansion Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to move forward with plans to complete the new gymnasium at École Hammond Bay as Phase 1 of the Facility Plan. Background: At the Business Committee meeting on February 14, 2018, the Business Committee recommended the following motion: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to move forward with plans to complete the new gymnasium at École Hammond Bay as Phase 1 of the Facility Plan.
