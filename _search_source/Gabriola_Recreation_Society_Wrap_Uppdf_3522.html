THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: November 23, 2011 TO: Board of Education FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations SUBJECT: Regional District of Nanaimo – 2010 SCC Round 2 – Wrap up Discussion As the Board is aware, School Community Connections approved two rounds of funding to the District and the Regional District of Nanaimo with respect to licensing space to the Gabriola Recreation Society at Gabriola Elementary School. School Community Connections Round 1 grant was completed and the wrap up report submitted by June 30, 2011. School Community Connections Round 2 grant’s wrap up is due January 12, 2012. The construction is complete, the grant expended and the tenants have occupied the space. The project financial summary is as follows: School Community Connections Round 2 Budget $ 5,000.00 $ 10,000.00 $ 15,000.00 $ 8,000.00 $ 38,000.00 Gabriola Recreation Society SCC Round 1 SCC Round 2 2011/12 AFG Expenditure $ 5,000.00 $ 10,000.00 $ 15,000.00 $ 26,000.00 $ 56,000.00 The final summary will be reported to School Community Connections as indicated in the attached “2010 Final Report Form – Round 2”.
