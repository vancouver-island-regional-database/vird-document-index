Discussion Over the past year, staff have completed financial health checks at three elementary schools. The purpose of these reviews is to evaluate each school’s compliance with district financial processes, identify best practices, share operational knowledge, and ensure ongoing alignment between school operations and the central finance department. They also provide an added safeguard by identifying any potential fraud or unnecessary risk to the district. The audits did not identify any significant areas of concern. However, along with a few required process changes, staff issued several process improvement recommendations primarily focused on deposits, purchasing cards, and budget management. Staff will continue to conduct internal audits as capacity allows throughout the year. The next update will be provided in December 2025.
