638 Second Street, Nanaimo, BC V9R 1X9 (250.753.7524) 10 February 2016 Dear Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools Trustees: Thank you for your time and consideration regarding saving this historic landmark, Harewood Elementary School. We at Compass Church have a vision for this building and appreciate your willingness to hear it tonight. Harewood Elementary School stands in the very heart of historical Nanaimo, and a large residential core. The location is perfect for a variety of community services to the people of Harewood. Our church, along with a growing number of other non-profit organizations, is struggling to find usable space from which to serve the people of Nanaimo. We hope you will see the value of preserving this space for the benefit of the community, while relieving the school district of expense and liability. The following is a summary of the reasons we ask you to consider saving the school for our community:  Our Vision: To create a permanent facility as a centre for community activity and services for the Harewood area  Our activities at the moment are: o Adopt – A - Block: A weekly program that takes food into the Harewood community reaching approximately 500 homes in low income neighbourhoods. o Youth Programs: After school activities for kids from grade 4 through grade 12. Leadership is taught to those in grade 6 and up. o Summer Kids Activities: We hire a summer student every year to put on weekly activities in local school yards and parks, as well as a Block Party in the Harewood area. o Leadership Development: Training programs for young adults, men and women.  Our vision for this facility: o Music For Kids: We have interested musicians that would like to teach young people eager to learn but who have neither money nor instruments. o Daycare: We believe there is the need for accessible child care services in this area. o Community Health Services: We would like to see health clinics, education, and counseling for those who might not otherwise have access. o Student Housing: We envision one floor of this building being used for student housing. o Seniors Center: We see an opportunity to create neighbourhood centre specifically offering seniors drop-in, activities, courses, and companionship. o Education: We have the material and leaders to teach life skills and accredited bible courses. In addition, we have skilled leaders prepared to offer practical courses of benefit to the community. For example: o o o o o Cooking on a Budget Parenting Job Skills Back Yard Gardening and Food Production Dance, Sewing, Crafting, etc. o Cooperating with Non Profits: Our vision includes working with various non-profits cooperating to share the facility, making it a hub of community resources. To date we have interest from: o o o o o  Loaves and Fishes Community Food Bank (serving 3500 people) Jericho Road Nanaimo Harewood Community Church Light of Christ Church Others as they learn of our plans Heritage Value: It would be a great loss to the community to demolish this historical building. It appears on the City of Nanaimo heritage registry, recognized as an example of Classical Period Revival Style architecture of which there are no other remaining public buildings in our city. If you agree to save this building we would together be preserving an important piece of Nanaimo history! Other island communities have recognized the value of similar Classical Period school assets they possess, and have moved to preserve and repurpose them. Community effort and vision have made this happen. The success of these efforts in these communities, despite overwhelming odds they all faced, is undeniable.  Benefits to Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools: o Save $500,000 in demolition costs o Eliminate liability (immediately) o Preserve irreplaceable Nanaimo School District history  To move ahead we are asking you to consider: o Pausing abatement and demolition activities immediately o Grant access for inspection of the site o Rent Compass Church Nanaimo the entire property as-is, where-is from March 1, 2016 to December 31st, 2016 (existing rentals given courtesy) o Consider transferring ownership of the buildings (and the attendant liabilities) permanently to Compass Church Nanaimo Thank you for your attention to this pressing matter. We are approaching you in the belief that this building has tremendous value to the citizens of Harewood, but that this value can no longer be realized as a school. We appreciate the liability the facility is to Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools, as well as the significant expenses you face for demolition and disposal. Acting in cooperation, we have the opportunity to save this iconic building which has stood proudly at the centre of Harewood for over a century. It has no equal in our city, and we hope you will agree that saving it as we propose will prove both a relief to the District and a gift to the community. Respectfully submitted: Lisa Wengel Compass Church Nanaimo
