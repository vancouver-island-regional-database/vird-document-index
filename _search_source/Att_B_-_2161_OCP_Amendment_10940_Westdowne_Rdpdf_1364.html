Attachment B TOWN OF LADYSMITH BYLAW NO. 2161 A Bylaw to amend “Official Community Plan Bylaw 2022, No. 2200” The Council of the Town of Ladysmith in open meeting assembled enacts the following amendments to “Official Community Plan Bylaw 2022, No. 2200”: 1. “Map 1 – Future Land Use”, is amended by changing the designation from Neighbourhood Residential to Manufactured Home Park for the subject property at Lot 1, District Lot 72, Oyster District, Plan 24076 (10940 Westdowne Road), as shown in Schedule 1, attached to and forming part of this Bylaw. Citation 2. This Bylaw may be cited for all purposes as “Official Community Plan Bylaw 2022, No. 2200, Amendment Bylaw 2023, No. 2161”.
