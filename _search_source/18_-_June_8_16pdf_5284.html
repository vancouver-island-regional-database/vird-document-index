CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Charlene McKay President, DPAC RE: Transportation Policy Update 6:35 pm Recess 6:40 pm Reconvene Ms. McKay presented to the Business Committee her concerns regarding the new bus schedules. She has spoken to many parents in the district and starting the day after waiting 30 minutes after being dropped off by the bus for a young child is difficult for that child to start the day on the right foot. The new transportation plan needs to take into account the vulnerable students. She would like to request another committee that has the opportunity to be part of the policy discussion; see a rough draft and then be able to: help find any holes that may lie within the policy. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Solomon IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic Folio BC16/06/08-06 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that Board Governance Procedure 2.2 and 4.7.5 be amended as follows: 2.2 "A Closed Session of each Regular Meeting shall be held as early as 4:00 p.m. based on the agenda. This meeting will occur before the Regular Meeting of the Board unless there is insufficient business to warrant such a meeting or unless a change in starting time has been communicated at least 48 hours in advance. Should the business be incomplete by 6:00 p.m., the Closed Session shall be recessed and reconvened following the Regular Meeting." AND 4.7.5 "Individuals wishing to ask questions of the Board upon completion of Committee and Board sessions shall do so in writing on the form provided which includes their name, street address and email address. The back of the Question Period Form will display conditions outlined in 4.7.2, 4.7.3, and 4.7.4. The question will be reviewed by the Chair to ensure the question follows outlined procedures. The individual will be asked to verify his/her self and be afforded the opportunity to read out their own question. Each individual shall be limited to one follow-up question upon receiving a response. Questions and responses will be included in the video of the meeting and the meeting minutes and will be part of the public record". Opposed by Trustee Routley CARRIED The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that Board Governance Draft Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held June 8, 2016 procedure 4.1 be re-considered during a trial period beginning in September 2016 with recommendations brought to the Business Committee meeting in January 2017. Adjustments during this period are as follows: Trustees, the Superintendent, and the Secretary Treasurer will sit at the Board table. The NDTA, CUPE - Local 606, NSAA, DASG, DPAC and Joint First Nations Advisory Committee, shall be invited to name one representative to open meetings of the Education and Business Committees as an Advisory Resource. These representatives will be afforded opportunity to make presentations, provide input, and be available for clarification of agenda items prior to discussion of a motion. Such representatives may also attend Closed Committee Meetings when specifically invited to do so. Trustee Solomon read the following motion which did not have a seconder.
