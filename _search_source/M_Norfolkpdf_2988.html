KelseyToye From: Sent: To: Subject: Megan Norfolk Tuesday, January 26, 2016 5:48 PM KelseyToye To present to Trustees at Board meeting Jan 27 RE: French Immersion at Ladysmith Secondary School Hello, I am a parent of two daughters, aged 6 and 8, both of whom attend French Immersion at Ecole North Oyster. I am writing to voice my support for the implementation of French Immersion at Ladysmith Secondary School. As a graduate from a French Immersion School myself, I know the short term and long term bene?ts of this educationalmodel. It is my opinion that offering Ladysmith students the opportunity to continuetheir studies in their town, among their peers and within their community, is bene?cial to those individual students, the local school and the wider community of families. By supporting these students you have the opportunity to raise up a generation of Ladysmith students, which in turn raises up the town. I respectfully urge you to seize this opportunity and to feel the support of the community of students and families you work so hard for. Yours truly, Megan Norfolk
