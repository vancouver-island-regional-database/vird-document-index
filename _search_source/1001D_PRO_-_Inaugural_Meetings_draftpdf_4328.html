SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) PROCEDURE NO. 1001(D)P INAUGURAL MEETINGS/ANNUAL MEETING - Page 1 The Inaugural Meeting of the Board following the general local election of Trustees shall be held in accordance with Section 67 of the School Act and shall take place no earlier than the first Monday after December 1st following the election At the Inaugural meeting of the Board, following the general local election of Trustees, the elected Trustees shall make a prescribed oath of office in accordance with Section 49 of the School Act. The oath of office shall be administered by a Judge of a Court of Record or a Justice of the Peace. In the event that we are unable to secure a Judge of a Court of Record or a Justice of the Peace, the oath of office shall be administered by the Secretary-Treasurer. The annual meeting of the Board in years in which there is no election shall be the first meeting in December of each year. The date, hour, place of meeting and the order of business shall be arranged by the Board in November. Amended: 1999.05.26
