From the Department of Finance Date: June 12, 2018 To: Board of Education – Business Committee From: Department of Finance Subject: 2018-19 Financial Update - Q3 The following table summarizes year to date amounts to May 31, 2019 as compared to the Districts 2018-19 Amended Annual Operating Budget, submitted to the Ministry of Education in February 2019. Actuals provided within the report include Revenues and Expenditures based on all changes made to date, as presented in the Amended Annual Budget. The only change subsequent to the Amended Budget is the addition of a Local Education Agreement grant from the Ministry of Education in support of contract renewals as well as additional Rental and Leases income. Since the presentation of the Amended Annual Budget, there have been no changes in staffing levels. At May 31st, the district was 92% through the fiscal year 2018/19. The purpose of this update is to provide the Board its current financial status along with notes to explain the variances from Budget based on actuals. Expenses Educational assistants and Support staff reflect 86% expensed at May 31st, however, the actuals presented only include wages to May 24th which means one week, totaling approximately $698,000, is not included thereby overstating the budget surplus. Substitutes, at 82% expensed, is low due to the fact that the May 16-31st payroll has not yet been posted. The approximate cost of $300,000 is therefore not reflected in the actuals which overstates the budget surplus. Failure to fill is also contributing to the lower than expected actuals and will have an impact on the year-end operating surplus. Benefits are showing as only 85% expensed which is contributing to the overall budget surplus reflected, however, there are still three months’ worth of benefit costs pending as June expenses the costs for July, August and September. Services and Supplies are currently expensed at 78% of budget which is predominantly due to the volume of purchasing done at year-end. Budgeted Surplus Presented in the Operating Budget table is a surplus of $4,567,747, however, as indicated above there are wage expenses not yet reflected in actuals, triple benefit costs pending in June and year-end school based purchasing yet to be accounted for.
