Ladysmith H O M E B A S E D B U S I N E S S Ladysmith is an award-winning, small business-friendly community. Home based businesses are an important part of the Ladysmith economy, and represent over one third of the Town’s licensed businesses. During the development of the new Zoning Bylaw, residents expressed their continued support for home based businesses. In response, the Town expanded the regulations to provide more opportunity for home based businesses (i.e., permitting home based businesses in accessory buildings and allowing two businesses per property). Examples of home based businesses include bookkeeping, consulting, massage therapy, music lessons, esthetician, writing, and editing. There are also a number of trades-related businesses that retain an office at home, but conduct much of their work elsewhere, including electricians, contractors, and plumbers. In all cases, the operator must obtain a HOME BASED BUSINESS: A commercial business licence and renew it annually.
