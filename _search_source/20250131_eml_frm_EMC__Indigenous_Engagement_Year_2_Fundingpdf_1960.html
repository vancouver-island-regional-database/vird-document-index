From: Ryan Wainwright Sent: January 31, 2025 8:56 AM To: Allison McCarrick ; Subject: EMCR Indigenous Engagement Year 2 Funding - Next Steps and Instructions Good morning, EMC CAO Advisory – I hope you are all doing well and braced for the return of West Coast winter this weekend! I hope you also got to enjoy the sun while it was around. I had the opportunity to meet with EMCR earlier this week about their Year 2 funding for Indigenous Engagement (IER) related to the local authority requirements in the Emergency and Disaster Management Act (EDMA). Though our Year 1 project is just getting off the ground (Colliers Project Partners was our successful proponent, and their project team is currently gathering background and contacts to move forward), EMCR has advised that Cowichan should indicate the desire for Year 2 funding now. EMCR has assured me that we do not need a project plan for Year 2 until our current project is complete. I have provided them with documentation to support our regional approach to IER, including a copy of the Cowichan Local Authority Emergency Management Agreement and minutes from our last CAO Advisory meeting that conﬁrmed the combined IER approach. EMC will be completing the reporting on the Year 1 project. As we get closer to the March deadline, I'll send your staff the appropriate wording to direct EMCR to the combined report that EMC will submit. To indicate our combined desire to receive Year 2 funding, please direct your staff to: 1. Check the Year 2 box on the IERFP paperwork each of your organizations has received from EMCR Proceed with signing the Year 2 Modiﬁcation Agreement. If we do not need Year 2 funding once our Year 1 project is complete, we can return any disbursements and withdraw without penalty. I will bring an update on the Year 1 project and Year 2 funding to our next CAO Advisory in March. I apologize for any delay in getting this out. Our Provincial colleagues do not yet fully understand the Cowichan regional emergency management model, and it took some time to ensure we understood each other. Please don't hesitate to let me know if you have any concerns or wish for further discussion. Regards Ryan Wainwright Senior Manager, Emergency Management Cowichan
