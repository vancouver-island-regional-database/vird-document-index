Cameron, DPAC Rep Meeting No. SB10 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:05 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Chair Brennan acknowledged the passing of Ms. Linda McCourt, a long-term employee of several years and asked for a moment of silence. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio SBC14/04/22-01 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 22, 2014 UNFINISHED BUSINESS 2014-2015 Budget Mr. Hutchinson provided the Business Committee with information about administrative costs from a historic context; the mandate the Board has given the Superintendent/CEO; the value added by district exempt staff; and administrative reductions that have been made in advance of the budget process. Mr. Turin provided additional details related to proposed budget reductions, to follow up on questions asked by committee members as last week's Business Committee meeting. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Saunders That the Special Business Committee postpones consideration of budget reductions until additional information has been received. Opposed by Trustees Allen, Neary and Welch CARRIED IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Bard Folio SBC14/04/23-03 That the Special Business Committee cancels the Special Board Meeting to be held April 30th and reschedule it to a Special Business Committee meeting and that it convene a Special Board Meeting to be held as soon as possible following that meeting. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY CORRESPONDENCE Rutherford School NDTA Staff Members Correspondence from the Rutherford School NDTA staff members was provided as information to the Business Committee. QUESTION PERIOD Chair Brennan responded written questions from the audience. to the Draft Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 22, 2014 ADJOURNMENT Folio SBC14/04/22-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 7:33 pm.
