THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: January 29, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Tabled Motion re Ladysmith Zone Facilities BACKGROUND Subsequent to the Dec 18, 2013 Board Meeting, the Board Chair called for a Special Board Meeting to be held on Thursday, February 6, 2014. At the January 29, 2014 Board Meeting, Trustees will a) Pass a motion to lift the tabled motion from the table. b) Pass a motion to postpone discussions and decisions on the Ladysmith Zone Facilities until the Thursday, February 6, 2014 Special Board Meeting in Ladysmith. RECOMMENDATION That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) lifts Board Motion R13/12/18-19 from the table. “That the Board of Education tables the following motions to the January Regular Board Meeting. That the Board of Education approves the following Ladysmith zone 10 Year Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan proposals and enacts the by-laws required by the School Act regarding school closure: Ecole Davis Road • Close Ecole Davis Road, June 2014 and consolidate the English and French programs as follows: the K-6 French Immersion Program with Ladysmith Intermediate; the EDR English program grades 4-6 students with Ladysmith Intermediate; and the K-3 EDR English program students with Ladysmith Primary. Transportation will be provided as per Board Transportation Procedure 4400. The district will also apply Board Procedure 4011, The Disposition of Property, with respect to exploring the potential future use of the site. • Approve Ecole Davis Road Elementary School Closure Bylaw No. 2013-01 North Oyster Elementary • Close North Oyster Elementary in June 2015. This coincides with the opening of the newly renovated Cedar Elementary School in the fall of 2015, which may be a school of choice for North Oyster families. Provide an opportunity for further consultation around school choice and possible catchment area revisions with the North Oyster community in the fall of 2014. The district will also apply Board Procedure 4011, The Disposition of Property, with respect to exploring the potential future use of the site. • Approve North Oyster Elementary School Closure Bylaw No 2013-02” RECOMMENDATION That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) postpones discussions and decisions on the Ladysmith Zone Facilities until Thursday, February 6, 2014 at a Special Board Meeting to be held at Ladysmith Secondary School Gym.
