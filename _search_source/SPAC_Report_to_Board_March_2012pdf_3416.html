Report to Board of Education, SD68, from City of Nanaimo Social Planning Advisory Committee:  SPAC will host a Social Service Providers Workshop at the Rotary Building on March 30th to: - Review and reflect on programs or initiatives which have previously received Social development Grants or Healthy Community Grants. Provide social service agencies with information on the grant application process and provide guidance on how to write a strong application. Identify emerging needs in the community and help shape the SPAC grant allocation process.  Newly appointed members-at large will commence a three-year term in April, when SPAC will hold a visioning process and develop a work plan. Respectfully submitted by Trustee Dot Neary, SD68 representative to SPAC. March 28th, 2012
