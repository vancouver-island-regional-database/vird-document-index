Learn why this is important CAUTION: External Message Dear Trustees, Thank you for your continued dedication to our students and community. I know your work requires countless hours beyond what the public sees, and I genuinely appreciate your service. I'm writing as a parent of a child in the school district and a concerned advocate for public education who has been an educator with almost 20 years experience in three provinces, four school districts and at the ministry level supporting school boards with the various initiatives around student well-being and achievement. I have been listening to the recorded board meetings and Strategic Directions Committee meetings since my child started their education journey in this school district. While I was encouraged by several discussions, some decisions raised questions that I believe deserve thoughtful consideration and transparency. I've compiled these wonderings in the attached document. They address the narratives around frameworks, values and principles, and relationship building—areas that directly impact the climate and environment our children are immersed in and surrounded by. I've tried to come from a place of curiosity and inquiry, as I believe we share the same goal: ensuring all students have vibrant learning environments where they can thrive and reach their full potential . These wonderings reflect concerns I've heard from other caregivers and community members as well. Your responses would help us better understand the board's reasoning and strengthen the trust between our school system and the families and communities it serves. Please take a few minutes to review the attached wonderings. I appreciate your consideration in addressing these with a response. Nakurmiik for your time and consideration.
