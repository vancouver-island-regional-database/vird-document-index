Unparalleled in its neighbourliness, Ladymith is a growing community that maintains its small-town feel. Home to a vibrant downtown and charismatic waterfront – rich with heritage and public life – Ladysmith is known for its historic streetscapes, natural beauty, hillsides, and gorgeous views. A leader in climate action, Ladysmith is surrounded by cherished habitat areas that offer recreation, critical ecological services, economic prosperity, and connection with land and water. Everyone is welcome here.
