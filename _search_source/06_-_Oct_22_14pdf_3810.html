CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Hutchinson and Mr. Blain told the Board about some of the preliminary feedback that the District has received regarding planned changes to the school calendar. He said that feedback has led the district to review the variety of school day schedules at different schools. The district has an obligation to meet the Ministry requirements for hours of instruction while honouring the NDTA collective agreement. Over the next few months, schools will be asked to review and consult with parents and staff about their schedules, with the goal of making any changes for the next school year.
