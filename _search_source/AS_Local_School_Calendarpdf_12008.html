NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: May 22, 2024 Board of Education Laura Tait, Acting Superintendent 2024/2025 and 2025/2026 Local School Calendars Recommendation That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the attached revised 2024/2025 and 2025/2026 Local School Calendars. Background In the spring of 2023, the Board of Education approved the 2024/25 and 2025/26 local School Calendars. This Action Sheet will outline a proposed change to the dates for the Curriculum Implementation Day (CID). Discussion After receiving feedback from school leaders, teachers and the NDTA regarding professional learning, it was determined that having the CID fall at the end of the school year was not the best timing. On May 15, 2024, in an effort to be responsive to the system, the Calendar Committee met and selected the following alternative dates for the CID in the 2024/25 and 2025/26 school years: • • 2024/25: Move from June 26, 2025 to December 6, 2024 2025/26: Move from June 25, 2026 to December 5, 2025 It should be noted that the CID day is led by the District and is the 6th non-instruction day each year. This date change has no impact on the minimum number of hours of instruction that must be offered as per the School Act and Calendar Regulation, as directed by the Ministry of Education and Child Care Appendix A: Revised 2024/25 Local School Calendar Appendix B: Revised 2025/26 Local School Calendar
