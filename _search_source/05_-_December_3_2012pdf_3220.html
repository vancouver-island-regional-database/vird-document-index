CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Hutchinson said that the strategic plan will be a living document and that it focuses on learning and continuous improvement. Early in its term the Board has expressed a commitment to move away from a management board structure to a strategic policy governance model and to focus the work of the Board on district-wide goals that are aligned with Ministry of Education expectations and clearly linked to the improvement of student learning. Part of that commitment was to create a district strategic plan informed by consultation with a broad range of internal and external stakeholders and focused on student learning.
