TRUSTEE LIAISON COMMITTEE REPORT Trustee McKay Committee Name: Calendar Committee Update: Meeting Date: November 8, 2022. The committee gathered to hear feedback from the Joint Pro-D Committee on discussions related to professional development and planning where professional development days were best placed annually in the district calendar. A draft 3 year calendar was presented to the committee for comment. The drafts were generally accepted. A draft 3-year calendar with background information will be presented to the Board of Education at the Education Committee meeting for discussion on sending this 3-year calendar draft to the public for the required 30-day consultation process. Following consultation, the calendar committee may meet again in the new year to review public feedback and make any final recommendations to the Board of Education prior to Board approval and submission to the Ministry of Education.
