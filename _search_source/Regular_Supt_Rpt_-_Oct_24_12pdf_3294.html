Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORT Regular Board Meeting Wednesday, October 24, 2012 A. Superintendent/CEO’s Priorities (2012-2013): 1) Advancing the Learning Agenda:       An RTI “Design Team” has been formed to assist in the implementation of the model; Learning Services has made this a priority, both through the Learning Services Support Teams and professional learning for Principals, Vice-Principals, and Student Support Teachers; The Superintendent has communicated the RTI model by letter, blog, and video; The Self-Regulated Learning demonstration classroom set-up is close to complete, and Tier One Intervention Consultant Brenda Whittam-Neary has started delivering in-service in the classroom, beginning with the Instructional Coordinators. The BC Ed Plan reading initiative “Changing Results for Young Readers” is underway. With respect to the Strategic Plan, the proposed District goals have been developed. 2) Strengthening Communications:   Work on a proactive communications strategy continues: under the Office of the Superintendent ‘s Priority Program Objective connected to this, the Director of Communications has committed to developing a number of backgrounder documents on current district topics, including those connected to strategic planning; The Superintendent continues to embrace Twitter and blogging as effective communication strategies, and has recently moved into the realm of “YouTube.” 3) Developing a Performance Management Culture:   All departments have developed “Priority Program Development Objectives” and department leaders have, or are in the process of completing their Individual Performance Plans. The departmental objectives have been, and will continue to be reviewed at meetings of the Administrative Council. Strategic Plan foundational consultations are complete, and we are ready to develop the draft plan. B. Topics for Board Information and/or Decision:    The Board Strategic Planning Process Review and Proposed District Goals.
