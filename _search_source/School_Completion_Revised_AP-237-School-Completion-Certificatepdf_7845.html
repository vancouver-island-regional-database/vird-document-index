Appendix A Administrative Procedure 237 – School Completion Certificate Program (SCCP) (Evergreen Certificate) AP 237 – School Completion Certificate Program (SCCP) Purpose The Board will award an Evergreen Certificate based upon Ministerial Orders and regulations under the School Act. School Completion Certificates are intended to recognize the accomplishments of students who have identified disabilities and diverse abilities, who have succeeded in meeting the goals of their individual educational program (IEP) other than graduation. Guiding Principles The Board is committed to ensure that processes are in place for students who have identified disabilities and diverse abilities, which: ▪ ▪ accurately identify students who require modifications, and include a description of these modifications in the student’s IEP communicate accurately and regularly with parents and/or guardians regarding the educational program and progress of students using the School Completion Certificate Program (SCCP) Decision Review form. The Board is committed to provide educational programs and services to students who have disabilities and diverse abilities, which: ▪ ▪ ▪ are characterized by inclusionary practices founded on the principles of respect, acceptance, safety, and equity allows all students to experience success and challenges them to strive toward their maximum potential; and are organized to provide equitable access to curriculum and learning opportunities for all learners Adopted: January 14, 2009 Modified: February 19, 2016; June 6, 2018, March 3, 2021 Reference: Evergreen Decision Review AP 237 – School Completion Certificate (SCCP) Page 1 of 1
