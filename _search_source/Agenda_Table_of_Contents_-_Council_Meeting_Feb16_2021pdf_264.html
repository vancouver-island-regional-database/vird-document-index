FCM Grant awarded – Uplands Remediation Recommendation That Council authorize the Mayor and Corporate Officer to sign the FCM Green Municipal Fund Grant Agreement GMF 17055 titled “Uplands Environmental Assessment and Remedial Action Plan” for remediation of Town-owned property referred to as the “uplands” in the Waterfront Area Plan.
