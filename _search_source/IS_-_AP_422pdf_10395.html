NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: December 7, 2022 Business Committee Shawn Johnston, Executive Director of HR Administrative Procedure 422 – First Aid Background As part of the District’s ongoing process of ensuring the currency of our Administrative Procedures, Administrative Procedure 422 – First Aid (the AP) has been updated. This Information Sheet outlines the notable changes. Discussion Human Resources and our Health & Safety department have reviewed the AP. This process involved looking at other APs, (AP 414 – Occupational Health and Safety, AP 300 Student Accident or Serious Illness, and AP 329 Medical Rooms) for redundancy; and evaluating the language of the AP against our current practices. Language has been changed to reflect that the responsibility for maintaining first aid supplies falls to schools, not stores, as is the current practice, and antiquated language has been removed.
