Auditor General Update – The Auditor General ‘s audit of our budgeting process was to look at how school districts create their budgets and how they monitor their costs. School District 68 and four other school districts were selected for the review. A report will be forthcoming from the Auditor General Department and will likely be ready in the Spring of 2015. We are looking at changed our process for the upcoming 2015/16 Budget. The budget will be Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held December 10, 2014 done looking at our strategic plan and our goals. We will have discussions about our goals and priorities to assist us in developing the actual financial numbers.
