THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: December 16, 2015 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Routine Capital Program – Boiler Upgrades Georgia Avenue Elementary – Project No. 127019 Georgia Avenue Elementary – Project No. 127019: RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 127019 Boiler Upgrade at Georgia Avenue Elementary School in the amount of $360,000 and that it be given first reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 127019 Boiler Upgrade at Georgia Avenue Elementary School in the amount of $360,000 and that it be given second reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Capital Project Bylaw 127019 Boiler Upgrade at Georgia Avenue Elementary School in the amount of $360,000 at its Regular Board Meeting of December 16, 2015. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 127019 Boiler Upgrade at Georgia Avenue Elementary School in the amount of $360,000 and that it be given third and final reading.
