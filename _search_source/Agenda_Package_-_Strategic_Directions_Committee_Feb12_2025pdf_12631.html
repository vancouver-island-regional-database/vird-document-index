Decision – Is a decision, or lack thereof, by a District employee with respect to a student. Mootness – Refers to a decision that is no longer relevant or has no practical significance. Parent – Means a parent as defined by section 1 of the School Act. Reasonableness – Is a standard of review by which a reviewing body reviews a decision and decides whether the decision falls within a range of reasonable outcomes. Discussion The Ministry of Education and Child Care provides Boards of Education with an annual operating grant based on the number of students that will attend their schools. Accordingly, Ministry requires estimated enrolments in advance of the school year in order to provide a preliminary operating grant. Following that, in September of each year districts are required to submit their student data via the 1701 Student Data Collection process which confirms the actual number of students in attendance; Operating Grants are updated based on this information in December of each year. There are two additional data collections throughout the school year, the intervals and data collected within each is summarized below: September Enrolment Count – includes School-Age, Adults, Alternate and Continuing Education, Online Learning, Inclusive Education Level 1-3, Indigenous Education and English Language Learning students. February Enrolment Count – includes Continuing Education, Online Learning, Inclusive Education Level 1-3 enrolment growth and Newcomer Refugee Students. May Enrolment Count - includes Continuing Education and Online Learning students Once enrolment estimates are submitted, Ministry calculates preliminary operating grants using a Funding Allocation System (FAS) which applies per student rates, supplements for unique and diverse student abilities, as well as other supplement funding categories applicable to each district. In midMarch the Ministry of Education and Child Care announce the next school year’s Operating Grant amount, which provided the majority of funding to support that operational year and associated Annual Budget.
