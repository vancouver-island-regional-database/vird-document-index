NOTICE PROCEDURE BYLAW AMENDMENTS Amendments to “Council Procedure Bylaw 2009, No. 1666” In accordance with Section 124 of the Community Charter , notice is given that the Council of the Town of Ladysmith intends to consider amendments to “Council Procedure Bylaw 2009, No. 1666” at its Regular meeting on September 2, 2025. In general terms, the amendment bylaw will: a. Expand meeting conduct expectations to include attendees; b. Grant all Council members who voted on the prevailing side the right to bring a previously voted-on matter back for reconsideration within 30 days; c. Provide additional grounds for denying delegation requests; and d. Update language pertaining to legal matters. A copy of “Council Procedure Bylaw 2009, No. 1666, Amendment Bylaw 2025, No. 2218” is available for viewing on the Town’s website at https://www.ladysmith.ca/city-hall/newsmedia/publicnotices or in person Monday through Friday, except Statutory Holidays, between the hours of 8:30 am and 4:00 pm at Town of Ladysmith City Hall, 410 Esplanade, Ladysmith, BC V9G 1A2 (250.245.6400).
