Draft Minutes of the Education Committee Meeting held January 16, 2013 3 UNFINISHED BUSINESS WIFI Update - S. Sproston Manager of Information Systems Mr. Sproston joined the Committee members at the table. Mr. Sproston said that WIFI has a tendency to be an emotional item. Mr. Sproston said he was not an expert on WIFI safety and that he relied on the information provided by the BC Provincial Health Officer. He has stated that there is no convincing evidence that WIFI exposure constitutes a threat to the health of BC residents. Mr. Sproston said, that as part of our on-going commitment to student safety, the district will review the guidelines as set out by Health Canada on an ongoing basis. Trustees asked that the link to BC Provincial Health Office website be added to the document prior to being posted on the website. NEW BUSINESS Anti-Bullying ERASE F. Frisson, Asst.
