THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: June 26, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Capital Project Bylaw 126676 – Carbon Neutral Capital Program BACKGROUND: The Regional District of Nanaimo has advised that they will not be proceeding with the sewer line installation this summer. Therefore due to circumstances beyond the control of the School District the Hammond Bay CNCP project will not be moving ahead this year. SD68 will be resubmitting this project at a later date for CNCP funding once the Regional District of Nanaimo has completed the work and the sewer line is ready for connection to the school. The Ministry has enabled the project number (126592) to remain with this project in the event it is able to proceed in the future. As a replacement project, the School District asked for its other CNCP application to be considered. This project is for a boiler upgrade at McGirr Elementary School. The McGirr project is estimated to reduce 15.7 tonnes of CO2 emissions and save the School District $4,500 annually. This project fits with SD68’s boiler replacement strategy for lifecycle and energy efficiency. The Ministry of Education has accepted the McGirr application and has asked the Board adopt a Capital Project Bylaw No. 126676 to a maximum allocation of $218,750. As of June 17th the McGirr project is out to tender, closing on July 4th. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 126676 in the amount of $218,750 and that it be given first reading. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts Capital Project Bylaw No. 126676 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) in the amount of $218,750 and that it be given second reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126676 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) in the amount of $218,750 at its Regular Board Meeting of March 27, 2013. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126676 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) in the amount of $218,750 and that it be given third and final reading.
