Together…Making a Difference Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools Presentation May 24, 2023 Dan Morris, NLFS Board Chair Kathy Berg, NLFS Board Vice Chair Crystal Dennison, NLSF Exec Director Nanaimo-Ladysmith Schools Foundation Through partnerships and collaboration, we provide resources, programs and support to accomplished and vulnerable NLPS students Who We Are  Registered Charity with Constitution & Bylaws, incorp. 2008  Formerly Nanaimo Schools Financial Awards Society, est. 1982  Board of 10 community volunteers serving 2-year terms  Several committees and volunteers  Minimal staff & resources; consistent program growth How We Help Thanks to partnerships and generous donors:  Develop & implement efficient, direct-to-student supports  Quick and efficient response to requests  Allocate funds to meet student needs  Provide confidential support for students  Encourage graduating students to pursue post-secondary education and training  Provide supports to schools where funds are not otherwise available Diverse Programs to Help Students  Scholarships, Bursaries, Awards  Student Support Fund  Food4Schools  Music Initiative grants  Enhanced Learning Initiatives Scholarships Bursaries & Awards Community sponsored to honour graduating students in: Academics Field of Study Service/Leadership Affiliation Adversity Financial Need Incentive for Students to graduate and acquire additional education/training Proprietary system offering online applications Creating alumni to encourage past recipients to establish awards Commitment to increase available awards in 2024 Scholarships Bursaries & Awards 339 $411,000 132 Student Applications Scholarships, Awards & Bursaries District Authority Scholarships $165,000 District Awards Night – June 13 Port Theatre Student Support Fund Principals, teachers, staff request funds to directly support students Examples of needs: food | shoes | clothing | eye care | dental care | medical| school supplies | fees for field trips | band instrumental rental | student fees for grad, athletics Student Support Fund 900 Students received school supplies $61,000 Supports provided Emergency medical and dental expenses as well as eyeglasses. Food4Schools Providing weekly food deliveries to schools for breakfast, lunch and snack programs Food4Schools Creating equitable and sustainable support for all students regardless of where they attend. Food4Schools 34 schools providing breakfast, lunch and/or snacks to students every day 2000+ students accessing support during their school day (up from 1200 students in 2022) $340,000 food provided to schools for meals programs ($200k in-kind) $64,000 Equipment and supplies provided to numerous schools (fridges, commercial toasters, blenders, kettles etc.) Food4Schools Coming 2024!! Food4Schools Delivery Van (sponsored by Tom Harris Community Foundation) Proud to work with NLPS and numerous Community partners to ensure students have access to health meals and snacks at school.
