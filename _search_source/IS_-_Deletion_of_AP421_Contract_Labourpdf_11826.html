NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: March 6, 2024 Business Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Deletion of AP 421 – Contract Labour Background This Information Sheet is related to the ongoing process and review of district Administrative Procedures to ensure relevancy and alignment with current district practice and procedures. Staff intend to delete Administrative Procedure 421 – Contract Labour (the AP), and this Information Sheet will outline the rational behind the decision. Discussion The AP originated from the early 1980’s and district staff assume it was previously in Board Policy moved to an Administrative Procedure. The AP requires the District to hire unionized labour when contracting out. It is important to recognize that the AP is related to the contracting out of services only and has no impact on our CUPE 606 partners. Staff cannot find an instance where such preference has been sought in Request for Proposals (RFP) or Tenders and are unaware of how long it has been since a requirement was included. Rather than activate the AP, the District intends to delete it and apply our purchasing AP 513 which governs decisions associated with contracts and purchasing. We also note that the deletion of the AP allows us to follow government procurement best practices which requires competition and fair, open, and nondiscriminatory procurement processes. Finally, it should be noted the District works hard to have our own employees perform work as much as possible and the deletion of AP 421 has no impact on this practice. We have met with CUPE in advance of bringing this item forward to ensure that they are aware that the deletion of the AP has no impact on the District’s philosophy with respect to “in-housing” work, particularly associated with facilities.
