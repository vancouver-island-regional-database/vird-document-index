NEW BUSINESS 2013-2014 Amended Budget Mr. Turin reviewed the school district’s amended budget for the 2013-14 school year and said that school districts are required to prepare an amended budget once the funding for September 30th enrolment is finalized. Mr. Turin said that overall, there is no new money for our district this year. Enrolment in the district as of September 30 was 13,174, which is 89 students fewer than had been projected in the preliminary budget. Mr. Turin said that because it is projected that the district will be facing a shortfall in next year’s budget of $5.6 million, this year’s amended budget has earmarked $2.5 million to be carried forward to address that shortfall. This includes $1.5 million surplus carried forward from last year, plus an additional $1 million to be saved through a “clawback” in this year’s spending. The “clawback” is made up of $400,000 in staffing contingencies and $600,000 in services and supplies. Mr. Turin said that this leaves about $3 million in adjustments that will have to be made to balance next year’s budget and that we are facing a very difficult situation and will have to look at major restructuring.
