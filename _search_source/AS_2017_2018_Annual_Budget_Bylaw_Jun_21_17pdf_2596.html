Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: June 21, 2017 TO: Board of Education FROM: Secretary-Treasurer, Carrie McVeigh SUBJECT: School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Annual Budget Bylaw 2017/2018 Background: On April 12, 2017, the Board approved the 2017/2018 primary base budget. The annual budget bylaw reflects the full annual budget inclusive of the new Classroom Enhancement Fund (CEF). The CEF, created to fund the Supreme Court of Canada Class Size and Composition ruling, is a notional amount and the bylaw reflects the Ministry requirement that the CEF be fully expended. Staff recommends that the Board of Education pass the budget bylaw in all three readings of the bylaw during the June 21, 2017 Open Board meeting. Recommendation: Motion 1 (Pursuant to the School Act Section68 (4), a board may not give a bylaw more than 2 readings at any one meeting unless the members of the board who are present at the meeting unanimously agree to give the bylaw all 3 readings at that meeting.) That the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) give the School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 2017/2018 Budget Bylaw, all three readings at one meeting, June 21, 2017.
