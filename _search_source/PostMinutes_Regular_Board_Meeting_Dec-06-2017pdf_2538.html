THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF ANNUAL BOARD MEETING HELD DECEMBER 6, 2017 Trustees S. Rae S. Higginson N. Bob J. Brennan T. Brzovic S. Kimler B. Robinson J. Brennan N. Routley (5:04 pm) Staff J. Blain, Superintendent/CEO C. McVeigh, Secretary-Treasurer T. Davie, Assistant Superintendent D. Burgos, Executive Director of Communications K. Matthews, Senior Executive Assistant Absent Call to Order The Secretary-Treasurer called the meeting to order at 5:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Election of Board Chair Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh said she will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board and will confirm that each Trustee nominated confirms their acceptance of the nomination by asking "do you accept?" Trustee Robinson nominated Trustee Rae for the position of Board Chair. Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh asked Trustee Rae if he would accept the nomination. Trustee Rae stated that he did. Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh declared Trustee Rae Board Chair. Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 6, 2017 Secretary-Treasurer vacates the Chair and the gavel is turned over to the Incoming Chair Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh vacated the Chair and passed the gavel to Chair Rae. Election of Vice-Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Bob nominated Trustee Higginson for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Higginson if she would accept the nomination. Trustee Higginson stated she did. Chair Rae declared that Trustee Higginson is the Vice-Chair of the Board. Election of Business Committee Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Solomon nominated Trustee Kimler for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Kimler if he would accept the nomination for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Kimler stated he did. Chair Rae declared that Trustee Kimler is the Chair of the Business Committee.
