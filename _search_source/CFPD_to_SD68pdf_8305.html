CRANBERRY FIRE PROTECTION DISTRIST 1555 Morden Road, Nanaimo, BC V9X 1T6 250-754-6068 June 8, 2020 To the Board of Trustees, SD 68 This letter is written on behalf of the Board of Trustees, Cranberry Fire Protection District, to show our support of the former South Wellington School and grounds being signed over to either our community or the RDN. If this property was originally donated to be used in an educational capacity, we feel that our community could utilize the building and/or grounds within this capacity more so than the present situation of the building boarded up and the grounds not developed for public use. Such a transfer could also provide more public land and property (as park space and/or community school) available within Electoral Area A, for all RDN residents to access and utilize. We hope that some agreement can be reached, which will permit the residents of South Wellington to obtain public use of this property. Sincerely, CFPD Trustees Brian Campbell David Gregson Colen Hensen Al Bajec Richard Miller
