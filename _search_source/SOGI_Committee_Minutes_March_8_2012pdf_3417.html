SOGI Committee Minutes March 8, 2012. 3:30 pm Conference Room - Introductions - Acknowledge Trustee Allen and SOGI Committee members for work done to date. - Introduction and discussion of VSB SOGI Policy and Procedures. - Lauren concerned about first hand observations of lack of procedures in schools. Inconsistencies need to be addressed. - Samuel sees lack of representation in policy for trans and inter-sexed persons. Falling under the “plus” category is unacceptable to trans community. Policy is a great foundation to build on. Education is key. Teachers do not have the tools they need. - Terre offered some of the tools developed by the Multicultural Society as an example to adopt and possibly modify for the district. - Nanaimo Youth Q&A is doing a workshop in Vancouver, taking 6 students to develop leadership skills. Students teaching students. - Discussion around changes, goals, review and constructing an action plan to see where we are at and where we want to go. - POSTERS * reviewed contest winners  Need a “hook”  Catchy phrase/specific campaign. Members will bring ideas and suggestions to next meeting. - Letters to be written from SOGI Committee to all GSAs inviting sponsor teachers and one student rep from each group. - Bill will contact Learning Alternatives to see if they have a GSA. - GOAL to have GSA in every school/program.
