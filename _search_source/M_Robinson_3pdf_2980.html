KelseyToze Subject: FW: Harewood School ATTN: Nanaimo—LadysmithPublic Schools Business Committee RE: Harewood School Hello members and chair of the businesscommittee. Something interesting came up yesterday (Wed. Jan 6th, 2016) during the January meeting of the Nanaimo Culture and Heritage Commission. We had an excellent presentation by Chris Beaton, the executive director ofthe Nanaimo Aboriginal Centre regarding a ten million dollar cultural investment developing Indigenous Peoples Places of Culture located at Beban Park. If you are not familiar with the concept, basically it would be a centre built in the spirit of leaving a legacy and also delivering indigenous cultural activities and education for all in our community. This includes teaching languages at risk, historical teachings, learning how to prepare unique dishes, daycare facilities, the arts, recreational activitles-- and so much more. After the presentation at the end of the meeting, I got so excited that I made a motion to support the plan in principle and wouldn't you |
