MINUTES OF A MEETING OF THE MUNICIPAL SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, NOVEMBER 27, 2017 CALL TO ORDER 7:46 P.M. COUNCIL CHAMBERS, CITY HALL COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Rob Hutchins (Chair) Mayor Aaron Stone Councillor Cal Fradin Councillor Joe Friesenhan Councillor Carol Henderson Councillor Duck Paterson COUNCIL MEMBERS ABSENT: Councillor Steve Arnett STAFF PRESENT: Guillermo Ferrero Sue Bouma CALL TO ORDER AGENDA APPROVAL MS 2017-081 Felicity Adams Joanna Winter Councillor Hutchins called this Meeting of the Municipal Services Committee to order at 7:46 p.m. Moved and seconded: That the agenda for this November 27, 2017 meeting of the Municipal Services Committee be approved as amended by the following: • Remove item 7.1., “Discussion regarding Honours, Awards and Volunteer Appreciation” and refer to a closed meeting. Motion carried. MINUTES MS 2017-082 Moved and seconded: That the minutes of the Municipal Services Committee meeting held September 11, 2017 be approved. Motion carried. REPORTS Consultation Results - Keeping of Backyard Hens Council expressed appreciation for the consultation process regarding the topic of keeping backyard hens. They noted that the recommendations for a bylaw amendment would address the concerns expressed by the public. Moved and seconded: MS 2017-083 1. That the Committee recommend that Council introduce an amendment to Animal and Poultry Bylaw 1994, No. 1136 that would permit the raising of hens in backyards in appropriate residential zones in Ladysmith. Motion defeated. OPPOSED: Councillor Fradin, Councillor Friesenhan, Councillor Henderson, Councillor Paterson Building Inspector’s Report to September 30, 2017 Moved and seconded: MS 2017-084 That the Committee receive the Building Inspector’s Report for the months August to September 2017. Motion carried. Ladysmith Fire/Rescue Reports for July, August and September 2017 MS 2017-085 Moved and seconded: That the Committee receive the Ladysmith Fire/Rescue Reports for the months July to September 2017. Motion carried. Coastal Animal Control Services Reports for July, August and September 2017 MS 2017-086 Moved and seconded: That the Committee receive the Coastal Animal Control Services Reports for the months July to September 2017. Motion carried. COUNCIL SUBMISSIONS Cowichan Region Smoke Free Task Force – Update (Councillor Paterson) MS 2017-087 That the Committee recommend that Council direct staff to prepare a Town of Ladysmith Smoking Regulation Bylaw. Moved and seconded: Motion carried. Motion carried. ADJOURNMENT MS 2017-089 Moved and seconded: That this meeting of the Municipal Services Committee adjourn at 8:07 p.m. Motion carried. CERTIFIED CORRECT: Chair (Councillor R.
