March 22, 2016 Ref: 186779 All Superintendents All Secretary-Treasurers All School Districts Re: School Bus Replacement Program 2016/17 – Call for Proposals This letter is to invite you to submit your proposals for the School Bus Replacement Program for 2016/17. Ministry policy regarding bus acquisition/replacement is as follows: • • • • Type A2 mini-buses (20-29 passengers), which are more than 10-years old or have more than 250,000 km; Type C conventional buses (34-76 passengers), which are more than 12-years old or have more than 325,000 km; Type D-RE & Type D-FE (80+ passengers), which are more than 15-years old or have more than 400,000 km; or, None of the above, but the need for replacement can be substantiated. Each request will be reviewed on an individual basis. Priority will be given to those supporting new bus routes due to increased district enrolment and are without current service, and/or for the replacement of buses no longer operational or with the highest mileage. A Bus Funding Request Sheet is attached. Please return your completed sheet, in Excel format, to your Planning Officer by April 15, 2016. The ministry will review all submissions and respond to Districts by May 31, 2016. Supporting documentation must be submitted with the request. Once funding is approved, school districts must then procure school buses using the Request for Standing Offer (RFSO) process administered by the ministry and the Association of School Transportation Services of BC. You will receive Capital Plan instructions each spring with submission deadlines by June 30th; this will include school bus replacements.
