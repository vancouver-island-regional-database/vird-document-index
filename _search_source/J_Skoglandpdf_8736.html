*Note, this correspondence is from Jen Skogland Dear (insert Trustee’s name). I am writing in support of keeping all five of the Community School Coordinator’s (CSC’s) positions. I (we) have worked with them for years bringing our programming to the school district and will greatly miss the accessibility that we have relied on, by having the coordinators spread throughout the district. As we all know, this year has been a struggle to provide for our vulnerable youth. We recognize the importance of vulnerable youth having access to proactive educational and recreational programming. We’re proud to be of service to families who may not otherwise be able to access programming. We therefore, look forward to being able to once again offer programming once Covid restrictions have been eased. The CSC’s have been invaluable resources to me (us) in gathering information in what is needed and desired in the schools, access to the schools, and coordinating my (our) program(s) in the schools. With five positions, I (we) can talk to a coordinator at each of the high school catchment areas. Each catchment has it’s own special needs and qualities, which the coordinators are familiar with. The coordinators are our point person for each school that we service, which saves us time and effort. They have the knowledge and expertise which allows them to quickly and effectively process the many steps needed to run my (our) program. I trust every consideration will be given to my request. Thank-you,
