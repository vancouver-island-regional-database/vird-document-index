Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools School Consolidation Administrative Procedure (NEW) Schools approved for consolidation by the Board of Education will develop a transition plan. This plan will be developed by a School Consolidation Working Group (SCWG). The SCWG will be responsible for overseeing the various aspects of the transitions planning process outlined in the terms of reference below. The SCWG will meet 4-5 times during the school year and will be chaired by a Learning Services Assistant or Deputy Superintendent. The SCWG Chairs will report to the Board periodically and ensure that a summary report is presented to the Superintendent/CEO at the conclusion of the working group meetings. 1.
