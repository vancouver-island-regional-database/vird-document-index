May 21, 2026 Project No.2026-PRC-02 Frank Jameson Community Centre (FJCC) 2026 Roofing Program Addendum No.1 The following are changes to the Scope of Work for the above project. All costs related to these changes are to be included in the Bid Price. 1. Provide 10 mil poly plastic protection for the gym floor and pool area to protect all floor surfaces from falling debris. Ensure all exposed floor areas remain fully covered for the duration of the project. 2. The previous schedule has been revised as follows: a. Work shall commence no later than August 1, 2026, and the roof membrane installation must be completed by September 6, 2026. b. Installation of all metal flashings and miscellaneous detailing may continue after September 6, 2026. All disposal bins must be removed prior to this date. Regards, Joel Sharp, Civ.Tech.
