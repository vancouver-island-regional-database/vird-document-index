Proposed Changes to School Catchment Areas November 21, 2011 To: Parents of Forest Park Elementary School District 68 is consulting with parents on six proposed changes to elementary school boundaries in Nanaimo. The changes do not affect a large number of students, but we would like to give parents at the affected schools an opportunity to obtain more information and provide input. The following are the proposed catchment area changes:    Coal Tyee to Rock City Coal Tyee to Uplands Park Chase River to Park Avenue    Forest Park to Mountain View Pleasant Valley to Mountain View Quarterway to Mountain View More detailed information on all of these changes is available on the school district website – www.sd68.bc.ca. For the changes affecting Forest Park, we would like to provide the following information. Forest Park to Mountain View  The map on the reverse side of this information sheet outlines the area affected.  The reason for this proposed boundary change is to keep the catchment from being divided by the Nanaimo Parkway.  Effective September 2012, students living in this area would be assigned to attend Mountain View Elementary.  Currently there are two elementary students living in the affected area. Both of these students currently attend Mountain View.  The boundary change would only apply to new student enrolments.  The boundary change would also affect the secondary school that students in this area attend, since Mountain View students attend NDSS. You are invited to attend an Open House on Monday, November 28, from 6 to 8 p.m. The Open House will be held at the District Administration Centre, 395 Wakesiah Avenue, in the Board Room (enter from the upper parking lot). You can drop in any time from 6 to 8 p.m. to view the proposed changes in more detail, to ask questions of staff and provide your input. You may also provide input by emailing communications@sd68.bc.ca. The Board of Education will make the final decision on the proposed boundary changes.
