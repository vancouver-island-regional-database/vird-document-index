BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools REGULAR BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: October 24, 2012 TO: Board of Education FROM: Dave Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO RE: Learning Improvement Fund Spending Plan BACKGROUND: In the spring of 2012, the Ministry of Education established the Learning Improvement Fund (LIF). In total, the District will receive approximately 1.5 million dollars. The purpose of the fund is to provide assistance to students and teachers in classrooms with challenging compositions. The attached spending plan details how the District intends to achieve this through District Administration Centre restructuring, and the introduction of Response to Intervention (RTI), district-wide. DISCUSSION: The LIF Spending Plan was approved by the Board on June 12, 2012. It must be submitted to the Ministry of Education for approval by October 31, and is presented to the Board as information only. This copy of the Plan includes the following updates found in the Appendices: 1) New “School-Based Team Referral,” “District Consultation Requests to the Learning Services Support Teams,” and “Student Cumulative Review” forms to better enable the RTI process; 2) A copy of correspondence requested by the NDTA to all teaching staff, outlining the RTI process, and clarifying the role of Instructional Coordinators; 3) A copy of correspondence to school administrators clarifying the RTI model; and 4) A copy of the Support Staff LIF allocation for the District. RECOMMENDATION: That the LIF Spending Plan be received as information. Attach.
