Trustee Kimler Zone Report December 2016 Ladysmith Intermediate School (LIS)   Jingle Bell Hill – Mark your calendar for December 22nd at 7PM for the Ladysmith Intermediate production “The Lights of Jingle Bell Hill”, which will be held in the gym. PAC Donation – LIS PAC received a generous $5,650 donation from the Ladysmith Healthcare Auxiliary to help pay for sexual health education & swimming programs. Ladysmith Primary School (LPS)    Winter Craft Fair – Was held on Saturday December 10th. Book Fair – Congrats to PAC, staff & the community for helping the LPS book fair meet its goal of $3k this year! Christmas Concert – Such a popular event that tickets were required for the evening show & parents were encouraged to instead attend the afternoon show. Ecole North Oyster    SnowPASS – Did you know? Grades 4 & 5 students across Canada can apply for a SnowPASS, which costs $29.95 and can be used to ski 3 times at various resorts across Canada, including Mt. Washington & Whistler? Family Movie Night – PAC sponsored this fun event, which was held Friday night in the gym. The movie was “The Secret Life of Pets”! Spirit Day – This spirit day will be just two days before a late winter break. The theme is everyone’s favourite – “Ugly Sweater Day”. Ladysmith Secondary School (LSS)     Drug Awareness – As a means of addressing the recent increases in drug-related fatalities in BC LSS invited an RCMP officer to speak with students and parents on December 1st (1PM & 6:30PM) on dangers associated with a variety of drugs available on the street. LSS PAC on the Web – The LSS PAC is now active on the new ladysmithsecondary.ca website & is posting minutes and other informational items. Fresh Grade – Grade 8/9 teachers are now using “Fresh Grade” as their primary communication tool with parents. Early feedback from both teachers and parents is positive. This tool provides more robust methods of sharing student work (video, assessment rubrics & assignments). Post-Secondary Schools Visit – On Nov 22nd, LSS is hosting 12 Post-secondary schools so students can learn about programs & entry requirements. Trustee Kimler
