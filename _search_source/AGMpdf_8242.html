THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF THE SPECIAL BOARD MEETING ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING December 4, 2019 Trustees C. McKay, Chair L.M. Barron, Vice Chair T. Brzovic S. Higginson G. Keller B. Robinson C. O'Neill J. Stanley E. Wilkinson Staff S. Saywell, Superintendent/CEO C. McVeigh, Secretary-Treasurer T. Davie, Deputy Superintendent D. Burgos, Executive Director of Communications K. Matthews, Manager, Administrative Services 1. CALL TO ORDER The Secretary-Treasurer called the meeting to order at 5:03 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. 2. INTRODUCTIONS Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh introduced trustees and district staff members in attendance. Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh called for a motion to approve the Agenda. AGM19/12/4-01 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Keller IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee O'Neill That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 3. ELECTION OF BOARD CHAIR Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh said she will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Barron nominated Trustee McKay for the position of Board Chair. Trustee McKay confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. 1 Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh declared Trustee McKay Board Chair. 5. ELECTION OF VICE-CHAIR Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Wilkinson nominated Trustee Barron for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Barron confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Barron is the Vice-Chair of the Board. 6. ELECTION OF BUSINESS COMMITTEE CHAIR Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Robinson nominated Trustee O'Neill for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee O'Neill confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time. Trustee Stanley nominated Trustee Keller for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Keller confirmed his acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a third time. Trustees voted by ballot with Mr. Davie and Mr. Burgos acting as scrutineers. Results were provided to Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh and Chair McKay declared that Trustee O'Neill is the Chair of the Business Committee. AGM19/12/4-02 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Stanley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Barron That the ballots for the 2019 Election of Business Committee Chair be destroyed. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 7. ELECTION OF BUSINESS COMMITTEE VICE-CHAIR Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Keller for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Keller confirmed his acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. 2 Chair McKay declared that Trustee Keller is the Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. 8. ELECTION OF EDUCATION COMMITTEE CHAIR Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Stanley for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Stanley confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Stanley is the Chair of the Education Committee. 9. ELECTION OF EDUCATION COMMITTEE VICE-CHAIR Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee O'Neill nominated Trustee Wilkinson for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Wilkinson confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time. Trustee Keller nominated Trustee Brzovic for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Brzovic confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a third time. Trustees voted by ballot with Mr. Davie and Mr. Burgos acting as scrutineers. Results were provided to Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh and Chair McKay declared that Trustee Brzovic is the Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. AGM19/12/4-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Higginson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Stanley That the ballots for the 2019 Election of Education Committee Vice-Chair be destroyed. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 10. ELECTION OF BCSTA PROVINCIAL COUNCILOR REPRESENTATIVE Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councilor. Trustee Keller nominated Trustee Stanley for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councilor. Trustee Stanley confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time. Trustee O'Neill nominated Trustee Barron for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councilor. Trustee Barron confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a third time. 3 Trustees voted by ballot with Mr. Davie and Mr. Burgos acting as scrutineers. Results were provided to Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh and Chair McKay declared that Trustee Barron is the BCSTA Provincial Councilor. AGM19/12/4-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee O'Neill That the ballots for the 2019 Election of BCSTA Provincial Councilor Representative be destroyed. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 11. ELECTION OF BCSTA PROVINCIAL COUNCILOR ALTERNATE REPRESENTATIVE Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councilor. Trustee Brzovic nominated Trustee Wilkinson for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councilor. Trustee Wilkinson confirmed her acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Stanley for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councilor. Trustee Stanley declined acceptance of the nomination. Chair McKay called for nominations a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Wilkinson is the Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councilor. 12. ADJOURNMENT The meeting adjourned at 5:24 pm. AGM19/12/4-05 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee O'Neill IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY ________________________________ ________________________________ Carrie McVeigh, Secretary-Treasurer Charlene McKay, Chair 4
