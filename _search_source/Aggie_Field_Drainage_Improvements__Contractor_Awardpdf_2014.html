STAFF REPORT TO COUNCIL Report Prepared By: Meeting Date: File No: Re: Infrastructure Services Department October 7, 2025 1220-20 Aggie Field Drainage Improvements – Contractor Award RECOMMENDATION: That Council increase the Aggie Field Draining Improvement budget from $110,336 to $130,000, award RFT 2025-IS-10 to ADT Contracting Ltd. in the amount of $129,000 and amend the financial plan to fund the difference of $19,664 from prior year surplus. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: The Aggie Field Drainage Improvement Project involves replacing a failed 1980s drainage system with a new rain garden and overflow connection to the municipal storm drain to address persistent waterlogging issues. Following the Town’s purchasing policy, a tender was issued for the project work. There were 12 bids received; Ladysmith staff selected a contractor from among the bidders who demonstrated relevant work experience, adequate equipment, personnel and timeline, and met the Town’s “Best Value” criteria. ADT Contracting Ltd. was selected by staff for recommendation to Council. PREVIOUS COUNCIL DIRECTION: N/A INTRODUCTION/BACKGROUND: The Town tendered a project for the Drainage Improvements at Aggie Field. The tender for this project closed on August 22nd and the Town received twelve tenders as follows: Ocean West Tree Services Ltd. Hudson Procter Contracting Ltd. ADT Contracting Ltd. David Stalker Excavating Ltd. Sparker Construction Ltd. Lentz Contracting Ltd. Despard Contracting Ltd. Stone Pacific Contracting Ltd. Leuco Construction Inc. Echelon Creative Inc. The selected tender exceeds the budget by $18,664. Although Ocean West Tree Services submitted a bid well within the budget, staff were not convinced that the company has a sufficient track record in this type of work. After carefully reviewing the bidders’ experience with similar projects and their ability to deliver quality results within the desired timeframe, staff determined that Ocean West was not a suitable choice. Hudson Procter Contracting Ltd., while offering the second-lowest bid, proposed a project completion timeframe that exceeds the Town’s preferred window. As a result, staff recommend awarding the contract to ADT Contracting Ltd. ALTERNATIVES: Council can choose to: 1. Increase the budget for the project and award the tender to a different contractor. With this being a tender, there could be legal (and financial) consequences to this decision. 2. Fold the tender and not proceed with the project at this time. FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS: This project was originally introduced in 2023 in the amount of $125,000. In 2024, video inspections and tree work were completed reducing the budget by $14,664 leaving $110,336 as the budget amount for 2025. Should council approve the requested increase an additional $19,664 will be needed. Staff are recommending funding this additional requested monies from prior year surplus. LEGAL IMPLICATIONS: N/A CITIZEN/PUBLIC RELATIONS IMPLICATIONS: The Aggie Field is a valued and frequently used space by Ladysmith residents. Any changes that reduce its usability or disrupt its current function could cause concern. INTERDEPARTMENTAL INVOLVEMENT/IMPLICATIONS: The work program would be managed by Engineering staff with the assistance of the Parks department.
