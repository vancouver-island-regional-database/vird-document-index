R23/05/24-12 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Keller IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Lee The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) affirms its commitment to promoting and supporting SOGI inclusion within the District’s 5 schools, providing a safe, welcoming, caring, and inclusive environment where diversity is celebrated and every student feels valued, respected, and supported.
