To SD 68 School Trustees: We are writing to express our overwhelming support for offering French Immersion in Ladysmith beyond grade 7. We currently have children in grades 1 & 3 in French immersion at Ecole North Oyster School and believe that expanding the program beyond grade 7 is necessary to avoid attrition and keep the program strong in our community. Our children deserve enhanced educational programming such as French Immersion as an option in Ladysmith. We encourage you to vote in favor of this proposal and to sincerely consider continuing the program right through to grade 12 at Ladysmith Secondary School (LSS).We can say without hesitation that we willchoose LSSfor our children if the French Immersion program is offered and our children are able to graduate with their double dogwood. Sincerely, Brandie &Jeff Lewis
