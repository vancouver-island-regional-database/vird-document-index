Development Application Form This form must be fully completed and signed! Appointment of Agent Form (if applicable) This form must be signed by the owner(s), authorizing the agent to make the application and communicate with the Town on the owner’s behalf. Rationale Letter Provide a letter fully describing the proposed development and how it aligns with the Official Community Plan. Certificate of Title Provide a current title (within 30 days) and include any Charges or Interests such as easements, rights of way, covenants, geotechnical reports, permits, etc. Corporate Summary (if applicable) This is required if the property is owned by a corporation.
