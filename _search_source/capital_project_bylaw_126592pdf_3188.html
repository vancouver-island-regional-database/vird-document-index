BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 CAPITAL PROJECT BYLAW 126592 CARBON NEUTRAL CAPITAL PROGRAM (CNCP) A BYLAW by the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (hereinafter called the "Board") to adopt Capital Project Bylaw 126592 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) in the amount of $312,500. WHEREAS in accordance with provisions of the School Act the Minister of Education (hereinafter called the "Minister") has approved a capital plan of the Board. NOW THEREFORE the Board agrees to the following: (a) upon approval to proceed, commence the Project and proceed diligently and use its best efforts to complete the project substantially in accordance with the Project Agreement (where required); (b) observe and comply with any rule, policy or regulation of the Minister as may be applicable to the Board or the Project; and, (c) maintain proper books of account, and other information and documents with respect to the affairs of the Project, as may be prescribed by the Minister. NOW THEREFORE the Board enacts as follows: 1. The capital project of the Board approved by the Minister and specifying a maximum expenditure of $312,500 is hereby adopted. 2. This Bylaw may be cited as “School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) Capital Project Bylaw No. 126592 READ A FIRST TIME THE 27th day of March 2013. READ A SECOND TIME THE 27th day of March 2013. READ A THIRD TIME, PASSED AND ADOPTED THE 27th day of March 2013. _________________________________ Board Chair __________________________________ Secretary-Treasurer I HEREBY CERTIFY this to be a true and original School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Capital Project Bylaw No. 126592 adopted by the Board the 27th day of March 2013. ____________________________________ Secretary-Treasurer
