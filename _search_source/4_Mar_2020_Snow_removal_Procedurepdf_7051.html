March 4, 2020 Department of Facilities Snow/ice Removal NLPS Snow Removal Plan : Resources • City Bylaw 5000, Section 22 (1) oResidents, occupants, tenants, and businesses are required to clear snow and ice, within 24 hours of a snow event as per City Bylaw 5000, Section 22 (1). The City clears sidewalks around parks, bridges, and major City facilities.
