THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: March 26, 2014 TO: Board of Education FROM: John Blain, Deputy Superintendent SUBJECT: Three-Year Local School Calendar: 2014 to 2017 BACKGROUND: At the January 29, 2014 Board Meeting, the Board adopted Motion R14/01/29-25 as follows: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) consult with parents and representatives of employees in accordance with the School Act and School Calendar Regulation on the proposed Local School Calendar for the years 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17. As a result of this motion, a consultation process was undertaken on the possible changes to the traditional calendar for years 2014-15, 2015-16, and 2016-17 as follows: • Schedule two "Success for All" in-service days for all staff, with students not attending on these days: Tuesday, September 2, 2014 and Tuesday, May 5, 2015, (dates for the 2015-16 and 201617 school years to be announced, later). The Learning Services In-Service and Professional Development budget will support these days, at no additional cost to the Board. • The Board has received the consultation package received from the community during the consultation process. RECOMMENDATIONS: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Three-Year Local School Calendar for school years 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17, that schedules two “Success for All” inservice days for all staff, with students not attending on these days: Tuesday, September 2, 2014 and Tuesday, May 5, 2015 (dates for the 2015-16 and 2016-17 school years to be announced, later), as per the attached proposed Local School Calendar.”
