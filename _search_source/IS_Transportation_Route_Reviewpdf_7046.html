NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: March 4, 2020 Business Committee Pete Sabo, Executive Director of Planning & Operations Dave Prevost, Manager of Transportation Transportation Amin Policy 512 Recommendations No formal recommendations arising from 19/20 Bus Route Review. Background In accordance with AP512 – Transportation staff completed Route Hazard and Evaluation forms on each regular bus route, between September and December of 2019. Also, in accordance with the AP, staff reviewed each route with ridership below 50%. From this work the Bus Route Transportation criteria matrix was updated along with relevant staff comments. Routes below 50% ridership were “reviewed” and routes with considerations were noted. Staff then prepared a 19/20 bus route review recommendation. While there are no formal recommendations arising from the 2019/20 Bus Route Review, staff continues to monitor all bus routes and is considering recommendations for future years. Discussion Subject to discussion at the March 4th Business Committee Meeting, the District will notify schools, parents and riders of any pending informal changes. A formal consultation process is not envisioned for these changes.
