VIA EMAIL Ref: 185433 December 18, 2015 To: All Secretary-Treasurers All School Districts Re: 2015/16 Recalculated Funding Allocations The 2015/16 recalculated funding allocations have now been determined based on actual September 30, 2015 enrolment. The operating grant block has been increased by $6.7 million for 2015/16 to provide funding to all eligible students at the rates published in March 2015. Actual September 2015 enrolment at the provincial level is 6,577 full-time equivalents (FTEs) more than districts estimated in February 2015 and unique student needs enrolment has also exceeded district projections. As well, $10.3 million is redistributed from enrolment decline supplements within the block in response to the enrolment growth – provincial funding protection declined a total of $9.3 million and the Supplement for Enrolment Decline decreased by approximately $1 million since the March 2015 estimate. The complete recalculated funding allocation package is located on our website at: http://www.bced.gov.bc.ca/k12funding/funding/15-16/welcome.htm Grant adjustments will be disbursed to school districts on the following basis: April, May and June 2016 33.3 percent each month The full $55.5 million holdback along with the operating block increase of $6.7 million has been allocated to fund the unanticipated enrolment and unique student needs growth. Targeted Funding: The minimum spending amount for Aboriginal Education programs is identified in Table 4b, as prescribed under Section 106.4 of the School Act.
