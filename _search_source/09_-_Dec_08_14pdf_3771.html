THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF REGULAR BOARD MEETING HELD DECEMBER 8, 2014 Trustees N. Bob T. Brzovic J. Brennan S. Higginson S. Kimler S. Rae B. Robinson N. Routley J. Solomon Call to Order Welcome Jerry Snuneymuxw Elder Election of Board Chair Staff D. Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO G. Roberts, A/Secretary-Treasurer J. Blain, Deputy Superintendent P. Trick, Associate Superintendent P. Sabo, Director of Planning/Operations D. Burgos, Director of Communications C. Kelt, Executive Assistant K. Toye, Executive Assistant Meeting No. A09 The Acting Secretary-Treasurer called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Brown, Snuneymuxw Elder, Jerry Brown read the Oath of Office to the Trustees and Jerry Brown, Noel Brown and Darrell Brown sang and drummed a traditional Snuneymuxw song. Acting Secretary-Treasurer Roberts said he will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board and will confirm that each Trustee nominated confirms their acceptance of the nomination by asking "do you accept?" Mr. Roberts said that in the event there was an election, Mr. Blain and Mr. Burgos would act as scrutineers. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Rae for the position of Board Chair. Mr. Roberts called for nominations a second time. Mr. Roberts called for nominations a third time. Mr. Roberts asked Trustee Rae if he accepted the nomination for the position of Board Chair. Trustee Rae stated that he did. Mr. Roberts declared Trustee Rae Board Chair. Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 8, 2014 Secretary-Treasurer vacates the Chair and the gavel is turned over to the Incoming Chair Mr. Roberts vacated the Chair and passed the gavel to Chair Rae. Election of Vice-Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Brzovic nominated Trustee Bob. Trustee Kimler nominated Trustee Higginson. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair Rae asked trustees Bob and Higginson if they would accept the nomination. Both Trustee Bob and Trustee Higginson indicated they do. Chair Rae declared there would be an election. The ballots were distributed. The ballots were collected and counted. Chair Rae advised that Trustee Higginson is the Vice-Chair of the Board. Election of Business Committee Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Solomon nominated Trustee Kimler for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time. Chair Rae called for nominations a third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Kimler if he accepted the nomination for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Kimler stated that he did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Kimler Chair of the Business Committee. Election of Business Committee Chair Rae called for nominations for the position Vice-Chair of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee.
