Their Powerpoint presentation is attached to the Agenda. CYFSW work in a variety of roles that support students and their families with a mission to get kids to school and keep students in class. CYFSW build relationships with students and their families by establishing coping strategies, integrating selfregulation tools and social skills, and setting short and long term goals. It was shared that CYFSW are trained to work with children, youth and families in matters including: grief and loss; suicide and prevention; mental health; sexual education; gender orientation; child protection; abuse and neglect; substance addictions; eating disorders; family and relational violence; bullying and cyber safety; and threat assessments are among their many duties. In addition to providing busing to John Barsby Community Secondary there will also be bussing available (for the 2015-16 school year only) for students currently attending or wishing to attend Ladysmith Secondary School. The next step in the transition planning is to identify which Cedar and Cinnabar Valley area students wish to attend Ladysmith Secondary School in September 2015.
