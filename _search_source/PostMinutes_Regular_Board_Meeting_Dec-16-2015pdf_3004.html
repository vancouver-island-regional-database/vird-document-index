CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY TRUSTEE ZONE REPORT Trustee Brzovic: There are typical Christmas holiday events going on in schools; Into the Woods at Dover and McGirr is integrating all facets of art. Trustee Robinson: Finds that at Brechin Elementary it's difficult to getting parents out and involved. The rotary club (north) has provided 17 volunteers and tomorrow Page 4 of 8 Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 16, 2015 eight of them will be provided a pancake breakfast for the school. The Christmas door decorating contest turned into a hallway decorating contest. Cilaire's concert was more than half full of parents. Forest Park is having a craft fair in February and Woodlands soup and bowl event fundraised for Loaves and Fishes. SPAC is leaning towards using their money to support zero to sixth education. Trustee Kimler: Trustee Brennan: Trustee Higginson: Trustee Solomon: LSS held their Christmas concert last night. Prior to the Ladysmith Light-up & parade, Mrs. Milne & the LSS international students hosted a spaghetti dinner for all SD68 international students and their host families. LIS - Classes compete in a Christmas door-decorating competition. LIS is hosting their Christmas concert tomorrow night at 7PM – “Twas One Crazy Night Before Christmas”. LPS is performing their Christmas concert this afternoon & evening. Christmas “Store” – Parents & community members donate un-wrapped items, which kids can then pick out, gift wrap and then give to family members on Christmas. This way, kids get the experience of “shopping” and gift-giving. ENO - Eco-visits with Erik Piikkila – A local scientist & eco-school expert has been voluntarily working with ENO and delivering hands-on, outdoor eco-education to all classes. Kids have really been enjoying their lessons and learning lots. The whole school went to Port Theatre to see a performance of “The Nutcracker”. Thanks to the PAC for the experience. There is a Child Poverty Action Committee that has $2500 for a project that involves Georgia Avenue Community School students. They will be in touch with the school to figure out what will be done. At Brechin there is a lot of positive energy going on there. At Park Avenue craft nights are huge fundraising nights for the PAC's. Triple P Parenting is a great and important program for parents. Georgia Ave's craft fair was a success and Bayview has a Christmas concert tomorrow and their craft day is on Saturday.
