School liaison officers Sgt. Cheryl Armstrong and Cpl. Paul Minkly outlined work that has been done in schools this year and outlined plans for next year, when there will be three full-time liaison officers in schools. Cst. Gary O’Brien gave the Board an update on the School Crimestoppers program which will be reinstated in the fall. He also provided the Board with information about the School Watch program which invites neighbours to help prevent vandalism at schools.
