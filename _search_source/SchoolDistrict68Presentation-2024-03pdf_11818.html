Your City of Nanaimo Cycling Volunteer Ambassadors (VPAs), and co-leaders of GoByBikeBC Team Copenhagen can help you increase participation in three key interlocking bicycle programs, and assist with community garbage and litter clean ups. GoByBikeBC https://gobybikebc.ca/ 529 Garage https://project529.com/garage Nowik Bikes for Kids https://bikesforkidsvi.ca/ Your cycling VPA team consists of Melissa Griswold and Joel Canfield. Joel will be familiar to many of you, as Joel is one of your retired minions, who was tasked with maintaining mostly obsolete computer systems for 25 years. Long before Joel joined SD68, he served as a Provincial Park Interpreter, delivering education programs to visitors at Rathtrevor Beach Park. Since retirement Joel has completed the Cycling Embassy of Denmark’s “Bikeable Cities Masterclass” (1) in Copenhagen, DK. GoByBikeBC The GoByBikeBC Spring week is scheduled for June 3rd to June 9th, 2024. Over the years you have received numerous reports from Health officers, and consultants detailing the negative health effects of our sedentary lifestyles. The 2016 consultants’ report included X-rays showing bone loss in young people, and other evidence of diseases normally associated with old age, occurring at disturbingly early ages. For this brief presentation there is no need to duplicate that information. We are available (at no cost) to assist any school that does not have a GoByBikeBC team, to form a team. Being retired, we have the flexibility to do very brief presentations at staff meetings, or offer far more in depth support, depending on the time available at PAC meetings, specific bookings, or as a component of Pro-D. We would encourage Trustees to attend the Kickoff event for the Spring GoByBikeBC week or visit a Celebration Station. Detailed information will be sent to you as soon as the City of Nanaimo solidifies dates and times. 529 Garage 529 Garage is a FREE anti-theft registration program, that covers, Canada, the US, and New Zealand. You create your free online account, record your bicycle serial number, and upload pictures of your bicycle. If you are unfortunate enough to have your bike stolen, you login and flag your bike as stolen. Don’t forget to file a police report. Fellow 529 Garage members who have the app installed on their phones can opt to receive stolen bike alerts. Your VPAs have set their app to present all alerts from a 25 Km radius around Nanaimo. This is why 529 Garage is often referred to as Neighbourhood Watch for your bike. 529 Garage registration events for schools can be arranged by contacting Dan Hopkins, Community Policing Coordinator, at the City of Nanaimo. Daniel.Hopkins@nanaimo.ca ; 250-755-4531. Nowik Bikes for Kids Trustees are already familiar with Greg’s fantastic Bikes for Kids program. As one of your minions, Joel was under the impression that SD68 seldom participated in the program. Hence GoByBikeBC Team Copenhagen started arranging bike donations with our first donation to Ecole Ocean School. Since being connected to Nowik Bikes for Kids through Community Policing, Team Copenhagen has transferred our very modest stock of bikes to Greg’s HUGE program. Joel currently volunteers with Nowik Bikes for Kids. Having participated in deliveries for Nowik Bikes for Kids, it is interesting to learn that many staff in school offices had not heard of the Bikes for Kids program. Your VPAs can also assist Nowik Bikes for Kids and our students, by being available to brief staff, and PACs on the Bikes for Kids program. Team Copenhagen intends to enable a Nowik Bikes for Kids presence during the Spring GoByBikeBC week, by providing our event tent and tables kit to Greg.
