DELEGATIONS 7.1 David Slade, Vice-Chair of Friends of Rails to Trails Vancouver Island David Slade, Vice Chair of Friends of Rails to Trails Vancouver Island, spoke in support of converting the E&N rail corridor into a multi-use trail. He outlined the organization’s vision for an accessible active transportation network, highlighting economic, recreational, and commuting benefits. Mr. Slade cited examples of successful rail-to-trail projects, discussed cost and environmental advantages of building trails on existing rail beds, and noted funding opportunities through federal and provincial active transportation programs. Mr. Slade responded to questions from Council, and Council thanked him for his comprehensive presentation. 8.
