Re: Intent to lease space in Woodlands school Dear Sir/Madam Nanaimo Unique Kids Organization (NUKO) is a non—profitlearning centre supporting kids and youth who face neurological disabilities academically, recreationally, socially and behaviourally. We currently sublease space at the Longwood Station underneath Thrifty Foods. The space we are in is leased to ICBC and they graciously sub—letthe area to us at a reduced rate. The lease comes due in one year, and we will no longer be able to have a reduced lease payment. Our space also lacks a gym and outdoor green space for play which is an integral part of learning. — We are inquiring about leasing space at the Woodlands school site. It is in an ideal location and has all of the facilities that can enhance our program. As we are non—profit,we are also looking for space that can fit into our very limited budget. We would be willing to sign on a two year lease and would like to start at the beginning of the 2017-2018 school year. Change is difficult for the students in our program so to have space that is consistent for the next two years is important. Our Board of Directors would like an opportunity to speak with you further to discuss what options may be available at this ideal location. I look forward to hearing from you soon.
