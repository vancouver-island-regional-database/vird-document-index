Kelly, DASG Rep Meeting No. B06 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:08 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. ADDITIONS TO THE AGENDA There were none. DELETIONS TO THE AGENDA There were none. CHANGE IN ORDER There was no change. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held February 19, 2019 Folio B19/02/19-01 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Keller IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Stanley That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY APPROVAL OF THE MINUTES Minutes - January 16, 2019 Folio B19/02/19-02 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Stanley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Keller That the minutes from the Business Committee meeting held January 16, 2019, be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS SENIOR STAFF REPORTS Superintendent Saywell Re: 2019-2020 School Calendar Superintendent Scott Saywell presented the 2019-20 local school calendar. The calendar will be made available to the public immediately for the next 30 days. Staff will report their final recommendations at the Special Board Meeting in March. Director of Planning & Operations Sabo and Transportation Supervisor Prevost Re: 2018-2019 Bus Route Review Transportation Manager Dave Prevost provided the 2018-2019 bus route review and recommendations. The presentation is attached to the Agenda. Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh and Director of Finance Sutton • Amended 2018/2019 Budget • Draft Budget Calendar for 2019/2020 • Enrolment Projections Director of Finance Taunia Sutton presented the 2018-2019 Annual Amended Budget and provided an update on Ministry Enrolment projections. Both presentations are attached to the Agenda. Folio B19/02/19-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Keller IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee McKay That the Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) give all three readings of the 2018/2019 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held February 19, 2019 during the February 27, 2019, Open Board Meeting, and; that the Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopt the 2018/2019 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw during the February 27, 2019, Open Board Meeting. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM REGULAR BOARD MEETING There was none. UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was none. NEW BUSINESS BCSTA Provincial Council Member, Trustee Stanley Re: Funding Formula Review The Committee discussed the Report from the Funding Model Review Panel. This item will be moved forward to the March Business Meeting. Trustees to review Funding Formula Review Recommendations and formulate questions for staff prior to February 27th. FOR INFORMATION QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions. ADJOURNMENT Folio B19/02/19-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee McKay IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Stanley That the meeting be adjourned.
