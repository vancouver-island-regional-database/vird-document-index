Has the School District considered liability issues? To reduce the potential for an outbreak, the District could consider offering a hybrid option to reduce class sizes. Mask use could also be encouraged in classrooms, particularly for older students. If online options did not require withdrawal from catchment schools, perhaps more families would avail themselves of these options. Reduced school density would mean reduced transmission. Has the Board sought a legal opinion on the liability risks? Would such an opinion be shared with parents? Thank you for all your hard work at a difficult time.
