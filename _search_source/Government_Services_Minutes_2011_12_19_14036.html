TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE GOVERNMENT SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, DECEMBER 19, 2011 – 5:30 P.M. COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Steve Arnett, Chair Councillor Gord Horth Councillor Glenda Patterson STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Felicity Adams Joanna Winter Councillor Bill Drysdale Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Duck Paterson Sandy Bowden Pat Durban Erin Anderson Joe Friesenhan CALL TO ORDER Councillor Arnett called the meeting to order at 5:30 p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL Councillor Arnett requested the committee’s consideration of the following addition to the agenda: 7.1 2011-152 Announcement by S/Sgt Roger Plamondon, Ladysmith RCMP Detachment It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda for the Government Services Committee of December 19, 2011 be adopted as amended. MINUTES 2011-153 DELEGATIONS It was moved, seconded and carried that the minutes of the Government Services Committee meeting held November 21, 2011 be adopted as circulated. Judy Stafford, Cowichan Green Community Overview of Programs and Services J. Stafford presented a video and responded to questions regarding the programs and services offered by Cowichan Green Community in the Cowichan Region. Council thanked Ms Stafford for her informative presentation. S/Sgt Roger Plamondon Ladysmith Detachment, Royal Canadian Mounted Police S/Sgt Plamondon advised Council that he has accepted an offer Government Services Committee December 19, 2011 Page 1 for a one-year overseas deployment, effective in early 2012. He thanked Council for their support and commitment to the community. Council expressed their sincere appreciation to S/Sgt Plamondon for his work in the community, and congratulated him on his new opportunity. STAFF REPORTS 2011-154 Financial Plan Update It was moved, seconded and carried that the Financial Plan Update to the end of November 2011 be accepted. 2011-155 South End Pipeline Temporary Construction Shutdown It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that approval be given to stop work temporarily on the Stocking Lake Supply and Interconnection Main. 2011-156 Toilet Rebate Program Extension It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend that Council authorize the extension of the Toilet Rebate Program until December 31, 2012, and that staff be directed to include $15,000 for the program in the 2012 water utility operating budget. 2011-157 Building Inspector’s Report for November 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Building Inspector’s Report for November 2011 be accepted. 2011-158 Trolley Report for November 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Trolley Report for November 2011 be received. 2011-159 Fire Chief’s Report for November 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Fire Chief’s Report for November 2011 be accepted. 2011-160 Animal Control / Pound Report for November 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Animal Control / Pound Report for November 2011 be accepted. NEW BUSINESS Director of Public Works Council expressed to Joe Friesenhan, Director of Public Works, deep gratitude and appreciation for his contributions to the Town of Ladysmith during his 13 years of employment, and wished him a healthy and happy retirement.
