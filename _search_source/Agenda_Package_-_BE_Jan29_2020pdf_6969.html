To facilitate this we moved the $1,500,000 normally restricted in the Staffing/Unusual Expenses Reserve to the Targeted Constraints Reserve, under the heading of CEF Supplemental. As well, we redistributed the reserves in other restricted surplus categories to top the total CEF supplement up by $204,537 to the amount necessary which was $1,704,537. Accumulated Surplus Update: The District has now received the total 2019/20 Classroom Enhancement Funding so the supplement from accumulated restricted surplus is no longer required. As such, staff are now able to reinstate the Staffing/Unusual Expense Reserve that has been in place for such contingencies over the past 5 years (since 2014/15). This reserve is historically $1,500,000 but the surplus policy recommends that the District maintain this Reserve at 0.5% - 1.0% of actual operating expenses of the fiscal year. At June 30, 2019 this equated to a range of $660,860 - $1,321,721. Therefore, to adhere to the policy the reserve would be reinstated to a total of $1,300,000. The remaining balance of restricted surplus to be redistributed to other categories within the surplus policy is $404,537, of which $125,000 will be added to the Planning Reserve category to support Qwam Qwum Stuwixwulh School start up costs (total reserve would be increased to $500,000), and the balance of $279,537 will be placed under “other” in the Targeted Constraints Reserve.
