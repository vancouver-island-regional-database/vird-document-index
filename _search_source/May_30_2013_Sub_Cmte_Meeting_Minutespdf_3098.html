Transportation Department Sub-Committee Meeting Minutes May 30, 2013 In Attendance: Phil Turin, Pete Sabo, Scott Saywell Terry Lynn Saunders, Patrick Young, Alana Cameron, Dave Prevost Meeting started at 9:05 am - Pete gave an overview of current and future bus routes including bussing for the Enhanced Facilities for Learning 10 Year Plan. - Bell schedules were discussed. - Bus routing software was discussed and it was decided that this committee will recommend that the software be purchased from the 2012/13 budget. Please see recommendation below. - Policy changes were discussed and it was decided that this committee will wait for final decisions on the Enhanced Facilities for Learning 10 Year Plan prior to recommending any Policy changes. Pete & Dave to determine which the key Policies for required changes are. - Walk limits were discussed. - Electronic communication to Parents regarding bussing delays was discussed. - The Work Plan was reviewed and discussed. - Recalculating per Kilometer bus costs was discussed. - Bussing costs to schools was discussed. - The next committee meeting will be scheduled for late August or early September. The meeting was adjourned at 10:40 am. Transportation Sub-Committee Recommendation: That the Transportation Sub-Committee recommends to the Board of Education School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that staff be directed to acquire transportation management software as recommended in the MPS Evaluation of Transportation Services Report dated November 28, 2012 and that the source of funding be found in the 2012/13 budget. That the Transportation Sub-Committee recommends to the Board of Education School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that staff revise the setup of the automated vehicle locating technology (GPS) available on existing buses, as recommended in the MPS Evaluation of Transportation Services Report dated November 28, 2012 and that the source of funding be found in the 2012/13 budget.
