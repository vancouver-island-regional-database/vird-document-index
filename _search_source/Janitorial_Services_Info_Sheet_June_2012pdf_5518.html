THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: June 20, 2012 TO: Business Committee FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Diane Bunnah, Operations Supervisor SUBJECT: Report on SD68 Custodial Review Background The following motion was passed by the Board of Education on March 28, 2012 - Folio R12/03/28-11 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) refers the Cascade Facilities Management Consultants Report on SD68 Custodial Review be referred to staff to prepare a report on the status of the consultant’s recommendations, and provide that report to the Board by September 2012. Discussion Staff has had an opportunity to review the report, consider each recommendation, and build a plan by which to guide the work of implementing the recommendations. The plan includes the observation and recommendation from the review as well as the intended action, anticipated budget, timeline, the expected results as well as the dates the results are expected. This plan starts to form the basis of goals for the Operations Department but does not include all departmental priorities and goals.
