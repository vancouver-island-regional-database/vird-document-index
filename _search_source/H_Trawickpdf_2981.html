ll lI KelseyToze Subject: FW: French immersion at LSS Dear NLPSSchool Board Trustees, I am writing you today in hope that you will support the District Staff's recommendation to open a French Immersion class at LSSin September 2016. I attended the meeting when Cheryl and Carrie made their presentation to the Board on behalf of Ladysmith French immersion parents. I found their presentation to be clear and well documented and hope that you all had the time to look carefully at the information they provided. I did want to try to address a few concerns that trustees brought forward at this meeting. I am in a unique position to be able to do this because I live in Ladysmith and I am both a French Immersion teacher at EcoleNorth Oyster and a French immersion parent there as well. I have a full understanding ofthe benefits, issues and intricacies involved in the French immersion program from the both points of view: teacher and parent.
