THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: September 24, 2014 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Amended Capital Project Bylaw 116315-03 BACKGROUND: The Ministry of Education - School Funding and Allocation has approved the funds for: Project No. 116315 Transportation - $138,991 Replacement School Bus – 80+ passengers Due to an additional administration fee on the procurement of this bus, it is necessary to amend Project No. 116315-03 of $2,385 making the total amount of Project No. 11631503 $141,376. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Amended Capital Project Bylaw 11631503 Bus Replacement, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Amended Bylaw by reading the Amended Bylaw’s title and giving a summary of its contents. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Amended Capital Project Bylaw 116315-03 in the amount of $141,376 and that it be given first reading. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Amended Capital Project Bylaw 116315-03 in the amount of $141,376 and that it be given second reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three reading of the Amended Capital Project Bylaw No. 116315-03 in the amount of $141,376 at its Regular Board Meeting of September 24, 2014. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Amended Capital Project Bylaw 116315-03 in the amount of $141,376 and that it be given third and final reading. BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 AMENDED CAPITAL PROJECT BYLAW 116315-03 A BYLAW by the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (hereinafter called the "Board") to adopt Amended Capital Project Bylaw 116315-03 Replacement School Bus. WHEREAS in accordance with provisions of the School Act the Minister of Education (hereinafter called the "Minister") has approved a capital plan of the Board. NOW THEREFORE the Board agrees to the following: (a) upon approval to proceed, commence the Project(s) and proceed diligently and use its best efforts to complete the project substantially in accordance with the Project Agreement (where required); (b) observe and comply with any rule, policy or regulation of the Minister as may be applicable to the Board or the Project; and, (c) maintain proper books of account, and other information and documents with respect to the affairs of the Project, as may be prescribed by the Minister. NOW THEREFORE the Board enacts as follows: 1. The capital project of the Board approved by the Minister and specifying a maximum expenditure of $141,376 is hereby adopted. 2. This Amended Bylaw may be cited as “School District No. 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) 116315-03 Bus Replacement. READ A FIRST TIME THE 24th day of September 2014. READ A SECOND TIME THE 24th day of September 2014.
