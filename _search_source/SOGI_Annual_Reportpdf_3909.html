SOGI Annual Report to the Board June 25, 2014. The SOGI Committee met regularly this year to address issues related to the safety of staff and students in the District where Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity are concerned. Committee members consisted of Educators, RCMP, DPAC, and community members. Without each person’s enthusiasm and unique perspective I doubt we could have accomplishes as much as we have. We have had a huge impact here in SD68. We have gone from having just two of our Secondary schools having a QSA in 2011, to all 7 now providing a safe place to find shelter, support, unity and fellowship. In the Fall of 2011 we hosted the Districts 1st GSA Conference with students, Teachers, Counsellors, parents and other Educational leaders from all over the Island and Sunshine Coast. THIS fall we will be hosting our second Conference over two days that will focus on "Allies" and the invitation is going out province-wide! The SOGI committee has also helped to develop and implement a procedure to begin the provision of Gender Neutral washrooms in all our schools, with the input of the Deputy Superintendent on signage. In progress now are further procedural directives that will ensure school administrators will provide appropriate intervention and training of all staff on language, labels and specifically how exactly, step by step, to respond to a student coming out to them, in order to provide a safe and uniform method of transition District-wide. Few if any expenses were incurred. Snacks were provided at meetings paid mostly out of pocket by me. The majority of the 2013/14 (carried over) and 2014/15 budgets will be spent on the Fall 2014 QSA Conference at Barsby. The SOGI committee is in a good place to continue building on the successes it has achieved, and is in a good place for positive work in the 2014/15 school year. There is absolutely no doubt that the outreach, advocacy, and accomplishments thus far have, and will continue to ensure the safety and community of people in Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools for all students. Respectfully submitted on behalf of the SOGI Committee, Bill Bard, Chair
