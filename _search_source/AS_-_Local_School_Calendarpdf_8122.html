Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: February 12, 2020 Business Committee Superintendent/CEO, Scott Saywell Local School Calendar 2020-2021 Recommendation: That the Business Committee recommend that the staff make the attached draft 2020/21 Local School Calendar available to the public immediately and to report back to the March Business Committee meeting with recommendations. Background: In accordance with the School Act and based upon discussions with the NDTA’s Joint Committee (Professional Development) staff has approved the attached Local School Calendar for the 20202021 school year. As outlined in the School Act 87.01 “School Calendar” the Board will give notice to the parents of the students enrolled and the representatives of employees of the Board. A 30 day notice period will be provided, as required. Attachment: Draft 2020 – 2021 Local School Calendar
