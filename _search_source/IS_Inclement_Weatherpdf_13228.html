The CUPE collective agreement allows for up to two snow days per employee. Employees working (those deemed essential) receive a day off in lieu for the snow day worked. Discussion On mornings where there is a possibility of inclement weather, the transportation manager will begin the process to gather information to determinate how hazardous road conditions are for school buses. This information will also be used for the superintendent or superintendent designate to determine if schools shall remain open or be closed due to snow or other hazardous road conditions. The superintendent may also consult facilities and other community partners with respect to conditions/status of school sites – condition of city streets, parking areas, entry/egress routes, weather warnings and forecasts, and walking conditions to school sites. Ideally, the decision will be made before 6:00 am. There are several possible outcomes: a. all schools are closed. b. all bus routes are cancelled, but schools remain open. c. accessible bus routes are cancelled, while other buses con�nue to run and schools remain open.
