Tine McLennan Rutherford Open and Thriving RE: Keeping Rutherford Open Rhonda Vandermast Rutherford PAC Chair RE: Proposed Closure of Rutherford Ruth Wainwright-Christmas Departure Bay RE: Highlights of Departure Bay Eco School Taryn Silver Parent RE: Departure Bay Eco School MLA Krog Opposition Critic for the Attorney General RE: Against Closure of Rutherford and Departure Bay UNFINISHED BUSINESS Becoming Sustainable, Facilities Plan Update Superintendent, John Blain presented Becoming Sustainable: Facilities Plan Update, as an update to the 10-Year Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan. The district has seen a steady decline in student enrolment over the last 13 years, where in 12 of those 13 years, the Board had to make significant budget cuts. The projected deficit for the 2016-17 school year is $2.7 million. The plan recommends school closures and consolidations allowing the district to reduce annual operating costs, increase capacity utilization, reduce future capital expenses, and identify capital assets for future consideration which will enable the district to focus towards student learning.
