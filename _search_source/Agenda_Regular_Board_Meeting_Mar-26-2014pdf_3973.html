GOALS It is the intent of the parties to:      Establish and maintain a long-term relationship based upon effective communication and collaboration; Promote and encourage open and constructive dialogue based on mutual trust, honesty, respect and understanding; Determine areas that may be of mutual interest to the parties; Develop a constructive process for meaningful collaboration on areas of mutual interest; and Promote understanding of each other’s governing structures, capacities, priorities, roles, responsibilities and current projects. 1 Page 28 of 102 10.1 ESTABLISHMENT OF THE SENIOR WORKING COMMITTEE The parties agree to establish a senior working committee (the “Working Committee”) comprised of senior members of both parties to be appointed by the parties after the execution of this MOU.
