My name is Tessa Stiven and I am the Farm to School BC Community Animator for the Central Island Region. I work with communities and partners to support schools in building comprehensive farm to school programs that support sustainable food systems, develop student food literacy, and enhance school and community connectedness. One of the ways that we support schools is through our annual Farm to School BC grant program. Our application period is currently open until November 13th. You can find more information here: https://farmtoschoolbc.ca/grants/. In the past 5 years, we have funded 10 schools in SD68. In addition to our grant program, I support schools though facilitating learning circles, hosting ProDs, connecting schools to community resources, offering school garden consultations, sharing provincial best practices, grant writing, organizing farm field trips, and more! I manage a regional Farm to School BC Listserv. I have included October highlights attached to this email as a sample of our monthly listserv posts. Please let me know if you would like to be added to the listserv to keep up to date with F2SBC opportunities in the region, or sign up here: https://groups.google.com/g/f2s-nanaimonorth-region-network We work closely with the BC Chapter of the Coalition for Healthy School Food. The Coalition is hosting a webinar this Thursday on using the Student and Family Affordability funds to advance school food programs.
