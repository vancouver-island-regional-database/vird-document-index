Educators Parents Students Reporting is Changing in Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools (NLPS) Why Change? The province of B.C. is currently implementing a redesigned curriculum and reporting procedure intended to provide opportunities for students to acquire the skills necessary for success in this ever changing 21st century. For more information, please visit: curriculum.gov.bc.ca What Changes will be seen in NLPS? We are moving from a model of Reporting TO Parents, to Communicating WITH Parents In NLPS, we are changing our approach to help our learners connect with and acquire the skills they need to succeed. on an ongoing basis. This involves how we assess, and how we communicate student learning to parents and to students. Communicating Student Learning in NLPS – What will it look like?
