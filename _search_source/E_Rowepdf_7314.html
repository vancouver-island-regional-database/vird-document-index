From: eden rowe Sent: June 21, 2020 9:38 AM Subject: South Wellington Demolition CAUTION: External Message To whom it may concern I write to you to express my concern over the demolition of South Wellington School. This building could be converted into a community hub where members of the area are able to socialize and establish a sense of unity. The space could be used for a number of activities and services such as food and beverage, fitness activities, art and crafts, counselling, daycare, and many more possibilities. It is my wish you will listen to the concerns of those who only want something that brings their community closer. Thank you for your time, Eden Rowe
