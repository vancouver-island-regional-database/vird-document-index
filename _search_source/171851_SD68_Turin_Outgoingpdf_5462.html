VIA EMAIL Ref: 171851 January 28, 2014 Phil Turin Secretary-Treasurer School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 395 Wakesiah Ave Nanaimo BC V9R 3K6 Email: pturin@sd68.bc.ca Dear Phil Turin: Re: 2013/14 Five-Year Capital Plan Echo Report This letter is to acknowledge your Board of Education’s submission of a Five-Year Capital Plan to the Ministry of Education and to advise that the Capital Management Branch has now completed its review of all capital project funding requests for 2013/14 – 2017/18. Attached for your review is a copy of the 2013/14 Capital Plan Project Requests echo report, which provides the priority assigned by the Ministry to each requested project. These priorities are based on the criteria outlined for capital project submissions in the 2013/14 Five-Year Capital Plan Instructions. Funding requests made for new space, renovation, or replacement projects, and supported by a complete Project Identification Report (PIR), are assigned either a High, Medium or Low priority by the Ministry. Requests for buses, building envelope remediation or mechanical upgrades are also assigned one of the three priorities. Otherwise, projects where no PIR was provided, or if the PIR is incomplete, will not receive a priority and instead will be marked as “NPIR”. Finally, other project requests that are eligible Annual Facility Grant expenditures will be identified as “A” in the echo report. Please review these reports and notify your Planning Officer by February 14, 2014 of any incorrect or additional information related to specific projects or their ranking. The 2013/14 Capital Plan submitted by the Board of Education will be considered by the Ministry as the approved capital plan required for the purposes of the Local Government Act and associated School Site Acquisition Charge Regulation, with regard to the setting of school site acquisition charges for eligible school districts. …/2 Ministry of Education Resource Management Division Mailing Address: PO BOX 9151 STN PROV GOVT Victoria BC V8W 9H1 Telephone: (250) 356-7814 Facsimile: (250) 953-4985 Location: 5th Floor 620 Superior St Victoria BC -2- Once again, thank you for the time and effort required to develop your 2013/14 Five-Year Capital Plan. We look forward to our continued work with District staff to address the capital needs of the School District.
