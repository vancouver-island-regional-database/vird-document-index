SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) PROCEDURE NO. 2200P BUDGETARY OVERSIGHT The Board of Education, through the Business Committee, will receive a year end forecast report of its revenues and expenditures on a timely basis following the dates of October 31, January 31, and April 30. Further forecast reports will be provided as required. or when substantive budgetary changes occur due to expenditure or funding changes. Adopted: February 22, 2012 1
