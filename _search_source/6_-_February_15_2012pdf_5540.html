NEW BUSINESS 2012-2013 Enrolment Update Mr. Turin gave the Committee with an update on the projected student enrolment and provincial government grants for the next three years. Mr. Turin said that enrolment is projected to continue to decline for the next three years, going from 13,540 FTE funded students this year to 13,176 FTE funded students in the 2014-15 school year. He said that by using the Ministry of Education’s funding estimator software, the district is projecting funding pressures of $732,661 in 2012-13; $2.1 million in 2013-14; and $3.3 million in 2014-15. He said these numbers are very preliminary projections at this point and actual funding for 2012-13 will be announced in mid-March.
