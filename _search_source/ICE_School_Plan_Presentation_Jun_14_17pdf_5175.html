Sharing Our School Plan Our School Goals (2016-2017)  Goal 1: To foster an inclusive, welcoming and positive school environment focused on creating a sense of belonging where all students feel valued and talented. (Becoming ConnectEd)  Goal 2: To improve the success of all of our students in terms of course completions and academic engagement.  Goal 3: To increase our global citizenship initiatives and our positive interactions within our local community.  Goal 4: To increase students’ sense of inquiry through our use of technological tools in our DL courses, and face-to-face interactions. The Focus For Today Goal 1: To foster an inclusive, welcoming and positive school environment focused on creating a sense of belonging where all students feel valued and talented. (AKA – Becoming ConnectEd.) Rationale for focus on Goal #1  Year One: It’s “Year ONE” as a school; important to create a sense of identity and a sense of “Feeling ConnectEd”.  Student Diversity: The most diverse demographic in the district – (a) K-12 full-time school-aged students, (b) Grade 10-12 adult-aged learners, and (c) Grade 10-12 students cross-enrolled with other schools.  Feeling Valued & Talented: Many of our full-time students have atypical academic paths and/or needs that have lead them to Island ConnectEd. Due to these paths, many struggle to see their talents. We believe all students have talents that deserve and need to be celebrated. Challenges Faced with Goal #1  We are distributed. We never have all students (and rarely all teachers) onsite at one time.  1700+ students with three main demographics: Full-time K-12, Full-time Adult-aged, and Cross-Enrolled Students  We have ongoing enrolment. Students enrol, become active and complete courses at different times and rates. Our school community and our courses literally change day to day.
