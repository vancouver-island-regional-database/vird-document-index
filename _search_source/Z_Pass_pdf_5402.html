THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: October 16, 2015 TO: BUSINESS COMMITTEE FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Dave Prevost, Transportation Supervisor SUBJECT: Z Pass + Implementation Update At the update on September 16th bus registrations were reported at 300. Bus Registrations to date are 900 students. It is estimated that there are about 400 remaining riders who have yet to complete registrations. Registrations continue daily and a proposed deadline for registrations will be October 31, 2015. A notice is planned in conjunction with the Communications Department, to be delivered to students through the schools and as a bus hand out. After communication of the deadline, staff will identify non card holder students who indicate they would like to ride, make direct contact with family and assist with registration as necessary. Bus pass cards have been printed for all registered students. During the week of October 5th to 9th, bus pass cards were distributed to students and have been used to scan on and off the bus. Student bus pass cards are being distributed through our school Bus Safety Instructors and school offices. It is expected that student and bus route data will become available to staff mid to late November. A report from the Facilities Department, to the Business Committee will follow. In past years, the data was available within a few weeks of the start of the new school years and we expect this same process to occur for next year. Transportation Department staff continues to:      Provide bus driving staff with ongoing training on using Z Pass +. Continually monitor implementation and make improvements and recommendations as required. Register students on-line for 2015-16, and progressively offer assistance to attain 100% registration. Work with staff, schools, students and families to ensure the implementation of Z Pass + is successful. Prepare to report out on; student ridership (numbers), status (eligible and non-eligible), route type (regular, special needs, out of policy, etc.), ridership by schools and ridership eligibility by route.
