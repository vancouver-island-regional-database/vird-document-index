Minutes of the Parks, Recreation & Culture Advisory Committee Wednesday, March 20, 2019 at 7:00pm COMMITTEE MEMBERS PRESENT: Tim Richards, Chair Gord Horth Tara Pollock Bruce Mason Councillor Duck Paterson Mike Brocklebank STAFF PRESENT: Clayton Postings Robyn McAdam REGRETS: AGENDA Moved and seconded: 2019-06: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the agenda for the meeting as presented. Motion carried. MINUTES Moved and seconded: 2019-07: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the minutes of the February 20, 2019 meeting as presented. Motion carried. REFERRED BY COUNCIL Poet Laureate draft policy – Committee members discussed the Staff Report to Council and the Terms of Reference, recommending that the involvement of youth be included in the terms of reference. Moved and seconded: 2019-08: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee support the Poet Laureate draft policy as presented. Motion carried. Moved and seconded: 2019-09: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee recommends that Council consider including the involvement of youth in the terms of reference. Motion carried. Community Driven Capital Project Policy – Committee members agreed that the plan provides guidelines that would help both staff and groups. The plan helps clarify deliverables and frames timely discussions. Moved and seconded: 2019-10: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee recommends that Council consider approving the Community-Driven Capital Project Policy. Motion carried. Minutes to the Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee Meeting March 20, 2019 Page 2 NEW BUSINESS Regional Sport Tourism committee representative – The CVRD requested a representative from the PRCAC committee sit on the Regional Sport Tourism committee. Gord Horth volunteered to attend the next meeting. Lot 108 Fitness Trail – The committee discussed the Lot 108 Fitness Trail plan and agreed that it is a great starting project for various groups to work on together. OLD BUSINESS Membership update – New applicants for the PRCAC committee are being interviewed by Council and will be in attendance at a future meeting. UPDATES Arts & Culture Update – • • • • A Tea is planned for April 17 to celebrate the new kitchen and washroom at the Ladysmith Waterfront Gallery. The Vancouver Island Vixens and the Terry Boyle Irish night were both a great success at the Little Theatre. Two difference summer camps are planned for children at the Little Theatre this summer. ‘Rumours’ starts at the Ladysmith Little Theatre March 21st and runs till April 7th. Meeting adjourned at 8:30PM Next Meeting will be held at 7:00pm, April 24, 2019 at the Frank Jameson Community Centre.
