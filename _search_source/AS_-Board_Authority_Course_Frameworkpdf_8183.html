BAA courses help educators respond to the local needs of schools and their communities, and to provide choice and flexibility for students. Under the Ministry guidelines all locally developed courses that may count for graduation credit at the Grade 10, 11, 12 level must be submitted as Board/Authority Authorized Courses and be approved by the Board of Education.
