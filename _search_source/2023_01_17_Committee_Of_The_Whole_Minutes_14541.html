UNFINISHED BUSINESS 6.1 “Our Health, Our Community” Health and Wellness Survey CW 2023-010 That the Committee recommend that Council approve the following questions and direct staff to forward them to Our Cowichan Communities Health Network for consideration and possible inclusion in the "Our Health, Our Community" Health and Wellness survey: 1. Do you live within five minutes of green space (park) or blue space (body of water) and how often do you use these assets? 2. Do you have access to your own personal doctor? 3. How often do you need to take advantage of the services at Urgent Care? 4. Have you had an asthma diagnosis?
