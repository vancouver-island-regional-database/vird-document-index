ADJOURNMENT 2 Project Kick Off with Ladysmith Council Ladysmith Official Community Plan | 23 March 2021 | 5:30-6:30pm Purpose With the new Ladysmith Official Community Plan (OCP) project now underway, the purpose of this Special Meeting is to: provide background information; and seek input from Council on aspirations and ideas for the process. Agenda • Project Goals, Background, and Opportunities | Presentation by project consulting team • Proposed Planning and Engagement Process | Presentation by project consulting team • Questions, Comments, and Dicussion | Council with project consulting team & Town Administration Project Background The Town of Ladysmith is updating its OCP to help shape growth and development in a way that helps the community meet its goals. An OCP is a policy document that influences how people live, gather, recreate, shop, work, learn, and move around their community. At its heart, an OCP is about managing land use and physical growth of a town. As a result, an OCP influences greenhouse gas emissions, climate change preparedness, equity and place-making for all, transportation choices, housing affordability, community character, protection of ecological areas, community health, costs of Town services and infrastructure, and more. The planning process has just begun and will culminate in a new OCP in late 2022. Public input will play an important role in shaping the Plan, and there will be many community engagement opportunities throughout the process. Project updates including information on engagement opportunities will be posted at www.ladysmith.ca this Spring.
