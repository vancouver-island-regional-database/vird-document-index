NEW BUSINESS 2013/14 Financial Forecast as at January 31, 2014 Mr. Turin updated the Business Committee on issues related to the development of the 2014-15 budget. The current year’s budget is on track for the district to end the current year with approximately $1 million surplus. This is due to a mid-year “clawback” in a number of supplies and services accounts. When this is added to a surplus of $1.5 million carried forward from last year, this would give the district approximately $2.5 million to assist in addressing the expected shortfall in the 2014-15 budget. Mr. Turin said that we are currently projecting that the district is facing a shortfall of $5.6 million next year and that this shortfall is expected to rise to $8 million by 2017-18. 2014-2015 Enrolment Update Mr. Turin told the Business Committee that the district is projecting an enrolment of 13,150 fulltime equivalent students for the 2014-2015 school year compared to 13,174 this school year.
