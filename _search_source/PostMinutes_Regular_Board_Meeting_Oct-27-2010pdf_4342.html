CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Green explained that previously the GST was included in the published rental rates. The rates are being increased by 10 percent as part of the 2010-11 budget. Also, with the introduction of the HST, staff added 12 percent to the published rental rates. The result is that renters are paying an increase of between 10 and 22 percent, depending on the rental agreement and the applicability of tax on specific items.
