Agenda Item Number 7.1 Page 17 of 59 CONTINUOUS OPTIMIZATION B.C. Hydro - 2nd Quarter Update P. Sabo & B. Kingsley Significance of Real Time Monitoring • Utility meter is the “window” into the building’s energy use • Regular meters are read only once a month • Problems will only become known after 1 – 2 months • Web based Real Time Meters give more and timely data • Issues can be identified and resolved sooner ….saving a month’s worth of costs and assist in identifying additional cost saving projects within the facility.
