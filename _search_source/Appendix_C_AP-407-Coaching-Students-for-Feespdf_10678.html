Appendix C Administrative Procedure 407 – Coaching Students for Fees AP 407 – Coaching Students for Fees Purpose The Board does not favour teachers accepting fees for coaching students during the school term. In any case no coaching of pupils for fees shall be done on school premises. Adopted: March 9, 1983 Amended: November 14, 1984 AP 407 – Coaching Students for Fees Page 1 of 1
