Excerpt from the adopted minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held May 24, 2017 Governance Review - Budget Considerations IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Solomon IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson Folio R17/05/24-11 That the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) enter into a public consultation process with the groups outlined in the SecretaryTreasurer's report on the possibility of reducing the number of trustees from 9 to 7 in the 2018 municipal elections. Further, staff present the results of this consultation to the Business Committee meeting in September 2017. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY
