TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE MUNICIPAL SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, JANUARY 20, 2014 5:30 P.M. COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Bill Drysdale (Chair) Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Steve Arnett Councillor Glenda Patterson Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Duck Paterson Sandy Bowden John Manson Felicity Adams Clayton Postings COUNCIL MEMBERS ABSENT: Councillor Gord Horth STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Sue Bouma CALL TO ORDER Councillor Drysdale called the Municipal Services Committee meeting to order at 5:31 p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL GS 2014-001 MINUTES GS 2014-002 REPORTS GS 2014-003 It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda for this meeting of the Municipal Services Committee be approved. It was moved, seconded and carried that the minutes of the Municipal Services Committee meeting held December 16, 2013 be approved as circulated. City Manager’s Report It was moved, seconded and carried that the City Manager’s Report for the month of December 2013 be received. Parks, Recreation and Culture Fees and Charges The Director of Parks, Recreation and Culture gave a presentation summarizing the key points and current challenges of the Parks, Recreation and Culture Fees and Charges Review. He responded to questions and outlined the next steps of the process. GS 2014-004 Purchase of Fitness Equipment It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend that Council direct Staff to amend the financial plan to accommodate the replacement of eleven pieces of aging cardio equipment at the Frank Jameson Community Centre. Municipal Services Committee January 20, 2014 Page 1 GS 2014-005 Building Inspector’s Report – December 2013 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Building Inspector’s Report for the month of December 2013 be received. GS 2014-006 Fire Chief’s Report – December 2013 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Fire Chief’s Report for the month of December 2013 be received. GS 2014-007 Coastal Animal Control Services – December 2013 Pound Report It was moved, seconded and carried that the Coastal Animal Control Services Pound Report for the month of December 2013 be received. The Committee requested staff to direct Coastal Animal Services to increase patrols in the Forrest Field area and recommended that more visible signage be erected in the Forrest Field area regarding pet regulations. GS 2014-008 CORRESPONDENCE GS 2014-009 ADJOURNMENT GS 2014-010 Ladysmith Visitors Centre - 2013 Year End Statistics It was moved, seconded and carried that the Ladysmith Visitors Centre 2013 Year End Statistics Report be received. Shawn O’Toole Off Leash Area - Holland Creek Trail It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that the correspondence received from Shawn O’Toole regarding the establishment of Holland Creek Trail as an area where dogs must be leashed be referred to the Parks, Recreation and Culture Commission and that the Commission be requested to consider Committee’s recommendation that the Holland Creek Trail be deemed an on leash area. It was moved, seconded and carried that this meeting of the Municipal Services Committee be adjourned at 6:25 p.m. CERTIFIED CORRECT Chair (Councillor B. Drysdale) Corporate Officer (S.
