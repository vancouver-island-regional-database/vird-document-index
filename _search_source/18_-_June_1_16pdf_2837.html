CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held June 1, 2016 Inclusion Policy Folio SB16/06/01-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Higinson The Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves the Inclusion Policy as written and presented at the Special Board meeting on June 1, 2016. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The policy is based on the principles of respect, acceptance, safety and equity. It is believed to be one of the first in Canada. It identifies that all members of the Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools community has the right to expect that its policies, procedures, programs and communications are inclusive and respectful; taking into consideration visible and invisible diversities including but not limited to: race, sexual orientation, gender identity, ability, religion, culture and socio-economic status. Next steps are:  to have the Administrative Procedures in place for September 2017  the Social Justice Advocates will be tasked with ensuring the new policy is in line with the Code of Conduct in schools and using a common language; educative, preventative and restorative  educating staff, adults and students is key  empowering students that are involved in student leadership in the schools QUESTION PERIOD There were no Questions this evening.
