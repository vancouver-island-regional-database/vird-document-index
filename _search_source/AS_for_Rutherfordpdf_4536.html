THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: December 2, 2015 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Rutherford Elementary School Closure FIRST READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-06 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Rutherford Elementary School, 5840 Hammond Bay Road, Nanaimo, BC, V9T 5M6; Facility No. 068077; on June 30, 2017 or later, subject to the completion of a new wing at Frank J. Ney School. In the event capital funding is not approved for the Frank J. Ney construction, or construction is delayed such that it is not expected to be completed by September 2018, the issue of the closure of Rutherford Elementary School will be revisited by the Board. This will be given first reading. SECOND READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-06 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Rutherford Elementary School, 5840 Hammond Bay Road, Nanaimo, BC, V9T 5M6; Facility No. 068077; on June 30, 2017 or later, subject to the completion of a new wing at Frank J. Ney School. In the event capital funding is not approved for the Frank J. Ney construction, or construction is delayed such that it is not expected to be completed by September 2018, the issue of the closure of Rutherford Elementary School will be revisited by the Board. This will be given second reading. UNANIMOUS CONSENT REQUIRED TO READ THIRD AND FINAL BYLAW RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Rutherford Elementary School Closure Bylaw No. 2015-06 at its Special Board Meeting of December 2, 2015. THIRD AND FINAL READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-06 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Rutherford Elementary School, 5840 Hammond Bay Road, Nanaimo, BC, V9T 5M6; Facility No. 068077; on June 30, 2017 or later, subject to the completion of a new wing at Frank J. Ney School. In the event capital funding is not approved for the Frank J. Ney construction, or construction is delayed such that it is not expected to be completed by September 2018, the issue of the closure of Rutherford Elementary School will be revisited by the Board. This will be given third and final reading. RECOMMENDED MOTION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) seek to secure a project agreement from the Ministry of Education on the construction of a new wing at Frank J. Ney Elementary. RECOMMENDED MOTION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) allows for input from School Consolidation Working Group – Rutherford before finalizing catchment changes required on the closure of Rutherford Elementary. RECOMMENDED MOTION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) relocates the Strong Start located at Rutherford to Frank J. Ney as part of the closure of Rutherford Elementary.
