Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION REGULAR BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: June 22, 2016 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Green Network Request Background: At the June 8, 2016 Business Committee meeting, a presentation by the Student Energy Ambassadors requested that the Board continue to support the Green Network. For the current year, $1,700 was allocated to support these students’ activities to help reduce energy consumption and other green initiatives. In their presentation they requested $2,000 to be allocation for the 2016-17 school years. The following motion was passed by the Business Committee: The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that they refer the request for funding to staff and that staff bring their recommendation back to the Board meeting June 22. When preparing the 2016-17 Budget, the $1,700 allocated for the current year was maintained for the 2016-17 school year. Staff believe the extra $300 can be found in existing budgets to continue to support this groups initiatives. Recommendation to consider: The Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) will continue to support the Student Energy Ambassadors by providing $2,000 to support their activities for the 2016-17 school year.
