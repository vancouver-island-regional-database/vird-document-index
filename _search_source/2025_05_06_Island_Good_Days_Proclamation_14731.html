2025 05 06 Island Good Days Proclamation

Town of Ladysmith
2025-05-06
Original PDF

TOWN OF LADYSMITH PROCLAMATION Island Good Days WHEREAS: The Vancouver Island Economic Alliance (VIEA) created the Island Good brand in 2018 to support local producers, growers, and manufacturers by making it easier for shoppers to identify and purchase locally made products; and WHEREAS: The Island Good brand has strengthened the sustainability, selfreliance, and resiliency of the Vancouver Island and rural Islands regional economy; and WHEREAS: Island Good is proudly celebrating its 7th anniversary in 2025, with over 140 Island Good licensed businesses across the region, including four within the Town of Ladysmith; and WHEREAS: Island Good fosters community pride, supports local jobs, and inspires consumers to choose local first; and WHEREAS: This is an opportunity to recognize and celebrate the contributions of Island Good producers and manufacturers to our local economy and community. THEREFORE: I, Deena Beeston, Mayor of the Town of Ladysmith, do hereby proclaim that May 30- June 6, 2025, inclusive, shall be known as "Island Good Days" in the Town of Ladysmith, British Columbia. _____________________ Mayor D. Beeston May 6, 2025
