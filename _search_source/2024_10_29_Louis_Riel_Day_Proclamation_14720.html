Town of Ladysmith
2024-10-29
Original PDF

TOWN OF LADYSMITH PROCLAMATION Louis Riel Day WHEREAS: The Metis people are recognized as one of Canada’s aboriginal peoples; and WHEREAS: the Metis culture, rich in spiritual beliefs and colourful traditions, is an integral part of British Columbia’s multicultural character; and WHEREAS: throughout history, Metis citizens have made significant contributions to the development and success of our province; and WHEREAS: Louis Riel has made an enormous contribution towards the development of this country; and WHEREAS: British Columbia recognizes “Louis Riel Day”, November 16th as a national Metis holiday in commemoration of the 138th anniversary of the death of Louis Riel. THEREFORE, I, Tricia McKay, Acting Mayor of the Town of Ladysmith, do hereby proclaim November 16, 2024 as “Louis Riel Day” in the Town of Ladysmith, British Columbia. ________________________________ Acting Mayor T. McKay October 29, 2024
