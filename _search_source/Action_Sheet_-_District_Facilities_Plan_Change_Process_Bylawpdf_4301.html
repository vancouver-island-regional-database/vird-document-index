THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: December 10, 2010 TO: The Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: DISTRICT FACILITIES PLAN CHANGE PROCESS BYLAW BACKGROUND: At the November 24, 2010 Board meeting the following motion was adopted. R10/11/24-33That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) refers the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw to a Special Policy Committee Meeting with staff and trustees in attendance to process any concerns on the issues raised in the legal opinion and that a report be brought back to the Board. The Special Policy Committee met on Tuesday, December 7th and reviewed its concerns related to the Bylaw. The revised Bylaw is attached for the Board’s consideration. FIRST READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: SECOND READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: UNANIMOUS CONSENT ACTION REQUIRED: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw and that it is given first reading. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw and that it is given second reading. Unanimous consent of the Board must be given to authorize the SecretaryTreasurer to read the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw a third time at the same Board Meeting. RECOMMENDATION: THIRD READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw a third time at the current Board meeting. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw and that it is given third and final reading.
