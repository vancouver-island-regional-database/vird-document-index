REVISIONS N/A QUESTIONS Questions have been paraphrased. 1. Question: How much support is needed from the consultant for stakeholder engagement? Answer: The Town will likely not have the expertise to complete applications, discuss with regulators, provide drawings, etc. The Town would initiate engagement with Mosaic, Council, regulators, and other stakeholders and take care of reasonable responses and meetings; however, the Consultant will likely be needed to complete technical portions of applications, provide answers to questions, explain complex problems, and other related items. Stakeholder engagement will be an important part of the project. Although difficult to quantify the level of effort required, the Town is small with limited staff and resources so staff will rely on the Consultant throughout. 2. Question: In Appendix A, 3. Scope, 5. Preliminary Design, a. the Town notes “deconstruction of existing dams”. What is meant by this? Answer: The dams are not intended to be deconstructed and replaced entirely. It is anticipated that some deconstruction, such as removing rip rap, excavating the low level output, the abutments, etc. may be required to successfully raise the dam height. These activities should be captured during preliminary design. Town of Ladysmith RFP No. 2023-IS-07 Addendum # 2 Page 3 of 3 3. Question: In Section 4 the RFP mentions Council meetings. Are these expected to be in-person meetings? Answer: Although Council meetings are mostly held in person, the Consultant would only be required to attend via Zoom during the related topic. In-person attendance would also be acceptable, but not expected. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION This RFP now closes within 7 days and only administrative questions are able to be answered. End of Addendum #2
