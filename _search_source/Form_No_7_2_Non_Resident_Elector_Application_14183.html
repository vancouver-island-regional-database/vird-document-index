Form 7-2, Page 2 of 2 READ THESE INSTRUCTIONS CAREFULLY BEFORE COMPLETING THE APPLICATION FORM (1) Print applicant’s surname and given names in full. (2) Print the street address or legal description of the applicant’s real property in relation to which the applicant is registering. Applicant must attach satisfactory proof that he or she is entitled to register in relation to the real property referred to in part (2) of the application form (e.g., certificate of title, property tax notice, property assessment notice). (3) Print the applicant’s mailing address including street number and name, city/town and postal code. (4) Print either the last 6 digits of the applicant’s social insurance number or the applicant’s birthdate. This information is required pursuant to the Local Government Act . (5) Complete this section if the applicant’s name has changed since previously registering as a non-resident property elector. (6) Applicant must declare that he or she meets all the requirements listed in this section to register as a non-resident property elector. (7) Applicant must sign the application form in the presence of a witness. (8) The witness to the applicant’s signature must sign the application form. (9) Print residential address of the witness. Not required if the witness is an election official. (10) You must attach the “Non-Resident Property Elector Consent Form” (Form 7-3) if the land title registration of the real property in relation to which a person is registering under this section indicates that there is more than one individual who is the registered owner of the real property. The person may only register as a nonresident property elector with the written consent of the number of those individuals who, together with the person registering, are a majority of those individuals. (11) If person is registering at the time of voting, 2 pieces of identification (at least one with a signature) to prove identity are required. LGA s. 67, 71 and 73
