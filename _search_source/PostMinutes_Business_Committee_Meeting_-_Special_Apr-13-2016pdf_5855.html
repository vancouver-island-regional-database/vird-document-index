Hackwood, DASG Rep CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 4:33 and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio BC16/04/13-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS Secretary-Treasurer, Graham Roberts Budget Development - Meeting #2 Secretary-Treasurer Graham Roberts, continued the 2016-17 Becoming Sustainable budget process. His presentation provided an overview of the current budget year and a look forward to the 2016-17 school year. The presentation is Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 13, 2016 attached to the Agenda. CUPE President, Rob Zver requested to present to the Business Committee on March 25th. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic Folio BC16/04/13-02 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) to direct staff to provide qualitative and quantitative impact statements on district staff reductions and/or additions. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Restorative Needs Budget Some Trustees would like a restorative needs budget upon completion of the regular budget to be presented in tandem with the 2016-2017 budget. They would like the staffing levels from 2002-2003 budget to be used as a base year. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Higginson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic Folio BC16/04/13-03 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that upon completion of the 2016-17 budget, staff prepare a "needs based" budget using staffing levels from the 2002-2003 year to be submitted with the actual budget. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions from the audience this evening. ADJOURNMENT Folio BC16/04/13-04 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:00 pm.
