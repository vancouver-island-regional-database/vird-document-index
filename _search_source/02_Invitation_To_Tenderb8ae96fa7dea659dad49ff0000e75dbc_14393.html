INVITATION TO TENDER Town of Ladysmith Sandy Beach Lift Station Improvements Signed Tenders in sealed envelopes that are clearly marked "Tender for Town of Ladysmith, Sandy Beach Lift Station Improvements” will be received at the Town Hall, 410 Esplanade, Ladysmith, B.C., up to 2:00:00 pm local time, Thursday June 18th, 2026, after which time they will be opened by Town staff. The Tenders will not be opened in public. Tenderers may request unofficial results following the opening. The work includes the design, installation and operation of a temporary bypass pumping system, removal of existing Sandy Beach Lift Station discharge piping and installation of new discharge piping, valve boxes and butterfly valves as shown on the contract drawings. Tender documents will be available from Koers & Associates Engineering Ltd., after 11:00 am local time, on Friday May 22nd, 2026. Tenderers shall register at http://www.koers-eng.com/ for digital access to the Tender documents. Tenderers can register by clicking on the Contractor Sign-up link at the bottom of the homepage and following the prompts for registration information. Login information for access to the digital Tender documents will be emailed to Tenderers upon successful completion of Tenderer registration. Tender registration instructions are available by emailing tenders@koers-eng.com. Paper copies of the Tender documents will be made available upon request from Koers & Associates upon payment of $210.00 (incl. GST) per set. Tender Documents Available Friday May 22, 2026 11:00am Last Question & Answer Received Monday June 8, 2026 5:00pm Last Addenda/Q&A Response Wednesday June 10, 2026 5:00pm Tender Closing Thursday June 18, 2026 2:00pm Technical enquiries regarding the project shall be directed to Carsen Willans, P.Eng, of Koers & Associates Engineering Ltd., by email at cwillans@koers-eng.com. Tenders must be accompanied by the specified Bid Security, payable to the Town of Ladysmith. Award of the contract is subject to sufficient budget funds being available for the project. The proposed project superintendent, subcontractors, schedule of completion, size of workforce, proposed equipment, previous experience, and submission of suitable references from other similar sized projects may all be considered in review and acceptance of the Tender. The Town of Ladysmith reserves the right to waive minor informalities in or reject any or all Tenders, or accept the Tender deemed most favourable in the interests of the Town, as detailed in this invitation and the Tender documents. Tenders not conforming to the specified requirements may be returned to the Tenderer without consideration. The lowest or any Tender will not necessarily be accepted. Mr. Tim Tanton, P.Eng.
