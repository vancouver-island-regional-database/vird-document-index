Building on school and district-wide connections Keys to Success ● The 4 Cs: Collaboration, Commitment, Communication, Contribution ● Team members with experience ● Annual activities that students and teachers can anticipate ● Planning ahead: Set goals, make a calendar for the year, then create short term action plans ● Seeking out resources ● Creative and innovative approaches ● Being our own leaders by following through on our ideas and action plans Our recommendations 1. Continue and expand Energy Cup The Energy Cup provided district support for school conservation initiatives and 10 weeks of focus on energy 2. A district leadership position for sustainability a. Support educators and administrators in the adoption of sustainability curriculum, policies and systems.
