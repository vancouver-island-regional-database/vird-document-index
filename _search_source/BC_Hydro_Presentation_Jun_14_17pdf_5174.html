Lighting Updates 2017/2018 • Early Submission this year • Continuing exterior LED/interior occupancy sensors • Piloting T-Led bulbs at Hammond Bay & Quarterway • DAC interior LED feasibility study, DAC + compound exterior LED • Nights out, lights out feasibility & further piloting Nights Out, Lights Out • Call out to K-12 sector E.M.’s and EFMA members. • 10 SD’s (approx.17%) indicated they are using “Dark Schools” approach for energy management/vandalism reduction.  1 more asked for my findings and decided they would push this in their District • Most Districts are using this strategy for energy management only, but vandalism reduction is reported as high as 48%. • We are currently looking into energy saving feasibility, piloting. • Conducted our first after hours lighting audit on Feb. 27th. Heat Recovery/Electrification Opportunity • The Regional District of Nanaimo upgraded their sewer outfall line within 2m of Hammond Bay Elementary. • With the assistance of CNCP/AFG funding, NLPS will install water source heat pumps to absorb consistent and reliable heat from the outfall. • This upgrade will also be paired with a phase 2 mechanical/DDC upgrade through SEP/AFG funding. This will bring the Schools ventilation up to current ASHRAE standards. • Carbon footprint of the School is estimated to be reduced by up to 78%. • Substantial completion, August 2017.
