THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: November 28, 2012 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations SUBJECT: Transportation Department and Services Review References:  November 28, 2012 - Evaluation of Transportation Services (by Management Partnership Services, Inc.) Report Background A request for Expression of Interest and qualifications was issued April 2012 and the District received five responses. A Request for Offers was subsequently sent to three of the five firms and after District review a contract was awarded in June 2012 to Management Partnerships Services, Inc. of Rockville, Maryland. This firm was chosen by a committee as the best to meet all criteria in the statement of services/scope of work and the lowest price of $22,400 Canadian Funds. The contractor visited the District, collected data and met with key stakeholders in July 2012. Public and stakeholder input were sought in September and October via Thought Stream and the detailed results were summarized and provided to the consultant for consideration during the review. While the report is a standalone review of transportation services provided by the District, it is important for the Board to understand that the completed review must be taken into context with respect to all educational services/programs in the District. Staff must have an opportunity to ensure that the recommendations in the report are appropriately considered within the context of the District’s Educational Strategic Plan. Therefore, staff recommends that the report be referred staff, which will allow the recommendations to be considered in the context of all District educational goals.
