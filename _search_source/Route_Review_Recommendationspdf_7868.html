Appendix A Bus Route Review Recommendations The Annual Bus Route Review process started in the 2017/18 school year. The following provides information of Bus Route alterations made through this process. The alterations listed were presented and approved in the year prior to the dates listed: i.e. bus route changes made in 2018/19 were a part of the 2017/18 Annual Bus Route Review. 2018/19 Bus Route 1 – Trip 1 – Services Hammond Bay Rd. to Dover Bay. With 12 registered, eligible riders, safe walk conditions and availability of public transit bussing, it is recommended to eliminate this Trip of Route 1. Routes 5 & 6 – Trip 2 – Services Lantzville to Seaview School. With 33 registered riders on Route 6, Trip 2 and 27 registered riders on Route 5, Trip 2 unless registration increases, it is possible to combine the Trip on both routes into one route for the 2018/19 school year. Bus Routes 11 & 15 – Services Cassidy/Yellow Point areas to LSS. These bus routes drops students off at LSS well before school start time. Several complaints/concerns have been mentioned by parents and school based staff. Transportation Department staff have done a thorough review of all bus routes providing bus service in the South end of the District and propose changes that would affect bus routes 8, 11,12, 15 & 17 and provide service improvements to service for LSS students in the Cassidy and Yellow Point areas. It is expected that the proposed changes will increase ridership from current eligible riders who do not take advantage of the bus service due to time of route/drop off at LSS. These changes would affect the Cassidy and Yellow Point areas to LSS. _________________________________________________________________________________ 2019/20 Bus Route 3 – Trip 1 – Services Jingle Pot Rd. area to NDSS With 12 registered, eligible riders, reasonably safe walk conditions and availability of public transit bussing, it is recommended to eliminate this Trip of Route 3. _________________________________________________________________________________ 2020/21 Bus Route 3 – Trip 1 & 2 - Services Jingle Pot Rd. area to NDSS and Mountain View Schools Bus Route 3 alterations that moved 2 bus stop locations from Jingle Pot Road for traffic safety issues. The alteration provided bus stop in the Arbot Road and Westwood Road area and another in the Cathers Lake area. This would improve student safety and partially address the requests from Parents – to provide bus service in the Westwood Lake area to Mountain View School. For safety reasons, staff do not recommend providing bus service into this area.
