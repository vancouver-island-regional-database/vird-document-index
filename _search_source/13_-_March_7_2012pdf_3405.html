CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Local School Calendar - 20122013 Mr. Turin reviewed the Local School Calendar Action Sheet with Trustees. He said that under the proposed Local School Calendar, Spring Break would be extended to the Easter weekend for 2013. Spring Break is scheduled for Monday, March 18 to Friday, March 22 next year. The proposed Local School Calendar in SD68 would add Monday, March 25 to Thursday, March 28 to the Spring Break. Good Friday is March 29 and Easter Monday is April 1. The instructional time for those extra four days would be made up by extending each week in session by approximately 32 minutes in elementary schools and 35 minutes in secondary schools. These changes are being considered primarily for budgetary reasons. It is estimated that about Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held March 7, 2012 2 $366,000 can be saved next year as a result of a longer Spring Break.
