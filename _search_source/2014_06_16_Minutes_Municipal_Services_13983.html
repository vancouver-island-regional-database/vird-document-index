TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE MUNICIPAL SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, JUNE 16, 2014 5:30 P.M. COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Bill Drysdale (Chair) Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Duck Paterson Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Glenda Patterson (entered later) COUNCIL MEMBERS ABSENT Councillor Steve Arnett Councillor Gord Horth STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Joanna Winter CALL TO ORDER AGENDA APPROVAL MS 2014-031 MINUTES MS 2014-032 DELEGATION Councillor Drysdale called the Municipal Services Committee meeting to order at 5:32 p.m. Moved and seconded: That the agenda for this meeting of the Municipal Services Committee be approved. Motion carried. Moved and seconded: That the minutes of the Municipal Services Committee meeting held March 17, 2014 be approved. Motion carried. Gretchen Hartley, MaryAnn Deacon, Cowichan Valley Hospice Society Hospice Services in Ladysmith and the Cowichan Region G. Hartley and M. Deacon, accompanied by volunteers, staff and Board members, provided Council with an overview of the extensive services provided by the Cowichan Valley Hospice Society, with a focus on the 67 clients served in the Town of Ladysmith. Ms. Hartley outlined a request for the Town to support the Cowichan Valley Regional District bylaw that will provide funding to the Cowichan Valley Hospice Society. Council thanked Ms. Hartley and associates for the excellent services provided by the society. REPORTS MS 2014-033 City Manager’s Report Moved and seconded: That the City Manager’s Report for the months of March, April and May 2014 be received. Motion carried. Municipal Services Committee June 16, 2014 Page 1 MS 2014-034 Financial Update to May 31, 2014 Moved and seconded: That the Financial Update to May 31, 2014 be received. Motion carried. MS 2014-035 Building Inspector’s Reports -- March, April and May 2014 Moved and seconded: That the Building Inspector’s Reports for the months of March, April and May 2014 be received. Motion carried. MS 2014-036 Fire Chief’s Report – March, April and May 2014 Moved and seconded: That the Fire Chief’s Reports for the months of March, April and May 2014 be received. Motion carried. The Committee requested that the Protective Services Committee consider the potential cost savings of reducing the involvement of Ladysmith Fire/Rescue in accident traffic control and report back with any recommendations. MS 2014-037 ADJOURNMENT MS 2014-038 Coastal Animal Control Services – March, April and May 2014 Pound Reports Moved and seconded: That the Coastal Animal Control Services Pound Reports for the months of March, April and May 2014 be received. Motion carried. The Committee requested that they be provided with the number of dog licences issued for 2014 in Ladysmith. Moved and seconded: That this meeting of the Municipal Services Committee adjourn at 6:07 p.m. Motion carried. CERTIFIED CORRECT Chair (Councillor B. Drysdale) Corporate Officer (S.
