Public Engagement Budget Criteria for 2019 2020 Setting decision criteria is best practice when making organizational decisions. Decision criteria in a business setting are those variables or characteristics that are important to the organization making the decision. They should help evaluate the alternatives from which you are choosing. Decision analysis often involves weighting criteria but in this context, staff are recommending setting priority order for criteria. When faced with a budget decision, the priority order should be considered in respect of where funding should be directed first and so on.
