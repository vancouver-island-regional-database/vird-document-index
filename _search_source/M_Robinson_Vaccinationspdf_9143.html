From: To: Subject: Date: Karen Matthews Karen Matthews Vaccinations October 8, 2021 1:56:34 PM From: Mark Robinson <> Sent: October 8, 2021 9:31 AM To: District Administration Centre Manager Subject: Correspondence to School Board CAUTION: External Message Dear school trustees of Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools, RE: Vaccinations While recognizing that the provincial government should be the body making major public health decisions, nonetheless as we all know Premier John Horgan has come out and delagated responsibility to the local level on the issue of vaccinations and employment or being a pre-requisite to assemble on district property. My family believes that every tool available in the tool box should be used to keep as many stakeholders in our district as protected as possible, and we are happy to see the BCTF has publicly stated that they would not oppose such a decision. Therefore, please count my family as supporting mandatory vaccinations for everyone on school district property, including school trustees and volunteers; all of you would have our full support on this if this policy is enacted. We believe that as the owner of all Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools property, you have the ultimate ability to decide access accordingly. I should add that I write this as a parent, not as president of my children's school PAC. Thank you for all the work you have done for our community thus far in your capacity, and please be encouraged knowing that it is appreciated by those who are paying attention.
