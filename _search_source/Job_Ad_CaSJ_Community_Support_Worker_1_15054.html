Stz’uminus Summer Job Opportunity Job Title: Pay: Community Support Worker (6 positions) $18.25 per hour Contract Hours: Location: 8 Weeks Term, starting June 29, 2026 Mon to Fri, 35 hours per week Ladysmith, BC About The Role The Community Support Worker will assist in organizing and delivering programs and activities for the community, focusing on cultural, educational, and recreational events. This role is an excellent opportunity for students interested in social work, education, or community development. Responsibilities • Assist in planning and executing community programs and events. • Provide support during cultural and recreational activities. • Engage with community members to promote participation. • Help with administrative tasks, such as scheduling and record-keeping. • Ensure the safety and cleanliness of the community center during activities. Qualifications • Must be between 15 and 30 years of age at the beginning of the employment. • Must have a valid Social Insurance Number at the start of employment. • Interest in community development, social work, or education. • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills. • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment. SFN is committed to respecting diversity within our workforce; preference will be given to individuals who identify as First Nations, Inuit, or Métis. How to apply Please send your application to alan.francis@stzuminus.com, with subject, as title of the Job applied for. For more information about us, please visit: Stz'uminus (stzuminus.com)
