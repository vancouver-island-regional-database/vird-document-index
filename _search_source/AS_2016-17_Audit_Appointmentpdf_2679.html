Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION REGULAR BOARD ACTION SHEET DATE: March 15, 2017 TO: Board of Education FROM: Secretary-Treasurer, Carrie McVeigh SUBJECT: Appointment of Auditors Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) appoint MNP as auditors for School District No. 68 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2017. Background: In accordance with Section 158 of the School Act, the Board of each school district is required to appoint an auditor to audit the accounts and financial transactions of the school district and to issue an audit opinion on its annual financial statements. MNP has been the auditor for School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) since 2010. Given the limited time of staff to issue and evaluate a public procurement process for external audit services for this fiscal year, we recommend that MNP be appointed as the school district’s auditor for one more year. We intend to issue a request for proposals for external audit services in the first half of the 2017/18 fiscal year.
