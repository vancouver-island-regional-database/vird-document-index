Micheal Razberry, Razmataz Productions Proposed Script for Vancouver Island Miners’ Strike 3–5 Council will recall that it passed the following resolution in response to a request from M. Razberry for Town sponsorship of his documentary on the Vancouver Island Miners’ Strike. Resolution CS 2012-353, November 5, 2012 That the Town of Ladysmith provide a sponsorship in the amount of $1,500.00 for the proposed documentary about the Vancouver Island Miners’ Strike, to be produced by Razmataz Productions, with the funds to be allocated in the 2013 budget, provided that the Town and the Ladysmith and District Historical Society are permitted to review and approve the script prior to production and that the producer meet with the Ladysmith Historical Society. Mr. Razberry has met with the Historical Society. His script outline is attached for Council’s review.
