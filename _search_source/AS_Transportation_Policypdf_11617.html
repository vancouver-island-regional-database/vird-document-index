NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: February 14, 2024 Business Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Policy 3.8 – Transportation to and from School (Draft Revised) Recommendation: The Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopt revised Policy 3.8 Transportation to and from School. Background: On December 20, 2024, the Board of Education passed the following motion: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) send the feedback from the consultation on the Transportation Policy to the Policy Committee and that the Policy Committee make recommended changes (if applicable) for return to the Business Committee upon completion. This Action Sheet provides the recommendations from the Policy Committee and next steps. Discussion: The Policy Committee met to discuss the feedback provided. Most of the feedback related to specific situations and was forward to the Transportation Department for review and consideration. There is one substantive addition to paragraph 3, recommended as follows: “The Board believes that schools should be idle-free and supports infrastructure to educate our community in meeting this goal.” The impact of this change would be to ensure that infrastructure that discourages idling will be supported by the Board. Note that while employees currently have an administrative procedure that limits idling, the Committee determined that a Policy that specifically prohibits idling on Board property by the community could create additional burdens on staff in enforcement. The focus will be on education and can be revisited in future. Appendix A: Amended Policy 3.8 – Transportation to and from School
