THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: June 26, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Trustee Saunders, Chairperson of the Transportation Ad Hoc Committee SUBJECT: Transportation Ad Hoc Committee Report BACKGROUND The Transportation Ad Hoc Committee held its first meeting May 30th. The Committee members are TerryLynn Saunders, Phil Turin, Pete Sabo, Scott Saywell, Patrick Young, Alana Cameron, and Dave Prevost. Attached are the minutes from the meeting. RECOMMENDATION That the Transportation Sub-Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that staff be directed to acquire transportation management software as recommended in the MPS Evaluation of Transportation Services Report dated November 28, 2012 and that the source of funding be found in the 2012/13 budget.
