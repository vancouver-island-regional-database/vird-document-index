“SHADOW ANNUAL BUDGET BYLAW” A Bylaw of THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT No. 69 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (called the "Board") to adopt the “Shadow Annual Budget” to meet provisions of the Pre 2002 Teachers’ Collective Agreement of the Board for the fiscal year 2014/15 pursuant to section 113 of the School Act, R.S.B.C., 1996, c. 412 as amended from time to time (called the "Act"). 1. The Board has complied with the provisions of the Act respecting the “Shadow Annual Budget” adopted by this bylaw. 2. This bylaw may be cited as School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Shadow Annual Budget Bylaw for fiscal year 2014/15. 3. The attached “Statement 2" showing the estimated revenue and expense for the 2014/15 fiscal year and the total Shadow Annual Budget Bylaw amount of $39,139,079,254 for the 2014/15 fiscal year was prepared in accordance with the Act. 4. Statement 2, Statement 4 and Schedules 2 to 4 are adopted as the “2014-2015 Shadow Annual Budget” to meet provisions of the Pre 2002 Teachers’ Collective Agreement of the Board for the fiscal year 2014/15. READ A FIRST TIME THE 5TH DAY OF MAY, 2014 READ A SECOND TIME THE 5TH DAY OF MAY, 2014 READ A THIRD TIME, PASSED AND ADOPTED THE 5TH DAY OF MAY 2014 Chairperson of the Board (Corporate Seal) Secretary Treasurer I HEREBY CERTIFY this to be a true original of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) “Shadow Annual Budget” Bylaw 2014/15 to meet provisions of the Pre 2002 Teachers’ Collective Agreement, adopted by the Board the 5TH DAY OF MAY 2014.
