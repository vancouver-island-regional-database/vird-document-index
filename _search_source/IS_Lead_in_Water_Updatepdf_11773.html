NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: April 10, 2024 Business Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Pete Sabo, Executive Director Planning & Operations Brian Hackwood, Assistant Director Planning & Operations Lead in Water Update Background: As of March 31, 2023, all remediation works were completed, and the Facilities Department commenced the process of compliance and maintenance. See attached detailed background. Discussion: The Ministry requires that all the NLPS facilities be tested every 3 years for compliance (1/3 every year) as follows: • • • Water quality reports are forwarded to the Ministry and Island Health to determine if remediation works are successful. Based on long term results, flushing duration and signage requiring faucets to be run prior to consumption may be removed. It is imperative that flushing systems be maintained each year to confirm the operation and schedule of the flush valves. As a precaution, all signage has remained in place until it is proven that the system can operate reliably. The 2023/24 Annual testing results were as follows: • • • 13 Facilities tested 68 water samples from drinking/food preparation sources All tests returned within Guidelines for Canadian Drinking Water Quality Results have been submitted to both the Ministry and Island Health and discussions are underway with Island Health as to possibly shortening the flushing duration (currently 20 minutes).
