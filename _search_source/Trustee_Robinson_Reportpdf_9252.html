TRUSTEE LIAISON COMMITTEE REPORT Trustee Robinson November 1, 2021 October 2021 NLSF board meeting • Stock the Lockers was very successful this year - $30,000. • Food4Schools supports 29 schools plus 4 programs at John Barsby and 2 at NDSS for a total of 33 food programs being provided weekly food deliveries. • A discussion was held regarding the need for a NLSF vehicle for food delivery –- They are now sharing a Loaves and Fishes vehicle. They talked about them buying a vehicle and it then being put into the NLPS vehicle pool for regular service. • There were frequent comments of appreciation for the ongoing support being given by our district.
