Kelsey Toye i E Subject: FW: Proposed 2016/2017Bus Schedule Sent: Thursday, May 05, 2016 8:43 PM To: John Blain Subject: Proposed 2016/2017Bus Schedule Hello Mr. Blain, I am the parent of a primary school child who attends Chase River Elementary School. I have just become aware of busing schedule changes that will occur for children living within the community of Extension. I am extremely concerned that under this new schedule, my child will be arriving at his school at 7:55am, when school does not begin until 8:45. This is not acceptable in my opinion, for an eight year old child to be outside for 50 minutes before his instructional time begins. My child takes the bus every morning to school, he walks from our home to the bus stop, with this new schedule, in the winter months, he will be leaving our home in the dark in a rural area with no street lights, to catch a bus and then wait for his educational clayto begin in often inclement weather. I am also concerned on a safety level. Who will ensure supervision for our children? I trust that student safety and student success are your top priority, in no way will this new bus schedule provide either for my child or the children in the community of Extension. There is always another option, I urge you to explore them. I will be awaiting a response from you on this matter. Thank you in advance. KariO’Hara
