3137 Barons Road Nanaimo, BC V9T 5W5 p: f: e: w: 250 756-1237 250 756-0188 ndta@shaw.ca www.ndta68.ca August 20, 2020 Charlene McKay, Board Chair Scott Saywell, Superintendent School District 68, Nanaimo-Ladysmith 395 Wakesiah Avenue Nanaimo, BC V9R 3K6 Dear Ms. McKay and Mr. Saywell: The Executive of the Nanaimo District Teachers’ Association met on Monday, August 17, 2020, and adopted the following positions: • • • • • every staff member must be supplied with two reusable face masks and a face shield at the start of the school year; every student must be supplied with a reusable face mask for the start of the school year; supplies of PPE must be available as needed throughout the year; adults and students 10 years and older must be required to wear face masks when physical distancing is not possible or practicable, as long as there is not a medical condition that prevents usage; and classroom density must be reduced to allow for physical distancing. These safety measures are being practiced in the community at large and have been mandated in school settings in other jurisdictions. We do not feel they are unreasonable requests. If these safety measures are not in place for the commencement of the school year, we believe schools to be unsafe for staff and students, and that teachers will need to consider exercising their individual right to refuse unsafe work under WorkSafe legislation.
