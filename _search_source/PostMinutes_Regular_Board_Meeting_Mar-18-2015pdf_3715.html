CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY TRUSTEE ZONE REPORT Trustee Robinson - Has met with the Nanaimo Chamber of Commerce, the Executive Director of the Ladysmith Chamber of Commerce and the Right to Play organization. Trustee Solomon - Bayview School had an unveiling of their new mural today in which there was a ceremony that was well attended and beautiful. Trustee Routley - No news from his zone. Trustee Brzovic - The PAC's took part in YOUR VOICE 2015. Frank Ney had a Chinese New Year breakfast and the Wedding Singer at NDSS went really well. Trustee Bob - Visited Wellington and the seismic upgrades are coming along well. Trustee Kimler - There was a grad fashion show at Ladysmith Secondary School with about 50 students that were raising money for Grad 2015. There was a grade 5 DARE graduation. Trustee Brennan - There was an orientation session hosted by the student council at NDSS for incoming grade 8's. The Wedding Singer was wonderful.
