CUPE APPROACH Budget 2016-2017 A Balanced Budget with a Balanced Approach I am here tonight to ask you, as Trustees, to reconsider Senior Management’s proposals on cutting Support Staff to balance this years’ budget. We believe there is enough funding in this years’ allocation to allow this district to adopt a roll over budget that will still allow for the additional staffing that Senior Management has proposed. We have been constantly reminding you that there have been some additional costing being added to salaries and benefits for which are not being fully spent, leading to larger than normal surpluses. As support staff workers we hope that you will ask Senior Management to closely look at those costings and any savings be brought forward to eliminate the proposed cuts to support staff workers. Over the next few pages I will reflect on where some savings have been found to balance next years’ budget.
