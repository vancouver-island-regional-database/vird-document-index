ANNUAL TRUSTEE ZONE REPORT Trustee Pellegrin School Year: 2022/2023 School Names/Zone: Cilaire elementary Annual Highlights: Cilaire (Home of the cyclones) came back from being at woodlands for the year during Seismic upgrades. Lisa Frey is the principal she came from Georgia. There are 181 students at Cilaire and 4 different languages. The feeding futures program is going very smoothly. NLP S. Foundation has a new form that makes ordering lunches and breakfast items easy and already made. The number of students requiring more support for food has increased a since 2022. The good food box from Food share has helped bring fresh fruit and vegetables to families. The beautiful ocean view library is home to the arts club and games club, and the librarian, and has gone from half a day a week to a full day. Half of the lower field has been changed to a parking lot which has helped drop off and pick up significantly. There is more accessibility around the outside of the school thanks to a newly paved path, including two paved accessible parking spots towards the entrance of the building. Cilaire has a new playground that was finished in June mostly paid for by a grant. This beautiful school backs onto beach estates park and many student get to enjoy walks down to the beach with much learning along the way. Cilaire offers many sports and is a tight knit small but mighty school community.
