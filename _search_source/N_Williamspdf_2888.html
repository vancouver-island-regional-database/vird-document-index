From: N and T Williams[mailtc: Sent: May-O4-16 3:21 PM To: Communications Subject: Regarding Cancellation of the Route 10 Bus Regarding: Proposed Cancellation of Route 10 Bus Route As a parent of two children currently attending Chase River Elementary School, it is with great frustration that l am writing this email regarding the proposed cancellation of the Route 10 school bus. Stability and routine is something our family has focused on, to help our children strive as best they can, especially at schooL We have tried our best to keep stability while dealing with the closure of their previous school, South Wellington Elementary, as well as choosing which elementary school would be the best fit for them after the closure. We chose Chase River Elementary for a variety of reasons; the proximity to our community, we would not have to split up our children between two different schools, they would not have to endure another school closure, and the fact that the majority of South Wellington children would also be attending this school. As you can see from the transportation statistics, the majority of South Wellington children continue to attend Chase River and represent approximately 85% of Route 10 riders. i understand there are policies in place, but the needs of children in a rural community should continue to be acknowledged. Maybe try coming up with alternative solutions like a fee based system for courtesy riders? In the last seven years my children have gone from walking to school everyday, to having their community school closed, their peers split between three different schools, their high school catchment changed twice, and now their bus transportation potentially cancelled, forcing them to change schools yet again. As a parent I feel helpless as I watch my children adjust and re-adjust as the school district continually makes negative changes to their educational stability. I hope you will reconsider the cancellation of bus service from South Wellington to Chase River. Sincerely, Nyla Williams .,
