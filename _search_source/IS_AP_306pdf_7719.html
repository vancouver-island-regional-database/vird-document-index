NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATION COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: January 6, 2021 Education Committee Secretary-Treasurer, Mark Walsh AP 306 – Part-Time Students Secondary Schools Background As part of staff’s commitment to modernizing Administrative Procedures (“APs”) we are attempting to review all APs to ensure that reflect current practice so that they are reliable for guidance to our staff and community. In some cases a deletion is warranted. This month, we informing the Board that AP 306 – Part-Time Students Secondary Schools (“AP 306”) will be deleted. Discussion AP 306 was created in 1979 and amended in 1990. The manner in which we enroll and have students funded at the secondary level does not align with AP 306. We do not require a student to sign a written agreement to not attend full-time. Rather, we base course and program selection on the best interests (and personal interests) of our students. Further, given how far out of line AP 306 is with modern funding mechanisms, the organization of schools and individualized learning, we intend to delete it rather than update it. https://www.sd68.bc.ca/wp-content/uploads/Administrative-Procedures/300/AP-306-Part-timeStudents-Secondary-Schools.pdf
