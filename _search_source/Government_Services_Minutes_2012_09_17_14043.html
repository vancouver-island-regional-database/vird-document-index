TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE GOVERNMENT SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 17, 2012 4:30 P.M. COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Steve Arnett (entered later) Councillor Bill Drysdale Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Glenda Patterson STAFF PRESENT: Councillor Jill Dashwood (Chair) Councillor Gord Horth Councillor Duck Paterson Ruth Malli Felicity Adams John Manson Sandy Bowden Erin Anderson Joanna Winter CALL TO ORDER Councillor Dashwood called the meeting to order at 5:00 p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL Councillor Dashwood requested the Committee’s consideration of the following addition to the agenda: 7.1 Number 11 Bell GS 2012-085 It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda for the Government Services Committee meeting of September 17, 2012 be adopted as amended. MINUTES GS 2012-086 DELEGATIONS It was moved, seconded and carried that the minutes of the Government Services Committee meeting held July 16, 2012 be adopted as circulated. Staff Sergeant Larry Chomyn, Carla Sampson (Victim Service Program) Quarterly Update S/Sgt Chomyn provided Committee members with a summary of Ladysmith Detachment activities in the second quarter of 2012, and responded to questions. Carla Sampson, Program Manager of Ladysmith Victim Services, provided an overview of the program activities in Ladysmith, and responded to questions regarding the percentage of victims served from outside Ladysmith and program funding. Ms. Sampson stated that in 2012, 70 percent of individuals served were from Ladysmith and that Areas G and H of Government Services Committee September 17, 2012 Page 1 the Cowichan Valley Regional District do not provide funding for the program. Micheal Razberry, Razmataz Productions Proposed Documentary Commemorating the Vancouver Island Miners’ Strike M. Razberry gave an overview of his proposed documentary on the Vancouver Island Miners’ Strike and responded to questions form Council. 2012-087 It was moved, seconded and carried that the following item be added to the agenda: 7.2 Request for Sponsorship of Miners’ Strike Documentary Debby Funk, Wayne Bourque (Parks Canada), Brett Hudson (BC Parks) Southern Strait of Georgia National Marine Conservation Area Reserve Proposal W. Bourque and B. Hudson presented a proposal for a national marine conservation area reserve in the southern Strait of Georgia and responded to questions from Committee members. It was indicated that Council and citizens will have further opportunities to provide input into the proposed marine conservation area. REPORTS GS 2012-088 City Managers Report The City Manager provided a verbal report to the Committee and responded to questions with respect to staff work plans and priorities. Request for Permissive Tax Exemption -- Ladysmith Maritime Society It was moved, seconded and carried that the request for Permissive Tax Exemption on each of three areas outlined in the application for permissive tax exemption from the Ladysmith Maritime Society dated September 6, 2012 be considered in seriatum. GS 2012-089 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that Area 1 (Community Amenities) of the Ladysmith Maritime Society Community Marina as outlined in the application dated September 6, 2012 be granted a permissive tax exemption for 2013 on the full assessed value. GS 2012-090 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that Area 2 (Facilities for Marine Visitors) of the Ladysmith Maritime Society Community Marina as outlined in the application dated September 6, 2012 be granted a permissive tax exemption for 2013 on the full assessed value. Government Services Committee September 17, 2012 Page 2 GS 2012-091 *DEFEATED* It was moved and seconded that the Committee recommend to Council that Area 3 (Contract Moorage) of the Ladysmith Maritime Society Community Marina as outlined in the application dated September 6, 2012 be granted a permissive tax exemption of twenty percent for 2013. OPPOSED: Councillors Drysdale, Horth, D. Paterson, G. Patterson and Dashwood. MOTION DEFEATED. Mayor Hutchins declared a conflict of interest with the following item and excused himself from the meeting. GS 2012-092 *DEFEATED* Request for Permissive Tax Exemption – Royal Canadian Legion, Branch 171 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that the Class 6 (Bar Area) portion of the Royal Canadian Legion Branch 171 be granted permissive tax exemption for 2013. OPPOSED Councillors Drysdale, Horth, D. Paterson, G. Patterson and Dashwood. MOTION DEFEATED Mayor Hutchins returned to the meeting. Councillor Arnett entered the meeting. NEW BUSINESS GS 2012-104 GS 2012-105 Number 11 Bell (Town-owned Artifact) It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that the Number 11 brass bell currently housed in Council Chambers at City Hall be loaned to the Ladysmith Museum on a long-term basis, subject to staff review of ownership of Town-owned artifacts housed at the Ladysmith Museum and Ladysmith Archives. Documentary Commemorating Vancouver Island Miners’ Strike It was moved and seconded that the Committee recommend to Council that the Town of Ladysmith provide sponsorship of $1,500 to Razmataz Productions for a documentary commemorating the 100th anniversary of the Vancouver Island Miners’ Strike. MOTION REFERRED GS 2012-106 It was moved, seconded and carried that consideration of the request for Town of Ladysmith sponsorship of a documentary commemorating the 100th anniversary of the Vancouver Island Miners’ Strike be referred to the next meeting of the Government Services Committee, in order to receive input from the Heritage Revitalization Advisory Commission and the Ladysmith Historical Society.
