Trustee MARK ROBINSON February 8th, 2024 Nanaimo Chamber of Commerce Highlights: Dr. Deborah Saucier, President and Vice-Chancellor of Vancouver Island University spoke to attending members on how important VIU is for the entire island community. The majority (56%) of students end up following careers on the island. Post-secondary institutions across Canada are facing budgetary constraints and VIU is no exception. Programming cuts are expected which will decrease postsecondary educational choices and opportunities for our district’s graduating students who wish to remain at home while continuing on with their education.
