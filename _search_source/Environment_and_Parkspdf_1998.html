Meeting Time/Location: Thursday, September 25 2025, 4:20PM - 4:35PM PST Environment and Parks (Room 112), Parliament Buildings, Level 1 Community/Organization Information: Meeting ID: 1029 Community/Organization: Ladysmith (Town) Contact: Allison McCarrick, CAO Primary Phone: 250-245-6400 Primary Email: amccarrick@ladysmith.ca Secondary Email: jtierney@ladysmith.ca Cabinet Minister: Environment and Parks Community/Organization Attendees: Allison McCarrick, CAO;Deena Beeston, Mayor;Jake Belobaba, Director of Development Services;Tricia McKay, Councillor Topic #1: Contaminated Land/Water Lots on Crown Land Background: The Town applied to the Province for relief from investigation, delineation, & remediation beyond the Town’s lands on land/water lots owned by the Crown within Ladysmith Harbour. There is a complicated history and shared, area-wide contamination, generally related to poor quality fill across the area, irrespective of ownership. Contamination across property boundaries is related to the placement of contaminated fill rather than migration. The Town is in the process of remediating its own lands and applying for a Certificate of Compliance; contamination on Town-owned lands would be the Town’s responsibility, whereas contamination on Crown lots would be managed by the Province. Request: The Town requests assistance and relief from investigation, delineation, and remediation beyond the Towns Lands specifically DL17G, DL2016, DL651. The Town requests further assistance with an expedited Certificate of Compliance.
