CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Chair Neary distributed a News Release regarding the NDSS Redevelopment study. A report by an outside consultant recommends that Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools establish community-based task forces as it plans the redevelopment of the Nanaimo District Secondary School site. Cornerstone was hired by the district to study the NDSS site and make recommendations for the best use of the entire property, in keeping with the district’s goals. The report recommends that: • The district work with the City of Nanaimo and Vancouver Island University as it plans the redevelopment of the site. • A public information and engagement program be carried out as part of the planning process. • The district plan for a new 1,900-student school on the site. • The District Operations Centre be moved to a new site but the District Administration Centre does not necessarily have to be moved. • As it plans the redevelopment, the district work with the City of Nanaimo, with the district Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held June 25, 2014 advocating the redevelopment of Rotary Bowl on the site as a priority, in a form that meets wide community needs; while noting that this will have an impact on current uses.
