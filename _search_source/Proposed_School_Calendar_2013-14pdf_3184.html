DRAFT Local School Calendar for 2013-14 Please note that this calendar does not include non-instructional days and is subject to change. This calendar will be finalized by March 31, 2013. * Proposed staff inservice day – students do not attend Tuesday, September 3 Schools Open Wednesday, September 4 Thanksgiving Day Monday, October 14 Remembrance Day Monday, November 11 Last day of classes before Winter Vacation Friday, December 20 Schools Re-Open after Winter Vacation Monday, January 6 * Proposed staff inservice day – students do not attend Friday, January 31 BC Family Day Monday, February 10 Last Day of Classes before Spring Vacation Friday, March 14 Schools re-open after Spring Vacation Monday, March 24 * Proposed additional five days of Spring Vacation Monday, March 24 to Friday, March 28 Good Friday Friday, April 18 Easter Monday Monday, April 21 Victoria Day Monday, May 19 Administrative Day - Schools Close Friday, June 27 Notes: In addition, six non-instructional days will be scheduled during the school year. These are traditionally held in September, October, February (2 days), April, and May (secondary only) or the end of June (elementary only). * There is a proposal that school staffs meet as professional learning community groups during the first hour of each Wednesday and that students would come to school one hour later on that day. * Please note that the Board of Education will consider all of the proposals outlined above and will decide on the finalized calendar for 2013-14 by March 31, 2013. The Board could implement none or any one, two, or three of these proposals and still be in compliance with the minimum hours of instruction.
