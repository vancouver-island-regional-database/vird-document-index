Background: In June 2017, the Board approved the 2017/2018 Annual Budget and passed the associated bylaw. On January 17, 2018, Superintendent Blain provided an overview of the Amended Budget that showed increases in revenue and expenditures which, was received by the Business Committee. As presented on January 17th and tonight, February 14, 2018, the Amended Budget identifies increases in revenues in six areas: 1) Classroom Enhancement Funds resulting in revised approved MOE funding in respect of the Supreme Court Ruling and implementation of the 2002 contract Language. 2) Increased Enrollment 1 Page 8 of 220 8.1 3) Reduction of the Teacher Average Salary 4) Decrease in Benefits and Municipal Pensions Premiums 5) Operating Budget Adjustments 6) Aboriginal Education Rollover Overage Also identified in the presentations are corresponding additional budget expenditures for various items that were not identified in the preliminary Annual Budget, providing for a balanced budget. These include 1) internal balancing of accounts from the original budget and 2) original prioritized budget strategies discussed through the budget process 2017/2018. All of the known changes in revenues and expenditures are incorporated in the Amended Annual Budget, Amended Annual Budget Bylaw and clearly identified in the 2017/2018 Amended Annual Budget Report presentation which 6uyare attached to the agenda.
