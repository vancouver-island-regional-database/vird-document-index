Minutes Page 6 of 10 Agenda Item Number 6.2 2 Draft Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held September 21, 2011 UNFINISHED BUSINESS Catchment Changes - Timeline and Update Mr. Sabo updated the committee on the plans for a consultation process on proposed catchment area change at five schools. Mr. Sabo told the committee that the proposals are mostly housekeeping changes and would affect catchment areas for Coal Tyee, Pleasant Valley, Forest Park, Quarterway and Chase River. He said the district will carry out the consultation process in November, to provide input to the Board which will subsequently make decisions on any possible changes for September 2012. If the catchment areas were to be changed, students currently attending the affected schools would not be required to change schools.
