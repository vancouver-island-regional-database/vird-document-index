The amendment to the calendar included: adding one noninstructional day per year, for the purpose of Curriculum In-service. Rationale: The Ministry of Education has recently mandated a curriculum redesign and is also providing two days of curriculum in-service, during the 2015-16 school year, for teachers to prepare for the changes. The Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools (NLPS) local school calendar for 2015-16, therefore, requires an adjustment. As a result, the revised Local School Calendars for 2016-17 and for 2017-18 will also need to be amended to reflect the Ministry’s adjustment in the number of instructional hours (5 hours less for each school year), and its adjustment to the minimum hours of instruction. Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the current Local School Calendar and that it be amended by adding non-instructional days for the purpose of Curriculum In-Service. These non-instructional days (1 day per year on a date to be announced) are to be added to the Local School Calendar for the 2016-17 and 2017-18 school years.
