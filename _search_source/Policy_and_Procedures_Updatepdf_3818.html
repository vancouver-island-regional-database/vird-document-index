BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools INFORMATION SHEET DATE: November 26, 2014 TO: Board of Education FROM: Dave Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO SUBJECT: Update: Board Policy and Administrative Procedure Development Background: Following the Board of Education’s adoption of a strategic policy governance model in the fall of 2011, the District began the process of separating Board Policy from Administrative Procedures. During the 2011-2012 school year, Trustees codified and approved new Board policies that are currently available on the District website. Once the new Board policies were developed, it was necessary to convert the remaining former Board policies into Administrative Procedures. A number of the Administrative Procedures are also now available on the District website. Over the course of the 2012-2013 and 2013-2014 school years, a joint senior management/Trustee Board Policies and Administrative Procedures Steering Committee undertook the work of deciding which of the former Board Policies would be: a. deleted; b. converted to an Administrative Procedure without significant revision; c. converted to an Administrative Procedure with significant revision; or d. converted to a Board procedure. Trustees Welch, Allen and Neary are to be commended for their work on this extensive project. To date, and as is reflected in the attached list, the following have been undertaken: 1) All Board policies have been developed; 2) 105 former Board policies have been converted to Administrative Procedures 3) 38 former Board policies have been identified as requiring significant revision prior to conversion to Administrative Procedures; and 4) 12 former Board policies have been identified as requiring revision prior to conversion as Board procedures. With respect to next steps, District departments will undertake the revision of the remaining former policies that have yet to be converted to Administrative Procedures or Board Procedures.
