THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF SPECIAL BOARD MEETING HELD April 30, 2015 Trustees S. Rae, Chair S. Higginson, Vice-Chair N. Bob (arr. 4:14 pm) T. Brzovic S. Kimler B. Robinson N. Routley J. Solomon Absent J. Brennan Staff J. Blain, Superintendent/CEO G. Roberts, A/Secretary-Treasurer P. Trick, Associate Superintendent/HR P. Sabo, Director of Planning/Operations D. Burgos, Director of Communications K. Toye, Executive Assistant D. Sywak, Executive Assistant R. Gray, Assistant Superintendent S. Saywell, Assistant Superintendent Meeting No. 16 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 4:08 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio S15/04/30-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Local School Calendar IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Higginson Folio S15/04/30-02 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive and accept the local school calendar for the years 2015-16, 2016-17 and 2017-18 including all five staff recommendations with a letter of explanation from the Chair and Superintendent to be sent to our stakeholders within 30 days. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held April 30, 2015 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson Folio S15/04/30-02 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to create a feedback mechanism with our stakeholders to be implemented in January of the 2015-16 school year in order to be able to respond to concerns that may arise as a result of the changes in the school calendar and that this be referred to the Education Committee. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was no Unfinished Business this evening. QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions this evening. ADJOURNMENT Folio S15/04/30-04 Steve Rae, Chair That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Graham Roberts, A/Secretary-Treasurer
