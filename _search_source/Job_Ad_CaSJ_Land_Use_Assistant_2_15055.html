Stz’uminus Summer Job Opportunity Job Title: Pay: Land Use Assistant (1 position) $18.25 per hour Contract: Hours: Location: 8 Weeks Term, starting June 29, 2026 Mon to Thurs, 35 hours per week Ladysmith, BC About The Role The Land Use Assistant will provide support to the Land Use Department by assisting with research, mapping, and administrative tasks. This role is ideal for students interested in environmental studies, urban planning, or geography. Responsibilities • Assist with mapping and documentation of land use projects. • Assist conduct research on land use planning and environmental policies. • Support community engagement initiatives related to land use planning. • Help maintain department records and project files. Qualifications • Must be between 15 and 30 years of age at the beginning of the employment. • Must have a valid Social Insurance Number at the start of employment. • Enrolled in a program related to environmental studies, urban planning, or geography. • Familiarity with basic mapping tools (e.g., GIS) is an asset. • Strong analytical and organizational skills SFN is committed to respecting diversity within our workforce; preference will be given to individuals who identify as First Nations, Inuit, or Métis.
