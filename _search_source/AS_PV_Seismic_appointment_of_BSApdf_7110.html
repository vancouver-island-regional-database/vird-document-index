NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: April 22, 2020 Board of Education Pete Sabo, Executive Director of Planning and Operations Kelsey Bakewell, Facilities Planning Manager Rutherford Elementary School – Supplemental Project to Pleasant Valley Seismic Mitigation Project – Appointment of Architect Recommendation That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) directs staff to award the contract to Bradley Shuya Architect Inc., for the design and construction supervision for the Rutherford Elementary School renovations, supplementary to the Seismic Mitigation Project – Pleasant Valley Elementary School, subject to funding approval and the successful negotiation of a service agreement. Background Bradley Shuya Architect (BSA) were retained as the architect and led the team that developed a Project Definition Report (PDR) for the Ministry supported Seismic Mitigation Project. The PDR included a thorough site/building analysis along with estimated project cost. BSA specifically produced the architectural scope required for the Rutherford Elementary School swing space renovation, completed in January 2020. Discussion The scope of the Pleasant Valley Elementary School Seismic Mitigation Project will include the design and renovation of Rutherford Elementary School, to be managed as a supplemental project. Rutherford would then be used as a swing space for this and future Seismic Mitigation Projects. In consideration of District Administrative Procedure 513 (Purchasing) and AP501 (Selection of Architect), staff recommends a variance to the tendering requirements for the sole sourcing of a Consultant by engaging the services of BSA to provide a prime consulting role for the Rutherford Elementary School renovation. Given that BSA was the architect who prepared the initial PDR, the District will realize consistency of the design team and project efficiencies by continuing with this firm through the design and construction. BSA would assume the role of Prime Consultant and lead a team of qualified sub-consultants in preparing the construction drawings, issued for tender documents and construction supervision. The value of the contract with the consultant is a relatively minor portion of the overall value of the project. As noted, there will be a tender process for the construction phase of the project.
