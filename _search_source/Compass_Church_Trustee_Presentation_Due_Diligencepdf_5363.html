10 February 2016 Harewood Elementary School Refurbishment and Lease The following steps have been identified as prerequisite to signing a long term lease for the subject property and commencing with renovations to, and refurbishment of the buildings. Due Diligence  Inspect and quantify existing facilities  Investigate potential land lease with Provincial of B. C.  Priority list for damage mitigation, abatement, renovations, restoration  Preliminary budget  Verify zoning and permissible uses  Gather community input on best uses and services to provide  Gage community partner interest and support  Research Heritage Canada and provincial restoration funding  Confirm heritage grant eligibility, City of Nanaimo
