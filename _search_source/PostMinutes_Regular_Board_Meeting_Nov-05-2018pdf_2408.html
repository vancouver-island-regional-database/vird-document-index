THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF INAUGURAL BOARD MEETING HELD NOVEMBER 5, 2018 Trustees C. McKay, Chair L.M. Barron Vice-Chair T. Brzovic S. Higginson G. Keller C. O'Neill B. Robinson J. Stanley E. Wilkinson Staff S. Saywell, Superintendent/CEO C. McVeigh, Secretary-Treasurer T. Davie, Deputy Superintendent D. Burgos, Executive Director of Communications K. Matthews, Senior Executive Assistant Absent N/A Call to Order The Secretary-Treasurer called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Welcome - Jerry Brown, Snuneymuxw Elder Snuneymuxw Elder, Jerry Brown welcomed everyone by singing and drumming a traditional Snuneymuxw song. Oath of Office Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh swore the Oath of Office with all Trustees. Each Trustee signed an Oath of Office Declaration and Code of Conduct. Election of Board Chair Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh said she will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Wilkinson nominated Trustee McKay for Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held November 5, 2018 the position of Board Chair. Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh declared Trustee McKay Board Chair. Secretary-Treasurer vacates the Chair and the gavel is turned over to the Incoming Chair Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh vacated the Chair and passed the gavel to Chair McKay. Election of Vice-Chair Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee O'Neill nominated Trustee Barron for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Barron is the Vice-Chair of the Board. Election of Business Committee Chair Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Stanley nominated Trustee Brzovic for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Brzovic is the Chair of the Business Committee. Election of Business Committee Vice-Chair Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held November 5, 2018 Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee O'Neill nominated Trustee Keller for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Keller is the Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Election of Education Committee Chair Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Brzovic nominated Trustee Stanley for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Stanley is the Chair of the Education Committee. Election of Education Committee Vice-Chair Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Barron nominated Trustee Wilkinson for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Wilkinson is the Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Election of BCSTA Provincial Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held November 5, 2018 Councillor Representative Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Stanley for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Stanley is the BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Alternate Representative Chair McKay called for nominations for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Wilkinson nominated Trustee Barron for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Chair McKay called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair McKay declared that Trustee Barron is the Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Adjournment Folio A18/11/05-01 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Stanley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Higginson That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:37 pm. Carrie McVeigh, Secretary-Treasurer Charlene McKay, Board Chair Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held November 5, 2018
