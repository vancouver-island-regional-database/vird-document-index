Trustee Zone Report 26-Apr-2017 Dover Zone  Three Rutherford Grade 7 teams won 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place at the recent VIU “Spaghetti Bridge Building” Skills Canada event. The first place team went on to the provincials where they were the best Grade 7 team and came fourth in a field of Grade 8 to 10 students.  Check out our SD68 website for a video conceived and produced by MrGirr students.  I attended the last full-day Ab Ed SD68 staff Professional meeting organized by Ann Tenning. Bob Esliger and two EAs did presentations and we had short presentations reviewing the various Aboriginal languages on the island. Mandy Jones and Jerry Brown taught us some basic Hul’qumin’um greetings.  One of the presenters talked about an idea for a positive approach to students. When a student is caught doing something unacceptable, the suggested response from the adult is, “That was unexpected. In our school we ….” Focus on the positive. Trustee Robinson
