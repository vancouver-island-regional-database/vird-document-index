THE BOARD OF SCHOOL TRUSTEES OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF ANNUAL BOARD MEETING HELD DECEMBER 1, 2010 Trustees D. Allen, in the Chair N. Allen A. Bonkowski (via telephone) J. Brennan R. Dale C. McNamee D. Neary D. Murchie S. Welch Staff M. Munro, Superintendent/CEO P. Turin, Secretary-Treasurer F. Frisson, Assistant Superintendent C. Southwick, Assistant Superintendent D. Reimer, Director of Communications C. Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No. A10 Call to Order The Secretary-Treasurer called the meeting to order at 6:04 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Board Meeting Rules of Order Section I: Quorum Mr. Turin advised that as Trustee Bonkowski was attending the meeting via telephone that he had requested advice from BCSTA in the event that a secret ballot was required for tonight's meeting. BCSTA advised that a motion should be adopted by the Board to cover this situation. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Nelson Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Neary Folio A10/12/01-01 Election of Board Chair That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves that in accordance with Board Procedure No. 1030P Section 1 Quorum, paragraph 1.1, that Trustee Bonkowski be allowed to provide her personal vote this evening, where required by secret ballot, in confidence to the Executive Assistant, Ms. Cathy Kelt. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Turin called for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Donna Allen nominated Trustee Welch. 2 Draft Minutes of the Annual Board Meeting held December 1, 2010 Mr. Turin called for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board for a second and a third time. Hearing no further nominations, Mr. Turin asked Trustee Welch if she accepted the nomination. Trustee Welch acknowledged her acceptance of the nomination. Mr. Turin declared Trustee Welch Board Chair. Mr. Turin vacated the Chair and handed the gavel to Chair Welch. Election of Vice-Chair Chairperson Welch called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Donna McNamee. Allen nominated Trustee Trustee Brennan nominated Trustee Neary. Chair Welch called for nominations for a second and a third time. Hearing no further nominations, Chair Welch asked Trustees McNamee and Neary if they accepted the nomination. Both Trustees said they accepted the nomination. Ballots were distributed to Trustees. Chair Welch declared Trustee McNamee as Vice-Chair of the Board. Chair Welch asked for a motion to destroy the ballots. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Murchie IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan Folio A10/12/02-02 Election of Business Committee Chair That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) destroys the ballots for the election of the Vice-Chair. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Chair Welch called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. 3 Draft Minutes of the Annual Board Meeting held December 1, 2010 Trustee Dale nominated Trustee Brennan. Trustee Nelson Allen nominated Trustee Murchie. Chair Welch called for nominations a second and third time. Chair Welch asked Trustees Brennan and Murchie if they accepted the nomination. Trustee Brennan declined the nomination. Trustee Murchie accepted the nomination. Chair Welch declared Trustee Murchie Chair of the Business Committee. Election of Education Committee Chair Chair Welch called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Dale nominated Trustee Brennan. Chair Welch called for nominations a second and a third time. Hearing none, she asked Trustee Brennan if he would accept the nomination. Trustee Brennan said he accepted the nomination. Chair Welch declared Trustee Brennan Chair of the Education Committee. Election of Human Resources Committee Chair Chair Welch called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Human Resources Committee. Trustee Nelson Allen nominated Trustee Neary. Chair Welch called for nominations a second and third time. Hearing none, she asked Trustee Neary if she accepted the nomination. Trustee Neary said she accepted the nomination. Chair Welch declared Trustee Neary Chair of the Human Resources Committee. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Representative Chair Welch called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Nelson Bonkowski. Hearing none, she confirmed with Trustees Bonkowski and Neary that they accepted the nominations. Both Trustees said they accepted the nomination. Ballots were distributed. Chair Welch announced that Trustee Bonkowski was the BCSTA Provincial Councillor. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Nelson Allen Folio A10/12/01-04 Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Alternate Representative That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) destroys the ballots for the election of the BCSTA Councillor. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Chair Welch called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor (Alternate) Trustee Murchie nominated Trustee Donna Allen. Chair Welch called for nominations for a second and a third time. Hearing no further nominations, she asked Trustee Donna Allen if she would accept the nomination. Trustee Donna Allen said she would. Chair Welch declared Trustee Donna Allen as BCSTA Provincial Councillor (Alternate). Adjournment Folio A10/12/01-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Nelson Allen That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:18 pm.
