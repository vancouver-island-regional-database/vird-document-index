THE BOARD OF SCHOOL TRUSTEES OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF REGULAR BOARD MEETING HELD DECEMBER 5, 2011 Trustees J. Brennan B. Bard N. Curley K. Howland D. Neary B. Robinson T. Saunders S. Welch Staff D. Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO P. Turin, Secretary-Treasurer P. Sabo, Director of Planning/Operations D. Reimer, Director of Communications C. Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No.I8 Call to Order Secretary-Treasurer Phil Turin called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. He gave regrets for Snuneymuxw Elder - Geraldine Manson and the Pauline Haarer Choir who were not able to attend this evening. Election Results Election Results for November 19, 2011 Mr. Turin, the Chief Election Officer for the November 19 election, announced that Trusteeelect Donna Allen has written a letter indicating that she is “withdrawing as Trustee-elect.” He said if she does not take the oath of office by January 5, the position would be deemed to be vacant and a by-election would be held. Mr. Turin reviewed the election results with the new Board. Oath of Office Oath of Allegiance Mr. Turin stated that pursuant to Section 50 (1) of the School Act a person elected or appointed to office must make a prescribed Oath of Office within 45 days after the declaration of appointment. Mr. Turin asked trustees, individually, to swear the Oath of Office and the Oath of Allegiance. Not all Trustees chose to swear the Oath of Allegiance. Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 5, 2011 Election of Board Chair 2 Mr. Turin called for nominations for the position of Board Chair. Trustee Saunders nominated Trustee Brennan. Mr. Turin called for nominations a second time and a third time. Hearing none, Mr. Turin asked Trustee Brennan if he accepted the nomination. Trustee Brennan said he did. Mr. Turin declared Trustee Brennan Board Chair. Mr. Turin vacated the Chair and passed the gavel to Trustee Brennan as he took the Chair. Election of Vice-Chair Chair Brennan called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Board. Trustee Neary nominated Trustee Welch Chair Brennan called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair Brennan asked Trustee Welch if she would accept the nomination. Trustee Welch said she did. Chair Brennan declared Trustee Welch ViceChair of the Board. Procedure 1001(C)P Board/Standing Committee Board Procedure 1001(C)P Board Meetings and Standing Committee Meetings and Board Procedure 1030P Section IV - Inaugural Meetings - Rules of Order The Board considered a motion that would provide for two standing committees – Education and Business – for the next year. The two standing committees would include all nine trustees and partner group representatives. This temporary committee structure was recommended in the recent Governance Structures and Processes Report.
