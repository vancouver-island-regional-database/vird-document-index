School Trustees Association. Frank J. Ney Elementary Teacher - Terri Zolob & students Learning in Depth Project Frank J, Ney Grade 2 students and their teacher Terri Zolob gave a presentation about the “Learning in Depth” project they have been involved in since January. Each student is randomly assigned a topic to learn in depth and build personal portfolios on their topic. The topics students chose randomly included ships, space, fish, horses, bears and bridges. The project was designed from work by Kieran Egan about the way that learning in depth can transform schooling. Energy Conservation and Sustainability in our Schools. Jennifer Callaghan, BC Hydro and Chad Dalrymple, SD68 Energy Manager BC Hydro has, once again, been working with the outstanding secondary students of SD 68 in the area of energy conservation. BC Hydro has had the pleasure of connecting with students and teachers from four returning school teams: Dover Bay, Ladysmith, Nanaimo District and Woodlands. The students have all worked with Chad Dalrymple, SD68 Energy Manager and Jennifer Callaghan of BC Hydro. The students told us about the projects they have carried out this year as part of the BC Hydro Energy Ambassadors program. The students gather information about energy and resources used at their schools. The students said that they are seeing increased awareness but there are still areas where changes need to be made. NEW BUSINESS Disposal of Real Property Bylaw No. 2012-02 (Harewood Field) Mr. Turin outlined the bylaws that were required this evening. Four of the properties are part of the land exchange with the City of Nanaimo announced in December. The Harewood playing field; Northfield School; a portion of the Oliver Woods property; and Dickinson Road (Pioneer Park) are being transferred to the city, while the school district is obtaining properties that can be used for future planning. Other properties dealt with in the bylaws include three statutory rights of way and the sale of the other portion of the Oliver Woods property to a private party.
