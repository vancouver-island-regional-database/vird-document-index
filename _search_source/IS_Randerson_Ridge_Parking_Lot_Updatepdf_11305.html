NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: November 8, 2023 Business Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Pete Sabo, Executive Director of Planning and Operations Randerson Ridge Parking Lot Update Background: On September 28, 2022 at the Regular Board Meeting, The Parent Advisory Committee Traffic Team at Randerson Ridge presented their report to improve the drop-off and pick-up process at the school. At that time the Board recommended that the NLPS Staff review the Randerson Ridge Elementary School Safer Schools Drop off Zone submission and provide an action sheet to the Business Committee outlining the potential next steps. Discussion: A stakeholders meeting was held on October 24, 2022 at the school. In attendance were: • • • • • NLPS Facilities Staff Randerson Ridge Principal Assistant Superintendent Elementary Schools City of Nanaimo PAC Traffic Committee Team member During this meeting 2 different methods were concluded from the PAC Traffic Team and the AST Plan: 1. The AST plan promotes lessening the number of vehicles arriving to the school:  “Identifies and prioritizes engineering, encouragement, and enforcement actions aimed at creating a safer and more comfortable environment for students and their families to walk, bike, and roll to school.” 2. The PAC Traffic Team Plan attempts to:  “Identify problems associated with the drop off and pick up of students at school, and identify engineering, enforcement, education and encouragement solutions to these problems” This conflict of methods was discussed at the meeting and agreed that the principles of the AST should be the priority but items in the PAC proposal could be considered if it supports the AST plan for Randerson Ridge.
