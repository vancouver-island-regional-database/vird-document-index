Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No. S12 The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:01 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Stz'uminus people at Ladysmith Secondary School. Chair Neary said that tonight’s Special Board Meeting was to discuss the future of school facilities in Ladysmith and that the meeting was held at Ladysmith Secondary to allow Ladysmith residents to hear the Board decision-making process. Ecole Davis Road • Close Ecole Davis Road, June 2014 and consolidate the English and French programs as follows: the K-6 French Immersion Program with Ladysmith Intermediate; the EDR English program grades 4-6 students with Ladysmith Intermediate; and the K-3 EDR English program students with Ladysmith Primary.
