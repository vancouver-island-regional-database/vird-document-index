October 13, 2011 Ref: 148763 Sharon Welch, Chair Board of Education School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 395 Wakesiah Ave Nanaimo BC V9R 3K6 Dear Ms. Welch: Thank you for your letter dated September 21, 2011, requesting approval for the NanaimoLadysmith Board of Education to underspend its targeted Aboriginal education amount for the 2010/11 school year. Pursuant to section 106.4(2) of the School Act, approval is granted for the Board to underspend its 2010/11 school year Aboriginal Education target in an amount up to $78,984. This variation of direction is granted on the condition that these funds will be brought forward and added to the 2011/12 school year Aboriginal Education targeted amount to be expended on Aboriginal education programs. Yours truly, George Abbott Minister pc: Dave Hutchinson, Superintendent Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer Stella Bates, District Principal, Aboriginal Education Ministry of Education Office of the Minister Mailing Address: PO Box 9045 Stn Prov Govt Victoria BC V8W 9E2 Location: Parliament Buildings Victoria
