BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: RE: May 11, 2016 Business Committee John Blain, Superintendent/CEO Revised Transportation Policy Development Update BACKGROUND: Staff engaged the public in a conversation (April 12, Special Public Forum) to seek input about how district student transportation should be delivered. With the budget now complete and communities having a better understanding of the transportation issues which the district must address, it is recommended that we have a second community input session. The Ministry’s statutory requirement for school boards to provide student transportation service has been removed. Additionally, boards no longer receive dedicated funding to provide such service, and the service level to which the district will provide student transportation is at the discretion of local boards. There is, therefore, a need to develop a revised NLPS Board policy for student transportation that will provide guidelines for staff to develop a transportation plan to support students. The revised policy is proposed to take effect at the beginning of the 2017-18 school year. For policy development, we need to identify the critical components which should be addressed. For student transportation, this would include but may not be limited to; walk limits, safety concerns, alternate transportation options, student vulnerability, and fee for service. Further, these needs must be balanced with the overall objective of student learning, as any money allocated to student transportation takes resources away from supporting student learning in the classroom.
