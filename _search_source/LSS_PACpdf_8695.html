From: To: Subject: Date: Karen Matthews Karen Matthews FW: Stricter COVID Protocols Wednesday, March 3, 2021 10:05:02 AM From: President LSSPAC Sent: March 2, 2021 4:58 PM To: Charlene Mckay Subject: Stricter COVID Protocols CAUTION: External Message Dear Chair McKay, As a parent of a school district 68 student, I've been very pleased that students are in school full-time while also being very concerned about their safety. Especially as we've seen an increase in case numbers, as well as the concern over the new variants, I've been disappointed that face masks are not mandatory for everyone all the time. Everyone seems to put the responsibility on the Public Health Officer. I saw this article about the Qualicum District and realized there is more that can be done and that the SD68 School Board can do more. I wish I had known this in September. Please consider following their lead and implementing stricter COVID Guidelines. https://www.citynews1130.com/2021/02/26/vancouver-school-district-mask/ Thank you for taking my concerns into consideration. Please let me know if you have any questions.
