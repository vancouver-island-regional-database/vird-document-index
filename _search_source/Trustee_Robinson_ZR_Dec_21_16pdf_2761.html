Trustee Robinson Zone Report December 2016  Rotary North – Volunteers at Brechin Ski and Snowboard Swap and Pancake Breakfast  Rotary North – Volunteer servers at the Barsby Japanese Student Lunch, First Nations Feast Personal  Lined up 25 “Human Books” (business and community leaders) for the Third Annual Barsby Human Library Project.  Visited all of my schools and doing my best to get to all of the Christmas concerts.  Had a great time as the first guest reader of the year at the Seaview grade 2 and 3 students. I brought my favourite book, “Ordinary Mary’s Ordinary Day.”  Volunteered at the NLSF Christmas Craft Sale. This was the first year for this event. We had more than sixty crafters attend and sold eighty tables at $20 each. The final total raised has yet to be announced. There will be another one just before Mother’s Day.  I attended several Literacy Central Vancouver Island events as a new board member.
