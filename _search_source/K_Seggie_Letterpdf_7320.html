June 22, 2020 To the School Board of District 68 Nanaimo: I am writing with regards to the possible demolition of the South Wellington Elementary School building and disposition of the land. This building and land is invaluable to the community of South Wellington and surrounding communities as a recreational and community hub. School District 68 closed two schools in Cassidy, of which the buildings and the land were not procured for community use. This has resulted in zero public meeting space, zero playing fields and zero playgrounds in the area; a huge loss to an area with a large number of low income dwellings and young families starting out. Please work with SWACA, the RDN and the Cranberry Improvement District to ensure that this does not happen in South Wellington. Thank-you Krista Seggie Cassidy B.C.
