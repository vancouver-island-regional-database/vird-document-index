THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 SPECIAL BOARD MEETING TO BE HELD WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 5 , 2011 – 6:00 PM BOARD ROOM AGENDA 1. CALL TO ORDER The Chair will call the meeting to order and recognizes that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. 2. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA 3. NEW BUSINESS 3.1 Bargaining Model That the Board of Education, of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) consider recommending that the BCPSEA adopt the Hybrid Collective Bargaining Model for negotiations with teachers and further that the Board request the BCSTA Provincial Council Representative to have this issue placed on the Council agenda with the view of obtaining support for this model and further that the Board Chair be requested to call an open Board meeting on January 5, 2011 to make a decision on this issue. 4. UNFINISHED BUSINESS 4.1 Committee Assignments - Chamber of Commerce The Agenda Setting Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the assignments outlined below for the Chambers of Commerce be approved. Ladysmith - Trustee Dale and Trustee Neary Gabriola - Trustee McNamee Nanaimo - Trustee Welch 5. QUESTION PERIOD Questions from the audience must be submitted in writing and given to the Board’s Executive Assistant (Cathy Kelt) for submission to the Chair. The question period is intended to enable the public to obtain clarifying information regarding a current agenda item.
