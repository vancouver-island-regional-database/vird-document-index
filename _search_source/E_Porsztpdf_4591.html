KelseyToye Subject: '* FW:departure bay school closure From: Erin Porszt [m.ailLq;.%_zt;@_b91a1.a_iL..c_<1i_l Sent: Thursday, August 13, 2015 9:47 AM To: Kelsey Toye Cc: Erin Porszt; DPAC Subject: departure bay school closure Hello, I am writing to express my strong disapproval of the potential closure of Departure Bay elementary school. I live in the neighbourhood and have two preschool aged children who are slated to attend this school commencing in 2016 and 2018. I was very happy to have them attend such a great school, especially now that it is running its innovative environmentalprograms. Another bene?t of them attending this school was that its location would allow them to walk to and from school, which is so important for fostering good health and independence. Initially I had planned to send them to french immersionbut was planning to forego that program in order for them to attend Departure Bay elementaryschool. I know of numerous families in this area who are equally disappointed because they also had preschool-aged children slated to attend this school in upcoming years. How is the census data accounting for families like us who had planned to attend in upcoming years? I had a look at the facilities plan and the numbers do not seem to justify the closure of such a promising school. One of the reasons stated for consolidating schools was that enrolment is declining, however the plots and numbers shown in the report show that elementary school enrolment is increasing. And elementary enrolment is very close to reaching capacity which makes it counter-intuitive to start closing elementary schools. The numbers in the facility plan also show that Departure bay elementaryschool's capacity utilizationis expected to grow in future years, and the other North Zone-Departure Bay elementary schools are already at capacity and are expected to exceed that in future years. It doesn't make sense to force children to attend schools where there will not be room for them to properly learn. Rather than invest in renovating other North zone elementary schools that are already at capacity to take on more children,it would be much more worth while to invest in Departure Bay elementary school which is a Valuable asset to families in this neighbourhood. The closing of Departure Bay elementary school would be a tremendous loss to our communityand families like us with children that are already attending the school or plan to attend in upcoming years. The short-term bene?ts to closing the school do not out weigh the long—term losses to our community. Thank you, Erin Murrell
