TRANSFER OF ITEMS TO OPEN MEETING AGENDA To Superintendent's Report, 14.0 On January 24, 2017, staff received a letter from the Ministry of Education’s Capital Delivery Branch confirming their approval for the school district to access $1,326,852 in restricted capital funding for the purposes of the Frank J. Ney Elementary Expansion Project. Based on the previous Board motion and the recent receipt of the Ministry of Education letter, staff will proceed with the closure process of Rutherford Elementary and the building of an addition at Frank J Ney Elementary, to include: 1. A Letter to Rutherford parents and schools affected by the closure announcing the permanent closure of Rutherford Elementary at the end of June 2018; 2. News release; 3. Creation of a School Consolidation Working Group; and 4. Construction of an addition, as designed, at the Frank J. Ney school site.
