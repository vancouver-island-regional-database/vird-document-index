Attachment D VIA EMAIL March 3, 2025 Town of Ladysmith 410 Esplanade, PO Box 220 Ladysmith, BC V9G 1A2 Attn: Julia Tippett, Planner RE: FILE NO. 3090‐25‐01 –– WESTERN PARCEL – DEVELOPMENT VARIANCE PERMIT RATIONALE District Development Corp. is please to provide you with the application for a Development Variance Permit for the lands owned by Ladysmith (Hillside Development) Nominee Ltd. located in the Holland Creek Area, specifically the parcel referred to as the Western Parcel. The purpose of this application is to help facilitate the subdivision of the Holland Creek lands owned by Ladysmith (Hillside Development) Nominee Ltd from one lot into three separate lots. This Development Variance application is to vary the frontage requirements of the Western Parcel from the zoning bylaw requirements and to exempt the subdivision of this parcel from the immediate servicing requirements. Variance of Frontage Requirements Through the process of rezoning the subject lands a significant portion of the Western parcel is to be dedicated as park, as indicated on the survey plan completed by Williamson & Associates, which accompanies this application. This park dedication eliminated the southern crossing of Heart Creek originally contemplated as the primary access to this parcel. The proposed primary access for future development of the Western parcel is to be the existing easement through town lands at the north edge of the property. Once the corresponding subdivision application is complete this parcel will be accessed by easement only, therefore eliminating any public frontage for this parcel. Deferral of Servicing Requirements We kindly request the deferral of immediate servicing requirements to align with the triggers and timing outlined in Covenant CB1766286, see corresponding document. The breakdown and timing of the servicing requirements in relation to the Western parcel were heavily vetted between District Developments Corp and the Town of Ladysmith planning department through the rezoning process. It was agreed upon that the Western parcel will not be further subdivided (aside from the subdivision of the lands from the parent parcel as indicated in section 3.8 of the Covenant) or developed until the infrastructure requirements outlined in section 3.5 of CB1766286 are completed to the satisfaction of the Town of Ladysmith’s Director of Infrastructure Services.
