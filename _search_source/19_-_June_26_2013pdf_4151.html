CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY ANNOUNCEMENTS AND REMINDERS Friday, June 28, 2013 Wednesday, August 28, 2013 Tuesday, September 3, 2013 Wednesday, September 11, 2013 Wednesday, September 18, 2013 Wednesday, September 25, 2013 Graduation Day Closed Board Meeting Regular Board Meeting School Begins Education Committee Business Committee Closed Board Meeting Regular Board Meeting 4:30 pm 6:00 pm 6:00 pm 6:00 pm 4:30 pm 6:00 pm UNFINISHED BUSINESS Enhanced 10 Year Educational Facilities Plan Chair Brennan said that Trustees would make decisions this evening on a number of the proposed elements of the district’s 10-Year Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan proposals. He said the decisions come after a 60-day consultation process that involved thousands of parents, students and staff through an online process, meetings with PAC representatives and stakeholder groups, written submissions and four public forums. Mr. Hutchinson reminded trustees that enhanced facilities for learning is one of the district’s three strategic goals – and that goal is closely linked to the other two goals of meeting students’ unique needs and the continuous improvement of instruction and assessment.
