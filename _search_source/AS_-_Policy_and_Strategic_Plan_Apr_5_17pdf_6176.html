Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION EDUCATION COMMITTEE MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: April 5, 2017 Education Committee Dale Burgos, Director of Communications Board Policies and Strategic Plan Update Recommendation: That the Education Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the proposed changes to Board Governance policies 1.0 Foundation and Direction. Background: At meetings held on November 16, 2016 and January 23, 2017, the Board of Education and district staff discussed policies found in Board Governance 1.0 – Foundation and Direction. After discussion, it was determined that certain policies were out of date and in need of an update, as well, some policies did not belong in the district’s policy manual – but rather, in the district’s Strategic Plan. On February 22, 2017, a notice of motion was approved by the Board, at which time the community was able to provide feedback on the following proposed changes:        1.1 - Mandate o Now includes Board goals from policy 1.5 – Board Goals. 1.2 - Vision o No change – Remove from policy and include in Strategic Plan 1.3 - Mission o Updated – Remove from policy and include in Strategic Plan 1.4 - Values o Updated - Remove from policy and include in Strategic Plan 1.5 - Board Goals o Updated – Be renamed: 1.2 - Board Goals 1.6 - District Goals o No change - Remove from policy 1.7 - Continuous Improvement of Instruction and Assessment o No change – Be renamed to 1.3 - Continuous Improvement of Instruction and Assessment Next Steps: An updated Strategic Plan document will be created which will include District Goals that will be developed during the 2017-18 budget, 2020 Vision: A Three Year Plan, and Framework for Enhancing Student Learning processes and initiatives. 1
