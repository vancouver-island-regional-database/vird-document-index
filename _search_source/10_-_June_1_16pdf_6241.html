CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS Jill Bailey, Clarice Tyce and Emily Recalma Principal & Teachers, Woodlands RE: Woodlands Grade 8 Cross Curricular Aboriginal Inquiry Project on 'Cedar' Principal, Jill Bailey along with other Woodlands Secondary educators; presented the schools Grade 8 Aboriginal Inquiry Project. The project's focus was to enhance Aboriginal understandings in a meaningful way across the curriculum at the Grade 8 level. The full presentation is attached to the Agenda. CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM REGULAR BOARD MEETING L. Boon Chair, Greater Nanaimo Cycling Coalition RE: Traffic Education, Bike Skills and Active Transportation Trustee Brennan would like staff to collaborate with the Nanaimo Cycling Coalition and believes that this will be a positive driver in the Transportation Review. Staff is working on the Active Transportation plan and this summary will be used as part of that discussion moving forward. LEARNING SERVICES DEPARTMENT Laura Tait, Terri Zolob & Jo Cornthwaite Nacu Mat Tatulut Committee Learning Together The Committee, which is lead by Laura Tait Director of Instruction, and teachers Terry Zolob and Jo Cornthwaite provided information on the joint professional development committee's work from its inception to its current work with the district's curriculum implementation days. Their full presentation is attached to the Agenda.
