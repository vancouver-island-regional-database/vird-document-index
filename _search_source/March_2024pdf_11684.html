CHANGE IN ORDER There was no change in order. 1 5. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA E24/03/06-01 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Keller IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rokeby That the Agenda be approved. SENIOR STAFF REPORTS 8.1 Department of Learning Services Members of the Department of Learning Services presented on the Board's goal "To be a leader in Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability" and explained how students and staff can learn on, and from, the land in a safe and sustainable way. Staff from Dover Bay Secondary and syuwen'st Elementary provided a school perspective. An Information Sheet is attached to the agenda. 8.2 Laura Tait, Acting Superintendent Re: Administrative Procedure 249 - Animals in School Settings Laura Tait outlined changes made to Administrative Procedure 249 - Animals in Schools, that provide for expanded access to service dogs in our schools to support positive educational and mental health outcomes. An Information Sheet is attached to the Agenda. 2 9. CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM THE REGULAR BOARD MEETING There was none. 10. UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was none. 11. NEW BUSINESS 11.1 Chantelle Morvay, Education Committee Chair and Tania Brzovic, Education Committee Vice Chair Re: Notice of Motion - Policy Committee Work Plan Update Trustee Morvay turned the Chair over to Trustee Brzovic for the discussion and vote on this item. Trustee Morvay proposed a motion to update to the Policy Committee's current workplan. E24/03/06-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Morvay IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rokeby That the Education Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) update the Policy Committee’s current workplan to include a Justice, Equity, Diversity and Inclusion (JEDI) policy that will replace the current Inclusion Policy. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 12. QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions. 13. ADJOURNMENT The meeting adjourned at 5:24 pm. E24/03/06-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rokeby IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 3
