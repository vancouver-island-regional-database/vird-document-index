The nine individual awards are intended to honour the achievements of education professionals working in public, independent, First Nations and offshore schools. Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools is extremely proud to have three of its staff nominated for this prestigious award. Mandy Jones from Ladysmith Secondary and Emily Recalma from NDSS were nominated for the Indigenous Education Award. Tanya Adelborg from Randerson Ridge Elementary was nominated for the Extracurricular Leadership Award. Ms. Jones and Ms. Adelborg were the winners in their respective categories. Winners were awarded commemorative artwork, $3,000 personal bursary for professional learning and a $2,000 contribution to their school community for professional learning.
