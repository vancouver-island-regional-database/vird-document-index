Appendix D NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: June 22, 2022 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Revised Draft Policy 2.5 Board Committees Representation and Annual Workplan and Revised Draft Policy 2.6 Board Meeting Procedures Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive the revised Draft Policy 2.5 - Board Committees Representation and Annual Workplan and revised Draft Policy 2.6 – Board Meeting Procedures and circulate as a notice of motion for 30 days, utilizing the consultation process as per Board Governance, Section 1, 2.7 – Policy Development. Background: On June 8, 2022, the Business Committee passed the following motion: The Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) receive the revised Draft Policy 2.5 - Board Committees Representation and Annual Workplan and revised Draft Policy 2.6 – Board Meeting Procedures and circulate as a notice of motion for 30 days, utilizing the consultation process as per Board Governance, Section 1, 2.7 – Policy Development. This Action Sheet is intended to highlight a number of minor changes to the proposed policies being recommended by the Policy Committee as a result of feedback from trustees. Discussion: The attached draft policies include minor changes in red and a number of strikethroughs. The changes from the draft policies presented at the Business Committee are as follows: 1. 2. 3. 4. Clarity with respect to how individual trustees bring items forward to the agenda; Additional definitions also for clarity; The removal of a reference to electronic meetings that was inadvertently kept in the original drafts; The reduction of time that a member of the public is required to make a request to present to the Board; and 5. The clarification that additional question periods may be added at the will of the Board.
