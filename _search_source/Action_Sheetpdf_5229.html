BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools ACTION SHEET DATE: February 8, 2017 TO: Business Committee FROM: Carrie McVeigh, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Board Policies and Strategic Plan Update Background: At meetings held on November 16, 2016 and January 23, 2017, the Board of Education and district staff discussed policies found in Board Governance 1.0 – Foundation and Direction. After discussion, it was determined that certain policies were out of date and in need of an update, as well, some policies did not belong in the district’s policy manual – but rather, in the district’s Strategic Plan. The following changes have been proposed:        1.1 - Mandate o Now includes Board goals from policy 1.5 – Board Goals. 1.2 - Vision o No change – Remove from policy and include in Strategic Plan 1.3 - Mission o Updated – Remove from policy and include in Strategic Plan 1.4 - Values o Updated - Remove from policy and include in Strategic Plan 1.5 - Board Goals o Updated – Be renamed: 1.2 - Board Goals 1.6 - District Goals o No change - Remove from policy 1.7 - Continuous Improvement of Instruction and Assessment o No change – Be renamed to 1.3 - Continuous Improvement of Instruction and Assessment Recommendation: That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to enter into a 30-day consultation, during which time the public may provide feedback on the proposed changes to Board Governance policies 1.0 - Foundation and Direction. 1
