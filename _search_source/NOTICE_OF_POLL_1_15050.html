STZ’UMINUS FIRST NATION Polling Notice for The Election of Chief and Councillors Notice is hereby given to the electors of the Stz’uminus First Nation that a poll will be held to elect one (1) chief and nine (9) councillors on the 2nd day of April 2026 from nine o’clock (9:00) a.m. until eight o’clock (8:00) p.m. at the following location: Stz’uminus First Nation Community Centre 3949 Shell Beach Road, Ladysmith, B.C Please note that in lieu of voting by mail-in ballot, every elector who does not reside on reserve, and to whom a mail-in ballot was mailed or provided, may obtain a ballot and vote in person at the above-noted location on the day of the election if the elector returns the mail-in ballot to the electoral officer or to the deputy electoral officer or, where the elector has lost the mail-in ballot, the elector provides the electoral officer or the deputy electoral officer with a written affirmation that the elector has lost the mail-in ballot, signed by the elector in the presence of the electoral officer, the deputy electoral officer, a justice of the peace, a notary public, or a commissioner of oaths. The ballots will be counted immediately after the close of the poll on the 2nd day of April 2026 at the Stz’uminus First Nation Community Centre,3949 Shell Beach Road, Ladysmith, B.C.and the results will be declared immediately following the count. A COPY OF THE VOTERS LIST IS POSTED AT THE BAND OFFICE AND ANY VOTER MAY ASK FOR A PERSONAL COPY FROM THE BAND OFFICE. Given under my hand at Stz’uminus, BC this 3rd day of March 2026.
