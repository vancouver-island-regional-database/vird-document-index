CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS SOGI/Race Relations Budget(s) Mr. Turin advised the Committee that currently there is a budget of $2600 for the Multicultural & Race Relations Committee and the SOGI Committee. Mr. Turin said both Committee Chairs had approached him about their committee budgets. He said this should be a Board decision and that he could not allocate funds on his own to a Board committee. Trustee Bard reviewed what the SOGI committee had done with its budget this past year. He said the Committee wants to make approximately 300 posters and distribute them amongst the schools. He said that he believes the Committee addresses a number of barriers in the district. He is asking the Business Committee to recommend to the Board that it increase the budgets for the SOGI and Race Relations Committee to $7500 per committee. Trustee Allen provided a brief history of the Race Relations Committee since 2005. Trustee Robinson said he would have a report on the Multicultural Race Relations Committee's progress for the June Board meeting.
