Matthews , DASG Rep CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. ADDITIONS TO THE AGENDA There were no Additions to the Agenda this evening. DELETIONS TO THE AGENDA There were no Deletions to the Agenda. CHANGE IN ORDER There was no Change in Order this evening. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio BC 16/12/14-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held December 14, 2016 APPROVAL OF THE MINUTES Minutes - November 9, 2016 Folio BC 16/12/14-02 That the minutes of the Business Committee meeting held November 9, 2016 to be approved. Opposed by Trustee Brzovic Trustee Higginson NOT CARRIED The Business Committee did not approve the minutes and asked for them to be brought to the next meeting with required changes. UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was no Unfinished Business this evening. NEW BUSINESS John Blain Superintendent RE: Draft Transportation Admin Procedure Folio BC 16/12/14-03 Mr. Esliger, Assistant Superintendent, presented to the Business Committee the Draft Transportation Admin Procedure. This is attached to the Agenda. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) to refer the Draft Transportation Admin Procedure to January's Business Committee meeting and staff to present a draft outline of safety procedures. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY FOR INFORMATION John Blain Superintendent RE: 2020 Vision - Spring Calendar Folio BC 16/12/14-04 Mr. Blain presented to the Business Committee the 2020 Vision calendar. This is attached to the Agenda. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the committee receive Draft Vision 2020 meeting schedule as presented by staff. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held December 14, 2016 John Blain Superintendent RE: AP 249 - Animals in School Settings Mr. Blain presented the Administration Procedure. Would the Board consider having staff email the draft procedure directly to all parents who currently have students registered as bus riders? - Charlene McKay Response: Chair Kimler responded "Yes, the Board would consider that option and appreciates the question, as continuous improvement in communication is what we strive for as a school board." Superintendent Blain responded that staff could do that. ADJOURNMENT Folio BC 16/12/14-05 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 7:35 pm.
