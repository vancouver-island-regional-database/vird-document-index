Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held October 28, 2015 SUPERINTENDENT'S REPORT Cedar Update Superintendent Blain provided the Board with an update on the secondary school choice for Cedar and Cinnabar Valley area students in Grades 812. Students were given the choice to attend Cedar Secondary or stay at their current school. Approximately 400 students residing in the Cedar and Cinnabar Valley area responded, with 202 confirming that they will be attending the Cedar high school. These numbers are preliminary and Mr. Blain indicated that they could change as the district creates the learning plan for the school. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Capital Plan - Mr. Sabo Director of Planning and Operations, Pete Sabo provided the Board with the proposed 2015-16 Five-Year Capital Plan. Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held October 28, 2015 Each year, Boards of Education across the Province are required to submit a five-year capital plan detailing the high priority capital projects needed for their school districts. Once the Ministry of Education receives submissions, it prioritizes the requests. The main elements of the Five-Year Capital Plan include; provision of new space, rejuvenation or replacement of schools, seismic mitigation, mechanical or energy system upgrades, building envelope remediation, routine capital and school bus replacements.
