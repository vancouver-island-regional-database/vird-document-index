NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATION COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: April 3, 2024 TO: Education Committee FROM: Laura Tait, Acting Superintendent SUBJECT: How Are We Doing Report 2022/2023 Background The “Aboriginal How Are We Doing?” Report is an annual, public-facing report focusing on Indigenous students in BC. The data in this report provides administrators, teachers, schools, school districts, and the Ministry of Education and Child Care with important information on Indigenous student outcomes and identifies areas for intervention or further action. This information should inform areas of action identified within the District's Strategic Plan and our annual Enhancing Student Learning Report. Overview The full How Are We Doing Report was provided to Trustees in advance and is also attached for your reference. For the Education Committee presentation, an overview of the report will be provided, with an emphasis on specific aspects of the report that are most closely connected to the board goals and strategic plan. The presentation will follow a “Here’s what, so what, now what” format, with insights provided on the report data, interpretation of what the data is telling us about our teaching and learning experiences, links to positive supports that are in place, as well as gaps in services and strategies to address areas in need of improvement. Specific data from the report that will be highlighted during the presentation include Foundation Skills Assessment, Secondary Course completion, Transition Rates, Adult Dogwood Completion Rates, OnReserve Graduation Rates, Post-Secondary Transition Rates, and Student Learning Survey results.
