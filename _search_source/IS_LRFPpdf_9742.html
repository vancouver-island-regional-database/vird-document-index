NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: May 25, 2022 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Long Range Facilities Update Background This Information Sheet contains a report outlining the progress on the Board’s Long Range Facilities Plan (“LRFP”), including some considerations for updating. Discussion As part of the commitment to tracking progress and ensuring the LRFP is meeting the current needs of the District, a summative report “Monitoring our Progress” contains the various recommendations in the LRFP and the current status. In addition to the progress being made, there are a number of recommendations for the Board to consider amending. We note that this item will come back to a future Business Committee for further discussion. Overall, the LRFP has consistently progressed in all focus areas. From capacity to partnerships, from policy to school site acquisition there has been demonstrable progress and the LRFP can be viewed as a successful guidance document.
