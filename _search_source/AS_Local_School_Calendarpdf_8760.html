NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATION COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: March 3, 2021 Education Committee Deputy Superintendent, Tim Davie Local School Calendars 2021-2022 and 2022-2023 Recommendation The Education Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the attached two-year draft Local School Calendars for 2021/22 and 2022/23. Background On February 3, 2021, the Education Committee passed the following motion: “That the Education Committee recommend that staff make the attached two-year draft Local School Calendar for 2021/22 and 2022/23 available to the public immediately and to report back at the March Education Committee meeting with recommendations”. The information was shared via email to staff, information to parents via their school, social media, and mobile app. The district received approximately 44 emails from the community. Feedback primarily focused on the following topics: • • • • The timing of Professional Development days with statutory holidays December and Spring Break timing Length of Spring Break Quarter System Appendix A: Draft Local 2021/22 and 2022/23 School Calendars Appendix B: Community Feedback
