Ronda Bratt 2300 Dodds Rd Nanaimo, British Columbia June 24, 2016 Nanaimo Ladysmith Board of Education School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 395 Wakesiah Ave Nanaimo, British Columbia Dear Jamie Brennan, Natasha Bob, Tania Brzovic, Stephanie Higginson, Scott Kilmer, Steve Rae, Bill Robinson, Noah Routley, and Jeff Solomon: As a parent in the Chase River Elementary school community I request that you reconsider your board’s decision to end the complimentary bussing of our students from South Wellington. I fully understand that operating a school district is an incredibly complex undertaking with a multitude of competing interests and that budgetary concerns must always be kept in mind. That said, a school is notjust a building or a budget; a school is the group of people who gather at said building and manage under the budget. Our school community went through fairly major growing pains when the students from South Wellington joined us three years ago. The new children and their families seemed shell shocked from the closure of their beloved school and our little school grew a great deal in a short amount of time. I am very proud of how well we have come together in this short time. It is disheartening that a large number of our children will be unable to remain in our community if their bus from South Wellington is revoked. The South Wellington children and their families will, again, have to deal with changing schools and building new relationships and the Chase River children and families left behind willsuffer their loss. The fairly low cost to Chase River until they have all moved on to high it would take to bus the “SouthWel|ies” school is not too much to ask when it means that our hard won community will remain intact. Thank you for your consideration. Sincerely, Ronda Bratt
