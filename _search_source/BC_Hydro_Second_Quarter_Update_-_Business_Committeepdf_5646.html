CONTINUOUS OPTIMIZATION 3. Continuous Optimization Program BC Hydro sponsored program that will save us money by: • Reducing the energy use at a facility through the use of real time metering • This is achieved by monitoring, evaluating and reacting to real time data to fine tune building systems and operations. CONTINUOUS OPTIMIZATION Significance of Real Time Monitoring • Utility meter is the “window” into the building’s energy use • Regular meters are read only once a month • Problems will only become known after 1 – 2 months • Web based Real Time Meters give more and timely data • Issues can be identified and resolved sooner ….saving a month’s worth of costs and assist in identifying additional cost saving projects within the facility. CONTINUOUS OPTIMIZATION BC Hydro continuous optimization program BC Hydro’s Commits to: 1. The cost of meter installation and software license 2. The cost of a consultant to: • Collect baseline data for a 9 month period • Review data and recommend upgrades • Coaching for SD68 staff on building operation The School District commits to: 1. The cost of data and power connection for meters 2. The cost of Implement recommended upgrades • only upgrades with 2 year (or less) payback are considered • Upgrades are limited to a Financial cap.
