Wellington Secondary Seismic Project March 2013 Status Update Pete Sabo Director of Planning and Operations  The Capital Project Agreement was signed February 1, 2013  The Board has passed the By-Law  The COA from the Ministry is next  Design meetings with consultants and the school are underway.
