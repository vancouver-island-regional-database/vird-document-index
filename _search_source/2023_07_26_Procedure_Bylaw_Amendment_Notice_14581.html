NOTICE PROCEDURE BYLAW AMENDMENTS Amendments to “Council Procedure Bylaw 2009, No. 1666” In accordance with Section 124 of the Community Charter, notice is given that the Council of the Town of Ladysmith intends to consider amendments to “Council Procedure Bylaw 2009, No. 1666” at its Regular meeting on September 5, 2023. In general terms, the amendments include the following: 1. 2. 3. Adding Procedures for Question Period during Regular Council meetings. Updating a reference to the Royal Family in which disrespectful speech by members of Council is prohibited. Deleting a sub-section in which participation by non-members at Standing Committees is facilitated. A copy of “Council Procedure Bylaw 2009, No. 1666, Amendment Bylaw 2023, No. 2153” is available for viewing on the Town’s website at https://www.ladysmith.ca/city-hall/news-media/publicnotices or in person Monday through Friday, except Statutory Holidays, between the hours of 8:30am and 4:00pm at Town of Ladysmith City Hall, 410 Esplanade, Ladysmith, BC V9G 1A2 (250.245.6400).
