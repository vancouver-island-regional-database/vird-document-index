CAUTION: External Message ﻿ ﻿Dear SD69 School Board, and whom ever else it may concern (BC, Canada, the world!), As a parent I have been patient, kind, and tolerant of the overbearing and harmful restrictions enforced on our children for over two years. I have also been grateful for my child’s school’s support throughout this time. Now — instead of referring to government ‘public health’ mantras — I would like school boards to begin legitimately and independently investigating the psychological and physical harm caused by school masking, closures, and other ineffective covid policies. I present you with the following carefully gathered evidence (with links to relevant original academic/scientific citations) and request that you present substantial counter evidence before forcing my child back into a mask — as seems inevitable in under the encroaching totalitarian rules of current Canadian governance and the endless waves of this virus: https://www.drpaulalexander.com/blogs/news/masking-a-careful-review-of-the-evidence For two years, I’ve been told to ‘follow the science’. I now ask: What science? Science is about asking questions, and it’s high-time adults stood up for the well-being of our children instead of dooming them to a future of quasi-liberalism and increasing fear of human connection. Many children have already had covid: here’s extensive information on the benefits of natural immunity that’s suddenly controversial to discuss (I’m using Dr Alexander because he has compiled the studies to make the scientific information more digestible— but I invite you to actually look into the sources yourselves instead of consuming bought one-sided and dumbed down media): https://brownstone.org/articles/79-research-studies-affirm-naturally-acquired-immunity-tocovid-19-documented-linked-and-quoted/ This doctor has been hard at work, along with many others now facing overt censorship and risking their careers for the health and well-being of our children: https://brownstone.org/articles/morethan-400-studies-on-the-failure-of-compulsory-covid-interventions/ Please, do the right thing, and begin conducting your own independent research (I.e. follow the links provided and read the original studies for yourselves to make up your own minds). Do not defer to ‘public health guidance’ which is quite clearly corrupted and doubling down in the face of substantial, and evidence backed, conflicting medical opinions. I suggest you begin this research now, as come Fall we will likely be forced back into mindless compliance — with our children suffering the most. Please, do the research and look out for our children: Early treatment works; The (originally undisclosed) risks of adverse effect after vaccination for children far outweigh the risks of the disease; And masks are ineffective and damaging to the socio-emotional development of our children. I’m sorry if I seem curt in this correspondence, but my patience is waning and our children are suffering. I know you have our children’s (and community’s) best interests at heart; please put bias aside and do your due diligence on this matter. Yours sincerely and with greatest concern, Bryony Dixon Nanaimo resident and Mother of a child at Seaview Elementary.
