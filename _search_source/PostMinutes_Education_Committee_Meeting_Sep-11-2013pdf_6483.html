CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Education Committee Meeting held September 11, 2013 PRESENTATIONS There were no presentations this evening. EDUCATIONAL UPDATE – Report from John Blain, Deputy Superintendent Superintendent John Blain gave an update on several items related to the startup of the school year. He said there were a number of learning opportunities for principals, vice-principals and teachers at the start of the year. These included the Summer Institute the week before school opening and several sessions for principals and vice-principals in the two weeks before school opening. Mr. Blain reviewed the preliminary feedback that has been received about the Tuesday, September 3 Success for All Day. Mr. Blain told the Committee about the work that has been done to help South Wellington students transition to their new schools. The majority of South Wellington parents chose to enrol their children in Chase River Elementary, with some attending North Cedar Intermediate. He also commented on the transition work that is underway to support Quarterway English students who have moved to neighbouring schools. Rose Tull & Lynn Brown Rose Tull & Lynn Brown gave the Committee an overview of some of the topics covered in the Summer Institute held August 27 and 28. They focused on building a community of learners.
