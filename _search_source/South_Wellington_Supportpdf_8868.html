Parent Advisory Council Cedar Community Secondary School 1640 MacMillan Rd, Nanaimo BC V9X 1L9 September 23, 2021 Board of Education of SD68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 395 Wakesiah Ave Nanaimo BC V9R 3K6 To The Board of Education of SD 68: This letter is written in support of the “South Wellington Project”. The Advisory Council of Cedar Community Secondary School represents the community of South Wellington as part of our student catchment area. The Advisory Council supports the decision to lease the land and building to the Regional District of Nanaimo, giving an opportunity for it to be utilised by the community. We also support the South Wellington and Area Residents Association in their efforts on behalf of our residents. We see this development benefiting our students in the future, as very little is available at this time. Thank-you, Parent Advisory Council Cedar Community Secondary School
