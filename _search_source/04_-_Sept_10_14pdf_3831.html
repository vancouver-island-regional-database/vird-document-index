Vanderhorst, MNP Meeting No. S4 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 5:07 and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio S14/09/10-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Secretary-Treasurer's Financial Documents Mr. Turin said that the school district ended the year with a larger operating surplus than had been projected, due in large part to the strike action in May and June. The surplus in the $120.2 million operating budget was $7.8 million, with $3.7 million being restricted for specific purposes in the 2014-15 school year. Mr. Turin advised the Board that it should be prudent with the remaining $4.2 million unrestricted surplus, noting that there are still many unknowns about the district’s future financial situation, in particular what costs might arise from the resolution of the current strike action and future enrolments.
