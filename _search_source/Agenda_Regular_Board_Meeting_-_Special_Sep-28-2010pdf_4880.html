THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 SPECIAL BOARD MEETING TO BE HELD TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 28 , 2010 – 6:00 PM NDSS - BIG GYM AGENDA Page 1. CALL TO ORDER The Chair will call the meeting to order and recognizes that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. 2. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA 3. UNFINISHED BUSINESS 3.1 District Wide Facilities Plan That the Facilities Planning Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the District Facilities Plan be approved in principle and distributed to the public for feedback prior to final approval on October 19, 2010. 3.2 Communications and Feedback Plan The Facilities Planning Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that public feedback on the District Facilities Plan be sought during the period from September 28, 2010 until October 14, 2010 through the following communication and feedback plan. Wednesday, September 29 • SD68 News and news release outlining details of the Facilities Plan, how to get more information and how to provide feedback • Information about the proposed plan and how to provide feedback posted on SD68 website Thursday, September 30 • Information sheet for parents sent home with students How to provide feedback: • E-mail to facilitiesplan@sd68.bc.ca • Write to Board via fax or mail • Presentations at public meeting Tuesday, October 12 • Deadline for feedback October 14 at 4 p.m. Page 1 of 3 Procedure No. 1001 E(P) - Closed Meetings Subject matter is restricted to salary negotiations; personnel matters- aqquisition, sale, lease, exchange, legal matters, confidential communication Policy No. 1070 - News Media Communications The Chairperson, or designate, shall be the official spokesperson to the news media for the Board. Page 3.3 Delegation to Meet the Minister That the Facilities Planning Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the Board Chair be requested to arrange for a delegation to meet with the Minister of Education to present the District Facilities Plan and ask for support for the replacement of Woodlands Secondary and the seismic upgrading of Wellington Secondary. 3.4 Board Motion R10/04/28-34 That the Facilities Planning Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that Board motion R10/04/28-34, be referred to the Facilities Planning Committee for future consideration relating to issues around the implementation of the District Facilities Plan. R10/04/28-34That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) directs staff to develop a planning process for the relocation of the Secondary Learning Alternative programs to the Dufferin site, and the relocation of District Resource Centre/Learning Resource Centre services to the NDSS site, for September 2011, with a consultation process to take place in the fall of 2010. 3.5 Bylaw That the Facilities Planning Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the Secretary-Treasurer be requested to work with the Policy Committee to draft a bylaw related to the District Facilities Plan that will outline the steps required to adopt policy changes relating to modifications to district programs and facilities that will necessitate changes to the District Facilities Plan and to bring the bylaw to the October 19, 2010 Special Board Meeting. 3.6 French Immersion Programs in the District That the Facilities Planning Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that staff be requested to develop terms of reference, for approval by the Board of Education, for a review of French Immersion Programs in the District, and that a consultant be engaged to complete the review and report to the Board of Education as soon as possible. 4.
