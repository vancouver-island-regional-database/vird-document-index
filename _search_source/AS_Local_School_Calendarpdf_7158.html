NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: March 4, 2020 Business Committee Superintendent/Acting Secretary-Treasurer Scott Saywell Local School Calendar 2020-2021 Recommendation That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the attached draft 2020/21 Local School Calendar, in principle, while the district continues to receive feedback, and that finalized document be brought forward to the regular March Board meeting. This will maintain the Ministry timeline for calendars to be submitted by March 31, 2020. Background On the February 13, 2020, the Business Committee passed the following motion: “That the Business Committee recommend that staff make the 2020/21 local school calendar available to the public immediately and report back to the March Business Committee meeting with recommendations.” The information was shared via email and district news to staff, School Messenger email directly to families and social media. It was also shared with the Leaders for Learning group. Feedback was requested through email until March 11, 2020. The district received approximately 41 emails from the community (as of February 27, 2020). Feedback primarily focused on the following topics:        Two week spring break Spring break coinciding with Easter holidays The timing of Professional Development days with statutory holidays Instructional days Educational hours December break timing Short lunch time on Monday and Friday Any feedback received after February 27, 2020, will be shared at the March 11, 2020, Regular Board Meeting.
