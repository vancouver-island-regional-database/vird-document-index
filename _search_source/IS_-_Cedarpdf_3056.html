Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION INFORMATION SHEET DATE: October 28, 2015 TO: Board of Education FROM: John Blain, Superintendent/CEO SUBJECT: Cedar - Secondary School Choice Background: On September 18, 2015, the Department of Learning Services sent a letter home to Cedar and Cinnabar Valley area students who will be in Grades 8-12 in September 2016. The letter surveyed students on which secondary school they wish to attend once Cedar Secondary School re-opens in September 2016. The designated secondary school catchment for Cedar and Cinnabar Valley area students is Cedar Secondary School. Alternatively, students can continue to attend John Barsby Community School, Ladysmith Secondary School or any other school in the district through the out-of-catchment application process. Process: Ladysmith Secondary School As reported by school administration: Communication with families via numerous avenues (i.e. email, phone) and also surveying students from class to class. School staff analyzed class lists, checked and cross-referenced addresses for all students and a final list of students living in the Cedar area was created. While contacting parents/guardians, the school received verbal or written response on their secondary school choice for the 2016-17 school year. John Barsby Community School As reported by school administration: The school arranged for every Cedar and Cinnabar Valley area student to pick up forms from the school office. Students that did not return their form was called by school office staff.
