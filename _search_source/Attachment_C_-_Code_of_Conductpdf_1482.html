Attachment C CODE OF CONDUCT 1. I am responsible for and will control my behaviour. 2. I will interact with others in a safe, courteous, and respectful manner. 3. I will treat all facilities and equipment with respect. Failure to comply at all times with code of conduct may result in limits on use of facilities and equipment and/or immediate suspension from facilities. Parks, Recreation & Culture
