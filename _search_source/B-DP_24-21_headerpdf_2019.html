ATTACHMENT B TOWN OF LADYSMITH DEVELOPMENT PERMIT (Section 489 Local Government Act) FILE NO: 3060-24-21 DATE: October 7, 2025 Name of Owner(s) of Land (Permittee) Applicant: District Group Subject Property (Civic Address): Lot A (Eastern Parcel) Holland Creek 1. This Development Permit is subject to compliance with all of the bylaws of the Town of Ladysmith applicable thereto, except as specifically varied by this Permit. 2. This Permit applies to and only to those lands within the Town of Ladysmith described below, and all buildings structures and other development thereon: LOT A BLOCK 192 DISTRICT LOT 103 OYSTER DISTRICT PLAN EPP63594 EXCEPT PLANS EPP67741, EPP75579, EPP80490, EPP98460, EPP98461 AND EPP117682 PID: 030-139-520 (referred to as the “Land”) 3. This Permit has the effect of authorizing the issuance of a building permit for the construction of a building or structure on the Land in accordance with the plans and specifications attached to this Permit, subject to the conditions, requirements and standards imposed and agreed to in this Permit. 4. With respect to the Land, Town of Ladysmith Zoning Bylaw 2014, No. 1860 is hereby varied, in accordance with section 490(1) of the Local Government Act, as follows: (a) Section 8.3.1(a) is varied, subject the to the conditions of this permit, to: i. reduce the required number of long-term bicycle parking spaces from 84 to 42; and Page 1 of 4 ATTACHMENT B ii. reduce the required number of oversized bicycle parking spaces from 8 to 0; (b) Subsection 10.8.5(d) is varied to reduced the required front yard setback from the future Colonia Drive dedication from 6 meters to 4 meters, as shown in Schedule A. (c) Subsection 5.19(c) is varied to allow a 16m section of retaining wall to be 3.95 meters, as shown in Schedule G, provided the retaining wall is designed by and constructed under the supervision of a professional engineer 5. This Permit does not have the effect of varying the use or density of the Land specified in Zoning Bylaw 2014, No. 1860. 6. The Permittee, as a condition of the issuance of this Permit, agrees to the following conditions: (a) The Permittee shall develop the Lands in accordance with the following: i. Schedule A – Architectural Plans ii. Schedule B – Lighting Plan iii. Schedule C – Stormwater Management Plan iv. Schedule E – Landscaping Plan v. Schedule F – Waste Management Plan vi. Schedule G – Grading Plan (b) High-efficiency, automatic, and water-saving (drip) irrigation systems shall be provided for any on-site landscaping that requires irrigation (c) at least: i. 50% of long-term bicycle parking spaces; and ii. 20% of short-term bicycle parking spaces, shall be provided with a 110-volt electrical outlet within 1.5 meters. (d) Where requested by the tenant, bike storage lockers shall be provided for dwelling units. (e) All buildings shall comply with the height requirements specified in the zoning bylaw. 7. This Permit is issued on the condition that the Permittee will provide to the Town of Ladysmith security in accordance with section 502 of the Local Government Act in the amount of $725,624. Page 2 of 4 ATTACHMENT B 8. The security under section 7 is payable: (a) upon issuance of a building permit; or (b) prior to any alteration of the land, whichever occurs first. 9. If the Town considers that: (a) a condition of this permit respecting landscaping has not been satisfied; (b) an unsafe condition has resulted from a contravention of a condition of this permit; or (c) damage to the natural environment has resulted from a contravention of a condition of this permit, the Town may undertake and complete the works required to satisfy the landscaping condition, correct the unsafe condition or correct damage to the environment, at the cost of the Permittee, and may apply the security in payment of the cost of the work, with any excess to be returned to the Permittee. 10. Requests for the release of the security shall only be made following: (a) Issuance of an occupancy permit; and (b) Receipt of a report from a landscape architect confirming: i. the landscaping conditions of this permit have been met; and ii. all plantings have survived for one year from date of planting. 11. Should there be no default as herein provided, or where this Permit lapses, the Town of Ladysmith shall return any security provided to the Permittee. 12. Pursuant to section 504(1) of the Local Government Act, if the Permittee does not substantially start any construction permitted by this Permit within two years of the date of this Permit as established by the authorizing resolution date, this Permit shall lapse. 13. For the purposes of section 12, construction shall be deemed to be substantially started where: (a) a valid building permit has been issued for the development; (b) a foundation for at least one principal building has been constructed; and Page 3 of 4 ATTACHMENT B (c) all works that have occurred on site are in compliance with this permit and the bylaws of the Town. 14. The plans and specifications attached to this Permit are an integral part of this Permit. 15. Pursuant to section 501(2) of the Local Government Act the Land described herein shall be developed strictly in accordance with terms, conditions and provisions of this Permit and any plans and specifications attached to this Permit which shall form a part thereof. 16. Notice of this Permit shall be filed in the Land Title Office at Victoria under section 503 of the Local Government Act, and upon such filing, the terms of this Permit or any amendment hereto shall be binding upon all persons who acquire an interest in the land affected by this Permit. 17. This Permit prevails over the provisions of the zoning bylaw in the event of conflict. 18. Nothing in this permit exempts the owner from any requirements to install infrastructure under the Local Government Act, the Town’s Subdivision and Development Servicing Bylaw or a requirement for excess or extended services under section 507 of the Local Government Act. For clarity, at the building permit stage, the Permittee will be required to, at their cost, construct the full width of Colonia Drive adjacent to the Land, complete with sewer, water, stormwater and utility mains. 19. Despite issuance of this permit, development of any kind is not permitted in Riparian Development Permit Area without separate Riparian Development Permit. 20. Despite issuance of this permit, construction may not start without a Building Permit or other necessary permits.
