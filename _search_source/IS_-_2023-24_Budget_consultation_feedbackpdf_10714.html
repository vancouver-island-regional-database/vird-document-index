NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: March 29, 2022 Board of Education Dale Burgos, Executive Director Communication, Privacy & Engagement Consultation Feedback – 2023-24 Budget Background On February 21, 2023, the district began the 2023-24 budget consultation process using the ThoughtExchange engagement tool and email feedback. Feedback on these two platforms completed on March 10, 2023, with a virtual open house to be held on Thursday, March 30, 2023. The community engagement webpage and background information with instructions on how to participate was posted on: https://www.sd68.bc.ca/board/community-engagement/ Information on the community engagement period was: • Emailed to all district staff via district email • Emailed to all families via School Messenger platform • Posted in district news • Shared on social media (Facebook, Twitter and Instagram) Consultation Timeline • • • Tuesday, February 21, 2023 o Email to families o District news for staff o Social media posts Monday, March 6, 2023 o Reminder email to staff and families o Social media posts Thursday, March 30, 2023 - 5-6 p.m. o Virtual open house session (TEAMS) ThoughtExchange big question: As we continue to plan the upcoming budget, what are some priorities we should consider?
