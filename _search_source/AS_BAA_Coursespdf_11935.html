NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATION COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: June 5, 2024 Education Committee Don Balcombe, Assistant Superintendent Board/Authority Authorized Course Framework Recommendation The Education Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the following courses as Board/Authority Authorized Course (BAA) as per Ministry Policy: Financial Literacy 12, Forensic Science 11 Immersion, Indigenous Cultural Connections 10, 11, 12. Background BAA courses provide an opportunity for educators to explore content beyond the boundaries of Ministry curriculum. BAA courses help educators respond to the local needs of schools and their communities, and to provide choice and flexibility for students. Under the Ministry guidelines all locally developed courses that may count for graduation credit at the Grade 10, 11, 12 level must be submitted as Board/Authority Authorized Courses and be approved by the Board of Education. Financial Literacy 12, Forensic Science 11 Immersion, and Indigenous Cultural Connections 10, 11, and 12, have been reviewed by District staff and recommended for approval by the Board of Education.
