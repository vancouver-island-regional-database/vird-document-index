THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: May 5, 2014 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Shadow Budget Bylaw for Fiscal Year 2014-2015 BACKGROUND: At the April 23, 2014 Board Meeting the following motion was adopted. Folio R14/04/23-14 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) submits to the Minister of Education, alongside the finalized 2014-2015 budget, a draft shadow budget that reflects the resources the district would need in order to be in compliance with the working conditions provisions of the pre 2002 Teachers' Collective Agreement. The Shadow Budget Bylaw for School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) for fiscal year 2014-2015 is attached and is referred to as the “Shadow Budget Bylaw for fiscal year 20142015”. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts the Shadow Budget Bylaw in the amount of $139,079,254 for fiscal year 2014-2015 and that it be given first reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts the Shadow Budget Bylaw in the amount of $139,079,254 for fiscal year 2014-2015 and that it be given second reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts all three readings of the Shadow Budget Bylaw in the amount of $139,079,254 for fiscal year 2014-2015 at its Special Board Meeting of May 5, 2014. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts the Shadow Budget Bylaw in the amount of $139,079,254 for fiscal year 2014-2015 and that it be given a third and final reading.
