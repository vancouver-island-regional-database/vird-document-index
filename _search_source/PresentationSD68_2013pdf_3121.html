Energy Ambassadors School District 68 Who are Energy Ambassadors? What do Energy Ambassadors do? • We gather information about energy and resource use. • We plan and deliver outreach activities and school events that raise awareness and change habits! • We kickstart projects that promote conservation. Here is a snapshot of the work of Energy Ambassadors from Nanaimo and Ladysmith Secondary schools … Ladysmith Secondary Ladysmith Secondary Energy Use and Conservation Behaviors at LSS • Large improvements seen from previous years • 100% of rooms turned off their lights when leaving a room (up from 70% last year) • Space around vents are always kept clear • Computer labs consistently turn off computers when not in use (another large improvement from last year) • 85% of student families only do laundry when there is a full load (compared to 75% last year) • 100% students always/sometimes turn off lights when they leave a room (up from 90% last year) • 9/10 teachers always/sometimes turn off lights when they leave a room Ladysmith Secondary The LSS Green Team and Our Accomplishments This Year: • Completed the energy audit and behavioral surveys • Toured the school’s heating system • Presented to a Ladysmith Primary class about Earth Day and how to save energy • Presented to classes in our school about the importance of composting • Our cafeteria decided to start using compostable soup bowls • Maintained the school garden Our goal at LSS: • To go paper and waste free • To encourage the use of reusable water bottles instead of recyclable plastic water bottles NDSS Environment Committee: Creating a sustainable and energy efficient community • • • • • . . .
