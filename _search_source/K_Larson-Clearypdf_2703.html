Lizabeth Burgos Subject: February 8, 2017 Re: School Closure and Road Conditions Attention AllWhom Are Able to Address this Problem Last Friday, Feb, 3rd, the city roads were not plowed adequately for morning traffic to flow safely. The School District (SD68) did not close schools. Many families rely on the school districts decision about whether or not school are closed during bad weather. Last Friday, many families, such as our started driving kids to school, only to find that traffic was stopped due to multiple accidents up ahead. Personally, I was able to turn around and get home with my kids safely. I could not get my kids to school due to the road conditions. I understand from the news that the City of Nanaimo did in fact have their plowing crew out clearing streets, but the fact that the snow was still falling quickly meant that the roads could not be kept cleared. I have also learned that many teachers were not able to get to the schools due to the roads, and therefore those few children whom were able to get to school did not benefit from any planned classroom work. Those children simply watched movies, and spent the day in the guy. It is important to know that I compliment the school for caring for the children as we as they did without al oftheir teachers.
