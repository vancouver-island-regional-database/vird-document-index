Munro, Superintendent/CEO DATE: November 24, 2010 Background: At the September 28, 2010 Board Meeting the following motion was passed: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) requests staff to develop terms of reference, for the approval by the Board of Education, a review of French Immersion Programs in the District. To capture the many interests that might be served by such a review, staff considered the current District Facilities Plan from which this motion arose, as well as the long-term needs of maintaining and possibly enhancing the quality of the experience of French Immersion in SD68. Consequently, staff is presenting a Draft Request for Proposals (RFP), attached. Staff believe that the terms outlined in this draft proposal would maximize the opportunity to complete a comprehensive review of the current French Immersion programs, and consider future scenarios for the program within SD68, while also addressing issues raised in the District Facilities Plan that require direction from the Board of Education, in order for the overall plan to proceed effectively. As the RFP submissions will outline the proposed undertaking of each potential consultant, as well the associated costs, it will be possible for staff to consider each proposal and make a recommendation to the Board for the awarding of a contract under the terms and conditions and price as agreed to by both parties. Staff is of the view that proposals submitted would range from a low of $10,000 to $30,000. Staff is recommending that the Board request staff to complete any final work on the RFP and submit it to public tender, as per usual practice, and for staff to review submissions and bring a recommendation to the Board for approval prior to proceeding with the proposed review. Recommendation: It is recommended that the Board of Education of School District 68, (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) request staff to submit for public tender the French Immersion Programs Request for Proposals and to bring forward recommendations for proceeding, based upon the submissions received.
