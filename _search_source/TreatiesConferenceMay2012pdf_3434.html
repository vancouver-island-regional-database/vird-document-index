Fulfilling Treaty Promises and Living in Treaty Relationships The conference grows out of a recognized need for further dialogue and research, as well as public education, about these distinct treaties in the history of Canada. The conference will feature four main themes. · The Challenges of Treaty Interpretation · Charting a New Course for Treaty Implementation · Decision Making · Unleashing the Promise of Economic Development These themes will be explored through presentations by panels of scholars and experts, followed by discussions with conference participants. Panel information, registration details and backgrounders about the four themes are available on the conference website at vancouverislandtreaties.org Vancouver Island University and Snuneymuxw First Nation are pleased to announce they will co-host a conference on the Pre-Confederation Treaties of Vancouver Island. May 10 & 11, 2012 vancouverislandtreaties.org The registration fee for the conference is $200 or $75 for students and includes: · conference materials · healthy lunches and snacks Tickets for the gala dinner, May 10, are $30.
