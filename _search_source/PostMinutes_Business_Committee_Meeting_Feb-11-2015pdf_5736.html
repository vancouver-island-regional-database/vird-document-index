CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Roberts presented to the Committee the draft 2014-2015 Budget. Business Amended Revenue Generating Programs - A/ SecretaryTreasurer, Mr. G. Roberts Mr. Roberts went through the Revenue Generating powerpoint attached to the agenda and answered questions from Trustees and stakeholders. A Board motion passed on December 17, 2014 for an overview of revenue generating programs within the district. This overview from Mr. Roberts included; operating grants, other Provincial grants, international tuition, rentals and leases, investment income and other sources of revenue. Mr. Roberts provided suggestions such as; payper-use busing, charging for parking in the district, expanding the international program and increasing rates for rentals. Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held February 11, 2015 Wellington Secondary Seismic Update Director, Planning and Operations, Mr. Pete Sabo Mr. Emil Bock, Acting Manager of Energy and Capital presented the Business Committee with an update of the Wellington Secondary Seismic Program. He provided the Business Committee with construction updates on each of the section of the seismic upgrade. The project should be completed by January 2016. Local Capital and Restricted Capital A/Secretary-Treasurer, Mr. G. Roberts Mr. Roberts gave a verbal update on Local and Restricted Capital as information to the Business Committee. AFG Funding - Director, Planning and Operations, Mr. Pete Sabo Mr. Pete Sabo gave a verbal update as inforamation on AFG Funding. Annual Facilities Grant (AFG) funding was released for the restructuring of Cedar Elementary in favour of other maintenance projects across the district. This came out of the recommendation to the Board from the January 14, 2014 Business Committee meeting. 2015-2016 Track and Field Projects Folio BC15/02/11-05 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae That the Business Committee approve the continuation of improvements being made on the Rotary Bowl facility.
