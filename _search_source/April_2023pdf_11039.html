SENIOR STAFF REPORTS 8.1 Department of Learning Services Presentation on the Board Goal: Truth and Reconciliation Senior staff from the Department of Learning Services provided an overview of how the Board's goal of Truth and Reconciliation is impacting all areas of the District. Ricki Bartlett shared how the District is committed to creating systems, structures and environments of equity – where Indigenous students and families feel a true sense of belonging in the school setting, and strong connections to the people within them. An Information Sheet is attached to the Agenda. School Administrators and staff from Dover Bay Secondary School and Seaview View Elementary School shared how the Board's goal of Truth and Reconciliation has impacted schools. Hul'q'umi'num Language Teacher, Adam Manson provided a mini hul'q'umi'num language lesson and resources available for continued learning. 8.2 Kerri Steel, Director of Instruction and Trina Norgan, District Vice Principal Inclusive Education Re: Elementary Inclusion Outreach Program 2 Kerri Steel and Trina Norgan provided information on the District's Inclusion Outreach Team and the support they provide school teams throughout the District. An Information Sheet is attached to the Agenda. 9. CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM THE REGULAR BOARD MEETING There was none. 10. UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was none. 11.
