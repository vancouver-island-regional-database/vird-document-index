NEW BUSINESS District Achievement Contract Ms. Southwick, Assistant Superintendent ,said that every year all school districts in the province are required to submit a plan for student achievement to the Ministry of Education. She said that School District 68’s Achievement Contract is built on the education plans developed by each school, and takes into consideration data from a variety of sources. Also, the Achievement Contract is closely aligned with the Aboriginal Enhancement Agreement.
