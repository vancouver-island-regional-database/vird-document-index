THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: January 13, 2016 TO: Business Committee FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Dave Prevost, Transportation Supervisor SUBJECT: Transportation and Z Pass ridership Information Update The total transportation budget for 2015-16 is $2,185,692 which consists of the following expense groupings: Transportation 2015-16 Budget Allocation Fleet Operations and Maintenance 15% Student Transportation 72% Courier and Cartage 4% Transportation Administration 9% As part of last year’s budget discussion, service level reductions and/or to charge fees for busing were considered. No recommendation or decisions were made to alter our transportation service model, in part due to the lack of accurate ridership data. Of the student transportation budget totaling $1,575,995, the amount of $1,381,174 is allocated in the following tables as variable costs.
