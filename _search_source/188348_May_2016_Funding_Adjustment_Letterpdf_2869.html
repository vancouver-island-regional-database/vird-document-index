June 22, 2016 VIA EMAIL Ref: 188348 To: All Secretary-Treasurers All School Districts Re: May 2016 Adjusted Funding Allocations The adjusted funding allocations have been determined based on actual enrolment as at May 6, 2015, for distributed learning and continuing education. Grant adjustments will be reflected in the June 29, 2016, payment. The complete package is located on our website at: 2015/16 Final Operating Grants Districts that submitted counts for Syrian refugee students will also be receiving allocations at the end of June for students claimed on May 27, 2016. As indicated in a letter to all districts on March 15, 2016, this funding will be provided as a discretionary grant to boards of education using a basic allocation rate of $1,790 per FTE. Supplemental funding of $345 per FTE will be provided for new Syrian refugee students who qualify for English/French Language learning. If you have any questions about this funding adjustment, please contact Michael Lebrun at (250) 356-0176 or by email at Michael.Lebrun@gov.bc.ca, or myself at (250) 580 9180 or by e-mail at Lynne.Holt@gov.bc.ca.
