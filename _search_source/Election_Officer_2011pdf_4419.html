BOARD OF EDUCATION SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) ACTION SHEET TO: Board of Education FROM: P. Turin, Secretary-Treasurer DATE: May 25, 2011 SUBJECT: Appointment of Chief Election Officer(s) BACKGROUND: In accordance with the Trustee Election Bylaw No. 2, the School Trustee Election is carried out under agreement with the City of Nanaimo, Nanaimo Regional District, Cowichan Valley Regional District and the Town of Ladysmith. There are a number of functions that are the responsibility of the School District and, therefore, there is a need to appoint a Chief Election Officer and one Deputy Chief Election Officer to carry out those duties exclusive to the School District Trustee Election. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) appoints Mr. Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer as the Chief Election Officer and Ms. Cathy Kelt, Executive Assistant as the Deputy Chief Election Officers for the purpose of the 2011 School Trustee Election.
