BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: RE: March 8, 2016 Business Committee John Blain, Superintendent/CEO Administrative Procedure 512 - Transportation Update BACKGROUND The Board of Education of Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools approved Board Governance Policy 3.8 – Transportation at the November 23, 2016 Regular Board meeting. The next phase was for district staff to develop draft transportation administrative procedures for the Board to consider. At the January 25, 2017 Regular Board meeting, the Board received Administrative Procedure 512 – Transportation and agreed to enter a 30-day notice of motion at which time the public may provide feedback. NEXT STEPS District staff will update AP 512 – Transportation as well as the Bus Route and Hazard Evaluation Form and implement its use for the 2017-18 school year. Page 1 of 1
