CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Budget 2010-11 Mr. Turin gave the Committee an update on the budget forecast for the current school year. He said that a net decrease in revenue of about $268,000, combined with a projected increase in expenses of about $410,000, results in a projected shortfall for the year of about $678,000.
