Trustee Solomon Zone Report December 2016 Cinnabar Valley:  4 students attended the WE Day event in Vancouver and reported back to the school. This is a youth empowerment event sponsored by Free the Children.  Boys Football took second place for small schools and Dec. 21 is the Christmas Concert at the Port. Cedar Elementary: Welcomed the kindergarten children from Woodbank.  The Christmas concert was held at two times on Dec. 08 with participation from all grades, parent, and teacher helpers. Very well done.  The Craft Fair was Dec. 15.  Caroling occurring between December 19 - 23rd. CSS: Has re- introduced basketball to the school. They are currently fielding grade 8 boys, jr. boys, sr. boys and a sr. girls team which is low on numbers. New uniforms were acquired via numerous methods. ConnectEd: Very involved PAC primarily consisting of parents from K - 7.  Christmas pageant on Dec. 15. We Team is selling Rafiki bracelets made by women in Kenya.  Art club workshop happen every Fri.  Winter skating starts on Jan 18 and runs to March.  Craft fair on Dec. 2.  Gr. 6 and 7's involved in the First Lego League with an inaugural robot game scrimmage in Victoria on Dec. 03. Their robot performed well and completed 3 of the challenges in the timed rounds. The group is made up of nine students who generally meet once a week to prepare for the big competition in Feb. They are shifting their focus to the Science component of the challenge, and have selected black bears as their focus. This is a great example of project based learning.
