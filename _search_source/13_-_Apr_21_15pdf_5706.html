APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio BC15/04/21-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS Mr. Steve Fielden Carpenter Projects Crew Foreman RE: Proposed 2015-2016 Budget Strategies Mr. Steve Fielden, Carpenter Projects Crew Foreman presented to the Special Business Committee on behalf of the maintenance, Draft Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 21, 2015 grounds, and operations employees in NanaimoLadysmith Public Schools. His report to the Board is attached to the Agenda. Mr. Fielden shared that their services have a direct impact on safety, health and hygiene in district schools and facilities. His presentation included a list of detailed duties for the following job groups: • Locksmith • Carpenters • Roofing • Glass repairs • Glazier • Electricians • Plumbers • Welder • Painters • Environment Management Technicians HVAC • Horticultural Work – Grounds Division • Grounds • Maintenance Clerk Mr. Fielden shared with Trustees that the school district has over 40 sites and fields that CUPE Local 606 maintains with a staffing equivalency of 38.6 FTE (Full Time Equivalent). NEW BUSINESS Revenue - Mr. Roberts Mr. Roberts handed out a couple of Revenue reports to the Trustees. They are available to view, as attachments to the Agenda. Draft Budget #1 - Learning Services The Learning Services Department provided a draft budget presentation to the Business Committee - it is attached to the Agenda. The department has two goals: 1) Meet each student’s unique needs 2) The continuous improvement of instruction and assessment UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was no Unfinished Business this evening.
