610 – 2525 Willow Street, Vancouver, B.C., V5Z 3N8 Tel: 604-737-9907 Toll-free: 1-888-393-2226 Email: info@optometrists.bc.ca Eye See Eye Learn® for all Kindergarten Students in Your School District Message to Parents and Guardians: Good eye health and vision are essential for academic and developmental success during children’s early learning years. 1 in 4 school-age children has a vision problem, and often parents and teachers don’t know it. Your School District and the British Columbia Association of Optometrists (BCAO) have partnered to provide an eye examination and FREE prescription eyeglass program called Eye See Eye Learn®, for all Kindergarten children in the Coquitlam School District #43. Instructions: Contact a participating BCAO Doctor of Optometry listed on the reverse side of this letter to book an appointment for an eye exam for your child. There will be no cost to you for this service. Bring this letter, as it will cover your child’s prescription eyeglasses if correction is needed. This letter is valid from April 1, 2014 to August 31, 2014.
