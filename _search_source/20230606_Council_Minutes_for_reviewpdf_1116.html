DELEGATIONS 7.1 Quentin Goodbody, President, Ladysmith & District Historical Society (LDHS) Quentin Goodbody, President of the Ladysmith & District Historical Society, provided an operational update to Council. He summarized the Society's many initiatives and accomplishments over the past year and noted that the Society would like to have discussions with the Town regarding financing for the Museum and Archives, updating the Heritage Strategic Plan and expanding the Heritage Register. Mr. Goodbody responded to Council's questions. Council expressed their appreciation for the presentation and thanked him and all the volunteers for their work in the community. 8.
