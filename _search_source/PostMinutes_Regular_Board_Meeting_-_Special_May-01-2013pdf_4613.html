Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No. S16 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Additions to the Agenda. Chair Brennan said that the Alternate School Calendar will be added to the Agenda as an urgent item and that it is item 3.2 under New Business. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Welch Folio S13/05/01-01 That the Agenda be approved as amended. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS 2013-2014 Budget Bylaw Mr. Turin said that over the past few weeks, the Business Committee has reviewed management’s proposals for strategies to meet a $4.7 million shortfall in the budget. The 20132014 budget is balanced and totals $133.7 million, with $119.6 million in operating expenses; $7.9 million in special purpose funds; and $6.2 million in capital expenses. Mr. Turin said the Board needs to adopt the 2013-2014 Budget Bylaw in front of them this evening. Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held May 1, 2013 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Bard Folio S13/05/01-06 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) rescinds their motion of March 27th R13/03/27-13 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts a Local School Calendar for 2013-2014, subject to meeting the requirements of the NDTA Collective Agreement pertaining to school year, that extends Spring Break in March 2014 by five days from March 24 to March 28 as per the attached proposed Local School Calendar.
