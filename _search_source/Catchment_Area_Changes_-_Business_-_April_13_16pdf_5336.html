BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: April 13, 2016 TO: BUSINESS COMMITTEE FROM: John Blain, Superintendent/CEO RE: Adjustments to catchment areas affecting Park Avenue, Bayview, Fairview, Georgia Avenue, Forest Park, Brechin, and Mountain View Elementary Schools BACKGROUND: Based on very preliminary projections for 2016-17, and considering the historical enrolment data/Baragar projections along with the current building construction projects occurring in the Park Avenue Elementary community, staff have compiled an analysis of future school enrolment capacities. During the Updated Facilities Plan consultations, there was discussion of catchment areas for schools, and facilities that have low-capacity utilization. Staff have been tracking a shift in student enrolment and home construction in the Park Avenue catchment, and believe that that school’s enrolment projections for next school year reinforce the need for some catchment area alterations to accommodate families new to the area.
