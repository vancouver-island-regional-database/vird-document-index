SOGI Report to the Superintendent Our October meeting was to prepare for the Districts first GSA Conference. The conference was held at Dover Bay Secondary School. Attendees came from all regions of Vancouver Island and included students, counsellors, teachers and GSA facilitators. Workshops were very well attended, although some attendees were unable to stay beyond the keynote and opening. Information tables were set up by the Vancouver Island Rainbow Assn., DPAC, and the PFLAG Victoria chapter. I was most encouraged by the enthusiasm expressed by DPAC’s support for Queer Youth. They have asked for contact information for a couple of workshop facilitators with the possibility of repeating them for parents in Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools. Morning coffee/Tea and snacks, as well as lunch were provided. Leftovers were sent along with those having to travel and a local community GSA that runs through NYSA downtown. Several students and adults gave feedback indicating they would be taking the skills and information they gained at the conference to return to their own communities and either start a GSA or build on the ones that are already existing. The conference Itinerary is attached. The SOGI committee will discuss, in the new year, the likelihood of doing another conference next year.
