CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS Francois Brassens Cedar Hockey Ball Challenge Association RE: Resurfacing of NCI Multipurpose Area Mr. Francois Brassens is the Vice President of the Cedar Hockey Ball Challenge Association (CBHCA). They are heading into their seventh annual Hockey Ball Challenge Tournament this year. For past events, they have used the North Cedar Intermediate multi-purpose court area for this event. The current status of the multipurpose court could be dangerous for anyone to play on. As it stands now, it is a safety concern. Mr. Brassens provided a quote from Hub City Paving of $36,768.75 and is prepared to contribute $20,000 that was raised by the community through fundraising efforts.
