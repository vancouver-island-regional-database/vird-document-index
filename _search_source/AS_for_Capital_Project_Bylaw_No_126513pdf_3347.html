THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: June 27, 2012 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Capital Project Bylaw 126513 – Playground Equipment BACKGROUND: In September 2011 the Premier announced a new $8 million capital program to fund playground equipment for elementary students. This funding is being allocated to school districts over two years – 20111/12 and 2012/13, under three separate phases. The following schools in District 68 are eligible for Phase 3 funding: Repair/Upgrades – Georgia Avenue - $25,000 Replacement – Chase River - $50,000 FIRST READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: SECOND READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126513 Playground Equipment, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the Bylaw’s title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126513 Playground Equipment, in the amount of $75,000 and that it be given first reading. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126513 Playground Equipment,, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the Bylaw’s title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts Capital Project Bylaw No. 126513 Playground Equipment, in the amount of $75,000 and that it be given second reading. UNANIMOUS CONSENT ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: In order for the third and final reading of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126513 Playground Equipment, to take place at the same meeting as the first and second readings, unanimous approval of the Board is required. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126513 Playground Equipment, for a third and final time. THIRD & FINAL READING ACTION REQUIRED: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126513 Playground Equipment, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126513 Playground Equipment, in the amount of $75,000 and that it be given third and final reading.
