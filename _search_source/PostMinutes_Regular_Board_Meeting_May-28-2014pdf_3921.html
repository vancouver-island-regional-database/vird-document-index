The students said they have conducted energy audits, made presentations to elementary students, undertaken awareness campaigns, and worked on establishing school gardens. They encouraged the Board to support a district-wide Earth Day celebration next year. The energy ambassadors have worked with Jennifer Callaghan of BC Hydro and SD68 Energy Specialist Blair McFarlane and Energy Manager Chad Dalrymple. Alana Cameron - DPAC President RE: Criminal Record Checks Alana Cameron, president of the District Parents Advisory Council, talked to the Board about criminal record checks for individuals elected to the Board of Education. She said that all employees and parent volunteers are required to have criminal record checks and DPAC believes that the same requirement should apply to trustees.
