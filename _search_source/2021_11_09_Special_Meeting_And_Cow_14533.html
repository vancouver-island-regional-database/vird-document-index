NOTICE SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING AND TIME CHANGE FOR REGULAR COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE MEETING The Town of Ladysmith Council will meet in person at Council Chambers, 410 Esplanade on Tuesday, November 9, 2021 at 6:00 p.m. for a Special Council Meeting immediately followed by a Regular Committee of the Whole Meeting. Purpose: The Special Council Meeting is for Council to engage in a 2022 Budget Workshop regarding Water and Sewer utilities. Time Change: The Regular Committee of the Whole Meeting start time is changed from 6:30 p.m. to immediately following the Special Council Meeting which commences at 6:00 p.m. Corporate Officer (D.
