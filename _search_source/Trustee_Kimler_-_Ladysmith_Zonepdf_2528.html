Trustee Zone Report 20-Dec-2017 Ladysmith Zone Ladysmith Secondary School (LSS)  Christmas Angels – Students interested in buying a gift for a child in need in Ladysmith can pluck an angel off the Christmas tree in the foyer. Each angel has the age and gender of a child in need. If you wish to donate, pick and angel off the tree and leave a new gift in the office with the angel attached.  Connie the Elf – From the middle of the month, staff have been hiding an elf inside the school. Clues to its location are posted on the student council Instagram account (@lsscouncil). Whoever finds the elf each day will win a prize. Ladysmith Primary School (LPS)  Christmas Concert – You all are missing the evening LPS Christmas concert, which is right now! (Dec 20th from 6PM to 7:30 … although I noticed on the website notice, it’s listed as 6 AM until 7:30 PM … so maybe it is meant to be an all-day concert?) Ecole North Oyster  Playspace Update – ENO PAC worked hard with the community & students to fundraise and they are happy to announce they have enough money to begin phase 1 of their playspace. Construction will begin in early spring 2018. Ladysmith Intermediate School (LIS)  Nanaimo Art Gallery – Three gallery artists visited LIS for four sessions including “Hip to the Hop”, “Equality Song” and “Something Fishy”. Through this TD-supported “Artists in the School” program, student experienced hands-on sculpture, pottery, drumming, singing & dance. Learn more at the Nanaimo Art Gallery website: www.nanaimoartgallery.ca  A Pirate Christmas – Students are getting into the holiday spirit with this holiday musical for young voices. Performances are tomorrow with an early 1PM showing & an evening showing at 7PM. Trustee Kimler
