TOWN OF LADYSMITH BYLAW NO. 2131 A Bylaw to amend “Town of Ladysmith Zoning Bylaw 2014, No. 1860” The Council of the Town of Ladysmith in open meeting assembled enacts the following amendment to “Town of Ladysmith Zoning Bylaw 2014, No. 1860”: 1. Amending Schedule B – Zoning Bylaw Map to change the zone from RU-1 (Rural Residential) to MHP-1 (Manufactured Home Park) for the subject property, at Lot 1, District Lot 72, Oyster District, Plan 24076 (10940 Westdowne Road), as shown in Schedule 1, which is attached to and forms a part of this Bylaw. Citation 2. This Bylaw may be cited for all purposes as “Town of Ladysmith Zoning Bylaw 2014, No. 1860, Amendment Bylaw (No.54) 2022, No. 2131”.
