According to BCSTA Bylaws, the role of the Legislative Committee is to “examine, correlate, amend, reject, and report on motions.” Additionally, for Provincial Council meetings the Legislative Committee is required to determine whether the motions are of an emergent nature, where emergent is defined as “matters that if delayed until the AGM will impact negatively on Boards’ ability to influence public policy or a public education issue.” The Legislative Committee does not assess the merits of the motions that are submitted to Provincial Council.
