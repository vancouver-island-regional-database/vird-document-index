She asked the Board to reconsider and revert to individual school wait lists. Ms. Pam Agnew Friends of Pioneer Park Ms. Agnew, on behalf of the Friends of Pioneer Forest told the Board about the property that the school district owns at Pioneer Park. She said that the property is the last piece of forest for the north end of Nanaimo and she asked the Board to work with her group to ensure that the land can be secured as a park.
