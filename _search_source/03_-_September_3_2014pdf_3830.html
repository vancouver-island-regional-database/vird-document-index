Nixon, Director of Labour Relations Meeting No. S03 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 5:25 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio S14/09/03-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Disposal of Real Property Bylaw No. 2014-01- Princess Royal Elementary School Mr. Turin told the Board that the sale of the building is part of the school district’s 10-Year Enhanced Facilities Plan and Asset Management Plan. The building has been purchased by Tardon Developments Inc. at a price of $900,000. The proceeds from the sale will go into the Board’s local capital fund, to be used for future school facilities construction. Mr. Turin read the Disposal of Real Property Bylaw No. 2014-01- Princess Royal Elementary School.
