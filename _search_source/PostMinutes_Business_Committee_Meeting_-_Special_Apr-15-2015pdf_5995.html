APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio BC15/04/15-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS Mr. Jeff Virtanen Maintenance Service Caretaker RE: Proposed 2015-16 Budget Strategies Mr. Jeff Virtanen spoke on behalf of the Maintenance Service Caretaker's. His report to the Board is attached to the Agenda. Mr. Virtanen shared with the Business Committee the daily duties which include, but are not limited to; sweeping of floors, taking out garbage, vacuuming carpets, and the cleaning and Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 15, 2015 disinfecting of bathrooms. Maintenance Service Caretaker's also provide security, ensure the upkeep of facilities, and rental space management. NEW BUSINESS Mr. Roberts - Budget Discussion #3 A/Secretary-Treasurer, Mr. Graham Roberts gave his third budget presentation. His presentation is attached to the Agenda. Information in his presentation included the Your Voice 2015 review and community input process that provided feedback for the development of the 2015-16 budget. Topics that were also covered were: class size, programs, suggestions to help reduce the deficit, "for-pay" services, busing/transportation and district properties. Mr. Roberts also included a budget sustainability plan for next year focusing on: • Significant reduction in service from facilities, operations and maintenance. • Reductions to administrative positions. • Significant reduction of replacement costs. • Reduce staffing contingencies necessary due to funding protection. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae Folio BC15/04/15-02 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) to refer to staff issues of bus rentals, transportation alternatives including; pay parking, car-pooling, bike use; expedited disposal of unnecessary facilities and rentals of currently free-use facilities to be brought to a future Business Committee meeting. Opposed by Trustee(s) Bob, Solomon and Higginson CARRIED Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 15, 2015 UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was no Unfinished Business this evening.
