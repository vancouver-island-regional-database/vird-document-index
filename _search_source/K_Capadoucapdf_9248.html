From: To: Subject: Date: Karen Matthews Karen Matthews FW: Vaccine Mandates October 27, 2021 7:27:31 PM From: katie capadouca < > Sent: October 27, 2021 4:28 PM To: Charlene Mckay Subject: Vaccine Mandates CAUTION: External Message To the Nanaimo-Ladysmith School Board Trustee- Ms. Charlene McKay, I am a concerned parent and teacher. Please do not mandate mandatory vaccines for teachers, staff and/or students. To my knowledge, none of the School Board Trustees are health professionals or research scientists in the field of epidemiology, immunology, or infectious disease; nor are they privy to the specific medical history of school staff and students. I find it highly inappropriate that school board trustees are being put in a position to even be considering dispensing medical advice, such as vaccine mandates. My medical history and my personal health choices are not the business of my employer. I have the right as a parent to choose what medical procedures are best for my children and that is not the function or role of the School Board. I feel very strongly about this issue. I do not wish our School Board to allow this kind of dangerous precedent to be set. I am sorry that you have been put in this position.
