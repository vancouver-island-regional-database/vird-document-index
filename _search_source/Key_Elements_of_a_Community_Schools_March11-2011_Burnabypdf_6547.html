Key Elements of a Community School Community Use of School Facilities After School & Summer Programs Community & Continuing Education Early Learning Initiatives Supporting & Enhancing Student Learning A Community School is the hub of the community. It is both a place and a set of partnerships between the school and other community resources. Its integrated focus on academics, youth development, family support and community development leads to improved student learning, stronger families and healthier communities.
