NEW BUSINESS Amended Budget for 2011-2012 Mr. Turin said that every year at this time, school districts are required to submit an amended budget to the Ministry of Education. This reflects the changes that have been made to the budget since it was adopted last spring. Mr. Turin reviewed the district’s amended budget for 2011-12 with committee members. He said that because the district qualifies for the Ministry’s funding protection program, the government revenue for the district has not changed significantly. Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held January 24, 2012 3 He said that the district is currently projecting a surplus of about $1 million in its $124.2 million budget, which will help cushion the expected loss of about $1.7 million in funding protection in the next school year. Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held January 24, 2012 4 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio BC12/01/24-06 That the Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the substantive motion regarding criminal record checks That BCSTA urge the Provincial Government to amend the Criminal Records Review Act (RSBC 1996) Chapter 86 and the School Act (RSBC 1996) Chapter 412 to require that candidates for election as school trustees submit a current Criminal Record Check with nomination papers and that the School District make the criminal record check report publically available in the same manner as it makes other nomination documents be submitted to the BCSTA for inclusion at the April AGM Meeting. Opposed by Trustees Brennan, Neary, Curly, Bard, Robinson and Saunders DEFEATED Trustee Brennan gave a "Notice of Motion" regarding criminal record checks and asked that it be included on the next Business Committee Agenda. The notice of motion was “That BCSTA urge the Provincial Government to amend the School Act so that Trustee–elects will be required to submit to a criminal record check prior to being sworn in and that such information will be confidential and held by the Board until the Trustees term is completed, which upon it shall be destroyed.” FOR INFORMATION Budget Schedule Received as information.
