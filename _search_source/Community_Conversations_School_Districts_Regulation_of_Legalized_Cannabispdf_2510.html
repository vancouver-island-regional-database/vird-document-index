January 9, 2018 Leonard Krog, MLA Honourable Rob Fleming, Minister of Education Honourable Mike Farnworth, Minister of Public Safety and Solicitor General Parliament Buildings Victoria, BC V8V 1X4 Nanaimo Ministers, Re: Community conversations school districts regulation of legalized cannabis am attaching a letter dated December the 19”‘from Steve Rae, Board chair of my local school district. had a lengthy conversation with him early in January about the existing impact of cannabis use in the school system and how our local board had tried to deal with it. The point of his letter written on behalf ofthe board is to be included in community conversations. I am assuming that either of your Ministries or probably both may well be engaged with the BC School Trustees Association and or other individual districts, but my board being progressive and forward thinking wants to get ahead of this train that is coming at all of us, and is not going to be stopped. I think it is a reasonable expectation and hope that your respective offices can each provide some response as to where we are at in terms of this conversation, and whether or not the school boards would be included either through representation of by the BC School Trustees Association or individual school districts who may have some particular experience in dealing with cannabis use. Thank you for your kind consideration, I look forward to your response which I would be happy to have you share directly with board chair Steve Rae.
