Appendix A Administrative Procedure 207 – Outside Services AP 207 – Outside Services Purpose Services regularly provided to the District by outside sources shall be reviewed as required. Procedure Services provided on a continuing basis to, or on behalf of, the dstrict by outside agencies such as: Auditing Banking Legal Appraisals Insurance, shall be authorized either job-by-job, or over renewable term not to exceed three years, whichever is appropriate. Continued use of a particular agency depends upon a favourable evaluation of services, after not more than one year. Factors used in the choice or continued use of an agency providing each service shall include:     competitive rates where obtainable; qualifications and expertise; quality and availability of service; and experience.
