NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: April 22, 2026 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Trustee Resignation Background This information sheet is intended to inform the Board of a Trustee resignation and outline next steps. Discussion On April 16, 2026, as per the requirements of section 51 of the School Act Naomi Bailey informed me of her resignation as Trustee eﬀective April 30, 2026. The letter is attached. With respect to next steps, Chair Lee will assume the role as Chair until the completion of the term. The election of a Vice-Chair will be added to the May regular Board agenda. Further, given the resignation occurred “after January 1 in the year of a general election” the School Act does not require an election. Appendix A: Resignation Letter
