THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: April 8, 2015 TO: Business Committee FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations SUBJECT: Cedar Secondary Student Attendance Pattern 2013/2014 Cedar Secondary - Attendance Information Below is a breakdown of the student’s attendance pattern for Cedar Secondary in 2013/2014. Of the 537 students from the Cedar catchment who attended a School District 68 school, 345 attended Cedar Secondary and 192 another school. Of the 410 students who attended Cedar Secondary, 345 were from the Cedar Secondary catchment area and 65 were cross boundary from other schools. 1
