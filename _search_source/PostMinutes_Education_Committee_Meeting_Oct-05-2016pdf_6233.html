CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY LEARNING SERVICES DEPARTMENT Ms. Laura Tait Director of Instruction RE: Reporting Order Laura Tait, Director of Instruction, provided an update on the district's year of exploration with the new provincial student reporting. Traditionally, communication of student learning took the form of a standard report card with comments and letter grades sent home to parents three times per year. The district's goal is to design a reporting system that engages students, parents and teachers. There will be school based and district wide parent information sessions occurring over the 2016-17 school year. To view Ms. Tait's presentation, please see the attachment to the Agenda. Mr. Ball has a request to the Board regarding timing of the order - a lot of work was done in August and September and was presented to teachers last week and now there is a change. The impact of this on top of the revised curriculum has been huge. The request to the Board is that they ask the ministry for a temporary order and urge the ministry through a letter to bring the permanent order out in the Spring.
