Myself, Kenton Dickand Ethan Olynyk were invited to play at the Monterey Next Generation Jazz Festival this past weekend through a blind audition process. Only six groups were selected. The students competed against schools such as LACounty School of the Arts (3000 students) and Las Vegas Arts Academy and won first place! Never in the history of the festival has an international group won, even placed. To top it off, never has a duo group won. Not only did these boys win first place, but they each earned outstanding solo awards a total of only four solo awards were given. Last night they were asked to perform again in the final concert where they received a standing ovation. This win has garnered them an performance at the Monterey International Jazz Festival in September -on stages that our own Diana Krallhas played. I am very proud of these young men. They have represented Wellington Secondary, Nanaimo, our province and Canada in a very historical achievement. - Carmella l
