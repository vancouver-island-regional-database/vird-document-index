Committee Format Meeting - June 02, 2016 Board Office Attendees: Regrets: John Blain, Graham Roberts, Tania Brzovic, Jeff Solomon Stephanie Higginson, Scott Kimler  Discussion regarding process. Proposed changes to Governance procedures to be brought before the Business Committee as representatives are able to provide input. If passed, motions brought to Board Meeting at the end of the month. "Housekeeping" matters 2.2 and 4.75 to be brought as one Motion. Review of each of these items with minor changes to wording.  Discussion regarding 4.1. Acknowledgement that there may not be full consensus and matter can be reviewed by the full Board. Concerns expressed of working subcommittees with partial representation from trustees. Trust factor may be an issue along with extra meeting time. Having all Board members present for input and discussion may be more efficient. Proposal to limit seating at the table for trustees, the superintendent, and the secretary-treasurer during Education and Business meetings. Input from representatives would continue. If the Board wishes to look at changes to seating it can be done in stages with assessment and review in a number of months. Motion to be created for discussion at the Business Committee on June 08. Should the Board wish to proceed a follow up Motion to be created directing staff to adapt physical arrangement of the Board room reflecting adopted changes. Some feeling of confidence that the Board room set up could be improved.  Motions to be sent to committee members on Thurs night. Notice of Motions to be sent to Kelsey for disbursement on Monday June 06.
