NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: January 13, 2021 Business Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Long Range Facilities Plan Update Background Staff is continuing the process of drafting the Long Range Facilities Plan (LRFP) based on the presentation in our working session. Our intention was to bring the draft LRFP to the January Business Meeting. This memo is to update the Board and the community on our status. Update Currently, our consultant assisting with our longer-term growth projections have provided us preliminary information, but due to a small delay in getting permissions from external parties, we have not yet been able to run various growth scenarios to finalize those numbers. We will be finalizing the longer-term growth pattern in the near term to support the LRFP and the School Site Acquisition Charge. In addition, due to COVID our short-term projections (Baragar) which arrived in December are problematic because of the significant variation in bricks and mortar enrollment this year. We understand this is a provincial issue. Currently, staff are in the process of: 1. Reviewing our Baragar projections; 2. Reviewing our distributed learning enrollments to determine which schools’ students came from, and will likely return to 3. Reviewing areas of expected growth or decline 4. Accounting for cohort retention and in-out migration, and 5. Estimating enrollment based on these factors. We also note that some of this year’s projection data has come in significantly lower than previously estimated which may impact potential space requirements or alter preliminary planning. Because of these factors (particularly our shorter-term projections), we will not be a position to finalize the LRFP assumptions for a January presentation, instead we anticipate a February date for presentation of the initial LRFP draft.
