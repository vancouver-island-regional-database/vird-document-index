COUNCIL REFERRALS Development Permit Application 3060-25-12 – 12 Roberts Street Planning Technician Sampreet Sidhu provided a brief overview of the proposal, which involves adding a two-storey addition to the rear of the existing building. Mr. Sidhu added that the Building Inspection department had concerns regarding the windows facing the eastern laneway that may not comply with fire safety regulations. An engineered sprinkler system was identified as a solution. Applicant Kevin Lamont provided a verbal and visual presentation of the proposal. He also noted the fire safety concerns and added that an engineered sprinkler rain screen will be included in the design. He also indicated that a chair lift will be added at the rear of the building to improve accessibility. CPAC sought clarification on the engineered sprinkler system and asked questions regarding parking, incorporating bicycle infrastructure, the plan for lighting, and how the proposal would align with Development Permit Area 2 guidelines for laneway design. Committee members offered suggestions to enhance the activation of the eastern laneway and adjacent sidewalk by adding street furniture and upgrades to the existing sidewalk planter. CPAC was generally in favor of the application. It was moved, seconded, and carried that the Community Planning Advisory Committee recommends that Council approve Development Permit application 3060-25-12 for 12 Roberts Street with the following considerations: • The preservation and enhancement of the existing exterior lights • The provision of bicycle parking • Investigate options for upgrading the existing street planter • Investigate options to increase interest in the eastern facade design 5.
