ATTACHMENT C TOWN OF LADYSMITH BYLAW NO. 2222 A bylaw to amend "Town of Ladysmith Zoning Bylaw 2014, No. 1860" The Council of the Town of Ladysmith in open meeting assembled enacts the following amendments to “Town of Ladysmith Zoning Bylaw 2014, No. 1860”: 1. Schedule A – Zoning Bylaw Text is amended by: a. relabeling “Figure 10.8.1” under subsection (e) of section 10.8.8 (Site Specific Regulations of the R-3-A Zone) as “Figure 10.8.3” and updating all references in subsection 10.8.8(e)(i) to 10.8.8(e)(iv) accordingly; and b. adding as subsection (f) under section 10.8.8 the following: “f) The maximum Units Per Hectare regulation in subsection 10.8.4(b), does not apply to the area shown in figure 10.8.4.
