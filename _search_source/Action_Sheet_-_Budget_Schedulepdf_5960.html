THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: February 16, 2011 TO: Special Business Committee FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Budget Schedule The Budget Schedule indicates that there is a public budget meeting scheduled for Monday, April 4, 2011 in the NDSS Big Gym at 6:00 pm. It has been suggested that all presentations related to the budget process be heard on this evening rather than groups or individuals speaking to Committees and/or the Board during the budget process. Any individuals or groups that wish to make a presentation to the Public Budget Meeting should contact Ms. Kelt (ckelt@sd68.bc.ca or 250-741-5238) by Thursday, March 31, 2011 – 4:00 pm. If there are too many presentations to be heard on April 4, 2011, we will continue on April 5th. The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) that the Monday, April 4, 2011, Public Budget Meeting, be held for public budget presentations on the 2011/12 Budget.
