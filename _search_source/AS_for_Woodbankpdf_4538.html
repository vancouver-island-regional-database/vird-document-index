THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: December 2, 2015 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Woodbank Primary School Closure FIRST READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-07 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Woodbank Primary School, 1984 Woobank Rd, Nanaimo, BC, V9X 1K6; Facility No. 068085; on June 30, 2016, and that it be given first reading. SECOND READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-07 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Woodbank Primary School, 1984 Woobank Rd, Nanaimo, BC, V9X 1K6; Facility No. 068085; on June 30, 2016, and that it be given second reading. UNANIMOUS CONSENT REQUIRED TO READ THIRD AND FINAL BYLAW RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Woodbank Primary School Closure Bylaw No. 2015-07 at its Special Board Meeting of December 2, 2015. THIRD AND FINAL READING OF BYLAW No. 2015-07 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Woodbank Primary School, 1984 Woobank Rd, Nanaimo, BC, V9X 1K6; Facility No. 068085; on June 30, 2016, and that it be given third and final reading. RECOMMENDED MOTION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) reconfigure North Cedar Intermediate as a K-7 grade school effective September 2016, and that students from Woodbank attend North Cedar Intermediate school in September 2016, and that effective September 2016 North Cedar Intermediate be known as Cedar Elementary.
