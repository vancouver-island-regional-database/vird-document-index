Motion SC23/02/15-02 C22/12/14-05 C22/12/14-06 C22/09/28-03 Comments Assigned Dale Burgos That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) refer the correspondence from the NDTA regarding PVP messaging to staff January 30, 2023, to the Board Chair for response. That the Board of Education of School District No. Shawn Johnston 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) recommend that the District explore the means of moving the remaining four (4) hul’q’umi’num language teachers that are paid at level four (4) to level five (5) in recognition of the value of their commitment to and success in studying and mastering the hul’q’umi’num language. That the Board of Education of School District No. Shawn Johnston 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) recommend that the District continue to pursue change with the TQS through the Ministry and BCPSEA, to recognize Indigenous language teachers across the province in an equitable manner, and pay them as such. That the Board of Education of School District No. Scott Saywell 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct the Superintendent to work together with Kw’umut Lelum to create a draft Protocol Agreement that formalizes the working relationship between the parties to support vulnerable youth including children in care subject to final Board approval.
