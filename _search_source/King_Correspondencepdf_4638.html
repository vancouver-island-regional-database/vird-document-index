1 To the board of trustees SD#68 Nanaimo-Ladysmith, Feb. 21, 2013 Cc DPAC SD#68 Nanaimo-Ladysmith. Following are the results of a survey conducted for your privy and consideration regarding the upcoming decision of additional closure days and later start time for Wednesdays. In this survey is more information I hope you will find useful and consider when seeking public consultation, especially in regards to the parents of students and their lack of understanding in engaging in the consultation process. There are 10 questions in total, with responses from 100 respondents. Pages submitted 15 in total. If someone on the board would like to contact me about accessing the results directly online please contact me at 758-5464. Sincerely, Jennifer C. King Nanaimo, BC Parent of 2 children attending public school in our district. Active parent volunteer SD#68 Past Vice-Chair DPAC SD#68 Parent Information Resource Co-ordinator SD68 2 1.
