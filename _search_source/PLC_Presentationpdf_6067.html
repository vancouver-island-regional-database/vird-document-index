Collective teacher efficacy deserves the attention of every educator because it was recently ranked as the #1 factor influencing student achievement. -Hattie, 2016 The ultimate goal of a Professional Learning Community (PLC) is to improve student achievement. Key Components of a PLC: What’s going on for our learners? What are we going to do about it? How do we know we have made enough of a difference? What’s next? “Innovation floats on a sea of inquiry and curiosity is a driver for change.” (Timperley, Halbert & Kaser, 2014)
