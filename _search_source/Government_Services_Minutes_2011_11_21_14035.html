TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE GOVERNMENT SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, NOVEMBER 21, 2011 – 6:00 P.M. LADYSMITH COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Steve Arnett, Chair Councillor Lori Evans Councillor Bruce Whittington STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Felicity Adams Councillor Scott Bastian Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Duck Paterson Sandy Bowden Joanna Winter Erin Anderson CALL TO ORDER Councillor Arnett called the meeting to order at 6:04 p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL Councillor Arnett requested the committee’s consideration of the following additions to the agenda: 7.1 2011-144 Cell phone tower behind Travellers Inn It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda for the Government Services Committee of November 21, 2011 be adopted as amended. It was agreed that the matter of a possible roller hockey rink be referred to the Parks, Recreation and Culture Commission. MINUTES 2011-145 DELEGATIONS It was moved, seconded and carried that the minutes of the Government Services Committee meeting held October 17, 2011 be adopted as circulated. Cara Light – Hayes, Stewart, Little and Company (Town Auditors) 2011 Audit Plan for the Town of Ladysmith C. Light presented the proposed audit plan for the 2011 financial statements for Council’s consideration. Council thanked Ms Light for the information. STAFF REPORTS 2011-146 Financial Plan Update It was moved, seconded and carried that the Financial Plan Update Government Services Committee November 21, 2011 Page 1 to the end of October 2011 be accepted. 2011-147 Building Inspector’s Report for October 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Building Inspector’s Report for October 2011 be accepted. 2011-148 Trolley Report for October 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Trolley Report for October 2011 be received. 2011-149 Fire Chief’s Report for October 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Fire Chief’s Report for October 2011 be accepted. 2011-150 Animal Control / Pound Report for October 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Animal Control / Pound Report for October 2011 be accepted. NEW BUSINESS Cellular Communication Tower Council was advised that the new cellular tower behind the Travellers Inn has been installed temporarily to assist with communication during Light-Up. The City Manager was requested to ensure that the truck is parked on private property. ADJOURNMENT 2011-151 It was moved, seconded and carried that this meeting of the Government Services Committee be adjourned at 6:22 p.m. CERTIFIED CORRECT Chair (Councillor S.
