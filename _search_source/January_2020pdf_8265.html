Ney elementary schools, respectively, as they were honoured at the recent WE Day event in Vancouver. Five B.C. educators received the I Teach WE Award at the educational partner’s breakfast the morning of the WE Day event. Last school year, McGonigle and Poulin organized the WE Walk for Water initiative in which students carried milk jugs filled with water to represent the pails of water that global citizens and our Indigenous people carry from clean water sources into their homes. 10. QUESTION PERIOD There was one question this evening from Jeremy Inscho: Does the scope of the calendar committee referenced in Board Motion R19/12/18-09 include financial considerations and impacts in any way similar to the 2009 management committee that recommended a 5 day school closure to be attached to Spring Break as to save over $500,000?
