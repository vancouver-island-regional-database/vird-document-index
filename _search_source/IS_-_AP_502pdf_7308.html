BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: June 24, 2020 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Administrative Procedure 502 – Naming of School Facilities Background: As the Board is aware, many of our Administrative Procedures require updating. We are now recommending the review of Administrative Procedure 502 – Naming of School Facilities (AP 502) The original AP 502 was adopted in 1989. Information: Given the importance to the community of the naming and/or renaming of sites, staff will work on updating AP 502 and will bring the updated AP to the Board in fall 2020.
