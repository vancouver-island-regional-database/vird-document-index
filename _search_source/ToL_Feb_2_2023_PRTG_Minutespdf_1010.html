Minutes of the Poverty Reduction Task Group February 2, 2023, 10:30am Hybrid – Ladysmith Chamber of Commerce Boardroom & Zoom COMMITTEE MEMBERS PRESENT: Jacqueline Neligan Anne Marie Tallon Millie Sterling Carmen Hildebrand Cindy Lise Sandra Thomson Rosalie Sawrie STAFF/COUNCIL PRESENT: Sue Glenn Councillor Tricia McKay REGRETS: Gerry Busch Allison Blank CALL TO ORDER AND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT The facilitator acknowledged with gratitude that this meeting was taking place on the traditional, unceded territory of the Stz'uminus First Nation. AGENDA That the Poverty Reduction Task Group approve the agenda for the meeting as presented. Motion carried. MINUTES That the Poverty Reduction Task Group approve the Minutes from January 10, 2023 as presented. Motion carried. CURRENT BUSINESS Welcome & Introductions - Members of the PRTG each introduced themselves to the group. Project Updates: - Task Group Membership: o Announcement – Lydia Neubauer from Island Health is no longer available to participate in the PRTG. The task group will seek a new member from Island Health, potentially the member who is currently participating on the Food Equity Sub-Committee. - Cultural Workshops: o Cultural Connections workshops dates confirmed for the Stz’uminus Community Centre – March 9th full day workshop 9am – 3:30pm, March 30th half day follow-up workshop from 10am -1pm with lunch following. This will be for reflections on what was experienced during the first workshop and discussion of local actions to take. o Discussion around promotions with specific groups, businesses, organizations, etc. to ensure broad attendance for long-term local impacts and relationship building. Minutes to the Poverty Reduction Task Group Meeting February 2, 2023 Page 2 - - - - After-School Nutritious Snack Pilot: o The project is in progress with the four organizations moving ahead in their own ways based on their clients needs. o Discussion took place around how to promote the project in the community to continue after funding is depleted. Gleaning/Food Recovery Pilot: o SPC to send job description to LRCA for hiring a Project Coordinator. The two organizations will meet to discuss planning for promotions in time for the Neighbours Sharing Food Fair Event. Film & Dialogue Events: o Understanding Poverty, taking place on Wednesday, February 8, 2023 and Understanding Food Equity happening Wednesday, March 1, 2023 both with dinner served at 5:30pm and the film beginning at 6:30pm. o Suggested location at Stz’uminus for the March film night, will explore this possibility. o Planning of the event agenda and details took place along with discussion of what information and educational resources to share. UBCM Grant: o Staff to follow up on extension request and possibility of applying for a second round of stream 2 funds. Wrap up & Next steps - Summary of event dates: o Understanding Poverty – Community Dinner, Films & Discussion: Wednesday, February 8, 2023, dinner 5:30pm with film viewings at 6:30pm, LSS multi-purpose room o Understanding Local Food Equity - Community Dinner, Film & Discussion: Wednesday, March 1, 2023, dinner 5:30pm with film viewing(s) at 6:30pm, LSS multi-purpose room o Journey of our Generation Workshop (Part 1): Thursday, March 9, 2023 – 9am – 4pm, Stz’uminus Community Centre o Neighbours Sharing Food Event: Saturday, March 25, 2023 – 11am to 2pm, LSS Gym/Multi-purpose room o Journey of our Generation Follow Up Reflection & Discussion Workshop: Thursday, March 30, 2023 – 10am – 2pm, Stz’uminus Community Centre - The Food Security Sub-committee will continue to meet to plan details for Neighbours Sharing Food Event and plan next steps with promoting the After-School Nutritious Snack Pilot Minutes to the Poverty Reduction Task Group Meeting February 2, 2023 Page 3 - Continue to develop an evaluation framework for each action of the grant. SPC to continue to work with the Town of Ladysmith staff on project communications and promotion. Meeting adjourned at 11:28am Next Meeting scheduled for March 7, 2022 at 10:30am.
