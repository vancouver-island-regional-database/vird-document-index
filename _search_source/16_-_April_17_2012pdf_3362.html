APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Bard Folio S12/04/17-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS - BUDGET 2012-2013 Dereck DeGear, President, NDTA Derek DeGear, president of the Nanaimo District Teachers Association, said the district’s budget issues are a result of inadequate funding from the provincial government. Mr. DeGear said that while there are no budget cuts required this year, the district faces cuts in future years and he urged trustees to refuse to make further reductions. Ron Farino, President, CUPE Local 606 Ron Farino, president of the Canadian Union of Public Employees, Local 606, said that CUPE support staff are proud of the role that they play in supporting students as they move from Kindergarten to Grade 12, and he urged trustees to consider the impact that an extended spring break would have on students and CUPE Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held April 17, 2012 2 members. Chair Brennan said the Board will hold a special meeting on Wednesday evening to consider the proposal for a Local School Calendar in 2012-13 with an extended spring break.
