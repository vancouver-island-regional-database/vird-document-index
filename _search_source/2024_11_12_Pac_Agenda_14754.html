Public Art Committee Tuesday, November 12 at 4:30pm Frank Jameson Community Centre at 810 6th Avenue The purpose of the Public Art Committee is to assist the Town with providing advice and recommendations to Council on specific Public Art projects, including selection processes, acceptance of grants, gifts, donations, bequests and deaccession as referred by Council. The Public Art Committee has two functions: (a) To advise the Town on public art issues and trends relevant to public art initiative in Town. (b) To advise on specific issues, such as donations, deaccession, and commissions of work. 1.
