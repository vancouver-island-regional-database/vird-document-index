PRESENTATIONS Ms. Wendy DeGroot and the Kindergarten Teachers' Group RE: Resources and the Kindergarten Classroom On behalf of a district Kindergarten teachers' group, Wendy Degroot shared the many financial challenges a Kindergarten teacher faces in the classroom. The group requested Board financial support to aid in the purchase of materials such as; sand, water, building, dramatic play, books, music, puzzles, games and outdoor and science centres for kindergarten classrooms.
