Attachment A Parks & Rec Facility Maintenance 2026 Description Purpose Consquence of not funding FJCC Main Roof Replacement In 2023, roof assessments were conducted for all TOL facilities. During these assessments, it was identified that the main pool roof at FJCC was in poor condition, with a recommendation for replacement within the next 3 to 4 years. The main roof has reached the end of its serviceable life and requires full replacement. The roof is at risk of failure, with the potential for water leaks that could lead to a facility shutdown. This would result in service disruptions and potential revenue loss.
