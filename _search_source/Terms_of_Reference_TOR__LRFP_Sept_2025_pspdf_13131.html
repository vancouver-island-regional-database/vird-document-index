Appendix B Committee Name Board Advisory Committee Terms of Reference Long Range Facilities Plan Advisory Committee Type Board Advisory Committee Purpose To provide recommendations to the Board with respect to the implementation, resourcing and updates to the Long Range Facilities Plan (the “Plan”). Basis of Committee Board Motion Mandate • • • • • To provide recommendations to the Board with respect to which Plan goals should be prioritized in a given school year To provide the Board recommendations on resource allocation to assist in preliminary budget deliberations and potential expenditures associated with the annual surplus (if applicable) to support the Plan Receive updates from staff on the yearly progress of the Plan Review the Plan and provide recommendations with respect to any changes that may be required Recommending major and minor capital program submissions to the Board. Authority The Committee provides information and makes recommendations to the Board to aid the Board’s Long Range Facilities Plan. The Committee will seek to achieve consensus-based recommendations. Membership The Committee will be chaired by one of the Trustees appointed to the committee. The committee is comprised of the following members: • Trustees (2) • Superintendent • Secretary-Treasurer • Two members of District Leadership Team – from Learning Services and one from Facilities • A principal or vice-principal CREATED: SEPTEMBER 16, 2025 1 • • • • • Board Advisory Committee Terms of Reference One knowledge keeper A representative from NDTA A representative from CUPE A representative from DPAC Two students appointed by the student voice committee The Committee may also invite district staff, community representatives, and subject matter experts as needed. Meeting Arrangements There shall be four meetings a year as follows: 1. October 2. December 3. February 4. May Discuss areas of the Plan that should be prioritized in the given school year • Staff updates (on projects of interest). • Review/Revise annual work plan. Discuss resource priorities for the upcoming preliminary budget and to recommend to the Board potential allocations associated with annual surplus (if applicable) Staff shall report progress to the Committee • Review Proposed Annual AFG plan (for commencement in April). Review and discuss the yearly reporting of progress to the Board and to review the Terms of Reference. • Review of Capital Plan support letter (received in March). • Review proposed Major and Minor capital plan (due end of June and end of September). Additional meetings may be scheduled, as needed, to ensure the committee meets to fulfill their mandate. Reporting The Committee will report to the Board in January and May (or June). Review The terms of reference of the committee will be reviewed annually by committee, and any recommended changes will be brought to the Board for approval. CREATED: SEPTEMBER 16, 2025 2
