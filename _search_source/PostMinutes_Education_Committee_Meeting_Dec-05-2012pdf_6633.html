CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS McGirr Elementary Students Students from the Grades 4 and 7 classes at McGirr Elementary told the committee about their project “Everybody Deserves a Smile” initiative. The students, with the help of donations from the community, have filled decorated bags with warm socks, gloves, scarves and toques, decorated cookies and a Christmas greeting. Students will deliver the bags in person to people at the New Hope Centre next week. Ladysmith Secondary Students Students from the Ladysmith Secondary improv group entertained the committee with two improvisations – theatre made up in the moment – both with Christmas themes. The performance showcased the students’ innovation and imagination as they created stories on the spot based on suggestions from the audience. Chair Howland told the students how much they appreciated the presentations, which illustrated the many great things happening in schools.
