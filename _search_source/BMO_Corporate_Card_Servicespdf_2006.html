STAFF REPORT TO COUNCIL Report Prepared By: Meeting Date: File No: Re: Financial Services Department October 7, 2025 1690-20-25 BMO Corporate Credit Card – Credit Limit RECOMMENDATION: That Council award RFP 2025-FIN-02 to BMO and authorize the establishment of a monthly credit limit of $30,000 with BMO. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: Staff issued a Request for Proposal for the provision of a purchasing card program. Two bids were received; one from the existing provider which is US Bank, and another from BMO. Staff are recommending switching to BMO based on cost as well as the fact that they are the selected Provincial Shared Services Commercial Card Service provider. PREVIOUS COUNCIL DIRECTION: N/A INTRODUCTION/BACKGROUND: The Town currently uses US Bank, which is affiliated with CIBC, for corporate purchasing card transactions. In keeping with the purchasing policy, staff issued a request for proposal for a purchasing card (p-card) program to ensure the Town was still receiving the best value for money. Two bids were received: US Bank and BMO. After evaluating the bids, staff are recommending switching to BMO. The BMO option is based on a shareservices agreement that was made with the Province, and BMO and allows the Town to partake in a rebate program which is not currently available with US Bank. The anticipated rebate amount is approximately 1% of spending. ALTERNATIVES: Council can choose to: 1. Keep with the existing provider of US Bank. 2. Reduce the monthly credit limit, though some operations may be impacted. FINANCIAL IMPLICATIONS: Apart from the rebate, there are minimal financial impacts for switching p-card providers. All purchase card transactions must adhere to the Town’s purchasing policy. LEGAL IMPLICATIONS: N/A CITIZEN/PUBLIC RELATIONS IMPLICATIONS: N/A INTERDEPARTMENTAL INVOLVEMENT/IMPLICATIONS: Should Council approve the switch to BMO, new purchasing cards will be issued to the approved employees, and the transition will take place over a few months when time permits. Allison McCarrick, Chief Administrative Officer
