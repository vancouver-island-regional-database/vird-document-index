Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: September 14, 2016 TO: Business Committee FROM: A/Secretary-Treasurer, Carrie McVeigh SUBJECT: Boardroom Layout Update Background: In June 2016, the Board passed two motions with respect to Board meeting location and room setup. The motions are listed below for reference. Following discussions with staff, it was agreed to continue holding Board and Committee meetings in the DAC Boardroom, as NDSS meeting rooms are booked for other use. Staff obtained optional design layouts for the room set-up to address concerns expressed by the Board that the current layout is not optimal for productive meetings. It was determined that the layout selected would be set-up on a “trial basis” to ensure the layout works effectively before equipment including mics and cameras are permanently re-installed. Therefore, any wiring required for the “trial meeting” will be temporary until a decision is made by the Board for a permanent room set-up/layout. Once a decision is finalized, carpet, paint and any other necessary room upgrades/changes will be made.
