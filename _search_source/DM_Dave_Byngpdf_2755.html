December 16, 2016 Ref: 191741 Steve Rae, Chair Board of Education School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Email: steve.rae@sd68.bc.ca Dear Mr. Rae: Thank you for your letter of October 21, 2016, regarding the Ministry of Education’s support for the students from the Nanaimo-Ladysmith School District in the aftermath of the Bastille Day terrorist attack in Nice, France. Your letter outlined the exceptional work of some of our team, and as such, I was pleased to recognize Jennifer McCrea, Sherri Mohoruk and Jason Keenan with a letter of commendation for the role each of them played during that difficult time. I would like to take this opportunity to sincerely thank you for your efforts as well. Importantly, you made the safety and security of the students and chaperones your top priority. Again, thank you for your thoughtful letter, and good luck with your ongoing efforts to support the students affected by this terrible incident. Sincerely, Dave Byng Deputy Minister Ministry of Education Office of the Deputy Minister Mailing address: PO Box 9179 Stn Prov Govt Victoria BC V8W 9H8 Telephone: (250) 387-2026 Facsimile: (250) 356-6007
