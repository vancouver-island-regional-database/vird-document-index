April 9, 2026 Via email: Gillian.Robinson@sd68.bc.ca 150122 Naomi Bailey Board Chair Board of Education, Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools Dear Naomi Bailey: The Honourable Adrian Dix, Minister of Energy and Climate Solutions, has asked me to respond to your letter of March 18, 2026, regarding Nanaimo Ladysmith Public School's (NLPS) support for the Province’s goal of lowering climate-changing emissions by 2030. Thank you for sharing the strides your organization has made in reducing its greenhouse gas emissions. It is exciting to hear that NLPS has been making strong progress towards meeting CleanBC goals by 2030. As a Public Sector Organization (PSO), NLPS participates in the Carbon Neutral Government (CNG) Program each year. The Clean Government team who administers the CNG program will book a meeting with Secretary-Treasurer Mark Walsh and others from your organization to hear more about your climate plan and progress. Additionally, if your team is interested in presenting its work, the Clean Government team would be pleased to work with NLPS to share this success story with other PSOs. Thank you for your letter.
