THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF ANNUAL BOARD MEETING HELD DECEMBER 5, 2012 Trustees D. Brennan, in the Chair D. Allen B. Bard N. Curley K. Howland D. Neary B. Robinson T. Saunders S. Welch Staff D. Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO P. Turin, Secretary-Treasurer J. Blain, Deputy Superintendent D. Reimer, Director of Communications C. Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No. A6 Call to Order Secretary-Treasurer Turin called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Election of Board Chair Secretary-Treasurer Turin said he will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board and will confirm that each Trustee nominated confirms their acceptance of the nomination by asking. Do you accept? Mr. Turin said that in the event there was an election, Mr. John Blain and Ms. Donna Reimer would act as scrutineers. Trustee Robinson nominated Trustee Brennan for the position of Chair. Trustee Howland nominated Trustee Neary for the position of Chair. Mr. Turin asked Trustee Brennan if he accepted the nomination. Trustee Brennan indicated he did. Mr. Turin asked Trustee Neary if she accepted the nomination. Trustee Neary indicated she did. Mr. Turin said there would be an election and asked Ms. Kelt to distribute ballots to Trustees. Mr. Blain passed the ballot box amongst the Trustees. Mr. Blain and Ms. Reimer left the room to count the ballots. Mr. Blain advised Mr. Turin Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 5, 2012 2 of the outcome of the election. Mr. Turin declared Trustee Brennan as Board Chair. Mr. Turin vacated the Chair and passed the gavel to Chair Brennan. Chair Brennan asked for a motion to destroy the ballots. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio A12/12/05-01 Election of Vice-Chair That the ballots be destroyed. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Chair Brennan said he will call for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Saunders nominated Trustee Neary. Chair Brennan called for nominations a second time. Chair Brennan called for nomination a third time. Hearing none, he asked Trustee Neary if she would accept the nomination for Vice-Chair. Trustee Neary indicated she did. Chair Brennan declared Trustee Neary Vice-Chairperson. Election of Business Committee Chair Trustee Brennan called for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Business Committee. Trustee Saunders nominated Trustee Neary. Chair Brennan called for nominations a second time. Chair Brennan called for nomination a third time. Hearing none, he asked Trustee Neary if she would accept the nomination for Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Neary indicated she did. Chair Brennan declared Trustee Neary Chairperson of the Business Committee. Election of Education Committee Chair Trustee Brennan called for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Education Committee. Trustee Robinson nominated Trustee Howland. Chair Brennan called for nominations a second time. Chair Brennan called for nomination a third time. Hearing none, he asked Trustee Howland if Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 5, 2012 3 she would accept the nomination for Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Howland indicated she did. Chair Brennan declared Trustee Howland Chairperson of the Education Committee. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Representative Trustee Brennan called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Howland nominated Trustee Saunders. Chair Brennan called for nominations a second time. Chair Brennan called for nomination a third time. Hearing none, he asked Trustee Neary if she would accept the nomination for BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Saunders indicated she did. Chair Brennan declared Trustee Saunders BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Alternate Representative Trustee Brennan called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor (Alternate). Trustee Bard nominated Trustee Robinson. Chair Brennan called for nominations a second time. Chair Brennan called for nomination a third time. Hearing none, he asked Trustee Robinson if he would accept the nomination for BCSTA Provincial Councillor (Alternate). Trustee Robinson indicated he did. Chair Brennan declared Trustee Robinson BCSTA Provincial Councillor (Alternate). ADJOURNMENT IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Saunders Folio A12/12/05-02 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:07 pm. Jamie Brennan, Board Chair Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer
