Introduction The Town of Ladysmith is seeking proposals for the removal, disposal and replacement of activated carbon filters for odour control at the wastewater treatment plant. Although a site meeting is not mandatory, the proponent may request to have a site visit. For further details and to arrange a site visit please contact the Manager of Operations. 2. Required Project Deliverables To remove, replace and dispose of approximately 19.2m3 of activated carbon from three locations at the Town’s wastewater treatment plant. • • • Headwork’s 4.27m3 Centrifuge Bin Room (Dewatering) 4.17m3 MBBR / Sludge Tanks 10.76m3 3. Response Content All respondents should include the following information in their proposal: • • • • • • Name of the product (activated carbon) complete with information regarding the product’s excellent H2S absorption capability (removal of odour and H2S from air) Life expectancy of product, warranty Methodology regarding removal, disposal and replacement of new carbon Permit requirements, safety requirements, environmental requirements Disposal site (location) All costs associated with the proponent being 100% responsible for removal of activated carbon, disposal, replacement, permitting and all other associated costs. As part of the submission review process, proponents may be required to present their proposal and approach to the Town staff. Proposals will be reviewed and evaluated by a committee comprised of Town staff. During the evaluation process, any or all of the proponents may be asked for clarification by telephone or email. 4.
