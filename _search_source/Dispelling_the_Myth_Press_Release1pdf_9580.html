News Release Dispelling the Myth of a ‘Pandemic of the Unvaccinated’ February 4, 2022 (MONCTON, NB) – The presentation Dispelling the Myth of a ‘Pandemic of the Unvaccinated’ by the Canadian Covid Care Alliance explains how Ontario’s own COVID-19 data demonstrates that the COVID-19 vaccines and related mandates and passports are ineffective at controlling COVID-19 case growth and eliminating COVID-19 hospitalizations. The data also clearly shows that the COVID-19 pandemic is effectively over. Our recent analysis of Ontario’s own COVID-19 data definitively shows that: ● The current COVID-19 inoculations, including boosters, failed to control COVID-19 case growth; ● The majority of COVID-19 admissions to hospital are in the vaccinated and not the unvaccinated; and, ● The pandemic has been over since March 2021 when COVID-19 deaths all but disappeared. Government has unethically engaged in medical propaganda and censorship for more than two years and has orchestrated the systematic medical coercion of Canadians by requiring them to take a vaccine, for which the long-term safety and efficacy has yet to be proven, in order to move freely and maintain their livelihoods. Now Ontario’s own data importantly shows that: ● COVID-19 no longer qualifies as a pandemic as there is evidence of population level immunity; additionally, the disease is no longer lethal to the large majority of Ontarians; ● Omicron is highly infectious and not prevented by current vaccines. There is therefore little to no benefit to using these injections to “vaccinate” children or boost adults; ● The unvaccinated are not driving infection and it is time to end all discriminatory policies including vaccine mandates and passports; ● It is time to end the exclusive focus on vaccines and broaden our management approach to COVID-19 by acknowledging natural immunity and focusing on early treatment; and, ● It is time to uncouple health policy from politics and allow independent emergency management professionals to implement a plan that leads all aspects of society back to normal life. The most important element in a free, open and democratic society is the preservation of the rights and freedoms of every individual. The Charter of Rights and Freedoms was written to protect Canadians from all forms of government overreach, including well-intentioned overreach. The rights to informed consent, bodily autonomy, free speech, freedom of movement, and freedom to earn a livelihood are non-negotiable and must be defended at all times. It is time to uncouple health policy from politics and follow the scientific evidence. The time for the cessation of all COVID-19 related restrictions, mandates and passports has come. The Canadian Covid Care Alliance represents over 500 independent doctors, scientists, and health care practitioners committed to providing quality, balanced, evidence-based information to the Canadian public about COVID-19 so that hospitalizations can be reduced, lives saved and our country safely restored to normal as quickly as possible.
