NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: June 24, 2020 The Board of Education Secretary-Treasurer, Mark Walsh Inclusion Policy 2.10 and Administrative Procedure 350 – Honouring Diversity and Challenging Racism Recommendation That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) refer Inclusion Policy 2.10 to the Policy Advisory Committee for review and revision and to provide any recommended changes to the Board for consideration in the fall of 2020. Background Inclusion Policy 2.10 Inclusion Policy 2.10 (the “Policy”) requires that it is monitored on an annual basis. Given the Policy Advisory Committee is relatively new, 2020 is the first time the Policy Advisory Committee will be able to review the Policy. Further, this is the first opportunity to review the Policy in the lens of the strategic plan. Specifically, the Board is committed to providing a: Safe, caring and healthy learning and working environment that is inclusive of the diversity of our entire learning community Finally, the Board is committed to addressing racism in our District, including from an individual and systemic basis. The Policy Advisory Committee is well placed to ensure that the Policy reflects these goals and recommends changes, and the appropriate consultation process to the Board to ensure the Policy is updated in a manner that meets the Board’s objectives and the expectations of our staff, students and broader community. The recommended motion will prioritize the review of the Policy so it can be implemented early in the 2020-21 school year. Action Sheet: Inclusion Policy 2.10 & Administrative Procedure 350 Page 2 Administrative Procedure 350 – Honouring Diversity and Challenging Racism Given there will likely be revisions to the Policy, there may be required revisions to Administrative Procedure 350 – Honouring Diversity and Challenging Racism (AP 350). AP 350 is also a very important resource for our staff, students and community to ensure the Board’s commitment to challenging racism. Therefore, it should be reviewed and updated often by staff. Staff will review AP 350 for currency, as well as consult with our District partners, including the Safe Schools Committee, to make changes to ensure it is effective. This review and update will coincide with the review of the Policy. Appendices Appendix A: Inclusion Policy 2.10 Appendix B: Administrative Procedure 350 – Honouring Diversity and Challenging Racism
