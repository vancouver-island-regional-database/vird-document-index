THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: May 13, 2015 TO: Business Committee FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations SUBJECT: Cedar to Ladysmith Bus Route Implementation Plan The Board of Education passed the following motion on April 22, 2015: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) provide busing for one year (2015-16) and that staff develop the required next steps as outlined in their presentation. For information, the next steps are provided by staff with respect to the above motion. April 2015 On April 27, staff discussed the process for identification and registration of students. It was determined the Learning Services department will work with John Barsby Secondary School (JBS) and Ladysmith Secondary School (LSS) regarding students registered from Cedar. This should also confirm any students changing schools. It was agreed that a deadline for registration and criteria for ridership be established.    The student must be registered at LSS. The student must live in Woodbank/North Cedar Intermediate or Cinnabar catchment areas. The student must register for the service. Communicate to students who will attend LSS and confirm those who wish to register for the bus service. The Transportation department will work with the Finance department on required 2015/16 budget items. The Transportation department to consult with LSS on bus routes.
