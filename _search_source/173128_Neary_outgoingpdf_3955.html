April 8, 2014 Ref: 173128 Dot Neary, Chair Board of Education School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 395 Wakesiah Ave Nanaimo BC V9R 3K6 Email: dneary@sd68.bc.ca Dear Dot Neary: Re: Wellington Secondary School (Project No. 115385) I am responding to Phil Turin’s letter of February 26, 2014, addressed to Phillip Chambers, concerning increased project costs as a result of a pre-tender review for Wellington Secondary School. A Project Agreement was approved for this project in February, 2013, in the amount of $22,562,718. Also included was a risk reserve of $1,933,923 to cover potential increases in costs for identified risks and economic adjustments. As a result of a pre-tender budget review, I understand the economic market adjustment has increased construction costs by $573,227 and the tax adjustment is $33,844 for a total project increase of $607,071. I also understand you are requesting access to Restricted Capital funds to cover these increased costs. This letter is your approval to increase the project budget from $22,562,718 to $23,169,789, an increase of $607,071, and to access Restricted Capital funds to cover this increase. An amended budget (Schedule B) that documents these changes is enclosed. I wish the School District every success with the upcoming tendering and construction of the Wellington Secondary School project, and look forward to its completion. Yours sincerely, Peter Fassbender Minister Enclosure pc: Phillip Chambers, Regional Director, Capital Management Branch Kathleen Kane, Planning Officer, Capital Management Branch Ministry of Education Office of the Minister Mailing Address: PO Box 9045 Stn Prov Govt Victoria BC V8W 9E2 Location: Parliament Buildings Victoria
