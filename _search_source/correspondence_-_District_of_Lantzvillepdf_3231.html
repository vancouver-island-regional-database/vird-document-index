From: Jack de Jong [Jackdejong@shaw.ca] Sent: January-21-13 12:55 PM To: Sharon Welch Cc: Brian Dempsey; Graham Savage; Jennifer Millbank; Denise Haime; Andrew Mostad; Councillor Bratkowski Subject: Meeting with Lantzville Council To Ms. Sharon Welch Trustee Board of Education S.D. 68 From Jack de Jong Dear Ms. Welch. I had earlier send you a note requesting a meeting and susupect it must have been lost in cyberspace. I note from the school families allocation that you have been assigned the Seaview Elementary School (Principal Patrick Young) as representative. As you are aware Lantzville District is only a small community with a population of 3600 and the Seaview school is an anchor for our young families. In addition our neighbour the Nanoose first nation also have a significant young population with strong interest in our local school. Our secondary students attend a variety of schools in Nanaimo, including French immersion. Recently that the board indicated a strong desire to work with local governments in the area of: early childhood education, transportation, exchange of information etc. In the broad spectrum of education You priorities are ours. With this in mind I would like to invite you and other board members to meet with Lantzville Council and staff at your convenience sometimes in the new year. Jack de Jong Mayor Lantzville
