TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE MUNICIPAL SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, JUNE 16, 2014 5:30 P.M. COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Bill Drysdale (Chair) Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Duck Paterson Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Glenda Patterson (entered later) COUNCIL MEMBERS ABSENT Councillor Steve Arnett Councillor Gord Horth STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Erin Anderson CALL TO ORDER AGENDA APPROVAL MS 2014-031 MINUTES MS 2014-032 DELEGATION Joanna Winter Councillor Drysdale called the Municipal Services Committee meeting to order at 5:32 p.m. Moved and seconded: That the agenda for this meeting of the Municipal Services Committee be . Motion carried. Moved and seconded: That the minutes of the Municipal Services Committee meeting held March 17, 2014 be approved. Motion carried Gretchen Hartley, MaryAnn Deacon, Cowichan Valley Hospice Society G. Hartley and M. Deacon spoke to Council about the services that Cowichan Valley Hospice Society provides through the Cowichan Region, including to 67 individuals in Ladysmith in 2013, and requested council’s support for a Cowichan Valley Regional District Bylaw that would provide ongoing funding to the Society. Council expressed gratitude to the organization on the excellent work it does for patients and their families. REPORTS MS 2014-033 City Manager’s Report Moved and seconded: That the City Manager’s Report for the months of March, April and Municipal Services Committee June 16, 2014 Page 1 May 2014 be received. Motion carried. MS 2014-036 Fire Chief’s Report – March, April and May 2014 Moved and seconded: That the Fire Chief’s Reports for the months of March, April and May 2014 be received. Motion carried. The Committee requested that the Protective Services Committee consider the potential cost savings of reducing the involvement of Ladysmith Fire/Rescue in accident traffic control and report back with any recommendations. MS 2014-037 ADJOURNMENT MS 2014-038 Coastal Animal Control Services – March, April and May 2014 Pound Reports Moved and seconded: That the Coastal Animal Control Services Pound Report for the months of March, April and May 2014 be received. Motion carried. The Committee requested that they be provided with the number of dog licences issued for 2014 in Ladysmith.
