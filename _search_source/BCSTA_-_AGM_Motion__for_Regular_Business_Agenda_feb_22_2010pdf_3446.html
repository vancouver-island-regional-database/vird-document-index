Substantive Motion to 2012 BCSTA AGM Deadline for submission: February 24, 2012 MOTION TITLE: REQUIRE CRIMINAL RECORD CHECK FOR BOARD OF EDUCATION TRUSTEES SUBMITTED BY: BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMOLADYSMITH) BE IT RESOLVED: That BCSTA urge the Ministry of Education and the Provincial Government to amend the School Act and or other relevant legislation to ensure that all School Board Trustees must obtain and submit a criminal record check to the district upon being elected to local school boards. RATIONALE: Recently media has brought to our attention that there is a gap in legislation in regards to the requirement of criminal record checks for elected trustees. Employees within school districts are required to have criminal record checks, as they have access to children within our school community. As elected trustees in our school districts we have open access to the schools and the children within these communities. We are invited to take part in the events, and visit classrooms and schools. This helps us to stay connected and informed to what is truly happening in our schools, our classrooms and with students. To support student safety and equity in regards to the requirement of Criminal Record checks within our education system, government needs to amend the school act and any other legislative portions that might be required to ensure that we are supporting a safe environment for all students under our supervision.
