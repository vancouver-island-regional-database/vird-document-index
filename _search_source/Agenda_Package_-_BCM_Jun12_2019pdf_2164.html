Purpose The development, monitoring and updating of policy is a primary responsibility of the Board of Education. Policies are detailed expression of intent and should reflect the Board’s own Strategic Plan and statements of Mission and Vision. Policies must also be consistent with the School Act, ministerial orders, and other relevant legislation. Scope The scope of the committee pertains to development, review and monitoring of Board Policy and related Board Policy Procedures (Section 1 - Board Governance, 2.7 Policy Development) for recommendations to the Board of Education. Authority The committee has the authority to develop and review Board Policy and related Procedures for recommendation to the Board of Education pursuant to Section 1 – Board Governance 2.7 Policy Development. The committee may strike subgroups to provide expert guidance or input on changes to Board Policy and related procedures. The committee will seek to achieve consensus-based recommendations. When consensus is not achieved, trustees on the committee shall vote on a primary recommendation. Alternative recommendations may also be presented to the Board for their consideration.
