Kelsey Toye Subject: FW: prayer rooms g1:nai..1.com> From: Ken Martinson Subject: prayer rooms Dear SD68 Trustees, It has come to my attention that some schools in New Brunswick are providing "prayer rooms" to some students. I Want you to know that I believe in separation of church and state, and that taxpayer money should not be spent providing religious support in a school. Let's keep our schools non-segregated. Best Regards, Ken Martinson
