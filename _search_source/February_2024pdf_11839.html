SENIOR STAFF REPORTS 8.1 Laura Tait, Acting Superintendent Department of Learning Services presentation on the Board Goal: Safe, caring healthy learning and working environment that is inclusive of the diversity of our entire learning community. • McCreary Report • CHEQ Kindergarten parent/caregiver Survey • Presentation on Functional Skills Intervention Laura Tait and members of the Department of Learning Services provided the District's completion rates together with results from the 2023 BC Adolescent Health Survey and the Childhood Experience Questionnaire. Staff explained how the District collects student data at the local level and how that data is shared with parents and guardians. Trina Norgan, District Vice Principal of Inclusive Education presented on Functional Skills Intervention. There are two Information Sheets attached to the agenda. 2 8.2 Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Re: Bill 40 Update Mark Walsh provided a brief update on Bill 40, which makes changes to the School Act that impact a number of areas associated with Indigenous education and the manner in which the District interacts with Rights Holders and community partners. An Information Sheet is attached to the agenda. 8.3 Laura Tait, Acting Superintendent Re: Ministry of Education and Child Care Announcement - Cell Phones Laura Tait provided information and next steps including a recommended motion, with respect to the government of British Columbia's initiatives on technology including the intention of restricting access to cell phones in schools. An action sheet is attached to the Agenda. E24/02/07-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Lee IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Morvay The Education Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) request that pursuant to Policy 2.5 - Board Committees Representation and Annual Work Plan, that it request feedback from the Student Voice Circle on issues associated with cell phone limitations in schools in anticipation of potential Board policy.
