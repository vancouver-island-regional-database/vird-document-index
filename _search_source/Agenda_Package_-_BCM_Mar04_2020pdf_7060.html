Page 41 of 83 Annual Bus Route Review – Bus Service Request Process Bus service requests from stakeholders that have an impact on costs, staff time or service to students are referred to the Annual Bus Route Review Process. This enables staff to consider requests while the entire bus route system is being reviewed. Requests (3) from Parents have been received to provide bus service in the Westwood Lake area to Mountain View School. For various reasons, it is not being recommended to route a bus into this area. Staff have concerns regarding the increased volume of traffic on Jingle Pot Road, between East Wellington Road and the Nanaimo Parkway. Staff are recommending bus route alterations that would see the 2 bus stop locations removed from Jingle Pot Road. The alteration would see a bus stop in the Arbot Road and Westwood Road area. This would improve student safety and partially address the requests from Parents mentioned above as there would be a new bus stop closer to the area of request. Due to there being no impact on bus route costs, staff time or service to students this is not considered part of the Annual Bus Route Review. Plans for the expenditure of the AFG funding were developed considering the following:  AFG criteria  Priority support for the Long Range Facilities Plan Update  Alignment with Ministry Capital Submissions and planning  Alignment with Learning Services priorities  Facilities condition reports  Reported facilities issues  Request from Schools/Departments You will note that an overall program contingency is not apparent on the summary listed below. In accordance with ministry reporting procedures the contingency shown on the detailed project plan is apportioned to the projects listed on the sheet. This also applies to the administration item. The incorporation of an overall contingency allows staff to be flexible with project budgets and allows room for further alignment with other programs as well as room for the addition of emergent projects as they arise. Also note that many of the projects include some design and construction and that the budgets, in some cases are Notional, this would see the project estimate include expected soft costs, design contingency and construction contingency and may not be reflective of the final ‘construction only’ cost.
