Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION REGULAR BOARD MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: June 22, 2016 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Redirected Administrated Savings Background: As announced May 31, 2016, the Ministry of Education has redirected $25 million of administrative reductions into supports for student learning. For Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools, this amounts to $593,079 which was not considered as part of the 2016-17 Budget process. Staff’s plan is to solicit ideas from the partner groups (CUPE, DASG, DPAC, NDTA, and NSAA) to develop recommendations as to how best to spend this allocation to enhance student learning. Further, staff feel it is prudent to not make any recommendations until enrolment is confirmed at the end of September. Therefore, the recommendations will be brought to the October Business Committee meeting for consideration.
