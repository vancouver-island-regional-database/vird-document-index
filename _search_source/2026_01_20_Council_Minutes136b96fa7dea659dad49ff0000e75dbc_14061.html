REQUEST FOR RECONSIDERATION OF REMEDIAL ACTION 6.1 670 Farrell Road Council returned to the remediation reconsideration request for 670 Farrell Road, as the property owner was in attendance. Prior to consideration, the Deputy Mayor outlined the process under section 78(3) of the Community Charter, including time limits, the scope of submissions, and the procedure to be followed by Council. The property owner appeared before Council to request reconsideration of the remediation requirements for the property and provided a presentation in support of the request. Council received the presentation. CS 2026-010 That Council: 1. Having heard from the owner pursuant to section 78(3) of the Community Charter, confirm the Remedial Action Requirement under Council resolution CS 2025-344; 2. Require the owner to complete the remedial actions as specified in Council resolution CS 2025-344 no later than February 23, 2026; and 3. Direct staff to undertake the remedial action outlined in Council resolution CS 2025-344, without further notice, and at the owner’s expense, if the remedial action is not completed by the owner by February 23, 2026. Motion Carried Town of Ladysmith Regular Council Meeting Minutes: January 20, 2026 5 10.
