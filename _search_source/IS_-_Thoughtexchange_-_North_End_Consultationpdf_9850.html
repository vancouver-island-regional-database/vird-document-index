NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: April 13, 2022 Business Committee Dale Burgos, Executive Director Communication, Privacy & Engagement Thought Exchange Feedback – North End Capacity Background Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools’ Long Range Facilities Plan illustrates the need to address capacity issues in its schools as the population in the region continues to grow. The North End of Nanaimo is currently above capacity and the district relies on the use of portables to serve students. On February 23, 2022, the Board of Education directed staff to initiate a consultation process with potentially impacted communities in the North End and report back to the Board with recommendations and next steps for it to consider. While anyone was able to participate, the focus of the consultation was on the anticipated impacted school communities including: • • • • • • • • • • • Cilaire Elementary Departure Bay Elementary Dover Bay Secondary École Hammond Bay Elementary Frank J.
