CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS Kendra-Ann Van Shaik Child, Youth and Family Support Workers RE: "What We Do" The district Child, Youth and Family Support Workers (CYFSW) presented to the Business Committee this evening. Their Powerpoint presentation is attached to the Agenda. CYFSW work in a variety of roles that support students and their families with a mission to get kids to school and keep students in class. CYFSW build relationships with students and their families by establishing coping strategies, integrating selfregulation tools and social skills, and setting short and long term goals. It was shared that CYFSW are trained to work with children, youth and families in matters including: grief and loss; suicide and prevention; mental health; sexual education; gender orientation; child protection; abuse and neglect; substance addictions; eating disorders; family and relational violence; bullying and cyber safety; and threat assessments are among their many duties.
