UNFINISHED BUSINESS Trustee Fund - Trustee(s) Kimler & Solomon The Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) has created a legacy fund with the intention of further enhancing student learning in the district. A grant in the amount of $250-$500 funded directly through voluntary payroll deduction of Board members. The fund will be managed by the Nanaimo-Ladysmith Schools Foundation (NLSF). The NLSF will also manage the selection process and will be funded throughout the year, available to district staff and students. Busing / Z-Pass Update - Mr. D. Prevost Mr. Sabo and Prevost provided the Board with and Information Sheet update, which is attached to the Agenda. NEW BUSINESS Enrolment Update - Mr. G. Roberts Secretary-Treasurer, Mr. Roberts, provided a preliminary enrolment analysis for schools and programs in the district.  The preliminary elementary enrolment for 2015 is 7,840 students. This is an increase of 98 students over projection and an overall increase of 322 students over last year. Draft Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held October 14, 2015    The preliminary secondary enrolment for 2015 is 4,983 students. This is an increase of 54 students over projection and an overall decrease of 164 students compared to last year. The preliminary enrolment for district programs (Continuing Ed, VAST, Learn@Home K-7 and 8-12) for 2015 is 770 students. This is a decrease of 153 under projection and an overall decrease of 292 students compared to last year. According to the preliminary analysis, overall the district is seeing a decrease of one student under projections and 134 less students when compared to 2014.
