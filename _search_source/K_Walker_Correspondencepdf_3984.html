From: Kristine Walker Sent: March-05-14 4:40 PM To: Dot Neary Cc: Dave Street Subject: thanks for the letter! Dear Ms. Neary, I recently read in the Nanaimo Bulletin that SD 68 trustees penned a letter to Minister Fassbender asking that the government not waste more money on appeals to Justice Griffin's recent ruling. As a parent and teacher in this district, I would like to thank the trustees for standing up for public education by taking this courageous move. Most would agree that the money spent on a ruling that has been fought and lost twice in the courts is a huge waste, and that the money could better be spent on helping students in our district. It is not easy to stand up to this government, and your letter and resolve reflect a bold commitment to reminding the government to put the interests of students first. Thank you once again, Kristine Walker Ladysmith Secondary.
