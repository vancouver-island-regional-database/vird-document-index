Stz’uminus Summer Job Opportunity Job Title: Pay: Public Works Laborer (2 positions) $18.25 per hour Contract: Hours: Location: 8 Weeks Term, starting June 29, 2026 Mon to Thurs, 35 hours per week Ladysmith, BC About The Role Reporting to the Public Works Manager, the Public Works Laborer will provide support to the Public Works Department by assisting with maintenance tasks. Responsibilities • Assist with building maintenance tasks as mowing grass, litter pickup, and basic landscaping. • Assist with task related to sewage and water system maintenance such as painting of fire hydrants and cleaning of buildings in both sewer and water and general site duties. • Assist with pothole repairs, boulevard maintenance and clearing. Street sign replacement and cleaning. • Perform other tasks within the Public Works Department as required. Qualifications • Must be between 15 and 30 years of age at the beginning of the employment. • Must have a valid Social Insurance Number at the start of employment. • Must be able to work in outside weather conditions and physical tasks. SFN is committed to respecting diversity within our workforce; preference will be given to individuals who identify as First Nations, Inuit, or Métis.
