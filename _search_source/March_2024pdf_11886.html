B24/03/06-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Keller IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic The Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) continue providing the Before and After School Care Programs at Pleasant Valley and Hammond Bay Elementary and support, in principle, the expansion to an additional 1-3 sites subject to the assurance that the expansion will be on a without prejudice basis with respect to labour relations issues and that additional external funding is made available.
