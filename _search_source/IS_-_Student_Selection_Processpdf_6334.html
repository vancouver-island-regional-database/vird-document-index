Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools EDUCATION COMMITTEE MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: September 9, 2015 TO: Education Committee FROM: John Blain SUBJECT: School Selection Process for Cedar Secondary Background: Staff is working to prepare Cedar Secondary School to open for September, 2016. As the facilities department processes and prepares the building for students next September the Department of Learning Services will begin to prepare for student transition. Reference Motions: 1) Motion to Reopen Cedar Secondary R15/04/22-13 “That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) reopen the Cedar Secondary/Elementary site as a high school with a grade 8 -12 configuration for the start of the 2016 school year.” 2) Motion for bus service to LSS R15/04/22-13 “That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) provide busing for one year (2015-16) to Ladysmith Secondary and that staff develop the required next steps as outlined in their presentation.” Defaulting to policy suggests that there will be NO busing to John Barsby or Ladysmith Secondary School on the reopening of Cedar Secondary in September 2016. 1 Rationale: The Department of Learning Services recommends that we begin to communicate with Cedar Students who are currently attending John Barsby Secondary and Ladysmith Secondary Schools regarding where they plan to attend school for the 2016-17 school year. Students will have the choice of either continuing at their present school or return to Cedar Community Secondary School. At the completion of this survey process the Department of Learning Services will make a recommendation to the Board of Education with best case grade configuration scenarios and whether a grade 12 program at Cedar Community Secondary School is feasible for the 2016 opening. Next Steps: The Department of Learning Services will administer a survey to all Cedar students who are currently enrolled in Nanaimo Secondary Schools to project September 2016 enrollment at Cedar Community Secondary School. The Department of Learning Services will create a grade configuration model and implementation plan to be presented to the Education Committee on October 7, 2015. This process is on a very tight timeline in order to begin timetabling students and staffing for opening Cedar Community Secondary. 2
