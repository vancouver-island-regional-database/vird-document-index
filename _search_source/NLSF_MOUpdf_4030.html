MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT made as of the ____ day of ______________, 2014. BETWEEN: THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) (Hereinafter called the “Board”) AND: NANAIMO-LADYSMITH SCHOOLS FOUNDATION (Hereinafter called the “NLSF”) Background: Since 1982, the Board has enjoyed a mutually supportive relationship with the Nanaimo Schools Financial Awards Society that, in 2008, became the Nanaimo-Ladysmith Schools Foundation (NLSF). The NLSF is a legally incorporated, non-profit entity. The NLSF, with the single purpose of supporting students served by the Board, is led by a representative board of community members and is governed by a constitution and bylaws. This Memorandum of Agreement formalizes a mutually beneficial relationship that has existed between the Board and the NLSF since the foundation’s inception. It details the principles and commitments that each organization makes to the other, and will be reviewed every 3 years or at the request of either party. Guiding Principles: 1. 2. 3. 4. To work positively together to achieve a student-centered mission. In other words, both parties will share a vision where all students enrolled with the Board experience programs and support aimed at maximizing their potential. To operate as collaborative partners whereby communication is open, regular, and respectful. The results of this regular consultation will be shared openly with the public in order to ensure that our communities are kept informed of joint efforts programs, and student successes. To share expertise and advice with one another in order to maximize the goals of both parties and to carry out the respective commitment identified under this agreement. To support a wide range of programming and to engage in public relations efforts to support student success and to strengthen the mission of both parties.
