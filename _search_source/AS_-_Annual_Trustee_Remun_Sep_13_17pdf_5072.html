BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools ACTION SHEET BUSINESS COMMITTEE DATE: Wednesday, September 13, 2017 TO: Board of Education FROM: Carrie McVeigh, Secretary-Treasurer RE: Trustee Remuneration Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve a CPI adjustments to Trustee remuneration, effective September 1, 2017 in accordance with current Board Policy. Background and Rationale: By Board Motion dated January 15, 1997 (attached) and Board Policy 2.6, Section 7.1.1 (attached), Trustee remuneration is to be adjusted on September 1st of each year in accordance with the Vancouver Consumer Price Index. Attachments: 1) Appendix A: Trustee Remuneration – Board Motion, 1997 2) Appendix B: Trustee Remuneration per the Consumer Price Index – Costing 3) Appendix C: Trustee Remuneration – Board Policy 2.6
