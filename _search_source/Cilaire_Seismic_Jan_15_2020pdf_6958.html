January 15, 2020 Department of Facilities Cilaire Seismic Mitigation Project Incl. Woodlands Renovation Overview of the Project • Seismic upgrade, to address the SPIR findings: • Renovation of Woodlands Secondary School, to be used as swing space for this and future Seismic Mitigation projects • Two separate Design and Construction Management contracts Details of the Project • Cilaire: – Numerous upgrades to finishing, doors/frames, ceilings, walls and electrical/mechanical impacts – Retrofit from the interior of the school – Enhanced life safety in the Gym, including removal of hazardous materials – GBA+ improvements, including inclusive washrooms, accessibility adjustments Details of the Project • Woodlands: – Elementary play structures – Fencing, access, lighting and parking upgrades to the exterior – PA and Fire Alarm system upgrades – Classroom and washroom conversion – Busing Conceptual Design Schedule Capital Project Budget Total Project Budget - $11,815,055 Includes: 1.Construction Budget – Seismic upgrades to all three blocks, retaining wall upgrade, portable relocation, shear wall upgrade 2.Total Owner’s Costs Budget – Design, permits, etc. 3.Supplemental Items – Woodlands renovation, moving expenses, busing, etc. 4.Escalation – Considered at time of award 5.Risk Items – Post tender award Questions/Comments?
