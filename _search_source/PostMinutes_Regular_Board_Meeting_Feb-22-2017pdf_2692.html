Burgos, Executive Assistant Meeting No. R08 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:03 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Chair Rae recognized Pink Shirt Day. Today, across Canada, we celebrate Pink Shirt Day. It is a day where we encourage everyone to wear something pink to show that we are all working together to prevent bullying in our schools, in our communities and online. Thank you to all our students and staff that wore pink today for this very important initiative. Trustee Bob added to the acknowledgment of the importance Pink Shirt Day. Trustee Robinson thanked Safe Schools for all their work.
