COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE MEETING MINUTES Tuesday, September 9, 2025 6:30 P.M. Ladysmith Seniors Centre 630 2nd Avenue Council Members Present: Councillor Ray Gourlay, Chair Mayor Deena Beeston Councillor Tricia McKay Councillor Duck Paterson Councillor Marsh Stevens Councillor Jeff Virtanen Council Members Absent: Councillor Amanda Jacobson Staff Present: Allison McCarrick Matt Foster Chris Barfoot Sue Bouma Tim Tanton Nick Pescod Chris Geiger Hayley Young _____________________________________________________________________ 1. CALL TO ORDER AND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT Councillor Gourlay, Chair, called this Committee of the Whole meeting to order at 6:30 p.m., and acknowledged with gratitude that it was being held on the unceded territory of the Stz'uminus First Nation. 2. AGENDA APPROVAL CW 2025-052 That the agenda for this September 9, 2025 Committee of the Whole meeting be approved as amended to include the 2025 Spring Cleanup survey results as an attachment to item 6.1 “Verbal Report - Spring Cleanup Survey”. Motion Carried Town of Ladysmith Committee of the Whole Meeting Minutes: September 9, 2025 1 3. MINUTES 3.1 Minutes of the Committee of the Whole Meeting held July 15, 2025 CW 2025-053 That the minutes of the Committee of the Whole meeting held July 15, 2025 be approved. Motion Carried 4. REPORTS 4.1 Building Inspector's Report for May to August 31, 2025 CW 2025-054 That the Committee receive the Building Inspector’s Report for the months May to August 2025. Motion Carried 4.2 Ladysmith Fire/Rescue Reports for May to July 2025 Chris Geiger, Manager of Protective Services, introduced Matt Foster as the new full-time Deputy Fire Chief. Council welcomed him to the Town. CW 2025-055 That the Committee receive the Ladysmith Fire/Rescue Reports for the months May to July 2025. Motion Carried 4.3 Coastal Animal Control Services Reports for April to June 2025 Councillor Paterson requested that future Animal Control Service reports include information on infractions at events. CW 2025-056 That the Committee receive the Coastal Animal Control Services Reports for the months April and May 2025. Motion Carried Town of Ladysmith Committee of the Whole Meeting Minutes: September 9, 2025 2 5. COUNCIL SUBMISSIONS 5.1 Active Transportation Budget Reserve - Councillor Stevens CW 2025-057 That the Committee recommend that Council allocate 1% of annual municipal taxation to the Active Transportation Reserve in each year of the 2026 to 2030 Financial Plan. CW 2025-058 AMENDMENT That Council amend resolution CW 2025-057 to reduce the allocation from 1% to 0.5%. Amendment Defeated OPPOSED: Mayor Beeston, Councillors Paterson and Virtanen. CW 2025-057 The question was called on the main motion as presented. Motion Defeated OPPOSED: Mayor Beeston, Councillors McKay, Paterson and Virtanen. 5.2 Traffic Calming Considerations - Councillor Gourlay and Paterson CW 2025-059 That the Committee recommend that Council direct staff to present a report at the November Committee of the Whole meeting detailing potential traffic calming measures for the downtown core and key park access locations. Motion Carried 6. UNFINISHED BUSINESS 6.1 Verbal Report - Spring Cleanup Survey Nick Pescod, Communications & Engagement Specialist, presented the results of the Annual Spring Cleanup survey, noting that 162 responses were received. He provided an overview of service usage and key themes, including communication effectiveness and concerns. Pescod recommended that, if Council proceeds with the program, a communications plan be considered, and noted that the Communications Department will issue a news release with the survey results. He responded to Council’s questions, and Council thanked him for his work on the survey. Town of Ladysmith Committee of the Whole Meeting Minutes: September 9, 2025 3 7.
