CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held January 25, 2012 7 NEW BUSINESS Board/Authority Authorized Course (BAA) Horsemanship11 Ms. Frisson said that Horsemanship 10 course was approved by the Board last year and is offered through distance education. Assistant Superintendent Chris Southwick said that the Horsemanship 10 course has been wellreceived and is an example of what school districts can do to give students choice. This year, 39 students from across the province are registered in the Grade 10 course. Ms. Frisson and Teacher Gayle Brase outlined the content of the new courses for the Board. They said the Grade 11 and 12 courses are progressions from the Grade 10 course.
