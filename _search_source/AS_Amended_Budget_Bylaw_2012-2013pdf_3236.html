THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: January 30, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw BACKGROUND: Section 113 of the School Act, “Adoption of the Budget” states: 113. (1) A board, by bylaw, (a) must adopt an annual budget on or before June 30 of each year for the next fiscal year, and (b) may amend the annual budget adopted under paragraph (a) (2) (3) If an Annual grant to a board is amended under section 106.3(6) or a grant is withheld or reduced under section 117(1), the minister may order that (a) the board, by bylaw, must amend its annual budget, and (b) the board must send a certified copy of the amended annual budget to the minister within 60 days of the order of the minister At the request of the minister, the board must send to the minister a certified copy of the annual budget. The Minister is requiring Amended Annual Budgets to be prepared, adopted by bylaw and submitted by February 28, 2013. The School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Amended Annual Budget Bylaw for fiscal year 2012-2013 is attached. FIRST READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: SECOND READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: UNANIMOUS CONSENT ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $137,207,625 and that it be given first reading. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $137,207,625 and that it be given second reading. Unanimous consent of the Board must be given to authorize the Secretary-Treasurer to read the 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw for a third time at the same Board Meeting. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read the 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $137,207,625 for a third time at the current Board meeting. THIRD & FINAL READING ACTION REQUIRED: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the 2012-2013 Amended Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $137,207,625 and that it be given third and final reading.
