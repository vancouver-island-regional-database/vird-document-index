Minutes of the Parks, Recreation & Culture Advisory Committee Wednesday, February 20, 2019 at 7:00pm COMMITTEE MEMBERS PRESENT: Tim Richards, Chair Gord Horth Tara Pollock Councillor Tricia McKay STAFF PRESENT: Clayton Postings Robyn McAdam REGRETS: Councillor Duck Paterson Bruce Mason AGENDA Moved and seconded: 2019-04: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the agenda for the meeting as presented. Motion carried. MINUTES Moved and seconded: 2019-05: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the minutes of the January 16, 2019 meeting as presented. Motion carried. DELEGATION Cowichan Trail Stewardship Society – Ladysmith Chapter – The group shared a presentation previously given to Council, outlining their new partnership with the Cowichan Trail Stewardship Society and their desire to improve and develop trails within the Ladysmith area. Ladysmith Sportsmen Club – Glenn Popenko from the Ladysmith Sportsmen Club provided information on the groups efforts to plant native plantings in the ravine between the Dogwood Ball Park and the Ladysmith Golf Course. The group is meeting March 9th at 9:00am for a Tree Planting Day with the help of volunteers from the community. OLD BUSINESS Public Arts Strategy – Committee members discussed the strategy and agreed that there was strong engagement with the community and that in turn creates strong expectations. The Public Arts Strategy will go back to Council for approval March 4th. Meeting adjourned at 8:38PM Next Meeting will be held at 7:00pm, March 20, 2019 at the Frank Jameson Community Centre.
