The Canadian circuit for mdigenous canoe races promotes a healthy way of living for our hwulmuxw (First Nations) people. The Canadian circuit entails weekend host Nations such as Malahat, Songhees, Musqueam, Snuneymuxw, Stz'uminus, and numerous others. Our race brackets included children from 5 years old all the way up to adults. Over 1,000 people came to Stz'uminus/The Town ofLadysmith last year to take part in the races.
