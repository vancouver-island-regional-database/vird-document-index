Appendix A Administrative Procedure 421 – Contract Labour AP 421 – Contract Labour Purpose Specifications for work contracted to outside sources shall stipulate that Union Labour be employed wherever possible. Tenders will be considered from all qualified contractors; however, union labour will be employed wherever possible. Procedure All invitations to tender shall include the following stipulations: 1. It is preferred that all workers, artisans, mechanics and labourers employed in the execution of this contract be members in good standing of the certified union for that trade. 2. If non-union labour is employed it shall be the responsibility of the contractor to ensure that the non-affiliation provisions in collective agreements of unions affiliated to the BC/Yukon Building Trades Council are not invoked. 3. Providing that they are fully competent, local workers shall be employed in preference to workers from outside the Nanaimo area.
