THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 SPECIAL BOARD MEETING TO BE HELD WEDNESDAY, MARCH 7, 2012 – 5:00 PM BOARD ROOM AGENDA Page 1. CALL TO ORDER The Chair will call the meeting to order and recognizes that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. 2. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA 3. NEW BUSINESS 3.1 Local School Calendar - 2012-2013 2-18 EITHER “That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves a consultation process to consider the adoption of a Local School Calendar for the 2012/13 (option 1 – 4 days local school calendar) for all District schools in accordance with the Board of Education's School Calendar Bylaw No. 5”. OR “That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves a consultation process to consider the adoption of a Local School Calendar for the 2012/13 (option 2 – 5 days local school calendar) for all District schools in accordance with the Board of Education's School Calendar Bylaw No. 5”. 4. QUESTION PERIOD Questions from the audience must be submitted in writing and given to the Board’s Executive Assistant (Cathy Kelt) for submission to the Chair. The question period is intended to enable the public to obtain clarifying information regarding a current agenda item. Forms are available in the information rack near the entrance of the Board Room 5.
