NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATION COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: June 3, 2020 Education Committee Deputy Superintendent, Tim Davie Board/authority Authorized Course Framework Recommendation The Education Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve Social Emotional Learning 11 as a Board/Authority Authorized Course (BAA) as per Ministry Policy. Background BAA courses provide an opportunity for educators to explore content beyond the boundaries of Ministry curriculum. BAA courses help educators respond to the local needs of schools and their communities, and to provide choice and flexibility for students. Under the Ministry guidelines all locally developed courses that may count for graduation credit at the Grade 10, 11, 12 level must be submitted as Board/Authority Authorized Courses and be approved by the Board of Education. Social Emotional Learning 11 has been reviewed by District staff and recommended for approval by the Board of Education. Appendix: Social Emotional Learning 11 Course Framework
