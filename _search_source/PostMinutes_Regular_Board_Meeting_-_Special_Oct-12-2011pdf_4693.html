CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Superintendent's Report on Class Size Mr. Hutchinson told the Board that at the elementary level, of the 140 intermediate divisions, only one exceeds 30 students. In addition, 13 elementary band/music/choir classes exceeded 30 students, with the larger grouping of students supporting the needs of a program. At the secondary level, of the 807 instructional blocks, 122.5 have more than 30 students. Of those, 104 blocks were organized to support the offering of a full array of programs at a school, while 18.5 support the needs of a program, such as band and choir. Mr. Hutchinson responded to questions from Trustees.
