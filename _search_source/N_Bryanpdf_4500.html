Kelsex Toxe Subject: FW: Thank you to SD68 Good Morning; I just wanted to send a quick thank you to Nanaimo SD68. On May 3, 2016 my family was evacuated from Fort McMurray due to the wildfire situation. After a few weeks we finally managed to settle in Nanaimo with my brother and I chose to have my two kids, Danielle (Grade 8) & Joshua (Grade 5) enter the school system there. My kids were welcomed and treated so well at Woodlands Secondary and Forest Park Elementary. Although we weren't in Nanaimo for long it was nice to give the kids some form of normalcy in such an abnormal situation. The schools went so far as to provide my kids with school clothing and supplies which made things much easier for us. Ijust want to thank the schools and the school district for accommodating us in such a stressful time. Nikki Bryan
