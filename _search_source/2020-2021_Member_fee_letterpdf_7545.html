July 9, 2020 Dear Board Chairs, On behalf of the BC School Trustees Association (BCSTA) Board of Directors, I write to thank your Board for renewing its membership in BCSTA for 2020/2021. In order to assure the long-term viability of the Association, its ability to provide services to member boards, and to be responsive to the priorities of the membership, the February 2017 Provincial Council resolved “that BCSTA member fees be increased each year by the amount of the most recent five-year average of the Vancouver Price Index as of January 1 of each year.” In April 2020, the BCSTA Provincial Council therefore approved a budget for 2020/2021 that included a minimal member fee increase. The February 2017 Provincial Council also resolved that annual member fee increases “be supported by any unbudgeted member equity surplus arising in the fiscal year preceding adoption of BCSTA’s annual budget”. As the 2018/2019 fiscal year ended with a budget surplus, we are happy to announce that member fees for the coming year will not increase. Any change in individual board member fees for 2020/2021 will therefore be the result of changes in FTE students only. Your Board’s BCSTA member fee for 2020/2021 has not otherwise increased. Your BCSTA Board of Directors, Finance & Audit Committee, and staff take the stewardship of member funds very seriously. Through ongoing monitoring and review of member input, we work to ensure that the Association’s operations continue to provide the best value possible to members. Additionally, we are doing everything possible to ensure direct services to member Boards are maintained at a high level, without the need for fee increases. We invite your Board to contact any of our Directors if you have suggestions regarding how we can better manage member fees and services. BCSTA is also well aware of the unique context we are all facing during the current COVID outbreak. We are committed to monitoring the financial challenges boards of education may face in the coming months, and doing our part to be fiscally responsible. There will be several opportunities during the coming school year for members to raise suggestions as well as concerns regarding BCSTA’s budget and fee structure. Please do not hesitate to speak-up as issues or ideas are raised at the local level. Your input is valued. Once again, on behalf of the BCSTA Board of Directors and staff, I thank you for your ongoing support and membership. If you have any questions about BCSTA fees or services, please do not hesitate to contact me directly. Sincerely, Stephanie Higginson President - BC School Trustees Association cc: Secretary Treasurers
