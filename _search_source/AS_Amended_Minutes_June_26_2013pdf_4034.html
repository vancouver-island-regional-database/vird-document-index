THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: January 29, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Minutes – June 26, 2013 Regular Board Meeting BACKGROUND The Regular Board Minutes of June 26, 2013 were adopted August 28, 2013. It has since been noted that there is a typographical error in two motions as follows: Item # 1 - Motion R13/06/26-18 This Board motion currently reads as follows: “That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Cedar Secondary School Closure Bylaw No. 2012-05 at its Regular Board Meeting of March 27, 2013.” (Minutes attached) The date in this motion should read “June 26, 2013”. RECOMMENDATION: “That the Board of Education amends the date in Motion R13/06/26-18 to be June 26, 2013.” Item # 2 – Motion R13/06/26-26 The Board motion currently reads as follows: “That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the North Cedar Elementary School Closure Bylaw No. 2012-08 at its Regular Board Meeting of June 26, 2013. (Minutes attached.) The correct name of the school is North Cedar Intermediate School. RECOMMENDATION: “That the Board of Education amends the name of the school in Motion R13/06/26-26 to be North Cedar Intermediate School.”
