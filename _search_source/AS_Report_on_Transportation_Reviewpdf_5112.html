THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: February 20, 2013 TO: Business Committee FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations David Prevost, Transportation Supervisor SUBJECT: Interim report on Transportation Review Background: The following motion was passed by the Board of Education on November 28, 2012- Folio R12/11/28-04 That the Board of Education of School District 68 refers the November 28, 2012 Evaluation of Transportation Services Report as completed by Management Partnership Services, Inc. to staff for review and to provide an interim report to the Board on the recommendations by February 28, 2013. Discussion: Staff has had an opportunity to review the report, consider the recommendations, and build a working plan to guide the process of implementing the recommendations. The working plan recommendations are broken into high, medium and low level priorities. High level priorities are items that should be addressed immediately. Medium level priorities are important to the operation but less urgent. Low level priorities are important but require some work to be completed before they can be addressed. Staff is suggesting a subcommittee be formed to review the working plan as presented. This subcommittee would review the working plan and make recommendation on a final work plan to the Business Committee. Staff suggests the makeup of the subcommittee as:  Transportation Supervisor  Director of Planning and Operations  Secretary-Treasurer  Trustee  Parent  Principal  Asst. Superintendent Note: Other members may be added to the Sub-Committee as necessary.
