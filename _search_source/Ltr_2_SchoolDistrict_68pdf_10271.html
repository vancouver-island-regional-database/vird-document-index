NODRA NORTH OYSTER~DIAMOND RATEPAYERS ASSOCIATION • South Cassidy, the Diamond, Shell Beach & Yellow Point • School District 68, Trustees September 17, 2022 Charlene McKay, Greg Keller, Jessica Stanley, Chantel Oneill, Elaine Wilkinson, Tania Brzovic, Stephanie Higginson, Bill Robinson 395 Wakesiah Ave. Nanaimo, BC V9R 3K6 Re: URGENT - Potentially contaminated fresh water supply to E’cole North Oyster Dear School District 68 Board of Trustees, I am writing to you today to share urgent information that has come to our attention regarding the potential contamination of the E’cole North Oyster fresh water supply. You are probably aware that the source of fresh water for the school is located at a private property at 13264 Cedar Rd., or what is currently known as the ‘blueberry farm,’ next to Thomas Creek. It has come to our attention that the Schnitzer Steel company has just posted a sign at the back of their property stating, “WARNING, INORGANIC LEAD POISON, Do Not Drink or Smoke, Respiratory Protection Required While Torchers Present” AUTHORIZED PERSONNEL ONLY.” (Please see attached photos). NODRA-North Oyster/Diamond Ratepayers Assoc., and VIWWC-Vancouver Island Water Watch Coalition have been working together with assistance from WCEL-West Coast Environmental Law, and our legal team, to address the drinking water well contamination issues of local property owners in the vicinity of Schnitzer Steel, following the re of 2020. I realize the NOCC-North Oyster Community Centre water supply is also connected to the water system in use by Ecole Nord Oyster, so I’m making attempts to contact their Board of Directors, as well. Please nd recent photos attached. Yours truly, Pauline Hunt and President NODRA-North Oyster/Diamond Ratepayers Assoc.
