Next Generation Network Upgrading the Provincial Learning Network (PLNet) to enable student learning was a shared priority for the sector. I am pleased to report that NGN is already providing significant benefits to districts, with over 1100 sites (out of 1650) transitioned to NGN and 80% of students now able to access the new network.
