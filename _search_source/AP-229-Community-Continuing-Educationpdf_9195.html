Appendix C Administrative Procedure 229 – Community Continuing Education AP 229 – Community Continuing Education Purpose The Board of Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools recognizes that learning is a lifelong process and the provision of appropriate learning opportunities enhances the personal development of community member and the quality of community life in general. Procedure The role of Continuing Education is to: 1. Identify and deliver quality courses, programs and services reflecting the evolving needs and interests of individuals and groups within the Nanaimo/Ladysmith area. 2. Liaise with other agencies in the community and the province to develop the coordinated delivery of programs within the community. 3. Coordinate and promote the effective and efficient use of school district facilities in providing continuing education opportunities. 4. Operate within a budget as provided by grants, fees and partnership agreements with other agencies and organizations. 5. Consult with the Continuing Education Advisory Committee in matters that relate to the philosophy and general operating practices as they pertain to continuing education. 6. Operate in congruence with the rules and regulations of the School Act and with the policies and procedures established by the Board of Education.
