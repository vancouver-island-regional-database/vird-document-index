We know that the majority of the current Grade 7’s at Ecole North Oyster will be coming to LSS for Grade 8 French Immersion. The hope of many families when enrolling our children in French Immersion is for them to complete French Immersion up until Grade 12 within our community and without needing to change schools. For example, in Grades 8 and 9, four of eight classes are required to be French Immersion. The requirement is reduced to three classes in Grade 10, two classes in Grade 11, and only one class in Grade 12. We hope that the Board considers continuing the program, so that our students can have the opportunity to complete their French Immersion studies and graduate with the ”Double Dogwood” diploma. Once again, thank you for introducing this pilot program at Ladysmith Secondary. It has been an exciting year for our children in the Grade 8 French Immersion program. I, as well as many other parents, would be happy to meet with you to discuss how the French Immersion Program at LSS is developing, and to answer any questions you may have regarding its continuation beyond Grade 9. We hope that our children may one day become the first graduating French Immersion class at Ladysmith Secondary School.
