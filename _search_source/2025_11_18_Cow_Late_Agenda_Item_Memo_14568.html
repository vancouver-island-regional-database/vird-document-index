Late Agenda Item Committee of the Whole Agenda November 18, 2025 Recommendation: That Council amend the agenda to add the following item, received after publication of the agenda, and re-number the agenda accordingly: 6.1. Council Submissions 6.1.1. RV Housing for Site Security – Councillor Paterson Councillor Paterson has requested that the Committee discuss permitting registered owners of light industrial or industrial property to have one housing unit, including an RV, on the property to accommodate 24-hour security. The unit must not accommodate more than two persons and must meet all Island Health regulations. including sewer and septic. The living unit can include modular buildings or RV but must be CSA approved.
