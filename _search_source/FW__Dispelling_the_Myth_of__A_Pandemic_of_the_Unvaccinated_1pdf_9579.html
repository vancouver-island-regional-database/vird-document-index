From: To: Subject: Date: Attachments: Karen Matthews Karen Matthews FW: Dispelling the Myth of "A Pandemic of the Unvaccinated" February 8, 2022 9:27:17 AM Dispelling the Myth Press Release.pdf From: governmentrelations@canadiancovidcarealliance.org Sent: February 5, 2022 12:32 PM To: Trustees BoardChair Subject: Dispelling the Myth of "A Pandemic of the Unvaccinated" CAUTION: External Message Dear School Board Chairs, Directors, and Trustees, Attached, please find a news release and presentation prepared by the Canadian Covid Care Alliance (CCCA), which explains how Ontario’s own COVID-19 data demonstrates that the COVID-19 vaccines and related mandates and passports are ineffective at controlling COVID-19 case growth and eliminating COVID-19 hospitalizations. The data also clearly shows that the COVID-19 pandemic is effectively over. Ontario’s own data shows that: COVID-19 no longer qualifies as a pandemic as there is evidence of population level immunity; additionally, the disease is no longer lethal to the large majority of Ontarians; Omicron is highly infectious and not prevented by current vaccines. There is therefore little to no benefit to using these injections to “vaccinate” children or boost adults; The unvaccinated are not driving infection, and it is time to end all discriminatory policies including vaccine mandates and passports; It is time to end the exclusive focus on vaccines and broaden our management approach to COVID-19 by acknowledging natural immunity and focusing on early treatment; and, It is time to uncouple health policy from politics and allow independent emergency management professionals to implement a plan that leads all aspects of society back to normal life. We invite you to review other evidence-based scientific articles on our website: www.CanadianCovidCareAlliance.org, and we welcome your questions. Note that we have also included a scientific paper with more detailed information related to the presentation.
