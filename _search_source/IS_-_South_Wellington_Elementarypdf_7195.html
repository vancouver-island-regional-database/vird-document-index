NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: May 27, 2020 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer South Wellington Elementary School Site Background At the April Board meeting, Board adopted the following motion: Folio R20/04/22-08 That staff meet with the South Wellington Area Community Association and the Regional District of Nanaimo as part of the business case development. Information On April 30, 2020, district staff met with a small group of community members on site, following social distancing requirements. District staff also held a public “online” information session on May 20, 2020. The purpose of the meeting was to provide information and answer any questions that the public may have. We also provided information on the decision making process and the public can provide feedback to the Board. District staff has also created an FAQ which includes all facilities’ condition information in possession of the District to allow the public access to this information. District staff have also engaged the Ministry of Education and have determined that we can extend the June 1, 2020 deadline to have our Demolition Business Case prepared to the end of June and/or beginning of July. In addition, the Regional District of Nanaimo has engaged a consultant to review the facility condition. Staff will be providing a report to the Board at the June 24, 2020, Regular Board meeting containing a recommendation with respect to the demolition.
