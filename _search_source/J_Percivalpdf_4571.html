Impact of Rutherford Elementary School Closure on Community Childcare: Currently, the After school childcare program on site at Rutherford Elementary is full at 22 school aged children with a wait list of 10. Similarly, the after school Childcare program at Frank Ney is full with a wait list. For licensed Family Child Care centers in the community, most will not be able to provide care for our neighbourhood children should the school close and the students need to be driven to alternate portables for substandard and a noninclusive school learning environment. Dramatic Play currently does pickups from Rutherford elementary but will not from Frank Ney or McGirr. Loving Care provides before and after school care for Rutherford students and will still do pickups from other schools depending on the dismissal time. North Nanaimo Early Learning Center does do pickups from Rutherford but that is because it is walking distance and they will not do pick ups or drop offs from the neighbouring schools as they don't have a vehicle. Jana's Family Child Care said they would not do after school pickups but would offer care dependent on the families dropping off their children after school. Cub Club Childcare currently provides before and after school care for Rutherford students but is within walking distance and will not be able to provide care for students at Frank Ney or McGirr. As a parent of a child at Rutherford Elementary and the owner and manager of Cub Club Childcare, I think that community childcare options are ideal because they allow the children and families more involvement with the school activities. Walking to and from school provides fresh air and exercise, and while we walk we interact with other students and individuals from the neighbourhood which, in my opinion, is helpful for building and fostering strong community and neighbourhoods. If I have to pick up and drop off my children from Frank Ney or McGirr, it will mean I will have to shorten my hours of operation, inconveniencing other families and parents, and negatively impacting my business. For parents who work elsewhere, I can only imagine the additional running around and childcare concerns that will arise if Rutherford Elementary school is closed.
