THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: June 16, 2011 TO: The Board of Education FROM: Policy Committee SUBJECT: Trustee Variation Bylaw BACKGROUND: At the October 28, 2009 Board Meeting, the Board adopted the following motion: R09/10/28-09 That the Board of Education receives the correspondence from Trustee Nelson Allen regarding policy development on governance representation for School District 68 and refers it to the Policy Committee. FIRST READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: SECOND READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: UNANIMOUS CONSENT ACTION REQUIRED: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the Trustee Variation Bylaw the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts Trustee Variation Bylaw and that it is given first reading. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the Trustee Variation Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts Trustee Variation Bylaw and that it is given second reading. Unanimous consent of the Board must be given to authorize the SecretaryTreasurer to read the Trustee Variation Bylaw a third time at the same Board Meeting. RECOMMENDATION: THIRD READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read the Trustee Variation Bylaw a third time at the current Board meeting. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of the Trustee Variation Bylaw, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts Trustee Variation Bylaw and that it is given third and final reading.
