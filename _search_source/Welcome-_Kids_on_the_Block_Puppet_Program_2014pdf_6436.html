Each topic is thoroughly researched and field-tested before it becomes available to schools, community service organizations, hospitals, and special interest groups. • Each puppet has a specific disability or issues around aging; cerebral palsy, hearing loss, visual challenges , mental health issues or strokes. Mid Island Abilities- Kids on the Block Puppet Program 3156 Barons Rd Nanaimo BC (250) 756 9455 5 More Facts • Designed for elementary school audiences, the puppets become ‘real’, but still encourage the asking of the “difficult” questions, helping to remove the stigma around disabilities and aging. • The Kids on the Block program is directed toward audiences in kindergarten through early middle school. Most programs include three to five scripts which address specific subject areas within the topic. • All of the puppets have distinct personalities and differences.
