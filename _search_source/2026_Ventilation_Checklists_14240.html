The operation of a high-volume exhaust fan in a small or tightly constructed dwelling can draw the venting flue gasses back into the dwelling, causing harm. • This test will determine if the safe threshold of –5 Pa (–0.02” WC) is exceeded with the fan running. Consider this procedure only if the authority having jurisdiction (AHJ) has agreed in advance to the acceptability of this alternative method (equivalency) as proof that chimney safety to 9.32.4.1 is maintained.
