Appendix B Administrative Procedure 203 – Trustee and Staff Travel AP 203 – Trustee and Staff Travel Purpose Trustees may travel to conferences, workshops, and for School District business as required, while staying within the confines of the amount budgeted. The Superintendent of Schools and the Secretary-Treasurer or their delegates, are empowered individually to authorize travel to conferences, workshops, and for school district business as required, for themselves and respective staffs within the confines of the amounts budgeted and, provided the conferences and workshops are related to school district affairs and would benefit the district. Procedure The Superintendent of Schools and the Secretary-Treasurer or their delegates are empowered individually to authorize travel to conferences, workshops and for school district business as required, for themselves and staff within the confines of the amounts budgeted, provided the conferences and workshops are related to school district affairs and would benefit the district.
