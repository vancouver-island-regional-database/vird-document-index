Dear Board Members, We are writing in response to the latest budget proposal in which the English stream students at Ecole Davis Road Elementary will be relocated to LPS and LIS for the start of the next school year. As parents of a current and future English stream student(s) at Ecole Davis Road we would like to voice our extreme displeasure with the proposal and ask the board members to vote down this incredibly short sited and destructive idea. After attending the information session at Ecole Davis Road on Monday, April 15th it became very clear that this proposal was tabled with the intent of mortally wounding Ecole Davis Road so that the end game of achieving a sole elementary and secondary campus in the center of Ladysmith could be achieved. If the overall intent is to create a “big box store” approach to the education of our children then we have the right to know so we can make the appropriate choices moving forward. As recent arrivals to this school district we don’t pretend to know everything pertaining to budgetary constraints but we do know one thing – Ecole Davis Road is a great school! Since arriving at Ecole Davis Road and enrolling our oldest son in grade two we have experienced what every parent hopes for when they send their little ones off to school. An environment filled with learning, caring, sharing and fun, a school that encourages creativity and individuality in a safe, comfortable and inviting atmosphere. Ecole Davis Road is that school. Being parents and making choices for our children is not always easy but when our son comes home every day with a smile on his face and is eager to learn and participate at school we know we made the right choice. We now ask you to make the right choice. A bigger school does not make a better school. Listen to the people at the heart of the matter, the staff, teachers, parents and most importantly the children. Your decisions affect our lives in more ways than you can possibly imagine. We believe in our children and we believe in Ecole Davis Road. Make the right choice. Sincerely, Jim & Suzanne Demeter
