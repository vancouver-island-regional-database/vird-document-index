Open Letter to School District 68 Superintendent & School Trustees: I think we speak for all residents of Nanaimo when we express how upsetting it is hearing that Graduation Ceremonies won’t be taking place like they usually do due to COVID-19. We all know the Graduating Class of 2020 deserves better. The Nanaimo Clippers Hockey Club want to reach out to School District 68 and offer our help and resources in holding some type of event to celebrate the Graduating Class of 2020. We would like to work with School District 68, Principals, Graduation Committees and Island Health to offer a ceremony whether be a Drive-in event, Virtual ceremony or any ideas we can come up with as a team to celebrate the close to 1,000 graduating students. We all know through their entire journey of their education; this is never how anyone expected it would end. The graduating class worked tirelessly over the years with one goal in mind, graduation and with graduation comes a celebration. It was a promise made to them; a reward for succeeding. This is not the graduation they have earned, and this is not the graduation they deserve. Together, we can come together to create a plan to make sure they get a celebration they earned. We look forward to hearing from you and how we can support and help. Sincerely, Tali Campbell General Manager #InThisTogether
