Page 9 of 25 7.1 Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held October 28, 2015 Each year, Boards of Education across the Province are required to submit a five-year capital plan detailing the high priority capital projects needed for their school districts. Once the Ministry of Education receives submissions, it prioritizes the requests. The main elements of the Five-Year Capital Plan include; provision of new space, rejuvenation or replacement of schools, seismic mitigation, mechanical or energy system upgrades, building envelope remediation, routine capital and school bus replacements.
