NOTICE OF PARTICIPATION CANOE PROCUREMENT January 16, 2026 Notice of Participation – Canoe Procurement Notice is given that the Town of Ladysmith intends to participate in one or more procurements conducted by Canoe Procurement Group of Canada January 16, 2026 – February 1, 2027 . For further information and access to the Canoe RFP notices, please visit the Canoe Procurement Portal at https://canoe.bidsandtenders.ca/Module/Tenders/en or review the BC Bid designated tendering site at https://bcbid.gov.bc.ca. For further information about Canoe, please visit their website at http://www.canoeprocurement.ca.
