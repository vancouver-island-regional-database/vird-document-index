Appendix A THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) CILAIRE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL CITY OF NANAIMO GEOTECHNICAL REPORT COVENANT, 2026 WHEREAS Section 65(5) of the School Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 412, provides that a board of education may exercise a power with respect to the acquisition or disposal of property owned or administered by the board of education only by bylaw; AND WHEREAS: (a) The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (the “Board”) owns the Cilaire Elementary School site (the “Property”); (b) the Property is facility number 6868024; (c) the address of the Property is 25 Cilaire Drive, Nanaimo, British Columbia V9S 3C9, and the legal description of the Property is: Parcel Identifier: 003-785-301 Lot 1, Section 1, Nanaimo District, Plan 18757; (d) the City of Nanaimo’s (the “City”) building inspector considers that the construction of two childcare studios on the Property is likely subject to risk of landslide and, as such, requires that a geotechnical engineering report be registered as a covenant over the Property (the “Covenant”) pursuant to Section 218 of the Land Title Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 250 to ensure that the Property is used by the Board in the manner as set out in the geotechnical report prepared by Lewkowich Engineering Associates Ltd. dated the 6th day of March 2026. (e) the Board is satisfied that the Covenant will benefit the Board and that the granting of the Covenant will not interfere with the Board’s use of the Property for educational purposes. NOW THEREFORE be it resolved as a Bylaw that the Board grant the Right-of-Ways; and BE IT FURTHER resolved as a Bylaw that the Secretary-Treasurer be and is hereby authorized, on behalf of the Board, to execute and deliver all documents required to complete the granting of the Covenant on such terms and conditions as the Secretary-Treasurer may consider advisable as witnessed by the signature of the Secretary-Treasurer. This Bylaw may be cited as “School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Cilaire Elementary School City of Nanaimo Geotechnical Report Covenant Bylaw, 2026”.
