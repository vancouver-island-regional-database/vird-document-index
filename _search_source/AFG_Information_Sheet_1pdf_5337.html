THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET TO: Business Committee FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations DATE: April 13, 2016 SUBJECT: 2016/17 Annual Facility Grant (AFG) 2016/17 Draft School Enhancement Program (SEP) 2016/17 Draft School Bus Replacement Program (SBRP) REFERENCE:  2016-17 AFG Plan (2 pages attached)  2016/17 Draft SEP Plan (3 pages attached)  2016/17 Draft SBRP (4 pages attached) BACKGROUND: Each year the Ministry of Education announces the amount of the AFG, to be used by the Districts for projects that meet Ministry of Education Policy 1.1.100. The Ministry of Education has called for SEP Projects for 16/17 due April 15, 2016. The Ministry of Education has called for SBRP submissions for 16/17 due April 15, 2016. DISCUSSION: The Ministry of Education announced ‘status quo’ AFG funding for 16/17 in the value of $2,707,680. The grant was reduced by a contribution of $47,298 toward the continuing implementation of Capital Asset Management Services and further, $94,595 for the Next Generation Network (NGN). The net amount of the grant remains equal to the funding received in 2015/16. Plans for the expenditure of the AFG funding were developed considering the following:  AFG criteria  Priority support for the Becoming Sustainable: Facilities Plan Update.  Facilities condition reports  Reported facilities issues  Request from Schools The Draft request(s) for the SEP funding was developed considering the 16/17 AFG plan and the Ministry Criteria. The Draft plan for SBRP was developed considering Ministry published criteria and the individual condition of the buses. Funding for SEP requests is uncertain and not known until May 31, 2016 Should any duplicate project be approved, staff will reallocate AFG in support of the highest priorities based on supporting District goals.
