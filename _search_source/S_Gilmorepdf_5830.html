KelseyToye Subject: FW: Seaview Bus From: Scott Gilmore [maiH:o:emuhEo@gmail.<:om] Sent: Monday, May 02, 2016 7:58 AM To: John Blain Subject: Seaview Bus The removal of the school buses from Seaview school for children closer than 4.8km is ridiculous. The buses are well used now, this was clear in your report. The reason they are so well used, and children do not walk now, is that numerous hazards exist between Upper Lantzville and the school. These hazards include 1: Highway 19, this is a major and accident prone intersection. 2: No sidewalks on any roads. Most of these roads also have no houses and hence traffic tends to move at well above the speed limit. 3: No street lights on roads. For children to get to school on time they will be walking in the dark in the winter! 4: Cougars and bears. These are seen regularly here in some years and every year multiple letters are sent home from the school. Most of the roads the children will walk on are forested on both sides with no houses. The expectation that children can walk to school is simply not true. The life of any child at the school is worth more than the budget cuts you will be saving. Yours sincerely Dr Scott Gilmore
