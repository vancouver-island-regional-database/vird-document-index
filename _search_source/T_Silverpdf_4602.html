Survey of Parents of Preschoolers Regarding the Proposed Closure of Departure Bay School Presented By Taryn Silver September 2, 2015 Survey of Parents of Preschoolers Regarding the Proposed Closure of Departure Bay School When it came to my attention recently that Departure Bay Eco School was being considered for closure in June of 2016. As a parent of a 2 year old boy and 3 year old girl living just up the road from the school it had always been our plan to enrol our children there and the Eco School Program having just been approved this spring is a really good fit for our children’s learning styles. I immediately wrote letters to the school board, MLA and Minister of Education. One of my concerns was the data regarding to projected enrolment. Because the federal census no longer collects information on where children of different ages are living, and because anecdotally I’ve heard many parents talking about the Eco-school initiative from both within catchment and outside of the area, I wanted to conduct a brief ad hoc survey to poll these parents and feel like their opinions can be counted. I was honestly overwhelmed with the number of responses I got without any significant effort. The Survey Six basic questions were asked of 56 parents representing 95 preschool aged children living in the school district 68 region. The parents volunteered their responses through Facebook messages, and e-mails between June 15th and June 19th, 2015 (yes, that’s 4 days!). Some of the parents chose to remain anonymous while others agreed to have their names shared. All of their comments are included in the last section of the full report. Question 1 Do you currently live in the Departure Bay catchment? Yes (28) No (28) 50/50 Question 2 Do you have any children who are under school age and if so what years were they born? Question 3 Are you planning to send them to Departure Bay School yes, no, or possibly considering it? Question 4 Have you heard about the Eco-school program and does this influence your choice of schooling options? Yes (53) No (3) 95% Question 5 If the Eco-program was moved to Cilaire School will you send your child to Cilaire? 62% would not send their children to Cilaire if the Eco program was moved there, 20% were undecided and only 18% would move to Cilaire if the Eco Program moved. Some the themes that emerged from the data are: • 50% of the parents surveyed live within the Departure Bay catchment and 50% live outside of the Departure Bay catchment, but 95% of parents surveyed were planning or seriously considering sending their children to Departure Bay • 95% had heard about the Eco program and felt it influenced their education choices • 62% would not send their children to Cilaire if the Eco program was moved there, and 20% were undecided and only 18% would move to Cilaire if the Eco Program moved.
