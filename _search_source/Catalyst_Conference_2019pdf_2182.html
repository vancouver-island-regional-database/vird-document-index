Who We Are DOVER NDSS BARSBY Presentation to City Council (June 10 2019) We asked Council for: - The creation of a Youth Sustainability Action Coordinator role at the City of Nanaimo - Support free bus passes for youth under 18 What City Council Asked of Us: -that our delegation make a similar presentation to the Regional District of Nanaimo and the SD68 School Board. What our dreams are for the district… What we are asking for from you: Can you support a sustainability action coordinator role at the district? Can you re-establish the funding for the green network of schools? -To create a vibrant and thriving green network of schools across SD 68 -Organize a Vancouver Island regional sustainability conference What is the Green Network of Schools? (established 2013/2014) A network of environment clubs across both elementary and secondary schools. Student groups get together and… -they share ideas and brainstorm more! -support initiatives at both school and district levels -organize a Vancouver island sustainability conference? Collectively the impact is huge! Participation in the network develops student leadership, advocacy and agency in environmental issues impacting their school and community. A vision of what a thriving and funded green network can do… Celebrate all sustainability initiatives inside and outside of School District 68 • School – wide composting • Active Transportation • Habitat Restoration • REDUCE energy consumption (Energy Cup) • Meatless Mondays • School Gardens • EARTH DAY • Green Network • Single-use plastics ban Activate our potential for positive social change! • FREE TRANSIT for students under 18 • Species Extinction Education • Ecosystem Restoration projects • School gardens and food forests • Future Earth Day Events and Conferences • Renewable energy infrastructure • Energy Audits • Climate justice social arts projects Help us empower and strengthen the SD68 Student Green Network We are requesting: • An annual budget of $2500.00 for the next two years • Support for a sustainability action coordinator role at the district that can liaise with the city and help coordinate meeting/events
