Charlene McKay, Since 2021, the Nanaimo & Area Land Trust (NALT) has been delivering the School Water Stewards to participating classes in School District 68, connecting students with their local watersheds through curriculum connected, place-based water stewardship lessons and activities. When you consider some of the current and predicted effects of climate change in our area, periods of intense rain events interspersed with prolonged periods of dry and drought, water stewardship education is climate change education. NALT applauds the School District's efforts to align operations and education opportunities with mitigation and adaptation to climate change. The Environmental Sustainability Action Plan provides an excellent framework to move forward as a resilient community. NALT looks forward to the implementation of the Plan and for opportunities to support its goals and actions.
