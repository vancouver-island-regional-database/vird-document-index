NOTICE SEWER PARCEL TAX WATER PARCEL TAX The Ladysmith Parcel Tax Roll Review Panel will meet on Monday, April 20, 2026 at 4:00 pm in Council Chambers at City Hall, 410 Esplanade, Ladysmith, BC, to hear any complaints and review the water and sewer parcel tax assessments for 2026. A complaint will not be heard by the Review Panel unless you have made notice of your complaint in writing to the Town of Ladysmith by 4:00 pm on Friday, April 17, 2026.
