CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY SUPERINTENDENT'S REPORT Mr. Hutchinson updated the Board on district accomplishments from 2011-13 and outlined his priorities for the 2013-14 school year. He also provided an update on the 10-Year Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan. He said there have been a number of key district achievements in the past two years. The achievements are: Page 5 of 8 Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held August 28, 2013 •Increased Aboriginal student graduation rates, and our graduation rates overall •The “Success for All” district-wide initiatives •The strategic policy governance model (streamlined decision-making process) •The establishment of the Department of Learning Services and interdisciplinary Learning Services Support Teams •The focus on instruction, assessment, and response to intervention •The Asset Management Plan •Human Resources Department development and restructuring •Enhanced senior leadership accountability and effectiveness through performance management •The development of the Board’s Strategic Plan and a 10-Year Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan •Children in Care support.
