NOTICE VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITY COMMUNITY PLANNING ADVISORY COMMITTEE VACANCIES Ladysmith Council is inviting applications from residents interested in the growth and future direction of our community. The Town’s Community Planning Advisory Committee (CPAC) has seven vacancies, each for a twoyear term, ending June 30, 2025. The mandate of the CPAC is to provide feedback to applicants and advice to Council on land use applications, policies, regulations and initiatives referred by Council. The CPAC will meet monthly when there are referrals to be considered. Applicants should have a broad range of skills and/or experience in economic and social development, housing design, heritage or the environment. If this committee seems like a good fit for you, applications are being accepted until July 27, 2023.
