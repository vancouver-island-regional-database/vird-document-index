Parks, Recreation & Culture Invatation to Tender No. 2025-PRC-02 Forrest Field – Phase 2 This Addendum contains: Addendum #4 - Q&A. For further information please contact: Chris Barfoot 250-245-6421 cbarfoot@ladysmith.ca Addendum Issue Date: November 14, 2025 RFT Issue Date: November 5, 2025 RFT Closing Date: November 28, 2025 1:30 pm Location of Bid Opening: Ladysmith City Hall Request to Tender – Forrest Field – Phase 2 RFT #2025-PRC-02 Page 2 Details of Addendum #4 1.0 Questions and Answers: 1. Item 1.4: Will a standard 8ft' tall fence be accepted instead of the specified 2.5m A1: Yes. An 8ft (2.44m) tall fence would be acceptable. 2. Item 2.6: Is there any required ditch restoration or a lift of specified surface material A2: Ditches will be considered disturbed ground to be finished with 100mm topsoil and seeded. 3. Item 3.12: Do the picnic table slabs require reinforcing? A3: No 4. Item 3.13: Is there project specific asphalt design or testing requirements A4: No. Standard hot mix asphalt to be used. Base gravels to be tested for compaction and asphalt core samples taken. 5. Item 3.18: It says to include disposal of excavated material with this Import item. Wouldn't any removal and disposal be captured in 3.2 Stripping & Common Ex? If not can you clarify the difference. A5: Correct. Removal and disposal is captured under item 3.2. 6. What pay item is the 1.2m tall berm captured in? A6: The berm is to be included in pay item 3.18 Imported Fill 7. Will Aluminum handrails be accepted for the stairs? A7: Steel handrails are to be priced for the purpose of this tender. Alternates may be considered after the tender is awarded. 8. Is the 100mm base and 250mm subbase under the concrete sidewalk/pads incidental to the sidewalk/pads items, or are the base gravels would be paid under item 3.3 - 20mm minus Base Gravels and 3.4 - 75mm minus Sub-Base Gravels. A8: All base and sub-base gravels are to be paid under items 3.3 & 3.4 END ADDENDUM 4 (Total 1 page)
