If you have any comments regarding this Procedure please email ckelt@sd68.bc.ca SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) DRAFT POLICY NO. 2200 BUDGETARY OVERSIGHT The Board of Education, through the Business Committee, will receive a year end forecast report of its revenues and expenditures on a timely basis following the dates of its revenues and expenditures on a timely basis following the dates of October 31, January 31, and April 30. Further forecast reports will be provided when substantive budgetary changes occur due to funding and/or expense changes. Adopted: -1 -
