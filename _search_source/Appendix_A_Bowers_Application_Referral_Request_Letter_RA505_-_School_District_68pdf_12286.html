Appendix A 2024-JAN-26 Via email: Mark.Walsh@sd68.bc.ca Our File: RA000505 Mark Walsh Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools Re: Rezoning Application – RA000505 – 6261 Hammond Bay Road The City of Nanaimo has received an application to rezone the property at 6261 Hammond Bay Road (presently Green Thumb Garden Centre) with details as follows: File No.: RA000505 Address: 6261 Hammond Bay Road Application Description: To rezone the subject property from Urban Reserve (AR2) to a Comprehensive Development (CD) zone, to facilitate a mixeduse commercial and residential development in accordance with the Bowers District Master Plan. The Master Plan was adopted by Nanaimo Council on March 7, 2022. Rezoning Proposal: Bowers District Rezoning Application File Manager: Caleb Horn It is anticipated that full buildout under the proposed zoning could result in over 2,500 residential dwelling units and over 20,000m2 of commercial floor area. This application is being referred to Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools – School District No. 68 (SD68) as the application is likely to be considered a Significant Development. Should SD68 wish to comment on the rezoning application, please provide any written comments to the City by 2024-FEB-26. If you have any questions with regards to the proposed rezoning application, please feel free to contact me at 250-755-4460 ext. 4344 or by email at: Caleb.Horn@nanaimo.ca Sincerely, Caleb Horn Planner, Current Planning Section Planning & Development City of Nanaimo EC: Pete Sabo, psabo@sd68.bc.ca
