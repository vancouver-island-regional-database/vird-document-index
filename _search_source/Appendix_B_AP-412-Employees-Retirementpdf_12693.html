Appendix B Administrative Procedure 412 – Employee’s Retirement AP 412 – Employee’s Retirement Purpose All personnel leaving the Board’s employ on Retirement shall be recognized at a function sponsored by the Board and at such time shall be presented with a gift of permanent value. The Board shall recognize persons retiring at an annual dinner in the month of June. Adopted: November 14, 1984 AP 412 – Employee’s Retirement Page 1 of 1
