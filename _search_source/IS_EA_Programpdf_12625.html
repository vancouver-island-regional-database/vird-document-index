NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS STRATEGIC DIRECTIONS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: February 12, 2025 Strategic Direc�ons Commitee Kerri Steel, Director of Instruction – Learning & Inclusion Building Education Assistants through Mentorship (BEAM) Program Board of Education Strategic Plan – Board Goal • • Student Success Student and Employee Wellness Background In response to our challenges with the recruitment and retention of Education Assistants, we have created the Building Education Assistants through Mentorship (BEAM) Program, with an initial cohort of 20-25 participants beginning in May 2025. There are a number of other school districts in the province who already have in-house EA training programs. After connecting directly with district staff in several of those districts and learning about the most successful elements of their programs, we have designed BEAM with this feedback in mind. Additionally, we have consulted with CUPE, who have expressed support for this initiative. Discussion The program consists of: • • • • Weekly in-person classes and school-based observations in May & June Self-paced learning assignments over the summer Weekly in-person classes and a 100-hour school-based internship in the fall EA Candidates will be coached by an in-school EA Mentor during their internship Topics of instruction include: • Interpersonal communication skills & teamwork • Professionalism & confidentiality • Understanding students with diverse needs & disabilities • Non-violent Crisis Intervention training • Fostering independence • Self-care • • • • • Understanding and supporting students with an Individual Education Plan (IEP) Responding to challenging behaviours Supporting communication Supporting personal care and physical needs Providing culturally sensitive support Participants who engage in a full-time internship will be fully qualified by our fall staffing day in late October. Tuition to participate in this program is $3750.00. Registration is ongoing until April 15. Our goal is to ensure we have removed as many barriers as possible for potential candidates. Information Sheet: Building Education Assistants through Mentorship (BEAM) Program Page 2 We will require only $750 of the tuition this spring, and candidates pay the rest in the fall, either as a single payment, in installments, or as a payroll deduction. Observation and internship hours will also be scheduled flexibly, to allow for individuals who wish to maintain part-time employment while participating in the program.
