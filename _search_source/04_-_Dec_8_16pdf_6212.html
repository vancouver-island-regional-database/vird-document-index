THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF REGULAR BOARD MEETING HELD December 8, 2016 Trustees S. Rae, Chair S. Higginson, Vice-Chair N. Bob J. Brennan T. Brzovic S. Kimler B. Robinson N. Routley J. Solomon Staff J. Blain, Superintendent/CEO C. McVeigh, Secretary-Treasurer P. Sabo, Director of Planning/Operations D. Burgos, Director of Communications P. Graham, Executive Assistant Call to Order The Secretary-Treasurer called the meeting to order at 5:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. Election of Board Chair Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh said she will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board and will confirm that each Trustee nominated confirms their acceptance of the nomination by asking "do you accept?" Ms. McVeigh said that in the event there was an election, herself and Mr. Burgos would act as scrutineers. Trustee Robinson nominated Trustee Rae for the position of Board Chair. Ms. McVeigh called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Ms. McVeigh asked Trustee Rae if he would accept the nomination. Trustee Rae stated that he did. Ms. McVeigh declared Trustee Rae Board Chair. Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 8, 2016 Secretary Treasurer vacates the Chair and the gavel is turned over to the Incoming Chair Ms. McVeigh vacated the Chair and passed the gavel to Chair Rae. Folio A16/12/08-01 Chair Rae asked if the Elections of the Business and Education Vice-Chairs be added to the Agenda tonight. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The Elections of the Business and Education ViceChairs was added to the Agenda. Election of Vice-Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Solomon nominated Trustee Higginson. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Higginson if she would accept the nomination. Trustee Higginson stated she did. Chair Rae declared that Trustee Higginson is the Vice-Chair of the Board. Election of Business Committee Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Kimler for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second and third time. Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 8, 2016 Chair Rae asked Trustee Kimler if he accepted the nomination for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Kimler stated he did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Kimler Chair of the Business Committee. Election of Education Committee Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Bob nominated Trustee Higginson for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Higginson if she accepted the nomination for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Higginson stated that she did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Higginson the Chair of the Education Committee. Election of Business Committee Vice-Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Kimler nominated Trustee Solomon for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Solomon if he accepted the nomination for the position of Vice-Chair of the Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 8, 2016 Business Committee. Trustee Solomon stated that he did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Solomon the ViceChair of the Business Committee Election of Education Committee Vice-Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Bob for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Bob if she accepted the nomination for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Bob stated that she did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Bob the Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Representative Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Bob for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Bob if she accepted the position nomination for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Bob stated that she did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Bob the BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 8, 2016 Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Alternate Representative Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Brennan nominated Trustee Brzovic for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Brzovic if she accepted the nomination for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Brzovic stated that she did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Brzovic the Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. ADJOURNMENT Folio A16/12/08-02 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 5:08 pm.
