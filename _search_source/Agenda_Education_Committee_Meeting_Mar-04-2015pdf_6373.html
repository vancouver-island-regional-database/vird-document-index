CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Joel Smith, teacher at NDSS proposed the implementation of a Field Lacrosse Academy at NDSS. There are steadily increasing numbers of registrants in the sport of lacrosse on Vancouver Island which indicates a growing interest in the sport. The goal of the NDSS Field Lacrosse Academy planning group is to have student athletes experience success through their academic and athletic endeavours while enrolled in the NDSS Field Lacrosse Academy. Ms. Lisa Frey Departure Bay RE: Eco-School Academy IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae Folio E15/02/04-04 That the Education Committee move this request to New Business. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Page 4 of 21 6.1 Draft Minutes of the Education Committee Meeting held February 4, 2015 Ms. Lisa Frey, Principal at Departure Bay Elementary presented on the Eco-School activities currently scheduled at the school. Departure Bay Elementary has been piloting the Eco-School program. The goal of the program is to; develop students' awareness and knowledge of our surrounding environment and to inspire others to develop an interest in becoming involved in science and sustainability in the community. Ms. Frey requested the Board to formally recognize Departure Bay Elementary as an EcoSchool Academy (K-7) with a tuition paying Nature Primary program.
