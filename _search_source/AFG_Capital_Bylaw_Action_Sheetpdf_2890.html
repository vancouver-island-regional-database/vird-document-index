Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION REGULAR BUSINESS MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: May 25, 2016 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Capital Projects Bylaw No. 127104 – 2016-17 Annual Facility Grant Background: The Ministry of Education has announced Annual Facility Grant funding for 2016-17 totaling $114,495,587 provincially with Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools gross allocation being $2,707,680. Of this allocation, $602,939 is funded through the Ministry of Education’s operating budget which is accounted as a special purpose grant. Of this allocation $461,046 is available to spend as $47,298 is withheld to support the Capital Asset Management System (CAMS) and $94,595 to support the Next Generation Network (NGN). The remaining allocation of $2,104,741 is funded from Government’s capital funding allocation. To access this capital funding, the Board is required to adopt a capital project bylaw. The total allocated to NLPS to spend on AFG Projects is $2,565,787. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 127104 2016/17 Annual Facility Grant in the amount of $2,104,741 and that it be given first reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts Capital Project Bylaw No. 127104 2016/17 Annual Facility Grant in the amount of $2,104,741 and that it be given second reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 127104 2016/17 Annual Facility Grant in the amount of $2,104,741 at its Regular Board Meeting of May 25, 2016. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw No. 127104 2016/17 Annual Facility Grant in the amount of $2,104,741 and that it be given third and final reading.
