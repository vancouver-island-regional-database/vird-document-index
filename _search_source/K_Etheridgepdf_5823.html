Kelsey Toye I FW: school buses Subject; Sent: Tuesday, April26, 2016 11:42 AM To: BobEsliger Subject: school buses To School District 68 School Board, I am a concerned parent. My children both attend Dover Bay Secondary School. They have been taking the bus to school since we moved to Lantzville in 2008. The school bus was part of the decision to move to this area. I am very concerned if the bus is to be discontinued. My children will have to walk for approximately an hour to get to and from school. In the winter, this means leaving and arriving in the dark. In our area, there are limited street lights. There are no sidewalks for most of their walk. Much of their walk would be along Aulds Rd traffic. The city buses are not a good option in our area from our house in a loop). —~ —— no sidewalks and lots of they are infrequent and take a long time (it travels away from the school My greatest concern, however, is that they would have to cross the Parkway. This is a very busy highway, with regular speeding and people going through red lights. I witness this regularly. I urge to you to look at accident statistics at the intersections of concern Aulds, Ware and Superior. Kids will die at some point if you dramatically increase pedestrian traffic across the highway. A pedestrian overpass was put in at Nanoose due to fatalities. Safety should be your biggest concern. When the Parkway was built local parents fought for the bus system to ensure the safety of their children. —— If the buses are cancelled I will seriously consider pulling my children from the SD68 school system and go with a private school option as I would no longer trust that the school district has their safety in mind.
