ADJOURNMENT Page 1 of 4 3.1 BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools REGULAR BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: April 22, 2015 TO: Board of Education FROM: John Blain, Superintendent/CEO RE: Local School Calendar BACKGROUND: At the January 29, 2014, Board Meeting, the following motion was adopted: Folio R14/01/29-25 That the Board of Education of Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools consult with parents and representatives of employees in accordance with the School Act and School Calendar Regulation on the proposed Local School Calendar for the years 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17. The Department of Learning Services subsequently established a School Calendar Committee. This Committee met on four occasions (December 12, 2014, January 16, 2015, February 20, 2015, March 13, 2015) with the sub-committees for elementary and secondary schools meeting on separate occasions. The following topics were discussed: 1. Amending the Local School Calendar for the years 2014-15, 2015-16, 2016-17 and extending it to 2017-18. 2. Consistent School Schedules 3. Professional Learning Communities (PLCs) 4. Spring Break 5. District Implementation Day Page 2 of 4 3.1 Items 1. Extend the existing Three-Year Local School Calendar to 2017-18 2. Elementary School Local Schedule Guidelines 3. Secondary School Local Schedule Guidelines Recommendations “It is recommended that the existing Three-Year Local School Calendar be extended to 2017-18” “It is recommended that Elementary Schools adopt the following Schedule Guidelines:  Consistent early dismissal on Mondays  Consistent for students Monday to Friday bell schedules  Consistent short (morning) recess and long recess schedules” “It is recommended that Secondary Schools adopt the following Schedule Guidelines:  Consistent late start time (9:15 am) on Mondays to allow for PLC time.” 4. Spring Break “It is recommended that spring break be increased two weeks, within the duration of a 3-year calendar plan.” 5. Removal of one District Implementation Day and replace with an Instructional Day “It is recommended that one District Implementation Day be removed from the local calendar and be replaced with one day of instruction to allow additional hours to support PLCs.” From these recommendations came an amended Three-Year Local School Calendar extended to 201718, which is attached as a Draft for your review. Earlier Recommendation: That the Board of Education of Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools direct staff to begin a 30-day consultation on the five recommendations created by the Nanaimo Ladysmith School Calendar Committee. Current Recommendation: The Consultation is now complete, and all correspondence has been shared with the Board. The staff now recommend: That the Nanaimo Ladysmith Public School Board receive and accept the above Local School Calendar for the years 2014-15, 2015-16 and 2016-17 including all five staff recommendations.
