Derelict and Abandoned Vessels in Ladysmith Harbour Council reviewed past actions and options for further action with respect to the issue of derelict and abandoned vessels in Ladysmith Harbour. Members expressed disappointment that the new provincial manual Dealing with Problem Vessels and Structures in BC Waters does not resemble a successful program used in Washington State, and frustration with the lack of control over the issue due to multiple jurisdictions and legislation regulating coastal waters.
