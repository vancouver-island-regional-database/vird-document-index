Draft Minutes of the Education Committee Meeting held January 7, 2015 CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM THE REGULAR BOARD MEETING Foundation Skills Assessment (FSA) correspondence referred from December 17, 2014 Regular Board Meeting Mr. Ball, President, NDTA Nanaimo District Teachers' Association (NDTA) RE: Foundation Skills Assessment (FSA) Ms. Iverson, NDTA spoke to the FSA letter (attached to agenda).   NDTA feels that whatever choice the parent of a student identifies should be considered and extenuating circumstance as parents know their children best.
