R12/11/28-19 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the following Governance Board Policies and Procedures, in principle, that were distributed as a notice of motion from October 9, 2012 to November 2, 2012 and that the document be referred to an open Committee of the Whole meeting for further clarifications.
