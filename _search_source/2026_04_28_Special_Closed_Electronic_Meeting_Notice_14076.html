NOTICE SPECIAL ELECTRONIC MEETING AND CLOSED SESSION The Town of Ladysmith Council will meet electronically Tuesday, April 28, 2026 at 11:30 AM for a Special Meeting and Closed Session Pursuant to “Council Procedure Bylaw 2009, No. 1666” notice is hereby given that Council will hold an Electronic Special Meeting on April 28, 2026 for the purpose of proceeding into Closed Session in accordance with section 90 of the Community Charter to consider matters related to the following: • (g) litigation or potential litigation affecting the municipality; and • (i) the receipt of advice that is subject to solicitor-client privilege, including communications necessary for that purpose.
