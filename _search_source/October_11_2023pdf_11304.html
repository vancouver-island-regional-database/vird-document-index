An Action Sheet is attached to the Agenda. B23/10/11-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Robinson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Keller The Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct that Rutherford Elementary School be reopened as of September 2025 and that staff are directed to create a School Transition Working Group to address issues associated with reopening the facility and provide the Board with recommendations with respect to appropriate catchments for Rutherford and the surrounding schools and potential programming, if applicable, to ensure the long-term viability of the school.
