THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: May 15, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: David Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO SUBJECT: Draft Policy 1.8 “The Continuous Improvement of Instruction and Assessment” Notice of Motion BACKGROUND The Board’s recently approved strategic plan committed the District to the development of a Foundations and Direction policy on The Continuous improvement of Instruction and Assessment. A draft of this policy (attached) has been prepared and is ready to be distributed for notice of motion. Please note that as per Board policy 2.7, that following the 30 day notice of motion period, all submissions will be reviewed by the Board as a Committee of the Whole, prior to final approval. RECOMMENDATION That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) sends draft Policy 1.8 “The Continuous Improvement of Instruction and Assessment” out as a notice of motion for a period of 30 days as per Policy 2.7 of the Policy and Procedure Manual.
