Trustee MARK ROBINSON September 20, 2024 Nanaimo Chamber of Commerce Highlights: Nanaimo Child Development Centre put on a wonderful and eye-opening presentation that was both informative and interactive. NCDC is a registered charitable organization which offers a wide variety of health and health-related to over 1,900 children and their families and has been in operation since 1967. It’s family-centred approach has been successful in supporting children with developmental needs in areas such as speech language therapy, infant development programming, drop-in play groups, family resource navigation, and so much more. Staff offered a fantastic hands-on presentation putting us in the shoes of the children and families they support. Very eye-opening and enriching experience to have a sense of what it is like to live every day with a developmental disability. The main takeaway is that NCDC is an invaluable resource in our district that needs continual support and enhancement as our community keeps growing at unprecedented rates.
