School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 2012-13 Local School Calendar Survey Results As part of the consultation process for the proposed Local School Calendar for 2012-13, the district conducted an online survey from March 8 to April 12. The survey asked respondents to identify whether they were parents, students, employees or community members and then asked whether the respondents were in favour of the proposed Local School Calendar. Respondents were also given an opportunity to submit written comments. More than 2,500 responses were received. The results are outlined in the tables and charts below. It should be noted that while online surveys such as this can give a clear indication of people’s opinions, the results cannot be considered to be statistically valid for two reasons:  The survey can be answered by anyone who wishes to do so, and as a result the sample group for the survey does not meet the standards for statistical validity. It is possible for an individual to submit more than one response; however, we are able to determine if an individual has responded numerous times from the same computer, and have removed those responses from the statistics below.  Staff has noticed with this survey and past surveys that the pattern of responses is usually set after the first few hundred responses, and the results do not vary a great deal after that pattern is set. This gives us confidence that the survey results are an accurate reflection of the opinions of those individuals who chose to respond to the survey.
