Who are we? The Vancouver Island Children’s Book Festival Society (BookFest) is a non-profit, volunteer run organization entering its 40th year in 2026. Our mission is to promote literacy and a love of reading by introducing children to known and upand-coming Canadian authors and illustrators from diverse backgrounds. What do we do? We invite Canadian authors and illustrators to give presentations at SD 68 elementary schools and Vancouver Island Regional Library branches. 2026 Presenters Monique Gray Smith Lois Peterson Anuradha Rao Frances Backhouse Samantha Benyon Lee Edward Fodi Margriet Ruurs Nikki Bergstresser 2024-2025 Statistics & 2026 Plan Our Request The Vancouver Island Children’s BookFest requests a grant of $3,000. This would boost equity throughout School District 68 by allowing all schools the opportunity to participate in BookFest. This would build children’s literacy by experiencing in-person presentations. This would encourage children to consider becoming writers and artists. Testimonial "Author visits through Vancouver Island BookFest brought an incredible spark to our library.
