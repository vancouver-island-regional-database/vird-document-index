ATTACHMENT C POLICY TOPIC: POLICY No.: APPROVED BY: ORIGINAL DATE: AMENDED: SPECIAL OCCASION LICENCE APPLICATION POLICY February 17, 2014 RESOLUTION No.: AMENDMENT DATE: 2014-087 PURPOSE: To provide a consistent policy to establish locations, conditions and operational details on the approval and operation of special occasion licences on Municipal property. 1. Permitted Locations The Director of Parks, Recreation & Culture has the authority to approve Special Occasion Licences at the following locations: o Aggie Hall o Aggie Field o Downtown o Forrest Field o Frank Jameson Community Centre o Harmony Square o High Street Field o Transfer Beach Park 2. Application Requirements: a) an accompanying letter detailing the event(s) for which the licence is being required; b) name of event coordinator and their contact details; c) a clear map showing: o the location of the licence area o number and locations of portable toilets, if applicable o number and locations of additional garbage containers, if applicable o entrances and exits o food service location o location of liquor service o layout of tables and chairs o staffed locations; d) the hours of operation; e) confirmation that tables, chairs, benches, picnic tables, etc., have been or can be obtained, if necessary; f) details of control of entrants and exits to include: o number of staff o training; g) confirmation that staff will be provided to ensure vehicles are parking in a safe and legal manner, if necessary; i) security deposit, as identified within the Special Events Bylaw #XXXX assurance that food is available to patrons; j) Copy of the Special Occasion Licence approved by the BC Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch. 3. Conditions of Licence: a) the area created for the licence meets all requirements as set out through the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch for special events and special occasion licences. POLICY b) the hours of operation to be, pending RCMP review, not more than: o For Aggie Field and Forest Field between 11:00 a.m. and 8:00 p.m. o For Downtown, Harmony Square, and High Street Field between 11:00am and 9:00pm o For Transfer Beach Park between 11:00 a.m. - 10:00 p.m. o For Aggie Hall and the Frank Jameson Community Center, as set by the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch for Special Occasion Permits or as limited by staffing; c) a minimum of two hours closure of the licensed area prior to any other liquor permits being issued in the area (i.e. the area could be defined as the Community Park); concessions to be allowed to remain open for beyond the closing of a beer garden; licences not to exceed four days in duration for any one event; once an application has been approved, no change(s), by the applicant, will be allowed unless Council approval has been obtained; prior to holding the event, submit to the municipality evidence of public liability insurance coverage for the value and types of liability insurance as identified within the Special d) e) f) g) h) i) 4. Events Bylaw No. XXXX Applicants should obtain from the Insurance Corporation of BC copy(ies) of their Counter Attack Special Event Kit or like promotion. Cancellation of licence The licence can be immediately revoked by order of the RCMP, if in the opinion of the RCMP, the license is being abused, conditions of the licence are not being met, or it is in the public interest to revoke the licence.
