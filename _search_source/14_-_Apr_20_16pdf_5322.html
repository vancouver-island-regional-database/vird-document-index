UNFINISHED BUSINESS Draft Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 20, 2016 Superintendent's Report on the Consolidated Budget Superintendent Blain presented the 2016-17 Proposed Preliminary Budget plan. The primary focus in developing this budget was to create an environment to improve student learning by adding classroom support, while minimizing staff reductions outside of the classroom. To balance the budget $970,000 in savings must be identified. The following recommendations are from staff's proposed preliminary budget:    The district intends to maintain 2015-16 cleaning levels by fine tuning the team cleaning model that was implemented this school year. The proposed budget would focus on the reduction of 5.00 custodial FTE with 2.00 FTE realized through school closures. The district is focusing on core services with a priority on health and safety. For 2016-17, it is proposed to eliminate 2.00 FTE carpenters and 1.34 FTE grounds staff. The Ministry of Education does not provide funding for transportation. District 68 has been funding transportation from its operating budget. The proposed budget addresses its courtesy riders by eliminating routes 1,3,5,10,25 and 26. The report that was presented this evening is attached to the Agenda and on the School District 68 website. QUESTION PERIOD There were five questions asked and answered this evening.
