Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION REGULAR BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: May 24, 2017 Board of Education Pete Sabo, Director of Planning & Operations Emil Bock, Energy Manager Wellington Secondary School Utility Rights-of-Way Bylaw, 2017 SUBJECT: Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the Wellington Secondary School Utility Rights-of-Way Bylaw, 2017. Reference: Wellington Secondary School Utility Rights-of-Way Bylaw, 2017 – Appendix 1. (Attached) Background: During the Wellington Seismic Upgrade, a domestic water service upgrade to the building was required. Both the Civil Engineer and Construction Manager recommended the service be relocated from its original location at Departure Bay Road to Mexicana Road. This recommendation was initiated to align this service with an existing fire suppression service to minimize complexity and maximize cost efficiency. Discussion: As a result of this change, a Statutory Right-of-Way (SRW) enlargement of the existing SRW is required for the City of Nanaimo (CoN) to access and service the utility infrastructure. Once the Bylaw has been passed by the Board, the Secretary Treasurer can sign both SRW documents:  Appendix. 2 (Attached) Allows CoN general access using Standard Charge Terms ST100092.  Appendix 3. (Attached) Allows CoN to perform works within area specified on page.4 Standard Charge Terms ST100089.  Appendix 4. is provided to depict where the SRW resides on the Wellington Secondary site.
