This is only possible through the generous support of the people, businesses and organizations of Nanaimo like School District #68. — Forover 10 years, School District #68 has provided the site for the toy distribution and the tables and chairs required to hold the thousands of toys, as well as provide some relief for the hundreds of volunteers who ‘work’ setting up and distributing the toys. Together with other partners and individuals we have provided toys, books and gifts for between 1200 and 1600 children each year. All of these children attend schools throughout Nanaimo, all return to school in the new year and are able to share their Christmas experiences with their classmates. The gyms used for distribution have always been in the southern part of Nanaimo, most often the Harewood area, where many of our recipient families reside and buses pass by regularly. For many of these years, we have used speci?cally the Harewood School gym. Working together, The Great Nanaimo Toy Drive has paid the required costs, security, janitorial and proof of insurance and the school district has provided the gym for approximately two weeks, rent free. Once again we are seeking your support, as a partner with us, as we begin the 34”“year of The Great Nanaimo Toy Drive.
