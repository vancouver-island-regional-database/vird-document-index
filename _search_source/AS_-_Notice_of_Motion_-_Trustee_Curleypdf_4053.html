THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: January 29, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Trustee Curley SUBJECT: Notice of Motion for Reconsideration – Trustee Curley BACKGROUND At the December 18, 2013 Board Meeting, Trustee Curley stated that she was giving Notice of Motion that she would bring forth two motions for reconsideration pertaining to Grade 7 students attending Ladysmith Secondary at the February Board Meeting. Under Roberts Rules a motion for reconsideration is to be done at a current meeting for a motion previously passed at the same meeting – not at a future meeting. Trustee Curley is giving notice of withdrawal of her December 18th Notice of Motion and also giving a new Notice of Motion regarding Board Motion R13/06/26-30 and Board Motion R13/06/18-31 and requesting that this Notice of Motion be heard at the February 6, 2014 Special Board Meeting to be held in the Ladysmith Secondary School Gym. Trustee Curley has provided a Notice of Withdrawal of her Notice of Motion as follows: Notice of reconsideration of Motion carried June 26, 2013 Folio R13/06/26-30 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) reconfigures Ladysmith Secondary School as a Grades 7-12 school effective September 2014. Notice of reconsideration of Motion carried June 26, 2013 Folio R13/06/26-31 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) relocates the grade 7 students from Davis Road and Ladysmith Intermediate to Ladysmith Secondary School; and further that Ladysmith Secondary will become a dual-track school effective September 2014. Trustee Curley has provided a new Notice of Motion for the February 6, 2014 Special Board Meeting to be held in the Ladysmith School Gym as follows: Notice of "Motion to Rescind" Motion, Folio R13/06/26-30, carried June 26, 2013, That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) reconfigures Ladysmith Secondary School as a Grades 7-12 school effective September 2014; Notice of "Motion to Rescind" Motion, Folio R13/06/26-31, carried June 26, 2013, That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) relocates the grade 7 students from Davis Road and Ladysmith Intermediate to Ladysmith Secondary School; and further that Ladysmith Secondary will become a dual-track school effective September 2014.
