Winter) 2 Page 4 of 5 CITIZENS INVITED TO APPLY FOR THE SELECT COMMITTEE ON COUNCIL REMUNERATION Ladysmith Council invites applications from residents interested in serving on the Select Committee on Council Remuneration. The Committee’s mandate is to review the compensation paid to members of Council, and if required, to recommend reasonable changes to Council remuneration. The Committee will conduct research on council remuneration in other communities, develop recommendations and present its report to Council. Council will appoint three community members to the committee. The Committee’s final report to Council will take place before the end of 2019. If you have an open and enquiring mind and an appreciation for public service, consider applying to serve on this committee.
