June 20, 2016 Ref: 188328 To All Secretary-Treasurers, All Superintendents Re: Rural Education Enhancement Fund – Frequently Asked Questions Dear Colleagues, Further to the Minister of Education’s announcement on June 15 regarding the Rural Education Enhancement Fund, please find attached a list of questions and answers for your information. These questions were compiled by Ministry staff based on the feedback and queries that have been submitted to the Ministry to date. Recognizing how close we are to the end of the 2015/16 school year, timelines will be very tight for those of you wishing to make application to the program for the 2016/17 school year. Completed applications are due electronically to the Ministry at EDUC.SectorResourcingandServiceDeliveryBranch@gov.bc.ca no later than June 24, so that final decisions can be communicated no later than June 30. If you have further questions, please contact Kim Abbott ( Kim.Abbott@gov.bc.ca ). Your sincerely, George Farkas Assistant Deputy Minister Resource Management and Corporate Services Division Attachment – Rural Education Enhancement Fund – Frequently Asked Questions cc: All Board Chairs Joan Axford, Executive Director of BCASBO Juleen McElgunn, Executive Director of BCSSA Mike Roberts, CEO of BCSTA Ministry of Education Resource Management & Corporate Services Division Mailing Address: PO Box 9151 Stn Prov Govt Victoria BC V8W 9H1 Location: 5th Floor, 620 Superior St Victoria BC V8V 1V2
