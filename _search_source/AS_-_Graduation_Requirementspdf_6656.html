THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: October 5, 2012 TO: Education Committee FROM: K. Howland, Education Committee Chair SUBJECT: Graduation Requirements – Consultation Email from M. McEvoy, BCSTA President BACKGROUND: We received an email from Mike McEvoy advising that the Ministry of Education is currently in the process of determining new graduation requirements and that BCSTA is strongly encouraging our members to participate in regional meetings taking place around the province in the coming months. The Ministry of Education has provided a timeline stating that they will complete the regional public consultation sessions by December 2012 and will propose the new grad requirements by Spring 2013. There will be five open-ended questions available for discussion: Question #1: What do you think are the core or essential things all students should know, understand and be able to do by the time they leave secondary school? Question #2: Beyond the core, how could pathways for choice or exploration be provided? Question #3: Research is underway with a focus on the following five cross-curricular competencies: a) b) c) d) e) Communication Critical Thinking Creative Thinking and Innovation Personal Responsibility and Well Being Social Responsibility How do you think students could demonstrate these competencies? Question #4: How could student learning be communicated to: a) Students b) Parents/Guardians c) Post secondary Institutions/Employers Question #5: How would you design an awards program to recognize student success in a personalized learning environment?
