TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE GOVERNMENT SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, JANUARY 21, 2013 5:00 P.M. COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Gord Horth (Chair) Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Steve Arnett Councillor Duck Paterson Councillor Bill Drysdale Councillor Glenda Patterson Felicity Adams John Manson Erin Anderson COUNCIL MEMBERS ABSENT: Mayor Rob Hutchins STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Sandy Bowden CALL TO ORDER Councillor Horth called the Government Services Committee meeting to order at 5:00 p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL GS 2013-001 It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda for the Government Services Committee meeting of January 21, 2013 be adopted with the following additions under New Business: • • • • MINUTES GS 2013-002 DELEGATIONS Repairs to Knight’s Clock” Possible acquisition of Dunsmuir’s Parlour Car Asset Management in the Attorney General’s Office Recognition of staff for cleaning up alleyway behind Traveller’s Hotel It was moved, seconded and carried that the minutes of the Government Services Committee meeting held December 17, 2012 be adopted as circulated. S/Sgt Larry Chomyn, Ladysmith Detachment, Royal Canadian Mounte Police – 2012 Quarterly Report to Council S/Sgt Larry Chomyn of the Ladysmith Detachment of the RCMP provided an overview of the 2012 Quarterly Report and responded to questions from Committee members in this regard. S/Sgt Chomyn requested Committee members to consider issues they Government Services Committee January 21, 2013 Page 1 would like to see addressed by the RCMP during 2013. Committee thanked S/Sgt Chomyn for his informative presentation. GS 2013-003 REPORTS It was moved, seconded and carried that the RCMP 2012 Quarterly Report be received. City Manager’s Report The City Manager distributed a report to the Committee summarizing 2012 work plans and tasks and responded to questions in this regard. GS-2013-004 It was moved, seconded and carried that the City Manager’s report dated January, 2013 be received. GS 2013-005 2013 to 2017 Financial Plan Statement of Objectives and Policies It was moved, seconded and carried that it be recommended to Council that the report entitled “2013 to 2017 Financial Plan Statement of Objectives and Policies” be received and: a) the Statement of Objectives and Policies for 2013 to 2017 be confirmed as presented; and, b) the proposed dates to deliberate the 2013-2017 Financial Plan be confirmed as presented. GS 2013-006 Building Inspector’s Report – December 2012 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Building Inspector’s Report for December 2012 be received. GS 2013-007 Trolley Report – December 2012 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Trolley Report December 2012 be received. GS 2013-008 Fire Chief’s Report It was moved, seconded and carried that the Fire Chief’s Report December 2012 be received. GS 2013-009 Coastal Animal Control Services – December Pound Report It was moved, seconded and carried that the Coastal Animal Control Services December Pound Report be received. COUNCIL SUBMISSIONS GS 2013-010 It was moved, seconded and carried that the following issues be referred to the next Government Services Committee meeting: • Public Input Process Government Services Committee January 21, 2013 Page 2 • NEW BUSINESS Volunteer Appreciation Dinner Frank Jameson Community Centre Holiday Hours The Committee directed Staff to consider allowing the Frank Jameson Community Centre to remain open for all days other than statutory holidays during the December holiday season. . Crosswalk Signals in High Traffic Areas The Committee directed Staff to review the possible establishment of signaled crosswalks within Town boundaries. Outdoor Lighting in Vicinity of Frank Jameson Community Centre The Committee directed Staff to review the outdoor lighting at the Frank Jameson Community Centre and also on the west side of First Avenue from the Credit Union to Metheun and the pathway from the Ladysmith Maritime Society facility. Dunsmuir’s Parlour Car The Committee directed Staff to investigate the possibility of acquiring Dunsmuir’s Parlour Car. Repairs to “Knight’s Clock” The Committee directed Staff to investigate what is required to repair “Knight’s Clock”. GS 2013-011 Clean Up of Alley Behind Traveller’s Hotel It was moved, seconded and carried that it be recommended to Council that a letter of appreciation be sent to Staff for recent clean-up efforts of the alleyway behind the Traveller’s Hotel. ADJOURNMENT GS 2013-012 It was moved, seconded and carried that this meeting of the Government Services Committee be adjourned at 5:55 p.m. CERTIFIED CORRECT Chair (Councillor G.
