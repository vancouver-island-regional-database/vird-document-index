THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: November 27, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Acting Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Capital Project Bylaw 116315 BACKGROUND: The Ministry of Education - School Funding and Allocation has approved the funds for: Project No. 116315 Transportation - $138,991 Replacement School Bus – 80+ passengers Prior to establishing a capital account for this project, a Capital Project Pro-Forma Bylaw is required. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw 116315 Bus Replacement, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the Bylaw’s title and giving a summary of its contents. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 116315 in the amount of $138,991 and that it be given first reading. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 116315 in the amount of $138,991 and that it be given second reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three reading of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 116315 in the amount of $138,991 at its Regular Board Meeting of November 27, 2013. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 116315 in the amount of $138,991 and that it be given third and final reading. BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 CAPITAL PROJECT BYLAW 116315 A BYLAW by the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (hereinafter called the "Board") to adopt Capital Project Bylaw 116315 Replacement School Bus. WHEREAS in accordance with provisions of the School Act the Minister of Education (hereinafter called the "Minister") has approved a capital plan of the Board. NOW THEREFORE the Board agrees to the following: (a) upon approval to proceed, commence the Project(s) and proceed diligently and use its best efforts to complete the project substantially in accordance with the Project Agreement (where required); (b) observe and comply with any rule, policy or regulation of the Minister as may be applicable to the Board or the Project; and, (c) maintain proper books of account, and other information and documents with respect to the affairs of the Project, as may be prescribed by the Minister. NOW THEREFORE the Board enacts as follows: 1. The capital project of the Board approved by the Minister and specifying a maximum expenditure of $138,991 is hereby adopted. 2. This Bylaw may be cited as “School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 116315 – Bus Replacement. READ A FIRST TIME THE 27th day of November 2013. READ A SECOND TIME THE 27th day of November 2013. READ A THIRD TIME, PASSED AND ADOPTED THE 27th day of November 2013. _________________________________ ______________________________ Board Chair Secretary-Treasurer I HEREBY CERTIFY this to be a true and original copy of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Capital Project Bylaw No. 116315 adopted by the Board the 27th day of November 2013. ____________________________________ Secretary-Treasurer
