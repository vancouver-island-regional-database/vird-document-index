NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: June 23, 2021 Board of Education Assistant Superintendent, Don Balcombe Board/Authority Authorized Course Framework Recommendation That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the following courses as Board/Authority Authorized Course (BAA) as per Ministry Policy: Peer Tutoring 10 French Immersion; Peer Tutoring 11 French Immersion; Peer Tutoring 12 French Immersion; Westcoast Wilderness 11; Westcoast Wilderness 12 Background BAA courses provide an opportunity for educators to explore content beyond the boundaries of Ministry curriculum. BAA courses help educators respond to the local needs of schools and their communities, and to provide choice and flexibility for students. Under the Ministry guidelines all locally developed courses that may count for graduation credit at the Grade 10, 11, 12 level must be submitted as Board/Authority Authorized Courses and be approved by the Board of Education. Peer Tutoring 10 French Immersion, Peer Tutoring 11 French Immersion, Peer Tutoring 12 French Immersion, Westcoast Wilderness 11, Westcoast Wilderness 12, have been reviewed by District staff and recommended for approval by the Board of Education.
