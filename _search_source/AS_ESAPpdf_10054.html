NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: September 28, 2022 Board of Education Scott Saywell, Superintendent Environmental Sustainability Action Plan Recommendation That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopt the attached draft Environmental Sustainability Action Plan. Background As you are aware, the third goal of the Board’s strategic plan is to be a leader in environmental stewardship and sustainability. At the July 6, 2022, Special Board meeting the Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability Advisory Committee (the “Committee”) presented the draft Environmental Sustainability Action Plan (“ESAP” or the “Plan”) for consideration. The Board then passed the following motion: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) recommends that staff make the attached draft Environmental Sustainability Action Plan available to the public for consultation immediately, as per Policy 2.17 Public Participation, and to report back in September with public feedback and any proposed changes. Attached to this Action Sheet is the draft ESAP. Discussion Significant consultation with a variety of stakeholders led to the Plan as presented. Community Engagement Plan Utilized the community engagement webpage and included background information with instructions on how to participate during the consultation period: https://www.sd68.bc.ca/board/communityengagement/ Information on the community engagement period was: • Emailed to all district staff via district email; • Emailed to all families via School Messenger platform; • Posted in district news; and • Shared on social media (Facebook, Twitter and Instagram) Action Sheet: Environmental Sustainability Action Plan Page 2 The consultation period began on July 7, 2022. The community had until August 31, 2022 to provide feedback by emailing consultation@sd68.bc.ca Timeline • Thursday, July 7, 2022 o Email to staff and families o Social media posts • Monday, July 25, 2022 o Reminder email to staff and families o Social media posts Messaging Email to staff and families: July 7 message: The Board has begun a consultation period on the DRAFT Environmental Stewardship Action Plan.
