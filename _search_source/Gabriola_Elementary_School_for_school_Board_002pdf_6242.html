Gabriola Elementary School Embracing our Learners and our Community Did you know that there are Schools in BC that Educate outside their Walls? • Maple Ridge – environmental school, place based K-7 • Davis Bay – NEST (Nature Education for Sustainable Todays and Tomorrows) K-4 • Sooke District – Nature Kindergarten What do they all have in common? • Place based – process of using local community and environment as a starting point to teach concepts in language arts, mathematics, social studies, science, and other subjects across the curriculum. Sobel, 2004 • Connect classrooms and community • Based on principles of inquiry and inclusion • Hands on, experimental, ecological and imaginative • Integrate First Peoples Principles of Learning – well being of self, family, community, land, spirit, and ancestors; roles and responsibilities What is Gabriola Elementary School’s Vision? • Place based eco education school • Location • Educational • Permaculture days http://vimeo.com/171020029 • Exploring a partnership with SFU • Partnering with Community – GIRO, PHC, CYMHSU, Streamkeepers, Islands Trust • Partnering with Qwam Qwum Where are we going? • Teach awareness and critical thinking skills • Inquiry based learning • Linking LiD • Hands on learning • Project based authentic learning All using our environment as the vehicle How can you help us? • Support our Collaboration with SFU • Support the development of our Outdoor Classroom as a place for learning • Celebrate with us as we move in our new direction Comments and Questions?
