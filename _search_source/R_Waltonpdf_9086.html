To my knowledge, none of the School Board Trustees are health professionals or research scientists in the field of epidemiology, immunology, or infectious disease; nor are they privy to the specific medical history of school staff and students. I find it highly inappropriate that school board trustees are being put in a position to even be considering dispensing medical advice, such as vaccine mandates. I do not wish our School Board to allow this kind of dangerous precedent to be set. I am sorry that you have been put in this position.
