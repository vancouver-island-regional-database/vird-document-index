ANNUAL TRUSTEE ZONE REPORT Trustee Pellegrin School Year: 2022/2023 School Names/Zone: Rock City elementary Annual Highlights: Rock City is home of the tigers. Micheal Lundine has been the principal for a few years and brings a lot of passion to this awesome school. They are one of the sites of the new daycare centres, exciting changes for the neighborhood. The PAC’s fundraiser of choice has been for the library. The librarian Laura Jacobs is well loved and a huge asset. There are 12 languages at the school, newer this year are 3 different types of Arabic. The translation apps on phones are such a great tool and Dale Burgos was fantastic and figuring out how to get permission slips home to parents and guardians in the multiple languages. There is a vibrant energy in the halls of Rock City, with incredible art every direction. They have an annex with new clothes and shoes, runners for compassion have donated shoes and coats for a great community resource. The number of students accessing more food has grown a bit and NLPS foundation and the good food box are amazing resources for all our schools. Rock City has music club, board game club, life club (lgbtqia and inclusion) as well as athletics club. Pacific sports have been offering martial arts on Mondays for grade 3s and 4s, this has been great and well attended. The enrollment having gone a bit below the usual number has meant a loss for a vice principal here, a big adjustment to a school that has had this role filled for many years. Great staff and EA’s, a real sense of community at Rock City.
