May 15, 2014 Ref: 172925 Dot Neary, Chair Board of Education School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 395 Wakesiah Ave Nanaimo BC V9R 3K6 Email: dneary@sd68.bc.ca Dear Ms. Neary: Thank you for your letter of March 10, 2014, informing me of a motion recently passed by the Nanaimo-Ladysmith Board of Education. I appreciate you taking the time to share the views of the board with me. I understand you are concerned with Government’s decision to appeal the BC Supreme Court ruling on Bill 22, and I can assure you that the decision to appeal was not taken lightly. As you know, the BC Court of Appeal recently granted a stay on Justice Griffin’s ruling pending the resolution of the Province’s appeal. The stay is significant in that it allows districts to maintain their current class structures without fear of future court action or union grievances regarding previous collective agreement language. It is my understanding that the BCPSEA has provided advice to all school districts in this regard. With respect to your comments about teacher bargaining, it is my firm belief that the British Columbia Public School Employers’ Association continues to bargain in good faith with the BC Teachers’ Federation. Both their proposals and discussion with the union have been put forward in a genuine attempt to reach a fair and respectful new agreement. I have full confidence that the BCPSEA is representing school districts in a manner that is in the best interests of our students and communities, and our common future. Once again, thank you for sharing your concerns directly with me. Sincerely, Peter Fassbender Minister Ministry of Education Office of the Minister Mailing Address: PO Box 9045 Stn Prov Govt Victoria BC V8W 9E2 Location: Parliament Buildings Victoria
