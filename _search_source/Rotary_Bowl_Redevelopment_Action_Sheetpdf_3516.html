THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: November 23, 2011 TO: Board of Education FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations SUBJECT: Rotary Bowl Redevelopment Reference:  School District 68/City of Nanaimo Joint Use - Rotary Bowl Redevelopment Study Action Sheet Background: At the October 25, 2011 SD68/City of Nanaimo Joint Use Meeting, the City of Nanaimo and District discussed the Rotary Bowl Steering Committee’s recommendation that the Study be received as the guiding document for the long term redevelopment and management of Rotary Bowl. The Joint Use Committee recommended the following motion as attached: That School District 68/City of Nanaimo Joint Use recommend that: 1. The Rotary Bowl Redevelopment Study be received as the guiding document for the long term redevelopment and management of Rotary Bowl; and 2. Staff be directed to draft a co-management agreement for review and consideration as outlined in Rotary Bowl Redevelopment Study, to include School District 68, City of Nanaimo, Vancouver Island University, and the Nanaimo Track & Field Club. The Joint Use Committee discussed the wording “co-management” be changed to “joint-use management” agreement. It was the unanimously agreed upon by the Joint Use Committee, track representative, and VIU representative. It was discussed at the November 8th Facilities Planning Committee meeting. Therefore, staff proposes the following recommendation. Recommendation That the Board of Education of School District 68 recommends that: 1. The Rotary Bowl Redevelopment Study be received as a resource document for the long term redevelopment and management of Rotary Bowl; and 2.
