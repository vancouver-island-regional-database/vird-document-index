Enrolment estimates for funding purposes must be calculated based on a student’s Full Time Equivalent (FTE) enrolment which is 1 Headcount equals 1 FTE for Kindergarten to Grade 9 but for Grades 10-12 and Adult Learners is based on .125FTE per course taken so can be +/- 1.0FTE per Headcount.
