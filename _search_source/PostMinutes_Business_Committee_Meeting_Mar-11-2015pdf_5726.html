THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF BUSINESS COMMITTEE MEETING HELD MARCH 11, 2015 PRESENT: Trustees S. Kimler, Chair J. Solomon, Vice-Chair N. Bob J. Brennan T. Brzovic S. Higginson S. Rae B. Robinson N. Routley ABSENT Staff D. Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO G. Roberts, A/Secretary-Treasurer J. Blain, Deputy-Superintendent P. Sabo, Director of Planning/Operations D. Burgos, Director of Communications K. Toye, Executive Assistant IN ATTENDANCE M. Ball, NDTA Rep R. Zver, CUPE Rep C. Lintott, NSAA Rep S. Miller, DPAC Rep CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. ADDITIONS TO THE AGENDA There were no additions to the Agenda this evening. DELETIONS TO THE AGENDA There were no deletions to the Agenda this evening. CHANGE IN ORDER There was no change in order to the Agenda this evening. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held March 11, 2015 Folio B15/03/11-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY APPROVAL OF THE MINUTES Minutes - February 11, 2015 Folio B15/03/11-02 PRESENTATIONS That the minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held February 11, 2015 be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY There were no presentations this evening. CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM REGULAR BOARD MEETING Mr. P. Wyse-Seward & Ms. A. Wyse-Seward RE: Jr. All Native Basketball Tournament Folio B15/03/11-03 UNFINISHED BUSINESS IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that they waive the rental fees for this year's Junior All Native Basketball Tournament, but that they not waive the actual cost of the custodial fees and supplies. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY There was no Unfinished Business on the Agenda this evening. NEW BUSINESS Budget Process and 2015-2016 Budget Calendar - Mr. Roberts Folio Mr. Roberts addressed the Board on this year's budget approach. Staff would like Board participation and suggestions on how to create a balanced budget for 2015-16. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan The Business Committee recommends to the Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held March 11, 2015 B15/03/11-04 Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that they approve the schedule and the timelines of the budget schedule. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Transportation Overview - Mr. Sabo / Mr. Prevost Mr. Prevost went through his Transportation Overview presentation (attached to the Agenda) and answered any questions the Trustees had on Transportation. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) to direct staff to provide an updated report to the next Business Committee Meeting, the viability and cost of providing busing for the students from the Cedar/Cinnabar area to Ladysmith Secondary School for one year. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Folio B15/03/11-05 Specialty Programs Discussion - Trustee Brennan Mr. Blain review with the Board the 2013-14 and 2014-15 District Academies Data. Refer to the attachment on the Agenda for more information. That the Board of Education direct the Business Committee to have as an Agenda item on the March or April Agenda the matter of fees and charges to students and families for specialty programs and enrichment programs. BCTSA February 19-20, 2015 Conference Update - Trustee Brzovic Folio B15/03/11-06 FOR INFORMATION Trustee Brzovic provided a brief update on the BCSTA Conference she attended. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Bob IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that they initiate a communication strategy to raise awareness within the community of the budget shortfalls and oppositions to it. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held March 11, 2015 QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions from the audience this evening. ADJOURNMENT Folio B15/03/11-07 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Routley That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 8:38 pm.
