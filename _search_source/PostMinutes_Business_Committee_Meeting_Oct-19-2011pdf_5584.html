UNFINISHED BUSINESS Budget Calendar 2011-12 The committee reviewed the draft calendar for the budget-setting process for the 2012-13 school year. The calendar includes a number of Special Business Committee meetings, consultations with parents and employee groups. The calendar sets April 26 as the date for the budget to be finalized. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Allen Folio B11/10/19-03 That the Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that it approve the draft Budget Calendar for 2012-2013. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 3 Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held October 19, 2011 NEW BUSINESS Trustee Budget Mr. Turin reviewed the actual budget expenses for the Trustee budget for 2009-10, 2010-2011 and the preliminary budget for 2011-12. The Trustees’ budget covers the costs of trustees’ stipends; supplies; travel, conferences and training; and B.C. School Trustees Association fees. This year, there will be an additional cost for the school trustee election on November 19. Business Committee Work Plan The Committee reviewed a proposed annual work plan for the Business Committee that includes the budget-setting process; receiving financial and enrolment updates; and reviewing facilities topics. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary Folio B11/10/19-04 That the Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that Work Plan be referred to the Policy Committee to recommend procedural changes which would enable the Business Committee to maintain an annual work plan as follows: September Trustee Budget: Review Actuals/Recommend/Refer Prior Year’s October Set Committee Work Plan Recommend next year’s Budget Schedule to Board Enrolment: 1701 Received Enrolment: Receive/Refer/Recommend Catchment Change Report from FPC November Budget/Actual/Forecast Update # 1 as at October 31 Program/Initiative Review Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held October 19, 2011 4 December Trustee Remuneration: Refer/Recommend three year Trustee remuneration (AFG; Capital Plan; Budget Update No. 2; FPC Report on space issues January-April Budget Process for the next year January Review &recommend approval of current year’s Amended Budget CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY FOR INFORMATION There were no information items this evening.
