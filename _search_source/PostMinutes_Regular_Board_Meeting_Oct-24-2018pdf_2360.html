Opposed by Trustee Routley CARRIED SENIOR STAFF REPORTS Superintendent Saywell and Trustee Rae Re: Premier's Award for Excellence Finalists On October 5, 2018, Premier John Horgan presented the Premier’s Awards for Excellence in Education. The nine individual awards are intended to honour the achievements of education professionals working in public, independent, First Nations and offshore schools. Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools is extremely proud to have three of its staff nominated for this prestigious award. Mandy Jones from Ladysmith Secondary and Emily Recalma from NDSS were nominated for the Indigenous Education Award. Tanya Adelborg from Randerson Ridge Elementary was nominated for the Extracurricular Leadership Award. Ms. Jones and Ms. Adelborg were the winners in their respective categories. Winners were awarded commemorative artwork, $3,000 personal bursary for professional learning and a $2,000 contribution to their school community for professional learning. Superintendent Saywell Re: Board Accomplishments Superintendent Saywell presented a video of the Board's accomplishments from their four year term. Superintendent Saywell and Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh Re: Trustee Recognition Superintendent Saywell and former Superintendent Blain presented the outgoing and returning Board members with certificates of achievements for their service ranging from one to six terms.
