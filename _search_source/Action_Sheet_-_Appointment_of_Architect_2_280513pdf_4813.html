THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET TO: The Board of Education FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Brent Spohn, Facilities Planning Supervisor DATE: July 3, 2013 SUBJECT: Appointment of Architect  Conversion of Cedar Secondary to Cedar Elementary REFERENCE: 10-Year Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan Board Policy No. 4006 Selection of Architect Board Procedure No. 4006P Selection of Architect BACKGROUND: On June 26, 2013 the Board of Education approved the following closure bylaws:    Bylaw 2012-05 Close Cedar Secondary June 2014 Bylaw 2012-07 Close Woodbank Primary June 2015 (and relocate to new Cedar Elem.) Bylaw 2012-08 Close North Cedar Intermediate June 2015 (and relocate to new Cedar Elem.) The intent of the above Closures is to convert Cedar Secondary to an elementary school to accommodate students from North Cedar Intermediate and Woodbank Primary in September of 2015. The approved closures leave a construction window of approximately July 1, 2014 to July 30, 2015 to convert the building and August of 2015 to prepare for students in September of 2015. A conversion of this nature (a 425 student) secondary into an elementary school is a substantial planning project which requires the involvement of a qualified architect. In accordance with board policy on selection of architects, staff is recommending that the District engage the services of KMBR Architects Planners Inc. to provide the design and construction supervision required. Tight timelines demand that planning start immediately such that start up can happen over the summer with the school based design team meetings starting in the fall and completing in the spring of the 2013/14 school year to prepare for construction start in July, 2014. Funding for the project (design and then construction) would be budgeted in the 2013/14, 2014/15 and potentially 2015/16 AFG years. KMBR was the original design firm for Cedar Secondary, which was completed in 2001. KMBR has substantial elementary school design experience including District 68 schools, Randerson Ridge, Cinnabar Valley and the addition to Hammond Bay; as well they are currently working on Wellington Secondary Seismic upgrade. They have also previously completed a secondary to elementary conversion at Roy Stibbs Elementary School in School District 43 (Coquitlam). RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the immediate hiring of KMBR Architects Planners Inc. to complete the planning and design work required to convert Cedar Secondary to Cedar Elementary for school opening in September 2015.
