SENIOR STAFF REPORTS Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh Re: Capital Bylaw 2018/2019 Folio BC18/05/09-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve all three readings of the Capital Bylaw No. 2018/19-CPSD68-01 at the May 23, 2018 Board meeting. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh Re: Electoral Boundary Adjustment between SD#68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) and SD#79 (Cowichan Valley) Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh reviewed the Electoral Boundary Adjustment between School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) and School District No. 79 (Cowichan Valley). A map detailing the boundary adjustment is attached to the Agenda. The Order in Council making this change official is attached to the Agenda. Assistant Superintendent Tait & Assistant Superintendent Tait and District Vice Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held May 9, 2018 District VP, Aboriginal Education Tenning Re: Aboriginal Education Principal of Aboriginal Education Tenning presented an overview of Aboriginal Education in the district. The presentation is attached to the Agenda. CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM REGULAR BOARD MEETING There were none. UNFINISHED BUSINESS Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh Re: Operating Surplus/Deficit Policy Folio BC18/05/09-05 Superintendent Blain Re: Former Trustee Recognition Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh reviewed the draft Operating Surplus and Deficit Policy as distributed in previous meetings. The draft Policy is attached to the Agenda. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Solomon IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) enter into a 30-day consultation period regarding the Operating Surplus/Deficit Policy. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Discussion on guidelines/criteria for Trustee recognition. Superintendent Blain and Trustee Brennan will work together on criteria and will report out at the next Business Committee meeting. The Committee recognized that the BCSTA has a recognition program in respect of Trustee tenure. NEW BUSINESS Referred Motion from Education Committee Re: Graduation Rates/Board Strategies Folio BC18/05/09-06 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Higginson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) request staff to present at the October Education Committee a report on the various strategies in place to improve all graduation rates in the district. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held May 9, 2018 FOR INFORMATION QUESTION PERIOD There were none. ADJOURNMENT Folio BC18/05/09-07 That the meeting be adjourned.
