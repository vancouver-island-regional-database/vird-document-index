Brooks, DASG Rep CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:01 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio SB/05/05-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held May 5, 2015 PRESENTATIONS Mr. Rob Zver President, CUPE 606 RE: 2015-16 SD68 Budget CUPE 606 President Rob Zver presented the CUPE approach to the 2015-16 budget. They requested that the Board instruct district senior management to take the previous years' surplus and that money be used in maintaining services and allow all sides to take the next year to build an alternate plan to achieve a sustainable budget. NEW BUSINESS The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that it adopt the 201516 budget as presented. Mr. Blain presented and updated draft budget following last week's initial discussion on the 2015-16 proposed budget to the Business Committee. The following amendments were made:    $500,000 be moved from Cedar Secondary reserve to custodial FTE for the year 201516. A review of senior management/excluded staff organization for efficiencies to be completed by February 2016. That any district surplus for the year 2014-15 up to $500,000 be returned to the Cedar reserve fund. Trustee Higginson proposed they delay making a decision. A Special Business Committee meeting is scheduled for Wednesday, May 6, 2015 prior to the Education Committee Meeting. UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was no Unfinished Business this evening. QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions this evening.
