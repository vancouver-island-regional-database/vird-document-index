GREG & VOLUNTEERS! BIKES FOR KIDS Together with the RCMP, our mortgage team came up with a wonderful way of giving back. Our bikes are donated by the RCMP, local businesses, our clients and community. With the donation of time and repairs from volunteers we have these bikes serviced and ready to get out to children and adults in our community who could really use them. With the support of the school districts, Nanaimo Multicultural Society, Nanaimo Youth Association, and Nanaimo Volunteer Association, we are able to distribute these bicycles within the schools. We are also able to distribute bikes to those in need with a recommendation by individuals like you. Thank you! This is a 100% non-profit program we are doing. “I moved to Nanaimo in 1998 with Cindy and our children. The sense of community and giving back, whether it be monetary or volunteering, is strong in Nanaimo. I am proud to call this place home and happy to give back whenever possible. Our “Bike for Kids” program has provided 3400 bikes to children in our community to date, and continues to be successful. Thank you for participating.” Greg Nowik If you are in need of a bike please give us a call! You can pick one out, and donate a children’s size bike in return... or 2 or 3. 250.758.5524 We also know that children grow out of their bicycles. We are always looking for donations of small bikes that we can get road ready and give to children in need. Simply call us or drop them off at our office and we will do the rest. Like us on Facebook!
