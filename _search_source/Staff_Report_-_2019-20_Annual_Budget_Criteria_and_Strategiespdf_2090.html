From the Department of Finance Date: May 1, 2019 To: The Board of Education From: Taunia Sutton, Director of Finance Subject: 2019-20 Annual Budget Criteria and Strategies NLPS Goals under the strategic plan are: • • • • Meet each student’s unique needs The continuous improvement of instruction and assessment Reconciliation Organizational effectiveness and accountability to support student learning NLPS Budget Criteria for 2019-20 with associated Board Goal and Budget Strategies: 1. Maximize success/completion rates for our most vulnerable students Board Goal - Meet Each Students Unique needs 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o o o o o Maintain the 18/19 addition of 2 Graduation Success Coordinators Maintain the addition of 6 CYFSW’s positions added in 18/19 (total now 32) Maintain the Woodlands (VAST) Satellite School added in 18/19 from restricted surplus, now funded in operating with a cost of $275,000 Maintain the Focus School Expansion implemented in 18/19 which increased the number of schools from 4 to 12 Maintain the 18/19 increase to the EA Contingency hour bank by 329.5 hours Learning Services reorganization of Student Support Services and Diversity and Equity to better support student focus and outcomes 2. Optimize our schools and facilities as safe, healthy and inclusive learning spaces and work places Board Goal - Organizational effectiveness and accountability to support student learning 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o o Maintain the addition of 6 CYFSW’s positions added in 18/19 (total now 32) Support Facility maintenance and improvements through AFG and Local Capital Maintain the addition of the Mental Health and Addictions Manager to support Mental Health awareness and initiatives Increase Comprehensive School support from .20FTE to .3FTE to expand programing to Secondary Maintain the additional 1.867 Caretaker FTE added in 18/19 which includes .50FTE for Emergency Caretaker time to ensure school sites are hazard free for student, staff and public safety o o 3. Provide greater supports and opportunities to embed Indigenous culture, teachings and curriculum for all students Board Goal – Reconciliation 19/20 Budget Strategies: Addition of a Truth and Reconciliation Manager Allocated Reconciliation Budget of $50,000 for determined initiatives per the Reconciliation Committee o o 4. Provide a variety of pathways for every student to meet his/her desired learning outcomes Board Goal - Organizational effectiveness and accountability to support student learning 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o Maintain the reorganization and additional supports added to CTC, ICE and DL in 18/19 which included clerical, a CYFSW, an Outreach Teacher and an Administrator Continue the portable renewal program at Woodlands VAST which includes NDTA, CUPE and Facilities staff supporting student learning 5. Provide adequate and sustainable budgets for facilities and technology to sustain necessary infrastructure and space needs Board Goal - Organizational effectiveness and accountability to support student learning 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o o o o o $1,250,000 Budget in Local Capital for space needs that may be required and not funded by Ministry Capital $1,322,000 Technology budget in Local Capital to support District/Student needs Complete the Long Range Facilities Plan; budget for completion $25,000 Addition of 2 Information Technology CUPE positions to provide more IT in-service/support Addition of 3 Facilities staff to support additional facilities /space needs Continue Equipment replacement programs – year 3 of 3; TBD for future budgets 6. Maximize support for student learning in literacy and numeracy Board Goal - The continuous improvement of instruction and assessment 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o Maintain 4 Literacy Coordinator positions that were added in 18/19 (total now 6) Addition of 1 Speech and Language Pathologist 7. Support employee wellness throughout the school system Board Goal - Organizational effectiveness and accountability to support student learning 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o Maintain the 18/19 addition of the Mental Health and Addictions Manager to continue mental health awareness, support and initiatives Maintain the Health and Wellness Consultant position that was originally funded through a onetime Special Purpose Grant provided by the MOE in 2016/17; the District has now fully incorporated the position into the Operating Grant 8. Maximize opportunities for student participation in co-curricular programs Board Goal - The continuous improvement of instruction and assessment 19/20 Budget Strategies: o Shift Academy entrance from catchment based to open registration based where placement is by try-outs or lottery, thereby providing more equitable opportunity for students 9. Optimize support for students’ transitions into our schools, within our schools, and from our schools Board Goal - Meet each student’s unique needs 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o Added a second Secondary Curriculum/Instruction Coordinator to assist with successful transformation/transition to post-secondary Maintain the Strong Start and Ready Set Learn Programs to improve transitions into our schools 10. Optimize our capacity for system-wide leadership support and succession Board Goal - Organizational effectiveness and accountability to support student learning 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o o o o o o District Leadership Development Committee established; $10,000 budget for 1st year implementation Maintain the additional Administration time added to Secondary School in 18/19 to support improved Graduation rates, leadership, and succession planning = 3.3FTE Maintain the additional Administration time added to Elementary in 18/19 to support the Focus Schools expansion, leadership, and VP succession planning = 1.85FTE Maintain the $24,500 NVCI Training budget for established for Kindergarten Teachers Maintain the $10,000 budget for the Teacher Mentorship program Continue the VP Learning series implemented in 18/19 Continue the Teacher Meet and Eat initiative which focuses on the District’s Learning Framework 11. Optimize the use of technology as a tool to support student learning Board Goal - The continuous improvement of instruction and assessment 19/20 Budget Strategies: o o o Continue to ensure the Student to Device Ratio is 2.5 by allocating resources to Local Capital for Technology Upgrade the District’s Wi-Fi provided there is enough Local Capital funding to support the scope of work Maintain the Education Technology Coordinator added in 18/19 12.
