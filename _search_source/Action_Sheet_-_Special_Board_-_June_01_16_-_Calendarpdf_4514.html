BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools SPECIAL BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: June 1, 2016 TO: Board of Education FROM: John Blain, Superintendent RE: Local School Calendar Background: During Spring 2015, with Board approval, the district consulted with stakeholders and the community about a 3-year local school calendar that would include: - Extending the three-year local school calendar to the 2017-18 school year Implementing elementary school local schedule guidelines Implementing secondary school local schedule guidelines Increasing spring break to two weeks Removing a non-instructional day and replacing it with an instructional day Following consultation, the Board approved a 3-year local school calendar to include 2015-16 up to the 2017-18 school year. Information: Based upon the Board’s approved 3-year school calendar, and discussions with the NDTA and the Nacu Mat Tatulat Committee, staff has approved the attached Local School Calendar dates for the 2016-17 school year. One non-instructional day has been added for the purpose of Curriculum In-service and is identified on the attached Local School Calendar for the 2016-17 school year. A 30-day notice period has been provided to the district, community and stakeholders, as required. Recommendation: The Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves the Local School Calendar for the 2016-17 school year.
