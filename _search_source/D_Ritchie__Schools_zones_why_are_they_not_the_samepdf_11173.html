I have questions regarding some of your school zones.   Why is there no consistency with all of them being the same? They should be all the same. Examples The schools on Departure Bay Rd. You have at the school down by departure bay rd, with a very small one block in front of the school, the school higher up departure bay says school zone also and has the posted speed just in front of the school. Then you get the the senior Wellington school and there is no school zone sign, there is not speed limit so people zoom through.   Then when you go up on Uplands drive you have about 3 blocks of school zone with no school, the school is up a side road. This makes no sense, at least 3 blocks of a school zone with no school. Then I also believe on Hammond there is another school with no school zone. How are you to protect children when you have no consistency in regards to school zones. For visitors to the area it becomes very confusing. I suggest that all of the school districts get together and have the same consistency across all of BC.   I would suggest that someone fixes these issues.
