NOTICE ABANDONMENT OF LEASE DISTRICT LOT 2016, COWICHAN DISTRICT Notice is hereby given that the Town of Ladysmith intends to abandon Crown land lease 106431, file 1407751, over District Lot 2016 to the Province in accordance with section 45 of the Land Act. This abandonment will take place no later than December 31, 2023. The Town will not receive any compensation upon abandonment. Information regarding this proposed disposition can be obtained from Allison McCarrick, Chief Administrative Officer, 250.245.6401, amccarrick@ladysmith.ca. This Notice of Disposition (sale, lease or licence) is provided in accordance with section 26(3) of the Community Charter.
