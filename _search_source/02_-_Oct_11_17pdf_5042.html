UNFINISHED BUSINESS Trustee Reduction Folio BC17/10/11-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Solomon IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae That the Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District No. 68 direct staff to begin the process of implementing a reduction of the number of trustees in the district to 7. This change will be in place for the upcoming election in November 2018. Opposed by Trustee(s) Brzovic, Routley, Kimler, Robinson, Rae, Brennan Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held October 11, 2017 DEFEATED NEW BUSINESS Policy Development Discussion RE: Donation Requests Folio BC17/10/11-04 Chair Kimler advised that this is a preliminary discussion for policy development regarding donation requests. Staff to supply a list of requests on how many annually that are received. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Solomon IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan That the Business Committee recommend the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to develop, with input from the Board, a policy and/or administrative procedure regarding donation requests and bring this policy and/or administrative procedure forward in the spring of 2018.
