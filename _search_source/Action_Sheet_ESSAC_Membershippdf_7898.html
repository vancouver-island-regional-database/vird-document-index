NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: March 10, 2021 Board of Education Charlene McKay, Board Chair Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability Advisory Committee (ESSAC) Recommendation That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the membership of the Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability Advisory Committee; and that the membership draft a Terms of Reference for the committee for approval by the Board. Background The Board of Education Strategic Plan includes a number of objectives, including to establish a Board Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability Committee. Information This committee will be established as an advisory committee to the Board of Education according to Policy 2.5 Roles of Committees and Representatives, Section 2 Advisory Committees. The Superintendent, Board Chair, and Vice-Chair have worked collaboratively together to create the following draft membership for the Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability Advisory Committee (ESSAC): • • • • • • • 2 students Partner group representation (1 each): CUPE, NDTA, NSAA, DPAC NLPS staff: Energy Manager, Department of Facilities (1), other relevant staff positions as determined Syeyutsus Saays’um 2 Trustees, one of whom will be appointed as Co-Chair of the committee Superintendent, or representative Other committee members may be invited as needed. Action Sheet: Environmental Stewardship and Sustainability Advisory Committee Page 2 In drafting the proposed membership of ESSAC, we discussed the inclusion of Indigenous Rights Holders within the geographic area of SD68, and concluded that an open invitation to join this Board Advisory committee at any time will remain in place for the duration and life of the committee for Rights Holders. Furthermore, the Syeyutsus Saays’um will facilitate the participation of the Indigenous Knowledge Keepers should they wish to provide guidance within the capacity of this committee. Upon the Board’s approval of the membership, invitations to participate in the committee will be sent out. Once members have been identified, an initial committee meeting will be called to determine a draft Terms of Reference for ESSAC. The Terms of Reference for ESSAC will be brought to the Board for final approval according to Policy 2.5, Section 2.4. Finally, Vice-Chair Stanley is assigned as the Board appointed Co-Chair of ESSAC.
