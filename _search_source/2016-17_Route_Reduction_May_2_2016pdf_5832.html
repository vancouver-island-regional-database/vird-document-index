THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: May 2, 2016 TO: Board of Education FROM: John Blain, Superintendent/CEO SUBJECT: Bus Route Reduction Information The following is an updated version of budget reduction information for the Transportation Department. Bus Route 7 – For 2016-17, the trips on this route can be combined creating a savings. Savings can also be obtained by not providing service to JBS from the Cedar attendance area. Service to students would remain the same with the exception of adjusted bus pick up and drop off times meaning this change would not create safety issues for students. Bus Route 10 – can be eliminated with little adverse effect to student safety. Trip 1 would see no service from the Cedar attendance area to LSS being provided so the only students affected would be LSS students using the bus service from the former Davis Road School site to LSS, presently there 3 to 8 students utilizing this service. In regards to safety, these 3 to 8 secondary students would have use of sidewalks, street lights or transit service to get to and from LSS. LSS is 3.6 KM from Davis Rd. Trip 2 (Route 10 S. Wellington to N. Cedar, Woodbank) would be combined with Route 8 with no impact on safety for students. There would be no additional costs to provide this service on Route 8. Bus Route 14 – Savings can be obtained by not providing service to JBS from the Cedar attendance area. No safety impact to students. Bus Route 25 – Scheduled to be eliminated June 2016 as per Board Motion: R15/04/22-12. Bus Route 26 – Scheduled to be eliminated June 2016 as per Board Motion: R15/04/22-12.
