Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION INFORMATION SHEET DATE: February 22, 2017 TO: Board of Education FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Brian Hackwood, Manager of Maintenance SUBJECT: Testing for Lead Content in Drinking Water Background: This is an update to the previous Information sheet presented to the Board on November 23, 2016. On February 24, 2016, the Ministry of Education directed School Districts in British Columbia to begin a program of testing for lead content in all facilities built prior to 1990. Districts were to work with their local Health Authorities to implement a testing program. On September 23, 2016, a plan for the implementation of the Ministry’s policy was approved at the staff level with the following criteria: 1. Environmental Engineering firm to be retained through tender process to test for lead content and interpret the results 2. Consultant to develop testing strategy based on Ministry policy and VIHA recommendations 3. Consultant to develop remediation options On November 18, 2016, Tetra Tech Environmental was awarded the tender to test for lead content in the drinking water  Testing at two high schools, eight elementary schools and three Support buildings commenced early December 2017. Next Steps     Testing is expected to wrap up the last week of February. The tender deadline for the complete report with recommendations is March 10, 2017. Staff foresee no issues with Tetra Tech meeting this timeline. Testing to be submitted to Ministry by March 31, 2017 School District to work with VIHA to establish mitigating procedures, if required.
