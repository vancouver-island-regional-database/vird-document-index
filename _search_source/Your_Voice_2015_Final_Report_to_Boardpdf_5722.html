Your Voice 2015 Report to Board of Education Your Voice 2015 Board motion On January 14, 2015, the Board of Education of School District No. 68 Nanaimo Ladysmith passed a motion suspending the completion of Cedar Elementary for the 2014-15 school year. By passing the motion, AFG project funding for Cedar Elementary was released in favour of other maintenance projects across the district. Furthermore, the motion states that the Board enters into a 90-day consultation process to begin a full review of the Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan. Sixty days will be used to gather input from stakeholders and the public and 30 days will be for review and reporting back. At the end of the 90 days, the Board will make a recommendation on the educational use of Cedar School and the Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan. This process will start no later than February 9 and end no later than May 13. Your Voice 2015 The YOUR VOICE 2015 review and community input plan was presented to the Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools Board of Education on Wednesday, January 28, 2015. The Board voted in favour of adopting the plan. The goal for Your Voice 2015 was to seek feedback on the Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan and the 2015-16 budget. Your Voice 2015 The Board initiative launched on Tuesday, February 10, 2015 and was open to district staff, students, their parents/guardians and the general public until the closing date of Friday, March 20, 2015.
