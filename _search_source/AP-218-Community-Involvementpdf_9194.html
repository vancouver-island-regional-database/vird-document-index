Appendix B Administrative Procedure 218 – Community Involvement AP 218 – Community Involvement Purpose The Board of Education wishes to encourage the active participation of the school administrators, staff and students in those community-based activities and programs that affirm and reflect the internal programs and policies of Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools.
