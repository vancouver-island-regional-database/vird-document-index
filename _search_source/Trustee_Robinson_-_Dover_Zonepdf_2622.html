Trustee Zone Report 24-May-2017 Dover Zone  Seaview has a fundraiser each month or each year to raise money for the Grade 7 camping trip. Every division is involved. This past month they had a, “Break a Rule” day. The idea is that students can “break a rule” if they pay a loonie. I paid a few dollars when the kids caught me turning up in classes wearing a hat or chewing gum. It was great fun and a great idea.  Frank J. Ney had an extraordinary art show. The students showed off their favourite art project from the year. The library was filled with banners featuring art projects, the Kindergarten classes welcomed parents into their classroom and the gym was full of tables and each of the older students stood with their work to tell us why they chose each piece they selected. One of my highlights was getting a tour of Mrs. Zolob’s Kindergarten class by one of her students.  Pleasant Valley was the school selected by the Port Theatre this year to produce their spring show on May 8. They did an amazing, Beatles Review. I visited Pleasant Valley and FJN on the same afternoon. Without a doubt it was the best few hours I have ever enjoyed as a Trustee. Trustee Robinson
