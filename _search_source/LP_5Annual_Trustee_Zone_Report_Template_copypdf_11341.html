ANNUAL TRUSTEE ZONE REPORT Trustee Pellegrin School Year: 2022/2023 School Names/Zone: Uplands Park Elementary Annual Highlights: Uplands Park is home of the Panthers. Mike Ingalls joined as a new principal late last year, he came to Uplands from Cedar as vice principal. He dove right in and brings a lot to the school. I love the close knit community feeling, every morning there’s breakfast offered and smiles from the early bird students especially with french toast day. Students deliver fruit and veggies to the classrooms on delivery days. Uplands helps families with the good food boxes, backpack buddies and lunches and snacks. The PAC’s main fundraiser has been a playground and they are hoping to have this installed in the spring. On active student transportation week some of the classrooms had 100% participation. There’s a group of grade sevens in a leadership program who help plan spirit days! uplands has a very artistic librarian, four days a week and they do fine arts club in the library as well, there is a musical in the works for Christmas right now. Volleyball is big at uplands and there are 10 languages at this school.
