THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 688 MINUTES OF SPECIAL BOARD MEETING HELD MAY 2, 2012 Trustees J. Brennan, in the Chair D. Allen B. Bard N. Curley K. Howland D. Neary B. Robinson T. Saunders S. Welch Staff D. Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO P. Turin, Secretary-Treasurer D. Reimer, Director of Communications C. Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No.S19 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio S12/05/02-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Annual Budget 2012-2013 and Bylaw Mr. Turin said that thanks to a forecasted budget surplus in the current year, next year’s budget does not include any reductions in services. He said the budget totals $134.5 million and includes • Operating budget - $121.1 million • Special purpose fund budget - $7.5 million • Capital fund expenditures - $5.9 million Mr. Turin then proceeded to read the budget bylaw for 2012-2013. First Reading Folio S12/05/02/-02 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Welch IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Neary That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $134,543,865 for fiscal year 2012-2013 and that it be given first reading. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 2 Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held May 2, 2012 Second Reading Folio S12/05/02-03 Unanimous Consent Folio S12/05/03-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Saunders IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Bard That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $134,543,865 for fiscal year 2012-2013 and that it be given second reading. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $134,543,865 for fiscal year 2012-2013 for a third and final time. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Third and Final Reading Folio S12/05/02-05 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $134,543,865 for fiscal year 2012-2013 and that it be given a third and final reading. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions this evening. ADJOURNMENT IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Howland IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Saunders Folio S12/05/02-06 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:06 pm. Jamie Brennan, Board Chair Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer
