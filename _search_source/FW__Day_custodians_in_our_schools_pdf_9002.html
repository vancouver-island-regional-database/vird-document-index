CAUTION: External Message Dear SD68 Board Members, I wish to acknowledge these unprecedented times and the hard work you all do to support our children and communities. I am also compelled to express my concern and extreme disappointment that the province is not providing day custodians this year. I hope this district will make room in the budget to support additional cleaning. I fully support any motion that includes advocating for day time cleaning similar to last year. I will have a kindergarten age child attending school in your district and feel keeping cleaning standards high will have nothing but positive effects for our teachers, support staff and students.
