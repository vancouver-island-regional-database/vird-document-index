NEW BUSINESS 10.1 Trustee Brzovic Re: Notice of Motion - Masks Trustees discussed mask wearing in elementary schools. Local Medical Health Officer, Dr. Sandra Allison, attended for this portion of the meeting and answered Trustee questions. 2 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Barron That the Board of Education for School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) require masks for Grade 6 & 7 students in all indoor areas, including when they are in learning groups, with exemptions for students who cannot wear masks for health or behavioural reasons, effective upon the return from Spring Break. R21/03/10-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Barron IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Stanley Motion to Amend That the Board of Education for School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) require all students in grades 6 & 7 to wear face masks in schools, both within and outside their learning groups, in a manner consistent with the most recent health and safety guidelines for middle and secondary schools outlined by the Ministry of Education. OPPOSED: Trustees Higginson, McKay, Wilkinson, Robinson CARRIED On the Motion as Amended: R21/03/10-05 That the Board of Education for School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) require all students in grades 6 & 7 to wear face masks in schools, both within and outside their learning groups, in a manner consistent with the most recent health and safety guidelines for middle and secondary schools outlined by the Ministry of Education.
