Discussion The draft Policy contains a various structural and substantive changes. Structural The following are structural changes. The Policy: • has been put into the new policy template; • has merged the responsibilities with respect to the agenda and presiding at a meeting into a single section; • has action oriented language; • has clarified duties associated with the Superintendent and duties associated with trustees; • has made it clear that while the requirement to sharing information is present, however there is some discretion on the Chair’s part with respect to this matter; • has assigned the monitoring of the Superintendent’s expenses. Action Sheet: Revised Policy 218 – Role of Chair and Vice Chair Page 2 Substantive The following are substantive additions. The Policy: • commits the Board to distributed leadership - it specifically envisions the Chair making room for trustees to perform leadership activities as appropriate; • recognizes that the Vice-Chair shall have all the responsibilities of the Chair in the event of the Chair’s absence; • provides clarity that the Chair shall assign committee and family of school assignments; • requires the Chair to develop relationships with Rightsholders and local and provincial officials and third parties; • requires the Chair to model leadership qualities and adhere to Board policies; and • requires the Chair to address conflict and breaches of the Code of the Conflict.
