Opposed by Trustee Bonkowski CARRIED Mr. Turin presented the most current enrolment projections for the district and outlined the impact of those projections on the district’s budget for the next two years. He said that the district’s enrolment in 2011-12 is projected to be about 13,476, compared to 13,604 this current school year. In 2012-13, the enrolment is projected to be 13,192. It also expected that there will be a significant reduction in the number of students who qualify for special 3 Draft Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held February 16, 2011 needs funding.
