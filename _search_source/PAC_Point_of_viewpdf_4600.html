PAC Point of view. Having our school go through the closure consultation process again is extremely difficult for parents and children. The instability caused by the consultation process interferes with most of the PAC functions. Parents and community members are less likely to be involved with fundraising for a school that "might close". We are part of a Neighbourhood school that is in a PERFECT location surrounded by homes. This allows many parents to be involved in before and after school activities. Many parents at our school are also able to help out during school hours as well. Our "HUGE" playground has been paid for by PAC fundraising efforts. The majority of it being less than 6 years old. We have been fundraising the last few years to upgrade the remaining areas. This includes "Our Life Skills" program. Our school Library Annually funded by PAC is one of the best in the district. It takes time for parents and students to feel part of a school. The longer they are at the school the more comfortable they are being involved. We have a great group of parent volunteers that work hand in hand with the staff of our school. As a PAC we spend much of our valuable time doing everything for our children and their school.
