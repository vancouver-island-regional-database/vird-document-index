From: Doug catley Sent: May 28, 2020 3:42 PM To: Superintendent ; District Administration Centre Manager Cc: Doug Catley ; Toth Susan; Stanley Jessica Subject: South Wellington School Property CAUTION: External Message Request. could this please be forwarded to all trustees and involved staff. Thank you. South Wellington has suffered the loss of a community centre when the school was closed. It has always been hoped that the school would fall into the hands of the community and that the once donated property would return to the community as it was donated by a community member to serve the community. More than ever it has become evident that we need to invest in our communities. I would ask that the school district facilitate the transfer of the school and property to the community. Thank you for your consideration of this matter. Doug Catley
