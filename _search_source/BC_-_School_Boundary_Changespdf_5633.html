THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET TO: Special Business Committee FROM: Facilities Planning Committee DATE: March 9, 2011 SUBJECT: Consultation on School Boundary/Catchment Changes References    Policy 3020 (attached) Rationalization of Elementary School Boundary Changes (attached) Boundary Change Illustrations (attached) Background At the March 1st Facilities Planning Committee the following motion was passed: That the Facilities Planning Committee recommends to the Business Committee that Staff should proceed with the consultation process for the above recommended boundary adjustments and return to the Board of Education with recommendations for final boundary adjustments after the consultation process is complete. Staff recommended that catchments be adjusted at the following elementary schools in accordance with the attached “rationalization”:  Coal Tyee  Pleasant Valley  Forest Park  Quarterway  Chase River The changes at Coal Tyee and Pleasant Valley are due to the closure of Mt Benson and housekeeping. Forest Park, Quarterway and Chase River adjustments are housekeeping items and mainly due to the Parkway cutting off a portion of the schools catchment area. This recommendation is made in accordance with Policy 3022. The recommended adjustments reflect realist attendance patterns, support existing choices by families, clean up issues from the Parkway construction and affect few students. Staff expect to grandfather existing student affected by the changes. Consultation would proceed in the fall of 2011 and it is expect that new boundaries would take affect for September of 2012.
