Kelsey Toye From: rutherford@openandthriving.comon behalf of Rutherford Open and Thriving Sent: To: Subject: Thursday, June 09,2016 4:12 PM KelseyToye Keep Rutherford School open June 9, 2016 4:11pm — Dear Sir or Madam: The proposed closure of Rutherford ElementarySchool will have a permanent effect on the education of current and future students. Rutherford has 227 schoolchildren,along with 9 Life Skills students,who deserve a quality education in their community. Please do not make Rutherford students walk to Frank Ney (5.2 km), Randerson Ridge (2.6km) or McGirr (2.6km) each day to only to be faced with overcrowding and limited access to the resources they need. As School Board trustees you have been elected and entrusted with the education of local children. The time has come to speak on behalf of those children who are counting on you to do the right thing. Please keep Rutherford Elementary open and thriving. Also, as a current UVic student, I am grateful for the wonderful experience I had during my time at Rutherford Elementary. I have countless memories which I still cherish, and I am very thankful for the great education I received. Please do not close the school. Regards, Brenden Johnston brendenj@uvic.ca 4049 Gulfview Drive
