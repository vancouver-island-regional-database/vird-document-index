Appendix C Administrative Procedure 108 – Board Recognition AP 108 – Board Recognition Purpose Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools will recognize Board members based on total completed terms of public service. Procedure The following levels of recognition are at the sole discretion of the Board and are proposed for all current and past School Trustees:  One to Two Completed Terms o Acknowledgement Certificate  Three or more Completed Terms o A Trustee will receive a pin for the duration of their time on the Board of Education. Trustees with more than three completed terms will receive a pin that corresponds with their most recent completed term.  Four to Five Completed Terms o Individual photo and bio in Trustee’s album. If the Trustee is elected for another term, this information will be updated at the end of the term.  Six or more Completed Terms o Commemoration plaque with photo and bio. The plaque, must first be nominated by the Board of the day, and will be displayed on a designated wall in the Boardroom. Adopted: September 26, 2018 AP 108 – Board Recognition Page 1 of 1
