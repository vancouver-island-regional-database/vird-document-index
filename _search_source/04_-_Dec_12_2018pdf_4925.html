NEW BUSINESS Trustee McKay Re: Committee Structure IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee McKay IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Keller Folio BC18/12/12-03 That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) request that staff reconfigure the physical layout of the committee meeting structure to support inclusive participation by all of the partner groups and staff. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held December 12, 2018 Trustees agreed that all committee partners be able to participate in discussions at the table once a motion have been brought forward. Trustee Keller Re: Community Engagement Folio BC18/12/12-04 Trustee Keller made a motion to re-enact the Policy Committee and to have that committee create a policy on community engagement. Trustee Stanley suggested an amendment to the motion, which was motioned as noted below. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Keller IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Stanley That the Business Committee recommend that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) in partnership with senior staff create a Policy Committee, including terms of reference, for the purpose of reviewing, updating, and drafting Board Policies and Procedures.
