Meeting Time/Location: Thursday, September 25 2025, 9:40AM - 9:55AM PST Forests (Room 337), Parliament Buildings, Level 3 Community/Organization Information: Meeting ID: 335 Community/Organization: Ladysmith (Town) Contact: Allison McCarrick, CAO Primary Phone: 250-245-6400 Primary Email: amccarrick@ladysmith.ca Secondary Email: jtierney@ladysmith.ca Cabinet Minister: Forests Community/Organization Attendees: Allison McCarrick, CAO;Deena Beeston, Mayor;Duck Paterson, Councillor Topic #1: Forestry Access to Economical Viable Fibre Background: As forestry continues to be a major economic driver in both rural and urban communities across BC & as these communities continue to see needless job losses due to impacts of extreme tariffs imposed by the US and lack of access to economically viable fibre; the Town urges the Province to increase access of economically viable fibre as well as explore new export markets to grow a stronger, more diversified and resilient economy. Request: The Town requests that the Province take immediate steps to secure at least 45,000,000m3 of economically viable fibre for 2025 & continue to increase access to economically viable fibre by 5,000,000m3 each year until achieving the annual allowable cut totals set by the Chief Forester.
