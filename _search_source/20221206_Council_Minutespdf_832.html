DELEGATIONS 7.1 Bikers Against Child Abuse Tom Goudreau of Bikers Against Child Abuse (BACA) discussed the mission of this international organization and provided an overview of the services they provide for abused children, both locally and throughout the world. Mayor Stone thanked him for his presentation, and for the work that BACA does. 7.2 Students Taking Action Against Racism TJ Nyhan, representative from the Students Taking Action Against Racism (STAAR) group at Ladysmith Secondary School, provided an overview of racism in the community and advised Council that they could address racism through their hiring procedures, data collection, dialogue, policies and bylaws. Mayor Stone thanked TJ for the presentation and expressed enthusiasm about working with TJ and the members of STAAR in the future. 8.
