CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Busing Implementation Director of Planning and Operations, Mr. Sabo, provided an update to the Board, stemming from a previous Board motion that provides busing from the Cedar area to Ladysmith Secondary (LSS) for the 2015-16 school year. To receive bus service to LSS the following criteria must be met: • The student must be registered at LSS. • The student must live in Woodbank/North Cedar Intermediate or Cinnabar Valley catchment areas. • The student must register for the service. The report is available online, attached to the Agenda. Draft Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held May 13, 2015 Cedar Conversion Director of Planning and Operations, Mr. Sabo, provided the Business Committee an update of the Cedar Elementary to Secondary School conversion. As of January 2015, the conversion of Cedar Secondary to an elementary school was halfway through the process and renovations were not completed. District forces working on the conversion were re-assigned to other projects. Mr. Sabo provided three summary steps to advance and implement conversion: 1) Restart the re-design, planning and consultation process for the Cedar conversion. 2) Re-engage KMBR Architects and Planners Inc. and begin discussions regarding the redesign work required. 3) Remobilize district forces to begin construction as soon as resources are available. The report is available online, attached to the Agenda. QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions from the audience this evening.
