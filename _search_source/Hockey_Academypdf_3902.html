Dear Mr. Hutchinson, My name is Jamie Davis and I am the President of the Nanaimo Minor Hockey Association. I am writing to request an opportunity to present a plan for extending the Hockey Canada Skill Academy to students at the elementary school level. Last year the NMHA facilitated over 850 minor hockey players on four rinks, with the majority of players at the grade 7 and under age group. Our presentation will include: - The academic and athletic benefits to students and the community. - Examples of similar programs in other schools and communities. - The logistics of how the self funded program can be implemented. - Parent and student interest. Given the success of the Woodlands Hockey Academy, and in view of the many other communities that offer similar programs (see attachment), we feel that there is a positive appetite from parents/players/coaches for extending the program to the Pee Wee (grade 6-7) level. We look forward to your response.
