Committee of the Whole Recommendations to Council January 23, 2024 At its January 16, 2024 meeting, the Committee of the Whole recommended that Council direct staff to: 1. Prepare a report for a future Committee of the Whole meeting regarding the possibility of dedicating 1200 Christie Road, and road allowances at King Road and Gill Road as parkland. 2. Prepare a report for a future meeting of Council on requiring subdivisions to allocate active transportation space.
