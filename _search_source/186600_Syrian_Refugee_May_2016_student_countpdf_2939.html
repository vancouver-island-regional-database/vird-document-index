March 15, 2016 Ref: 186600 To: All Secretary-Treasurers All School Districts Re: May 2016 count for Syrian refugee students In support of school districts who are welcoming new Syrian students into their classrooms, government has committed to an additional enrolment count on May 27, 2016 to fund Syrian refugee students enrolled in BC schools after the February 12, 2016, newcomer refugee student count. The funding will be provided as a discretionary grant to boards of education using a basic allocation rate of $1,790 per FTE. Supplemental funding of $345 per FTE will be available for new Syrian refugee students who qualify for English/French Language learning. Additional information on the process and specific timelines for claiming funds for these students will be relayed shortly by the Ministry’s Data Management Unit. In the meantime, districts should ensure they are collecting appropriate supporting documentation for the Syrian students arriving in their schools. As British Columbia’s teachers and schools begin accepting Canada's largest refugee resettlement movement of this generation, the Ministry thanks you for your continued support as these new Canadians settle into our local communities.
