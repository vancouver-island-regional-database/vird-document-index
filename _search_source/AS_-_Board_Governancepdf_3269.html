BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools REGULAR BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: November 28, 2012 TO: Board of Education FROM: Dave Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO SUBJECT: Notice of Motion - Governance BACKGROUND: Following the election, the Nanaimo-Ladysmith Board of Education identified ‘improving governance’ as a priority. In support of this, the Trustees participated in a governance development process during the first several months of their term of office. A critical step in the Board’s development process was to affirm the governance model change in new policies. In support of this, the Board distributed revised draft policies and procedures for notice of motion.
