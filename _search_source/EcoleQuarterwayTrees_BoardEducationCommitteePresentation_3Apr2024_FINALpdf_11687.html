Ecole Quarterway Trees Project Presentation to SD#68 Board’s Education Committee April 3rd, 2024 PROJECT DETAILS • PAC to plant a mixture of 17 native and non-native coniferous and deciduous trees in three areas of the school grounds • PAC to purchase trees and watering accessories ($7,500 total) and has committed to ensuring trees are watered until established (~3 years) • City of Nanaimo to donate tree watering bags (~$650), provided Urban Forestry Coordinator’s expertise on tree species selection and siting, and will allow PAC to use City discount at Green Thumb Nursery • Trees planted in April 2024 when ground dries out for machine access; Grounds crews to install trees • $4,400 total NLSP staff labour: Planning ($2,000) and Grounds installation ($2,400) PROJECT BENEFITS • Increase opportunities for our children to develop their ecological literacy, environmental stewardship, and connection to the land. • Help restore ecological function, provide shaded outdoor spaces, and enhance environmental learning opportunities. • Regenerating, being stewards of, and honouring our interdependence on the land is part of our District’s path towards Truth and Reconciliation. • Trees will help buffer our children from noise and pollution and have been found to promote increased active play and improved mental health. • Opportunity for Ecole Quarterway students, parents, and staff, the District, the City of Nanaimo, and the broader community to unite around a project that will provide a positive environmental legacy for current and future generations.
