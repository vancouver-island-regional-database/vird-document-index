July 19, 2017 Ref: 195032 John Blaine, Superintendent Carrie McVeigh, Secretary Treasurer School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Email: jblain@sd68.bc.ca; carrie.mcveigh@sd68.bc.ca Dear Mr. Blaine and Ms. McVeigh: Further to our conference call in June 2017, I am writing to confirm that the Ministry of Education has identified an error in the metes and bounds descriptions that set out the boundaries of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) and School District No. 79 (Cowichan Valley). Specifically, the metes and bounds descriptions create a small gap in the boundary between the two school districts, in an unpopulated area west of the City of Duncan. To address this issue, the Ministry has retained a geomatics consultant to assist in the revision of the metes and bounds descriptions. Currently, the metes and bounds description of the boundary for the Nanaimo-Ladysmith School District is set out in Order-in-Council 1882 (dated August 21, 1980). The portion of the boundary in question is described as following a “due west” direction from Stocking Lake to Block 1111: “… along the middle line of Stocking Creek to the southerly outlet of Stocking Lake; thence due west in a straight line to the easterly boundary of Block 1111; thence northerly and westerly along the easterly and northerly boundaries of Blocks 1111, 1396…” The boundary, when following a “due west” direction, in fact intersects with Block 1396 rather than Block 1111. As a result of this inconsistency, there is a small gap in the boundary between the two school districts. The Ministry proposes to correct the metes and bounds description to reflect the fact that the boundary intersects with Block 1396. The following language may be used to correct the description: “…along the middle line of Stocking Creek to the southerly outlet of Stocking Lake; thence due west in a straight line to the easterly boundary of Block 1396…” Ministry of Education Governance and Analytics Division Mailing Address: PO Box 9146 Stn Prov Govt Victoria BC V8W 9H1 Tel: 250-356-6760 Fax: 250-953-3225 www.gov.bc.bc/bced -2As you may know, the School Act provides Cabinet with the authority to modify school district boundaries. In order to address this boundary issue, the Ministry will be making a proposal to Cabinet to amend the Order-in-Council that sets out the boundary of the Nanaimo-Ladysmith School District, and to make a corresponding amendment to the Order-in-Council that sets out the boundary of the Cowichan Valley School District. As we understand from our teleconference in June, you plan to take this matter to your board. Once the board has considered the matter, I would ask that you please provide any feedback they may have to Ms. Sarah Loveday, Senior Legislative Analyst, by September 30, 2017. Ms. Loveday may be reached by email at sarah.loveday@gov.bc.ca or telephone at (250) 507-8028. Thank you for your ongoing assistance with this matter.
