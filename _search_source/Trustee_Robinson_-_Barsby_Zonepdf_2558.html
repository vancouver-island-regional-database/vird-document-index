Trustee Zone Report 18-Oct-2017 Barsby Zone  I have had time to visit all of my schools at least twice and had lunch with all of my elementary teachers  The most frequent thing I am hearing from teachers and principals is the need for more EAs when the budget can manage it.  I thoroughly enjoyed attending and speaking at the McGirr inclusive playground official opening at the school and the celebration the next evening at the golf club.  A couple of days later I visited the District Life Skills class at Georgia and checked out their playground. There are three kids in wheelchairs in that program who can’t use that playground because of the gravel surface. I know correcting that issue is expensive but I think we need to make that a priority if at all possible. L4L Patti Edwards (Sna-Naw-as)  Patti reported that as they move forward with their economic development, they are very please with the number of your, many educated in SD68, who are stepping forward and getting involved. Joy Bremner (Mid Island Metis)  VIEX received a grant to create a reconciliation blanket  They have had 200 applications for status in the past few months  They have money for youth programs which they will be working on  A Metis “guide” has been hired for 20 hours a week to support Metis students at VIU Inga Nielsen Cooper (Tillicum Lelum)  Christmas fundraiser is coming up next week at the Coast Bastion. This raises money for Christmas baskets for many kids in our inner-city schools  The Youth Hub program is working well  They lost the grant that supported the fitness program at the Neutral Zone as did the Metis  There is a continuing problem with drug abuse around the building Snuneymuxw  Hul’qumi’num lessons ae continuing on Tuesdays and Thursdays Derek Beeston and Jeff Hasenauer (Learning Alternatives and John Barsby Community School) Derrek:  CUPE employees did a great job organizing the move from NDSS  Things are “tight,” but they are making the best of it. They lost two classrooms in the move from NDSS, but they are about to gain one back soon.
