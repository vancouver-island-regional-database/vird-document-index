NOTICE TRANSFER OF LEASE DISTRICT LOT 2016, COWICHAN DISTRICT November 25, 2022 Notice is hereby given that the Town of Ladysmith intends to assign a crown land lease over District Lot 2016. A marina is currently located on the property. The Town intends to seek provincial approval to assign Lease 106431 to the Stz’uminus First Nation. Lease 106431 is a crown land lease held by the Town and expires in 2029. The Town will not receive any compensation in exchange for transferring the lease. Marina infrastructure on site is not owned by the Town. Information regarding this proposed disposition can be obtained from Matt O’Halloran Corporate Officer, 250.245.6417, mohalloran@ladysmith.ca. This Notice of Disposition (sale, lease or licence) is provided in accordance with section 26(3) of the Community Charter.
