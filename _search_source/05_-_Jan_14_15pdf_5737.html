Draft Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held January 14, 2015 The Business Committee discussed the Cedar document provided. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Higginson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae Folio BC15/01/14-04 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) to suspend the completion of Cedar Elementary for the 2014-15 school year, thereby releasing project AFG funding in favour of other maintenance projects across the district. Furthermore, that the Board of Education enter into a 90 day consultation to begin a full review of the Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan (EFLP). 60 days will be used to gather input from stakeholders and the public and 30 days will be for review and report back. At the end of the 90 days the Board of Education will make a recommendation on the educational use of Cedar School and the EFLP. This process will start no later than February 9 and end no later than May 13. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Motion to Amend IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Robinson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Higginson Folio BC15/01/14-05 That the motion be amended and separated into two parts: 1. The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education to suspend the completion of Cedar Elementary for the 2014/15 school year, thereby releasing project AFG funding in favour of other maintenance projects across the district. 2. The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education to enter into a 90 day consultation to begin a full review of the Enhanced Facilities for Learning Plan (EFLP). 60 days will be used to gather input from stakeholders and the public and 30 days will be for review and report back. At the end of the 90 days, the Board will make a recommendation on the use of Cedar School and the EFLP. This process will start no later than February 9, 2015 and end no later than May 13, 2015.
