FROM THE FACILITIES DEPARTMENT Annual Facilities Grant (AFG) 2013-2014 Folio B13/03/13-03 Wellington Seismic Upgrade Update Mr. Sabo reviewed the proposed priorities for the 2013-14 Annual Facilities Grant with ther Business Committee. The grant, which is expected to be about $2.6 million for School District 68, is for school facilities projects such as roof replacements, mechanical and electrical system upgrades. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley That the Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education that the 2013-2014 Annual Facility Grant Plan in the value of $2,613,085 be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Sabo updated the Business Committee on the Wellington Secondary seismic project. He said that The Capital Project Agreement with the Ministry of Education has been signed and the Board has passed a bylaw authorizing the project. Staff are working on issuing the Construction Manager Request for Proposals and design meetings with consultants and school representatives are underway. The project is Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held March 13, 2013 expected to take more than three years, with completion in 2016. UNFINISHED BUSINESS NDSS Pete Sabo gave the Business Committee an update on discussions the district has been having with City of Nanaimo traffic staff and the RDN Transit about improvements that could be made to improve safety along Wakesiah Avenue in front of NDSS. He said that some of the changes would be made by the city and others by the school district. It is expected that the plan will be finalized soon, with work to take place during the summer.
