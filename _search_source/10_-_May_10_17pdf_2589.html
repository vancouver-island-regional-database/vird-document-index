CARRIED Trustee Higginson Abstained Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held May 10, 2017 Folio BC17/05/10-06 Folio BC17/05/10-07 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Bob That the Business Committee recommends the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive the report dated April 12, 2017, on reducing the number of trustees and potential ward system. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Higginson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae That the Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) enter into a public consultation process with the groups outlined in the Secretary-Treasurer's report on the possibility of reducing the number of trustees from 9 to 7 in the 2018 municipal elections. Further, staff present the results of the consultation to the Business Committee meeting in September 2017. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Higginson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic Folio BC17/05/10-08 That the Business Committee recommends the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive the report dated April 12, 2017, on establishing the position of student trustee. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY That the Business Committee recommends the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive the report dated April 12, 2017, on establishing the position of student trustee and provide notice of motion of its intent to amend Policy 2.2 Approach to Governance by adding the following wording: "Respectful and meaningful student participation in District governance: to give voice to the views and aspirations of students in District governance by establishing the position of Student Trustee." IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Solomon AMENDED IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held May 10, 2017 Folio BC17/05/10-09 That the Business Committee recommends the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive the report dated April 12, 2017, and refer the matter to public consultation to amend the current policy on Trustee Remuneration such that Policy 2.6, Section 7.1.1 be amended to read: "7.1.1 Trustee remuneration is to be reviewed by the Board in September in each year; such review is to be based on remuneration paid to trustees of comparable British Columbia School Districts." Opposed by Trustee(s) Brzovic, Solomon CARRIED NEW BUSINESS Trustee Solomon RE: Financial Literacy & It's Relation to Poverty Issues Folio BC17/05/10-10 Trustee Solomon presented to the Business Committee on awareness to financial literacy in curriculum and the importance of this knowledge to our youth. The reference material to the presentation is attached to the Agenda. Acting Superintendent Saywell advised the Business Committee of where currently financial literacy resides in the K-12 curriculum. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Higginson That the Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to explore the need to establish a pilot project to address the issue of financial literacy and report back to the Board before January 2018.
