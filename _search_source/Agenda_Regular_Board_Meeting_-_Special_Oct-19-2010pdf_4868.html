Agenda Item Number 4.1 BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 DISTRICT FACILITIES PLAN CHANGE PROCESS BYLAW A BYLAW by the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (hereinafter called the "Board") to adopt a process to effect changes to the District Facilities Plan (hereinafter called the “Plan”). WHEREAS THE Board has adopted a long term district facilities plan after an extensive public consultation process; and, WHEREAS THE Board in adopting the long term facilities plan adopted certain facilities planning documents and policies, including, but not limited to guiding principles and a capital planning framework; and, WHEREAS THE Board is desirous of establishing a process whereby changes to the long term facilities plan will only be made from a policy change perspective and be totally transparent NOW THEREFORE the Board agrees to the following process: (a) any proposed change(s) to the Plan will be based on a policy decision made by the Board; (b) such proposed change(s) to the Plan will be referred to the district’s Facilities Planning Committee with direction to prepare a report back to the Board on the implications to the district’s long term capital planning direction that the proposed change will have on that direction, accompanied by recommendations for changes to the Plan; (c) any proposed change(s) to the Plan will be made available publicly for the period during which the Facilities Planning Committee completes its work, said period shall not be less than one month; (d) recommendations to changes to the Plan, if accepted by the Board, will be distributed for public consultation, in a form and manner to be determined at the time, for a period of not less than two months; (e) the Board will consider all feedback from the public consultation process prior to making any change(s) to the Plan.
