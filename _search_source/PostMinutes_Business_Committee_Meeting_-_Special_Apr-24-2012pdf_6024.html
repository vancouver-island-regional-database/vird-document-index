CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS Budget 2012-2013 Mr. Turin presented the draft budget for 2012-13 to the Committee which has been restated using the Public Sector Accounting Board (PSAB) standards. He said that the provincial government has directed school districts and other public organizations to start using the PSAB standards, for school districts effective July 1st. The PSAB format includes a number of changes from the way that school district budgets have previously been structured. Under PSAB, the school district budget bylaw will be made up of Draft Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 24, 2012 2 three sections: • Operating expenditures • Special purpose fund expenditures • Capital fund expenditures A number of revenues and expenses that were previously included in the operating budget, such as CommunityLink and StrongStart funds, will now be reported in special purpose funds. The district must also include information pertaining to investments in capital assets and its associated revenues and expenditures in its budget documents. As a result of the changes, the district’s budget bylaw for 2012-13 will total approximately $134.5 million.
