Property Owner/Tenant Consultation: The Film Company must attempt to obtain consent from affected property owners and tenants if filming will: block access to private property (e.g. driveway or door), affect access for persons with disabilities (e.g. block a wheelchair ramp), create a nuisance on private property (e.g. excessive noise), or disrupt services to private property (e.g. electrical, water, solid waste collection, transit, etc.). Note if some property owners refuse to provide consent it does not necessarily mean a film permit application will be denied and brief interruptions to access/services do not require consent. Property and property owner information is available from Parcel Map BC and the Land Title Office.
