SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) DRAFT PROCEDURE NO. 3841P STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICES STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICES MISSION Supporting Student Diversity STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICES VISION 1. 2. 3. 4. With a student-centered focus we advocate for each student’s success through respectful support, recognition of individual learning needs and preparation for life-long learning. Through a shared commitment we use our expertise strategically to provide leadership that is proactive, progressive and effective in building success for each student. By building strong relationships with students and each other, we foster inclusion through belonging, hope and pride. Through collaborative teamwork we are resourceful, informed and innovative as we align our practices to support student learning. PROCEDURE Student Support Services in School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) will strive to meet or exceed "Service Delivery" as per the guidelines established in the Ministry of Education publication "Special Education Services: A Manual of Policies, Procedures and Guidelines".1 School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) promotes integration as one of the major strategies used to achieve inclusion and it should be noted that with integration, students who have special needs are included in educational settings with their peers who do not have special needs, and provided with accommodations determined on an individual basis, to enable them to be successful there. The principle of "placement in the most enabling learning environment" applies when decisions are made about the extent to which an individual student is placed in regular classrooms, or assigned to a specialized alternative placement.
