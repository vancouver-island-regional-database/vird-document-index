THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 688 MINUTES OF SPECIAL BOARD MEETING HELD AUGUST 7, 2014 Trustees D. Neary, in the Chair B. Bard J. Brennan N. Curley K. Howland B. Robinson T. Saunders S. Welch Staff D. Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO D. Reimer, Director of Communications Absent Trustee Allen P. Turin, Secretary-Treasurer CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 4:31 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPOINTMENT OF ACTING SECRETRY-TREASURER Appointment of Acting Secretary-Treasurer Folio S14/08/07-01 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley NDTA Presentation IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio S14/08/07-03 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) appoints Superintendent Dave Hutchinson as Acting Secretary-Treasurer for this meeting. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) allows the NDTA to make a ten minute presentation to the Board. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held August 7, 2014 APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio S14/08/07-02 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley That the Agenda be approved as amended. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS NDTA Presentation Nanaimo District Teachers Association First Vice-President Shannon Iverson made a presentation, stating the union’s opposition to the government plan to provide parents with $40 a day if a settlement is not reached in the BCTF strike by the time school opens. Board Response to Provincial Government RE $40 Day Payment for Parents The Board discussed how a prolonged strike creates financial hardship and inconvenience for many families and that the offer of $40 a day to parents is impractical and will create inequities. The Board said that any savings from the strike should go back into the public education system. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio S14/08/07-03 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) write to the provincial government to express the Board’s opposition to the plan to provide funds to parents for daycare or tutoring assistance if a settlement is not reached in the BCTF strike by the time school opens, and to ask the government to rescind the plan immediately and put the funds back into the education system. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Saunders IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan Folio S14/08/07-04 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) write to the BCSTA to ask how the “Back to School Action Plan” was developed and how Boards might be able to participate in further discussions on these matters. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held August 7, 2014 QUESTION PERIOD Chair Neary responded to the questions from a member of the audience. ADJOURNMENT IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio S14/08/007-05 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 5:00 pm.
