THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: February 9, 2011 TO: The Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Proposed Local School Calendar 2011-2012 BACKGROUND: The Board needs to consider whether or not a Local School Calendar will be considered in the budget planning process for the 2011-12 budget. In order to be in compliance with the School Act, the Regulations and the Boardâ€™s Bylaw No. 5, a period of at least one month of consultation is required. A Board must adopt the school calendar at a meeting that is open to the public and at least a month before the meeting, give notice of the meeting, together with a copy of the proposed school calendar to the parents of students in the schools affected and representatives of employees assigned to the affected schools. If the Board approves the consultation process tonight, the Board will hold a Special Board Meeting on Monday, March 28th to give final consideration of a local school calendar for 2011/12. This will give more than a month of consultation assuming that the parents of the students and representatives of employees are notified by Friday, February 11, 2011 (approximately 46 days). The anticipated savings, if the local school calendar is approved, is estimated at this time to be similar to the estimate provided in the 2010/11 budget planning process at $500,000.
