An objective in the sustainability progress report of the Town of Ladysmith’s 2014 Annual Report is to ‘protect parks and natural ecosystems’. The indicator for this objective is to quantify the amount of protected natural greenspace and parkland within the Town boundary. Research and analysis associated with the Parks, Recreation and Culture Master Plan indicates a total parkland amount of 96.5 hectares, or 238 acres; representing an allocation of 110 square metres of parkland per person. Declining Participation in Organized Sports Participation in sport has continued to decline in Canada over the past decade. Sport participation levels decline with age beginning at the age of 12 and dropping off considerably beyond the age of 20. Aquatics Swimming is a favoured activity for people of all ages. Community Parks are diverse in nature, serving a broader purpose than the neighborhood or mini parks.
