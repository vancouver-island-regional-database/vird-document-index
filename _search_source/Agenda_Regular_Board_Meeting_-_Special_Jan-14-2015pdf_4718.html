NEW BUSINESS 4.1 Cedar - 30 Day Hold That the Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education to enact a 30 day hold on the construction and awarding of contracts at Cedar Elementary, but permit CUPE work that must be completed whether the board decides the school is best suited as a K-7 facility or a 8-12 facility. 4.2 FSA Question and Letter The Education Committee recommends to the Board that the board chair write a letter to parents that presents unbiased pro/con information on the FSA (Foundation Skills Assessment) exam, including checkboxes for opting out of the exam (as per BC legislation) and direct staff to honour parents requests to opt out of the exam, without further contact. 4.3 Correspondence ( to view correspondence, please go to the online agenda at: http://bit.ly/1xSqrJW ) 1.
