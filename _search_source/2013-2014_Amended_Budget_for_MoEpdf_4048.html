Board has complied with the provisions of the Act respecting the Amended Annual Budget adopted by this bylaw. 2. This bylaw may be cited as School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Amended Annual Budget Bylaw for fiscal year 2013/2014. 3. The attached Statement 2 showing the estimated revenue and expense for the 2013/2014 fiscal year and the total budget bylaw amount of $134,795,014 for the 2013/2014 fiscal year was prepared in accordance with the Act . 4.
