Mr. Roberts RE: Agendas & Printing There was a discussion around moving to a paperless Agendas for Committee and Board Meetings. Currently the Executive Assistant to the Board of Education prints copies of the Agenda for Trustees, SD 68 staff and spectators. To sustain this, a large amount of paper is used. Staff are looking to save administrative costs as much as possible and reduce our carbon footprint. Agendas are available online on our website days before the meeting and staff would have the Agenda and its attachments on the projector for spectators/staff and Trustees to be able to view during the meeting. Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held September 16, 2015 There was fulsome discussion with Committee members giving their opinion. No final decision was made, but a consensus to try only printing Agendas for Trustees and staff and continuing this approach until December. Mr. Roberts RE: Exempt Staff Compensation Review Mr. Roberts went over the Exempt Staff Compensation Review with Trustees. Western Compensation and Benefits Consultants (WCBC) will assist BCPSEA with a total compensation review of district-based exempt positions consistent with compensation industry standards/best practice and current policy for excluded staff. Mr. Burgos RE: Technology / Live Streaming Mr. D. Burgos, Director of Communications provided streaming communications options for all Board meetings. Mr. Burgos provided two options: 1. Continue to record audio of all Board meetings and begin uploading to an online streaming service that can be accessed after the Board meetings. 2. Live stream the Board meetings as they happen. The videos will be stored and can be accessed online at any time.
