TRUSTEE LIAISON COMMITTEE REPORT Trustee Higginson Committee Name: Syeyutsus Activities happening at school location: Syeyutsus held a joint meeting with the Nacu Mat Tatulut professional learning committee on January 29. The goal is to make the Syeyutsus Family bigger so we can spread the work further throughout the district. NLPS hosted the spring session of the Vancouver Island School Trustees Association meeting. Much of the professional learning for this event focussed on the Syeyutsus Framework as well as the Qwam Qwum Stuwixwulh School. The Syeyutsus Family worked hard on this event and were honoured to be able to share our work with the every board on Vancouver Island. Singing with Syeyutsus is happening across the district. This is a cultural learning session with language, drumming and soup and bannock. These are drop in sessions for people to learn language, songs and stories of the land upon which our schools are situated and gain a deeper understanding of what Syeyutsus is. The March sessions are being held on March 5 and 12 at NDSS. The board has requested a joint Board/Syeyutsus meeting to be held on March 30. The Syeyutsus family looks forward to hearing more about what the intentions of this meeting are before we embark on future work together. Possible areas of support from the Board for future planning/budgeting The Syeyutsus family continues to make itself available to the board for advice on implementing the Syeytsus Framework in a meaningful way in order to create the culture shift we are hoping to achieve with the goal of Truth and Reconciliation in our strategic plan. Our work together will need to follow protocol and ceremony that is embedded in Syeyutsus.
