BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools REGULAR BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: November 26, 2014 TO: Board of Education FROM: John Blain, Deputy Superintendent – Learning Services RE: Adjustment to Local School Calendar: Local Calendar Consultation Background The Board approved the 2014-15 Local School Calendar in the spring of 2014. As outlined in School Calendar Regulation 314/12, the Board may approve amendments to the local school calendar. Proposal 1) Due to the strike, the Ministry of Education has given school districts some flexibility this year by moving the first semester exams into the first week of February (from the last week of January). This allows the school district to balance the number of instructional days in each semester. 2) By moving the September PD day from Monday, September 29 to Monday, February 24 it will create more instructional time when students first return to school in September, and create further balancing of the number of instructional days in both semesters. This change to the Local School Calendar requires a 30-day notice period as stipulated within School Calendar Regulation 314/12. Both the NDTA and CUPE executive have been made aware of the above recommendations and are supportive. At this time, the consultation period has lapsed and no significant concerns were raised regarding the recommendations. Recommendation That the Board of Education approves the Local School Calendar adjustments by moving the end of Semester 1 to Friday, February 6; and the Monday, September 29 PD day to Monday, February 24.
