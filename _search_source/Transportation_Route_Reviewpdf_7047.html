recommendations from the 2018/19 Annual Bus Route Review was to combine Trips 1 and 3 of Bus Route 3 for bus service to NDSS and Mountain View Schools. Estimated savings were to be $5,000 per year. Due to issues with drop off times and student supervision at schools after school bus route times/service were put back to similar times/service as 2018/19. This caused estimated savings to be reduced by half. Morning bus service (Bus Route 3) still has a combined load of NDSS and Mountain View students, making our service more efficient than it was in 2018/19. Annual Bus Route Review – Bus Service Request Process Bus service requests from stakeholders that have an impact on costs, staff time or service to students are referred to the Annual Bus Route Review Process. This enables staff to consider requests while the entire bus route system is being reviewed. Requests (3) from Parents have been received to provide bus service in the Westwood Lake area to Mountain View School. For various reasons, it is not being recommended to route a bus into this area. Staff have concerns regarding the increased volume of traffic on Jingle Pot Road, between East Wellington Road and the Nanaimo Parkway. Staff are recommending bus route alterations that would see the 2 bus stop locations removed from Jingle Pot Road. The alteration would see a bus stop in the Arbot Road and Westwood Road area. This would improve student safety and partially address the requests from Parents mentioned above as there would be a new bus stop closer to the area of request. Due to there being no impact on bus route costs, staff time or service to students this is not considered part of the Annual Bus Route Review.
