Minutes of the Poverty Reduction Task Group July 17, 2024, 1:30pm In-person: Ladysmith Chamber of Commerce Boardroom & Zoom COMMITTEE MEMBERS PRESENT: STAFF/COUNCIL PRESENT: Shannon Crowards, Ladysmith Resource Centre Association (LRCA) Jacqueline Neligan, Ladysmith Family and Friends (LaFF) Griffin Russell, Island Health Cindy Lise, Our Cowichan Health Network Rosalie Sawrie, Social Planning Cowichan (SPC) Councillor Tricia McKay REGRETS: Isaiah Harris, Community Member Stephen Cochrane, Stz’uminus First Nation Sue Glenn, Supervisor – Community Programs & Services (staff) Roberta Bowman, Ladysmith Chamber of Commerce CALL TO ORDER AND ACKNOWLEDGEMENT The facilitator acknowledged with gratitude that this meeting was taking place on the unceded territory of the Stz'uminus First Nation. AGENDA That the Poverty Reduction Task Group approve the agenda for the meeting. Motion carried. MINUTES Motion to approve the May 24, 2024 and June 19, 2024 minutes. Motion carried. CURRENT BUSINESS Welcome Employer Seminar Series: - TogetherBC session – Wednesday, July 24, 2024 from 11am to 12:30pm at Eagles Hall A discussion took place to finalize promotions and logistics for the event. 2023/2024 Final Reporting: - UBCM Reporting is due September 7, 2024. In preparation the group discussed information that should be included in the final report and committed to participating in an evaluation to be shared by the facilitator. Future Action Planning: Minutes to the Poverty Reduction Task Group Meeting July 17, 2024 Page 2 - - A discussion took place to check-in on progress and share updates on the following topic themes/actions as identified during the Dinner and Dialogues. Each item has a list of community volunteers who have signed up to support these initiatives in the future: • Transportation Project Update • School Food program • Improving Mental Health Services • Food Equity activities and workshops/community building & food sharing events • Income Supports/Guaranteed Basic Income • Secondary Suites A discussion took place about potential ways to move forward with or without future funding. PRTG members were asked to consider this and share their thoughts in the evaluation. Action Items - - Host the TogetherBC Employer Seminar on July 24, 2024 SPC to send a PRTG evaluation to members for feedback on their participation and possibilities for continuing forward. SPC to draft the final report for the Town to submit to UBCM with activities to be completed by August 7, 2024 and the final report due September 7, 2024. SPC to check in with PRTG in early September about meeting on September 18, 2024 to check-in and explore how to move forward. Meeting adjourned at 2:32pm Next Regularly Scheduled PRTG meeting is for September 18, 2024 at 1:30pm Facilitator (Rosalie Sawrie) RECEIVED: Corporate Officer (Sue Bouma)
