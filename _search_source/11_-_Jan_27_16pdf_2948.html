CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY TRUSTEE ZONE REPORT Trustee Kimler: LSS - Congratulations to the junior and senior improvisation teams for advancing to the Island final in Courtenay next month. In mid February, LSS will be linking Synervoice to their MyEd attendance program. If all goes well, block-byblock attendance will be reported to parents daily via this automated call out system. Today is #BellLetsTalk day, an initiative to engage in conversation to help end the stigma around mental illness. Throughout February, some LSS grade 9-12 students will attend GRASP (Growth, Resiliency Acknowledgement, Suicide Awareness & Prevention and Planning). LIS - Term One Awards were handed out earlier this month, celebrating the successes of many students. Tigger ribbons, Perfect Attendance awards, honour roll ribbons and principal list ribbons were presented to many students. Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held January 27, 2016 LPS - Celebrated Family Literacy Day last Friday with a family open house, book exchange, contests and games. They are helping to promote a local initiative called "Coats for Folks". They accept clean, warm, clothes for drop-off or pick up Tuesdays - Fridays from 10-4. ENO - Family literacy day is a month-long event and the theme this year was "Wild About Reading". Events included: Read-a-Thon; Dressas-your-Favourite-Book-Character day; and a Family Literacy Celebration assembly. Through PLC work, ENO teachers have created "North Oyster Explorer Groups" which are 14 multi-aged groups of 22 students each. Teachers and groups rotate through seven different lessons - Forest Walk; Composting; Fish painting; Rock painting; Outdoor science & predator/prey games. Done on Wednesdays from 10:30-11:30. Goals: create a sense of wonder, expand friendships, allow students to connect with other teachers and provide enhanced learning through nature.
