TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE GOVERNMENT SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, AUGUST 16, 2010 – 6:00 P.M. LADYSMITH PRESENT: Councillor Duck Paterson (Chair) Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Steve Arnett Councillor Lori Evans Councillor Scott Bastian Councillor Bruce Whittington Pat Durban Sandy Bowden ABSENT: Mayor Rob Hutchins STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Joanna Winter CALL TO ORDER Councillor Paterson called the meeting to order at 6:00 p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL 2010- 063 It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda be adopted as circulated. MINUTES 2010- 064 DELEGATION It was moved, seconded and carried that the Government Services Committee minutes of July 19, 2010 be adopted as circulated. Staff Sergeant Roger Plamondon, Ladysmith Detachment, Royal Canadian Mounted Police S/Sgt Plamondon reviewed the detachment’s progress report and discussed local crime statistics and related issues with the Committee. Chairman Paterson thanked S/Sgt. Plamondon for meeting with the Committee and asked that Council’s appreciation be extended to the members of the detachment for their service. 2010-065 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Royal Canadian Mounted Police Ladysmith Detachment Progress Report for April to Government Services Committee August 16, 2010 Page 1 June 2010 be accepted. Trolley Advertising Program 2010-066 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that: a) Staff be directed to implement the Trolley Advertising program as proposed b) Advertising at Trolley stops be referred to the Heritage Revitalization Advisory Commission for input; and c) Staff be directed to review the Sign and Canopy Bylaw and propose amendments, if required, to accommodate the placement of advertising signage at trolley stops Fire Chief’s Report – July 2010 2010-067 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Fire Chief’s Report for June 2010 be received. Building Inspector’s Report – July 2010 2010-068 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Building Inspector’s Report for June 2010 be accepted. Trolley Report – July 2010 2010-069 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Trolley Report for July 2010 be accepted. Coastal Animal Control Services – July 2010 Pound Report 2010-070 MEMBER SUBMISSIONS It was moved, seconded and carried that the report from Coastal Animal Control Services for June 2010 be received. Councillor Evans – Canada Census Long Form 2010-071 It was moved, seconded and carried that it be recommended to Council that a letter be written to the Minister responsible for Census Canada requesting that the mandatory long census form be reinstated for the 2011 census. CORRESPONDENCE Peter and Cathy Barter, Top Drawer Ladies Consignment Boutique Sandwich Boards 2010-072 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that the issues raised in the correspondence from Peter and Cathy Barter dated August 6, Government Services Committee August 16, 2010 Page 2 2010 regarding sandwich board signs be referred to staff for review and recommendation. QUESTION PERIOD ADJOURNMENT 2010-073 It was moved, seconded and carried that the meeting be adjourned at 6:33 p.m. Chair (Councillor D. Paterson) CERTIFIED CORRECT Corporate Officer (S.
