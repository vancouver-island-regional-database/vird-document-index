Infrastructure Services Invitation to Tender No. 2024-IS-02 Roberts Street Watermain Replacement For technical enquiries please contact: Mitch Brook, P.Eng. Koers & Associates mbrook@koers-eng.com ITT Issue Date: April 22, 2024 ITT Closing Date: May 16, 2024 2:00 pm, ITT Opening: May 16, 2024 2:00 pm Location of Bid Opening: Ladysmith City Hall INVITATION TO TENDER Town of Ladysmith Roberts Street Watermain Replacement Signed Tenders in sealed envelopes that are clearly marked "Tender for Town of Ladysmith, Roberts Street Watermain Replacement” will be received at the Town Hall, 410 Esplanade, Ladysmith, B.C., up to 2:00:00 pm local time, Thursday, May 16th, 2024, after which time they will be opened by Town staff. The Tenders will not be opened in public. Tenderers may request unofficial results following the opening. The work includes the supply of all materials, labour and equipment required to install approximately 140 metres of 150 mm dia. and 165 metres of 200 mm dia. PVC watermain piping including water services, fittings, valves, tie-ins, and a fire hydrant. The work also includes the installation of approximately 32 metres of 200 mm dia. and 103 metres of 300 mm dia. storm sewer piping including catch basins and manholes. Tender documents will be available from Koers & Associates Engineering Ltd., after 11:00 am local time, on Monday April 22nd, 2024. Tenderers shall register at http://www.koers-eng.com/ for digital access to the Tender documents. Tenderers can register by clicking on the Contractor Sign-up link at the bottom of the homepage and following the prompts for registration information. Login information for access to the digital Tender documents will be emailed to Tenderers upon successful completion of Tenderer registration. Tender registration instructions are available by emailing tenders@koers-eng.com. Paper copies of the Tender documents will be made available upon request from Koers & Associates upon payment of $210.00 (incl. GST) per set. Tender Documents Available Monday April 22nd, 2024 11:00am Last Question & Answer Received Monday May 13th, 2024 5:00pm Last Addenda/Q&A Response Tuesday May 14th, 2024 5:00pm Tender Closing Thursday May 16th, 2024 2:00pm Technical enquiries regarding the project shall be directed to Mitch Brook, P.Eng, of Koers & Associates Engineering Ltd., by email at mbrook@koers-eng.com. Tenders must be accompanied by the specified Bid Security, payable to the Town of Ladysmith. Award of the contract is subject to sufficient budget funds being available for the project. The proposed project superintendent, subcontractors, schedule of completion, size of workforce, proposed equipment, previous experience, and submission of suitable references from other similar sized projects may all be considered in review and acceptance of the Tender. The Town of Ladysmith reserves the right to waive minor informalities in or reject any or all Tenders, or accept the Tender deemed most favourable in the interests of the Town, as detailed in this invitation and the Tender documents. Tenders not conforming to the specified requirements may be returned to the Tenderer without consideration. The lowest or any Tender will not necessarily be accepted. Mr. Ryan Bouma, P.Eng.
