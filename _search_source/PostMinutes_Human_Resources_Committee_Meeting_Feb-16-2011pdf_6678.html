THE BOARD OF SCHOOL TRUSTEES OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF THE HUMAN RESOURCES COMMITTEE MEETING HELD FEBRUARY 16, 2011 Trustees (Voting) D. Neary, in the Chair D. Neary N. Allen Trustees (Not Voting) Staff M. Munro, Superintendent/CEO F. Frisson, Assistant Superintendent C. Southwick, Assistant Superintendent D. Reimer, Director of Communications C. Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No. HR4 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognizes that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. ELECTION OF VICE-CHAIR Chair Neary called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Human Resources Committee. Trustee Bonkowski nominated Trustee Nelson Allen. Chair Neary asked if there were any other nominations. Hearing none, she declared Trustee Nelson Allen Vice-Chair of the Human Resources Committee. ADDITIONS TO THE AGENDA There were no additions to the Agenda this evening. DELETIONS TO THE AGENDA There were no deletions to the Agenda this evening. CHANGE IN ORDER There was no Change in Order to the Agenda this evening. UNFINISHED BUSINESS First Day of Absence - Budget Strategy The following motion was referred to the Human Resources Committee. R10/12/15-10 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) refers the information presented at the December 8th Business Committee related to the budget strategy "First Day of Absence" to the January Human Resources Committee Meeting. Mr. Munro reported that there had been 199 instances of EAs absences failing to fill between September and mid-February. The committee discussed the impacts that the non-replacement of staff on the first day of absence and specifically as it related to morale amongst employees. Mr. Hewitt volunteered to survey the elementary administrator and secondary administrator groups related to the issues related to the budget strategy "First Day of Absence". IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Nelson Allen Folio HR11/02/16-03 That the Human Resources Committee reports to the Board that the issue of first day of absence has been reviewed by the Human Resources Committee and the Committee has noted the morale issues expressed by the groups. Trustee Bonkowski opposed CARRIED Draft Minutes of the Human Resources Committee Meeting held February 16, 2011 3 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Bonkowski Folio HR/11/02/16-04 Succession Planning That the Human Resources Committee recommends to the Board of Education that it takes the ownership away from staff on the first day of absence and makes it the responsibility the administrators responsibility. DEFEATED UNANIMOUSLY The following motion was referred to the Human Resources Committee. R10/12/15-11 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) refers the topic "succession planning" to the January or February 2011 Special Human Resources Committee Meeting. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Nelson Allen Folio HR11/02/16-04 The Human Resources Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the topic of succession planning be deferred until a Director of Human Resources has been hired and other immediate human resources issues have been resolved and further that this this item be returned to a Human Resources Committee Agenda no later than October 2011. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Professional Development Opportunities Trustee Nelson Allen asked that this item be referred to the March meeting. He is hoping to more information available and possibly a resource person from BCPSEA. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Nelson Allen Folio HR11/02/16-05 QUESTION PERIOD That the item Professional Development Opportunities be referred to the March Human Resources Committee Meeting.
