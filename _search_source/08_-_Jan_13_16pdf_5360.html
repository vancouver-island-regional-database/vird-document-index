CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS Mr. Sabo & Mr. Prevost Transportation Update Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations and Dave Prevost, Transportation Supervisor provided a transportation and Z-Pass + update to the Business Committee this evening. The total transportation budget for the 2015-16 school year is $2,185,692 with the majority of funds going to student transportation. Other costs include fleet operation and maintenance, courier, cartage and administration. There are currently more than 1,700 riders in the district. This data was taken from the Z-Pass+ electronic tracking system that was implemented at the beginning of the school year. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Secretary-Treasurer - Financial Update Secretary-Treasurer Graham Roberts updated the Board on the financial status of the district. Draft Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held January 13, 2016 The district is no longer in funding protection and the Ministry of Education's funding allocation to the district has increased by $348.558 due to the 128.75 FTE students registered in the district above 2015-16 projections. The International Student enrolment was up to 297 from an estimate of 245, which results in a net contribution of $730,000. With the additional contributions of over $1 million, Mr. Roberts forecasted that the cost increases associated with the additional enrolment will result in an operation loss of $1,592,241, which will be funded from surplus. Mr. Roberts anticipates the remaining accumulated surplus to be reduced to approximately $3,500,000 by the end of the 201516 school year. With the increased enrolment, teaching staff has been increased by 4.623 FTE and education assistants by 11.766 FTE.
