-----Original Message----From: JOANNE BARTOLO Sent: Tuesday, June 23, 2020 6:51 AM Subject: South Wellington school demolition CAUTION: External Message Please consider South Wellington community to be able to acquire South Wellington school as a hub for our small community not only would it serve as an emergency location during these uncertain times as we are currently in a pandemic and live in an earthquake zone that perhaps going forward in the future this would be a spot that are locals and people travelling the island who are in need of emergency care would be able to stop and use our community as a source of shelter and emergency also having our fire department next door would compliment this idea. During positive times also and weekly and monthly this building would be a great source of community connection whether it be flea markets paint classes yoga classes after school care for young children in our community and surrounding areas . I am third generation to live in this community my siblings my mother my grandparents went to this community school not only is it held close in the hearts of my family but other families who are still living and joining our community I think it would be a great loss . I would hope that the school board and the regional district and local authorities would come together and see the need for us to continue to have this structure this piece of land in the hub of our community and how much benefit it would be today and in the future . Thank you for your time Sincerely Joanna Bartolo and family
