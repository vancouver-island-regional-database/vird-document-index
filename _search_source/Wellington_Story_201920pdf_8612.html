Wellington Secondary School is an inclusive, caring and engaging educational environment. We pride ourselves on a comprehensive program that offers a wide range of curricular and extra-curricular activities. Wellington Secondary is located on the ancestral and unceded territory of the Snuneymuxw First People who have been stewards of this beautiful land since time immemorial.
