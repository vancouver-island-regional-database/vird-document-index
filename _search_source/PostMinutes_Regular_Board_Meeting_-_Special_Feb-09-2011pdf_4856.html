CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Draft Resolution for BCSTA AGM The Board adopted a motion at the January 26th Board Meeting approving the development of a resolution for submission to the BCSTA AGM. The deadline for submission is Friday, February 11, 2011. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Nelson Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee McNamee Folio S11/02/09-02 WHEREAS The present system for teacher bargaining is recognized as dysfunctional for all parties involved and the present system has increased dramatically the cost of processing grievances and arbitrations to Boards of Education that could exceed the cost of funding bargaining locally for some issues, and parents, students and the Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held February 9, 2011 2 wider community require a stable and predictable system for teacher bargaining and a system that upholds and models the basic democratic principles of free collective bargaining: THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED: That the BCSTA requests that the Government of British Columbia to work with BCPSEA and the BCTF to develop changes in legislation that would establish a hybrid model for teacher bargaining in British Columbia. Opposed by Trustee Dale CARRIED Proposed Local School Calendar for 2011-2012 Folio S11/02/09-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Dale That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves a consultation process to consider the adoption of a Local School Calendar for all District schools in accordance with the Board of Education's School Calendar Bylaw No. 5. Opposed by Trustees Nelson Allen and Murchie CARRIED QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions this evening.
