UNDERSTANDING OF ASSIGNM ENT The CVRD Board of Directors has identified a need to review, in advance of the 2026 budget process, the Regional District's Function 121, which focuses on the provision of regional economic development services through Cowichan Economic Development, the CVRD's stand-alone economic development division.
