We have another, younger son, to get to preschool next year and taking our older son down to FJN and dealing with the parking (which is already horrendous) would be a huge imposition. To walk to the school is entirely too far for any elementary student (even if they walk the most expedient route on Hammond Bay Road). Had that happened on Hammond Bay, it would have had much worse results. We would most definitely be trying to get a transfer to Mcgirr or Hammond Bay if the bus is dropped. That would be unfortunate as FJN has been good for my son's first year. Please dont cancel this bus service.
