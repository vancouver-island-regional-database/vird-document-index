IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Welch Folio R12/09/26-17 SUPERINTENDENT'S REPORT That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receives Trustee Saunder's VISTA report. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Hutchinson outlined his priorities for the 201213 school year. He said his priorities for the last school year focused on: • Strengthening the Board of Education and senior Page 6 of 10 Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held September 26, 2012 6 management team • Building relationships with internal and external stakeholders • Advancing the learning agenda He said that this year, he intends to build on the previous year’s priorities by putting his energies toward: • Advancing the learning agenda • Strengthening communications • Developing a performance management culture, starting with District Administration Centre departments The Superintendent’s full report is available on the school district website at http://www.sd68.bc.ca/News/SuperintendentPage.a sp. As part of the Superintendent’s Report, Mr. Hutchinson and Deputy Superintendent John Blain outlined for the Board a proposal that staff from across the district be brought together a few times a year to discuss district initiatives related to educational topics, such as response to intervention and the strategic plan. Once the proposal is finalized, it will be brought to the Board’s Education Committee for discussion, and it would also require a public consultation process. Mr. Blain said that the Department of Learning Services believes that the sessions would be a very strong tool to help move the district forward towards its goals for student learning.
