ADDITIONS TO THE AGENDA Item 8.5 - Update on the Budget Surplus and item 8.6 - Budget Process Discussion. DELETIONS TO THE AGENDA There were no Deletions to the Agenda this evening. CHANGE IN ORDER There was no Change in Order to the Agenda this evening. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio That the Agenda be approved as amended. Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held June 10, 2015 BC15/06/10-01 CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY APPROVAL OF THE MINUTES Folio BC15/06/10-02 That the minutes of the Business Committee meeting held May 13, 2015 be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS Trustee Solomon - Meetings Format / Committee representation Trustee Solomon would like to form a Committee that would set meetings and is seeking participation from those Trustees that are interested. NEW BUSINESS Mr. Blain - Revised Facility Plan Mr. Blain presented the Business Committee with the Revised Facilities Plan. It is attached the Agenda as well as available on the SD68 website. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Routley Folio BC/15/06/10-03 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that it receive the Updated Facilities Plan 2015-2021, and that they make a recommendation in a September Special Board Meeting. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae On the Main Motion Folio BC15/06/10-05 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that it receive the Updated Facilities Plan 2015-2021, and that they make a Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held June 10, 2015 recommendation at the end of August in a Special Board Meeting. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Trustee Brennan Motion - Green Initiatives IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson Folio BC15/06/10-06 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) they explore the possibility of a district wide separation policy for all schools to include: plastics, metal, paper towel and compost and $1700 to support three (3) Green Network meetings per year: transportation plus one (1) annual project. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Trustee Kimler Motion - Trustee Composition IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Kimler IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan Folio BC15/06/10-07 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) to direct staff to report on the process to reduce the number of Trustees from nine (9) to seven (7). This report is to be developed as a cost saving measure and to explore the possibility of converting to a ward system. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Trustee Kimler - BCSTA. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae Folio BC15/06/10-08 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) they refer the discussion regarding potential cost savings/benefits in relinquishing membership with the BCSTA to the Meetings Format Committee. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Budget Surplus IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Routley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held June 10, 2015 Folio BC15/06/10-09 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that $500,000 be reallocated from surplus and/or $1.7M from staffing contingency into janitorial and maintenance services. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson Motion of Substitution Folio BC15/06/10-10 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to bring forward spending options following receipt of the 2014/15 Audit should there be a surplus. Opposed by Trustee Routley CARRIED Budget Process IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Routley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae Folio BC15/06/10-11 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) that the financial projection for the present fiscal year is presented to the Board prior to approval of next year's budget. Opposed by Trustee(s) Kimler and Solomon CARRIED QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions from the audience this evening. ADJOURNMENT Folio BC15/06/10-12 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 9:00 pm.
