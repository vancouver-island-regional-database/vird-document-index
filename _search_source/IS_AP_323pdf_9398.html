NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: December 8, 2021 Business Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Administrative Procedure 323 – School Contracts Background As part of our ongoing review of our Administrative Procedures to ensure currency, we have reviewed AP 323 – School Contracts. Discussion Although the intent of this Administrative Procedure remains valid, it contains several references to positions, departments and processes that no longer exist or have been renamed. It has now been revised and is attached for your information. In addition, we have added a provision that provides for a transparent process with respect to payments associated with Syeyutsus work.
