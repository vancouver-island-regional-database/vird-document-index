As you already know, I, myself have taken a huge interest over the past several months in trying to raise awareness and establish support from the community , maybe also obtaining some potential investors and community connections in order to set up a one stop mega shelter and community mental / addictions health facility at the currently decommissioned Woodlands Secondary School. Since the school structure and property are currently zoned to accommodate such a facility, why not have the school district 68 sign a one year lease to the city of Nanaimo so that an experimental homeless shelter combined with a Mental Health and Addictions facility centre for the homeless could be placed within the existing woodlands secondary school structure and property . Since the property currently sits unused, I myself and many others would support the idea and the use of taxpayers money that continues to maintain and have the building in operation. The location for this Community and neighborhood project is ideal.    The School has plenty of usable space that is required to safely house people for a short term basis. There are no other schools around the area (except for across the highway) that may raise public red flags or concerns of drug or mental health problems spilling out into the community. If an overdose or medical emergency happens within the facility, volunteer and paid staff would attend. If the medical issue is too much for the staff to handle the hospital is just minutes away, which would save lives. As you may know, two deaths have occurred at the Discontent camp. Nanaimo needs to step up and start caring for these people, and facilitate their needs and requirements. The City and the Nanaimo court have already planned to close down the camp of which action is days away, do to the dangerous conditions these people are living in. Both the structure and property itself can serve as a training facility for VIHA students, VIU Students and Community College Social Services and Medical Practise Program Practicums. As for the planned use for the entire structure and property are as follows        The Gym can be used as a drop in activity center for the homeless Physical activity is a healthier option for the homeless rather than taking part in crime and hanging out in unhealthy environments The upper and lower levels of classrooms can be divided up and used as sleeping quarters. For those homeless that have addictions and mental health issues, a wing can be dedicated for those who need the care to be segregated from the rest of the tenants, private quarters would be made available. The cafeteria and kitchen could be used for food services Community Kitchen and food bank use. The band shelter could be used to teach and instruct staff or the homeless that want one on one educational support programs The washrooms , showers and ample storage is what these homeless people request and need.
