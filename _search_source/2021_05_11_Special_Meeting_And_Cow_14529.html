NOTICE SPECIAL COUNCIL MEETING AND TIME CHANGE FOR REGULAR COMMITTEE OF THE WHOLE MEETING The Town of Ladysmith Council will meet electronically on Tuesday, May 11, 2021 at 6:00 p.m. for a Special Council Meeting immediately followed by a Regular Committee of the Whole Meeting Purpose: The Special Council Meeting is for Council to consider adoption of the 2021 Financial Plan Bylaws. Time Change: The Regular Committee of the Whole Meeting start time is changed from 6:30 p.m. to immediately following the Special Council Meeting which commences at 6:00 p.m. Please Note: Under the authority of Ministerial Order No. M192, Town of Ladysmith Council continues to hold its Council and Committee meetings electronically since despite its best efforts, the attendance of members of the public cannot be accommodated at a meeting that would otherwise be held in accordance with the applicable requirements or recommendations under the Public Health Act .
