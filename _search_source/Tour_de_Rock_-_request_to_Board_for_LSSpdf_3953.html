March 25, 2014 Nanaimo/Ladysmith Public Schools 395 Wakesiah Avenue, Nanaimo, BC V9R 3K6 ATTN: Cathy Kelt, Executive Assistant RE: Presentation / Placement on agenda for April 23, 2014 Dear Ms. Kelt, Please accept this letter as an official request to be placed on the Board’s agenda for the April 23, 2014 meeting. I am writing on behalf of the Tour de Rock, Ladysmith, Committee which is a group of individuals/volunteers who actively campaign for the Cops for Cancer event, raising money for childhood cancer research and Camp GoodTimes. (See www.tourderock.ca for full details) A significant portion of our fundraising effort is a “Red Serge Dinner” evening, which is a high profile community event, being held within Ladysmith Secondary School for the first time this year. The event is hosted by the Tour de Rock Committee, with attendees being served by the RCMP and local Fire Rescue members. The dinner is formal and is one of the major fundraisers during the Tour de Rock’s timeline, usually including the service of some alcoholic beverages which are served by the members. It is for this reason that we are requesting consideration from the Board to be able to serve this type of dinner/drinks within LSS. Preparation of food items in the kitchen is likely to involve students, however, food leaving the prep area and drinks served would all be handled by the RCMP/Fire Department and Tour de Rock Ladysmith committee members with their Serving-It-Right license(s). The committee is thrilled that LSS is willing to open its doors to host this event, as it became evident last year that the fundraiser had outgrown its venue. It is our sincere desire to involve the students to enhance their engagement in this particular community initiative, so that they can see the dedication required of the Tour participants and breathe the passion of the riders. Thank you for your consideration. I will be happy to answer any questions or concerns that the Board may have with regards to the event and/or service therein. Trusting all is in order, I remain, Sincerely, Kimberley Judson Chair, Tour de Rock Ladysmith Committee (250)713-8366 /kj
