CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held October 20, 2010 SECRETARY-TREASURER'S REPORT 2 Mr. Green provided the Committee with background information about changes in provincial funding for education this year and how the school district has been impacted by those changes. He gave the Committee an update on preliminary enrolment figures for this year. He said that the district is down 261 FTE students from our budget projections. While there will be a funding loss as a result of this decline, it is currently being projected that other revenue and expense changes, including an increase in the number of international students, will result in a balanced budget. UNFINISHED BUSINESS Vandalism Initiative - Mosquito Device Staff are recommending that electronic devices known as "Mosquitos" be installed at Rock City and Uplands Park schools. The devices emit a harmless, but highly irritating, high frequency sound that can generally be heard only by young people.
