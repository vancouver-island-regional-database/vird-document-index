May 23, 2019 Dear School District #68 Board of Trustees, Re: Budget for Community School Coordinator Positions The Uplands Park Elementary School PAC encourages this board to continue funding the Community School Coordinator positions as the school has benefited from the resources that are available to them, including a breakfast program that was recently started as well as affordable afterschool arts/science programs. Having a dedicated person to research and access the funding for these programs is invaluable and we hope that the Board seriously considers continuing these positions to help improve student learning in this district. Parent Advisory Committees work hard to earn money for extra programming, but that money can only go so far and to have a person available to access alternative funds and create affordable programs is fantastic. Thank you for your consideration. Uplands Park Elementary PAC
