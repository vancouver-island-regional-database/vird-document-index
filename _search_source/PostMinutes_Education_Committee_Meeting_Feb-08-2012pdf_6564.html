CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Education Committee Meeting held February 8, 2012 2 PRESENTATIONS There were no presentations this evening. EDUCATIONAL UPDATE Report from Ms. Frisson and Ms. Southwick Ms. Southwick updated the Committee about the kindergarten classroom project at Fairview Elementary. She outlined a number of projects and issues that she and Ms. Frisson have been working on the last month. Ms. Shelley Beleznay - District Literacy Coordinator Ms. Beleznay, District Literacy Coordinator, updated the Committee on a number of learning initiatives. She told the Committee about initiatives underway in our district that focus on the vision of Success for All. This year, a technology strand has been introduced to allow educators to better collaborate. She said that people are busily using Twitter, Facebook, wikis and blogs to share their learning. Ms. Elizabeth Pennell - Early Learning Update Ms. Elizabeth Pennell, Early Years Coordinator, reviewed with the Committee the number of ways the school district works with the community on early learning initiatives. She said that by collaborating with other agencies to focus on early childhood development, the community can help ensure that children enter school in Kindergarten better prepared to learn. She reviewed the data from the Early Development Indicators project that assesses the readiness of children in Kindergarten throughout the province.
