CALL TO ORDER The Chair will call the meeting to order and recognizes that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. 2. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA 3. NEW BUSINESS 3.1 Annual Budget 2012-2013 and Bylaw 3-19 First Reading That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $134,543,865 for fiscal year 2012-2013 and that it be given second reading. Second Reading That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $134,543,865 for fiscal year 2012-2013 and that it be given first reading. Unanimous Consent That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $134,543,865 for fiscal year 2012-2013 for a third and final time. Third and Final Reading That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $134,543,865 for fiscal year 2012-2013 and that it be given a third and final reading. 4. QUESTION PERIOD Questions from the audience must be submitted in writing and given to the Board’s Executive Assistant (Cathy Kelt) for submission to the Chair.
