2026 Council Meeting Schedule

Town of Ladysmith
2026-01-01
Original PDF

• • • • Regular Council Meetings begin at 7:00pm on the first and third Tuesday of each month (except in January, August, September and October). Committee of the Whole Meetings begin at 6:30pm on the second Tuesday of every other month (except in January). Meetings will be held at the Ladysmith Seniors Centre (630 2nd Avenue). Meetings are subject to change, so please contact City Hall to confirm.
