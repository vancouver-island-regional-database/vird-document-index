NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: May 27, 2026 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary Treasurer Cilaire Elementary School Geotechnical Report Covenant Bylaw, 2026 Recommendation That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) give the Cilaire Elementary School City of Nanaimo Geotechnical Report Covenant Bylaw, 2026 all three readings at this evening's meeting, and that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopt the Cilaire Elementary School City of Nanaimo Geotechnical Report Covenant Bylaw, 2026. Background As part of the proposed development of two childcare studios at the Cilaire Elementary site, the City of Nanaimo has identiﬁed that the area may be subject to potential landslide risk. As a condition of development, the City requires that a geotechnical report be registered on title as a covenant pursuant to Section 218 of the Land Title Act. The covenant will ensure that the property is used in accordance with the recommendations outlined in the geotechnical report prepared by Lewkowich Engineering Associates Ltd dated March 6, 2026. Discussion Registration of the geotechnical covenant is a requirement of the City of Nanaimo to proceed with the childcare project at Cilaire Elementary School. The covenant is intended to ensure that development and ongoing use of the property aligns with geotechnical recommendations and mitigates potential risk associated with site conditions. The proposed bylaw authorizes the Board to grant the covenant and enables the Secretary-Treasurer to execute all necessary documentation to complete registration. Staff have reviewed the covenant requirement and are satisfied that it will not interfere with the Board’s use of the property for educational purposes and will support safe site development. Approval of the bylaw is required at this time to maintain project timelines and advance childcare development. Action Sheet: Cilaire Elementary School – Covenant Bylaw, 2026 Page 2 Conclusion Approving the Geotechnical Report Covenant Bylaw for Cilaire Elementary School is a necessary step to meet City of Nanaimo requirements and advance the planned childcare development. The covenant supports safe site use by ensuring adherence to professional geotechnical recommendations, while not impacting the district’s educational operations. Timely adoption of the bylaw will enable project progression and help maintain established timelines. Appendix A: Cilaire Covenant Bylaw
