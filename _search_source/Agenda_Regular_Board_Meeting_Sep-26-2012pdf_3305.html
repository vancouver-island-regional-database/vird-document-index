Of that, $2.7 million was identified as restricted surplus, earmarked for specific purposes in the 2012-13 budget and $480,117 is an unrestricted operating surplus. He said when the Board set the 2012-13 budget, it planned that there would be $1.6 million in surplus funds from last year to bring forward into this year’s budget. Some of the purposes for which the surplus has been restricted include school supplies and learning resources, Aboriginal education programs, technology renewal, and equipment.
