NEW BUSINESS 10.1 Trustee Brzovic Re: Notice of Motion - Masks Trustees discussed mask wearing in elementary schools. Local Medical Health Officer, Dr. Sandra Allison, attended for this portion of the meeting and answered Trustee questions. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Barron 2 That the Board of Education for School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) require masks for Grade 6 & 7 students in all indoor areas, including when they are in learning groups, with exemptions for students who cannot wear masks for health or behavioural reasons, effective upon the return from Spring Break. R21/03/10-04 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Barron IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Stanley Motion to Amend That the Board of Education for School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) require all students in grades 6 & 7 to wear face masks in schools, both within and outside their learning groups, in a manner consistent with the most recent health and safety guidelines for middle and secondary schools outlined by the Ministry of Education. OPPOSED: Trustees Higginson, McKay, Wilkinson, Robinson CARRIED R21/03/10-05IT WAS MOVED BY On the Motion as Amended: That the Board of Education for School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) require all students in grades 6 & 7 to wear face masks in schools, both within and outside their learning groups, in a manner consistent with the most recent health and safety guidelines for middle and secondary schools outlined by the Ministry of Education. QUESTION PERIOD Question Period is intended to enable the public to obtain clarifying information regarding a current agenda item. Individuals wishing to ask questions of the Board upon completion of Committee and Board sessions shall do so in writing on the form provided which includes their name, street address and email address. The individual will be asked to verify his/her self and be afforded the opportunity to read out their own question. Each individual shall be limited to one follow-up question upon receiving a response. Forms are available in the information rack near the entrance of the Board Room. If the meeting is being held online, individuals wishing to ask questions of the Board shall type their question in the Q&A compose box on the right side of the screen and then select send. Your question will be read by the Board or Committee Chair. Questions and responses will be included in the video of the meeting and the meeting minutes and will be part of the public record.
