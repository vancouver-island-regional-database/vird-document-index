Kelsey Toxe Subject: FW: Bus service to Cinnabar Valley for John Barsby -----Original Message:---— From: Fiona Seyd [mai|to:fseyd@shaw.ca] Sent: Friday, September 18, 2015 2:49 PM To: John Blain Subject: Bus service to Cinnabar Valley for John Barsby Dear Mr Blain, school year. Our family My son came home with a letter today signed by Scott Saywell regarding the 2016/2017 appreciates that although Cedar Secondary School will be reopening for the 2016 school year, we were given the choice to stay at John Barsby Community Secondary or move our son to Cedar Secondary School. My concern is the loss of bus service. I am confused as to why if the bus comes as far as Chase River, that we cannot have at least one stop in Cinnabar Valley. Many of the kids in this community chose John Barsby Community Secondary School when Cedar Secondary was closed in June 2014. The kids are settled and happy and many want to remain at John Barsby. As a parent I feel that an effort should be made on your behalf of our children to assist in keeping them where they want to be. They are caught in the middle of many decisions that will affect their future. My hope is that you will reconsider busing our children to their chosen school if the need is there. Thank you for your time, Fiona Seyd (250)740-0282
