UNFINISHED BUSINESS John Blain, Superintendent Staff Presentation & Recommendations Page 2 of 3 Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held November 24, 2015 RE: Becoming Sustainable Facilities Plan Update The 60-day Becoming Sustainable : Facilities Plan Update public consultation concluded on November 15. After reviewing feedback from the community and the facilitator's report, Superintendent John Blain provided the staff recommendations report:          The amalgamation of North Cedar Intermediate and Woodbank Primary into the Woodbank school site as of September 2016; The Board approves the closure of Rutherford Elementary School within the next two years; The district works with the Ministry of Education in cost-sharing the construction of a new wing at Frank J. Ney Elementary; The Board approves, in principle, the shift in catchment for Rutherford students to Frank J. Ney, McGirr and Randerson Ridge upon the closure of Rutherford; The Board refers the Rutherford property to the Asset Management program to remain until the Board consults on future use; The District Elementary Skills for Life program relocates to Georgia Avenue Community School for September 2016; The amalgamation of Learn@Home K-7 and Learn@Home 8-12 into the Mount Benson school site as of September 2016; The Board approves the closure of Woodlands Secondary for June 30, 2016 (with hockey, soccer and beach volleyball academies moving to NDSS); and all construction information and data regarding district programs be shard with the NDSS Advisory Committee for future recommendations as it pertains to the development of the NDSS site.
