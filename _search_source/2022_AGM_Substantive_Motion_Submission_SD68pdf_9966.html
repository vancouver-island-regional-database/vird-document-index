Motion Please clarify who the recipient of the motion is/who should do the work associated with the motion. (e.g. Ministry of Education, Ministry of Finance, etc.) That BCSTA requests the Provincial Government to amend the Local Government Act and all other applicable legislation and regulations to enable School Districts to utilize funds collected through school site acquisition charge bylaws to be utilized for both land acquisition and for expanding existing school facilities on lands for which a school site acquisition charge is applicable. Rationale Provide a succinct description of why this motion is needed, plus any relevant background information. This motion is needed to address increasing student enrolments which can not efficiently be accommodated through new school site acquisitions. Allowing school site acquisition charges to be used to build new student capacity would provide districts with greater flexibility to make capacity decisions that best serve the needs of students. It allows districts to be more responsive to shifting demographics and development patterns and to utilize existing schools sites to accommodate growth at the neighborhood level in a more cost effective manner. This motion also allows districts with land, which can accommodate an addition to an existing school, to be able to move these projects forward more quickly using local funds collected through school site acquisition charges rather than relying exclusively on Ministry funding. Finally, districts would continue to be able to plan for the acquisition of sites in underserved areas, while balancing both immediate and long-term capacity needs.
