TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE GOVERNMENT SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, APRIL 18, 2011 – 6:15 P.M. LADYSMITH COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Steve Arnett (Chair) Councillor Lori Evans Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Duck Paterson Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Bruce Whittington Sandy Bowden Joanna Winter Felicity Adams COUNCIL MEMBERS ABSENT: Councillor Scott Bastian STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Erin Anderson CALL TO ORDER Councillor Arnett called the meeting to order at 6:18 p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL 2011-034 It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda for the Government Services Committee meeting of April 18, 2011 be adopted as circulated. MINUTES 2011-035 DELEGATIONS It was moved, seconded and carried that the Government Services Committee minutes for the meeting held March 21, 2011 be adopted as circulated. Ed Nicholson, Ladysmith & District Historical Society E. Nicholson of the Ladysmith and District Historical Society made a presentation outlining the need for a Ladysmith museum, and requesting assistance from the Town in investigating the feasibility of establishing a museum and finding grant funding. Greg Roberts, Ladysmith Community Gardens Society G. Roberts made a presentation regarding successes of the Society in its first year of operating a community garden, and invited Council to make suggestions regarding other contributions the Society could be making to the community. Committee members thanked Mr. Roberts for his presentation and thanked the society for their hard work, and congratulated them on their successes to date. Government Services Committee April 18, 2011 Page 1 CITY MANAGER’S REPORT REPORTS 2011-036 The City Manager reported that her priority for the month had been the preparation of the 2011 to 2015 Financial Plan. Condition of the Trees at the Ladysmith Arboretum It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend that Council request staff to schedule a seminar for staff of Vancouver Island municipalities on construction practices around existing trees and that the removal of a gingko and four other dead or dying trees in the Ladysmith Arboretum, and their replacement with the same species of trees. Staff were requested to ensure that the replacement trees will be suitable for the conditions at the Arboretum. 2011-037 Ladysmith Community Gardens It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend that Council enter into a Licence of Occupation and Use Agreement with the Ladysmith Community Gardens Society for the Ladysmith Community Garden/ High Street Green on a portion of High Street Park. 2011-038 Building Inspector’s Report for March, 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Building Inspector’s Report for March 2011 be received. 2011-039 Trolley Report for March 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Trolley Report for March 2011 be received. 2011-040 Fire Chief’s Report for March 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Fire Chief’s Report for March 2011 be received. 2011-041 Animal Control / Pound Report for March 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Animal Control / Pound Report for March be received. Staff were requested to determine whether the report can identify repeat offenders under the ‘Aggressive/ Large animals’ category. 2011-042 CORRESPONDENCE Ladysmith Detachment, Royal Canadian Mounted Police It was moved, seconded and carried that the quarterly report for January to March 2011 form S/Sgt. Roger Plamondon, Ladysmith Detachment, Royal Canadian Mounted Police, be received. Isabelle Ouelette, Ladysmith and District Historical Society Government Services Committee April 18, 2011 Page 2 2011-043 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend that Council refer the matter of the proposed Ladysmith Museum as outlined in the correspondence from the Ladysmith and District Historical Society dated April 5, 2011, to the Heritage Revitalization Advisory Commission and to the Parks, Recreation and Culture Commission. ADJOURNMENT 2011-044 It was moved, seconded and carried that this meeting of the Government Services Committee be adjourned at 7:00 p.m. Chair (Councillor S.
