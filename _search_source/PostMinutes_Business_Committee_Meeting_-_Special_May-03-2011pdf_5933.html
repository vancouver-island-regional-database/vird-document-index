APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Donna Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan Folio SB11/05/03-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS Budget 2011-12 Chair Murchie indicated that Trustee Welch would be providing some updated information regarding spring breaks from other Board Chairs. He said that Mr. Turin would follow with further information related to a "balanced budget". Mr. Turin told the Committee that last week that the Business Committee had come within Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held May 3, 2011 2 $122,838 of balancing the budget. The Committee asked staff to bring back a plan for further reductions in that amount. Mr. Turin advised Committee members that while absent, Trustee Bonkowski had advised Trustees and Ms. Kelt, via email earlier today, of her position on the budget and asked to be included in the vote. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Dale Folio SB11/05/03-02 That the Special Business Committee approves the use of an additional $75,000 of expected surplus funds from the 2010/2011 budget year to balance the 2011/2012 budget. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Donna Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Nelson Allen Folio SB11/05/03-03 That the Special Business Committee approves the recommended budget reductions for services and supplies in the amount of $47,838 as summarized on the Services/Supplies Reductions document dated May 3, 2011. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Dale Folio SB11/05/03/-04 That the Special Business recommends to the Board of Education that it approves all budget recommendations discussed to date and as summarized on the summary of Budget Proposals for 2011/2012 spreadsheet dated May 3, 2011 and further recommends to the Board of Education that it pass an Annual Budget Bylaw for fiscal year 2011/2012 in accordance with the School Act. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held May 3, 2011 3 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Dale Folio SB11/05/03-05 That the Special Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education that BCPSEA be brought in immediately to perform a high level review of the school district's Human Resources Department's organizational and administrative structure with recommendations for improvements and that this review provide an evaluation of the department's customer focus and services provided to schools and departments. Opposed by Trustees Welch, McNamee and Bonkowski CARRIED QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions this evening. ADJOURNMENT IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Dale Folio SB11/05/03-06 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:55 pm.
