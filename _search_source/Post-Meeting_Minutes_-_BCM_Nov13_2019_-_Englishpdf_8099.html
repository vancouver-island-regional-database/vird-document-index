QUESTION PERIOD There were two questions: 1. Mr. Hackwood stated that the filling stations are not filtered and the cost of $150 every 1.5 months is not manageable. However, our Principal says she spends one hour per day flushing all water sources. A rough estimate puts this cost at more than $10,000 per year. Why would this district not look at other districts who have found funds for filtration and allows staff to focus on their jobs rather than building maintenance? 3 2. Clarification regarding remediation: Our school (Ecole Hammond Bay) has 60% of water sources above limit; all water fountains are still enabled and no automatic flushing occurs (only manually by staff). Children are drinking contaminated water. At a minimum, fountains must be disabled as further testing takes place. There is no safe level for lead. Is this something this Board will help with? Both questions will be directed to staff for response. 14. ADJOURNMENT The meeting adjourned at 7:21 pm.
