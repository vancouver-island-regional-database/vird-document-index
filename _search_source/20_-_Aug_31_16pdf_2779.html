IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Kimler IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson Motion to Refer Folio SB16/08/31-12 That this be referred to the Business Committee Opposed by Trustee(s) Brennan, Brzovic and Routley CARRIED Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held August 31, 2016 NEW BUSINESS Berklee College of Music Scholarship Superintendent Blain introduced a video about Wellington graduate, Kenton Dick who is receiving a scholarship to Berklee College of Music in Boston. He is the first Canadian to receive this prestigious scholarship. Superintendent - 2016-17 School Year Project Timeline Mr. Blain handed out the 2016-17 Senior Management Project Timeline "working draft" to Trustees. The timeline is attached to the Agenda. Ms. Tait, Director of Instruction - LS Reporting Order Director of Instruction, Ms. Tait, gave a verbal presentation about the ministry's new guidelines for reporting: 1) Continue with the current reporting policy with the addition of student self-assessment on core competencies 2) Minimum of five reports, concluding with a written summative report. This option would require no letter grades; however, teachers would have to be prepared to provide one if requested. Student self reporting is required in both options.
