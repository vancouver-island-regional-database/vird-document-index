2026 05 19 National Accessability Week Proclamation

Town of Ladysmith
2026-05-19
Original PDF

TOWN OF LADYSMITH PROCLAMATION National AccessAbility Week WHEREAS: National AccessAbility Week provides an opportunity for Canadians to promote inclusion and accessibility in communities and workplaces, to recognize progress, and to reaffirm a collective commitment to identifying and removing barriers; and WHEREAS: Individuals with disabilities continue to experience barriers related to information, facilities, employment, transportation, and communication supports, which can limit their ability to fully and equitably participate in community life; and WHEREAS: Accessibility and inclusion are essential to ensuring equity of opportunity and enabling all community members to participate fully in civic, social, and economic life; and WHEREAS: The 2026 theme, “Building a Strong, Accessible Canada,” highlights the shared responsibility of governments, organizations, and communities to advance accessibility through inclusive policies, practices, and environments; THEREFORE: I, Deena Beeston, Mayor of the Town of Ladysmith, do hereby proclaim May 31 – June 6, 2026 as “National AccessAbility Week” in the Town of Ladysmith, British Columbia. Issued this 19th day of May, 2026 Deena Beeston Mayor, Town of Ladysmith
