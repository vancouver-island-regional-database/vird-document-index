KelseyToye Subject: FW:Bus transportation to Barsby from Cinnabar 2016/2017 [maiIto:kjbowei|l@shaw.ca]l Katie bowell From: jeff‘& Sent: Wednesday, September 23, 2015 9:35 PM To: John Blain Subject: Bus transportation to Barsby from Cinnabar 2016/2017 Dear Mr Blain, My daughter came home with a letter last week signed by Scott Saywell regarding the 2016/2017 school year. Our family appreciates that although Cedar Secondary School will be reopening for the 2016 school year, we were given the choice to stay at John Barsby Community Secondary or move our daughter to Cedar Secondary School. My concern is the loss of bus service. Many of the kids in this community chose John Barsby Community Secondary School when Cedar Secondary was closed in June 2014. in some ways it’s good to see the Cedar school re-open, but it has a negative effect for kids in this community that have already attended Barsby. High school is hard enough, Without having to switch schools partway through. The kids are settled and happy and many want to remain at John Barsby. As a parentl feel that an effort should be made on your behalf of our children to assist in keeping them where they want to be. They are caught in the middle of many decisions that will affect their future. My hope is that you will reconsider busing our children to their chosen school if the need is there. Kindest regards, Katie Bowell
