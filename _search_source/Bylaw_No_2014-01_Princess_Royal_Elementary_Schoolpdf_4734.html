THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) DISPOSAL OF REAL PROPERTY BYLAW NO. 2014-01 (PRINCESS ROYAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL) WHEREAS Section 65 (5) of the School Act requires that a board disposes of property owned or administered by the board only by bylaw; AND WHEREAS pursuant to Section 96 (1) of the School Act; “land” includes any interest in land, including any right, title or estate in it of any tenure; AND WHEREAS pursuant to Section 96 (3) of the School Act a board may dispose of land or improvements subject to the orders of the Minister; AND WHEREAS Section 3 of the Disposal of Land or Improvements Order provides that Boards must not dispose of land or improvements by sale and transfer in fee simple or by way of a lease of 10 years or more unless such disposal is to another Board or an independent school for educational purposes or is approved by the Minister in accordance with Section 5 of the Disposal of Land or Improvements Order; AND WHEREAS the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) wishes to dispose of the former Princess Royal Elementary School; NOW THEREFORE be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (a) determines that the Board of Education will not require the land or improvements on which former Princess Royal Elementary School is located for future educational purposes; (b) approves pursuant to Section 96 (3) of the School Act, to exchange its interests in the former Princess Royal Elementary School to Tardon Developments Inc. 200 – 1808 Bowen Road, Nanaimo, BC, V9S 5W4 for the sum of $930,000, such property more particularly described as: Princess Royal Elementary School 260 Irwin Street, Nanaimo, BC PID’s: 008-912-068; 008-912-084; 008-912-131; 008-911-860 Lot 2, Block6, Plan VIP584, Section 1; Lot 3, Block 6, Plan VIP584, Section 1; Lot 4, Block 6, Plan VIP584, Section 1; Lot 1, Block 6, Plan VIP584, Section 1 260 Irwin Street, Nanaimo, BC PID’s: 008-825-793; 008-825-807; 008-825-785 Lot 13, Block 6, Plan VIP 584, Section 1, Lot 12, Block 6, Plan VIP584, Section 1; Lot 11, Block 6, Plan VIP584, Section 1 262 Irwin Street, Nanaimo, BC PID: 008-825-777 Lot 10, Block 6, Plan VIP584, (c) confirms that the disposal of Princess Royal Elementary School has received the necessary ministerial approval pursuant to Section 5 of the Disposal of Land or Improvements Order. This bylaw may be cited as School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Disposal of Real Property Bylaw No. 2014-01. READ A FIRST TIME THIS day of 2014. READ A SECOND TIME THIS day of 2014.
