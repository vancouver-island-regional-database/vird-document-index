UNFINISHED BUSINESS Trustee Budget 2012-2013 Mr. Turin distributed an updated budget worksheet for the Trustees Budget for 20122013. The Committee reviewed the budget and expenditures for trustees over the past three years and the planned budget for 2012-13. The committee asked for the review because trustees wanted a better method for allocating the trustees’ travel and professional development budget of $28,300. The committee is recommending that $3,100 be made available to each individual trustee for workshops and other professional development activities. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Mr. Turin reviewed the draft budget calendar and said these are the meetings that we will hold with this committee. This template will be updated as more information becomes available. He said we have a much broader schedule that will include meetings with senior staff, union representatives, TTLC budget committee, etc. Enrolment Update Mr. Turin provided the Committee with the preliminary numbers for student enrolment as of September 17. He said this is the most up to date information he has at this time. He said the numbers include only “bricks and mortar” schools with numbers from alternate and distance learning still to be compiled. He said that the latest numbers show that enrolment has declined at both the elementary and secondary levels. There are 174 fewer students than were projected for this school year. Mr. Turin said that the numbers will continue to fluctuate until the official enrolment count at the end of the month. He said that because the district is receiving funding protection, the lower enrolment numbers will not have an impact on this year’s budget, but will have serious Draft Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held September 19, 2012 4 implications for the budget starting with the next school year.
