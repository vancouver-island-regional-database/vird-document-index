SD68 Transportation Route 1 Trip 2 Pleasant Valley Elementary School May 2, 2016 Michael & Regan Shepherd Outline • Introduction • The Route to School • Safety Considerations • ICBC Statistics • Future Parent Input Session Introduction • 41 children are currently registered for this Route • Ineligible/ Courtesy Rider:  Based on the out dated definition from 1986, these riders are defined as Ineligible / Courtesy Riders since they are within 4 km of Pleasant Valley School • Our bus stop is exactly 4km from PVS • We would like the definition of an ineligible rider to be revised • Safety of the children should be strongly considered in the revised definition before any these routes are cancelled The Route to School • To get to School Children would have to cross the Nanaimo Parkway and walk down the following busy streets  Jinglepot Road (some sections no sidewalks)  Mostar Road  Metral Drive Safety Considerations Extremely busy roads High accident rates & very high speeds Walking times are during rush hour Dark in winter months, challenging in the rain Length of the walk – approximately 1 hour for an adult.
