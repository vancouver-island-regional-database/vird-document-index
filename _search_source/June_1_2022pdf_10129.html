Matthews, Manager Admin. Services 1. CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 5:32 pm. 2. ADDITIONS There were no additions. 3. DELETIONS There were no deletions. 4. CHANGE IN ORDER There was no change in order. 5. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA S22/06/01-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 1 6. NEW BUSINESS 6.1 Scott Saywell, Superintendent Re: NDSS Community Field Renaming Scott Saywell and Joan Brown outlined the discussions with Snuneymuxw First Nation and the City of Nanaimo regarding the naming of the jointly operated sport facilities next to NDSS. An Action Sheet with proposed next steps is attached to the Agenda. S22/06/01-02 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Higginson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Stanley That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to commence a 30 day consultation period as per Policy 2.17 Public Participation to change the name of the NDSS Community Field to “q’unq’inuqwstuxw”. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY 6.2 Scott Saywell, Superintendent Re: Coal Tyee Elementary Name Change Scott Saywell and Joan Brown shared the work of the ad hoc Committee and proposed next steps with respect to the renaming of Coal Tyee Elementary School. Chair McKay provided a report from the Committee. An Action Sheet is attached to the Agenda. S22/06/01-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Keller IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to commence a 30 day consultation period as per Policy 2.17 Public Participation to change the name of Coal Tyee Elementary School to “syuẁén'ct”. 2 7.
