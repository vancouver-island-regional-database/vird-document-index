TOWN OF LADYSMITH BYLAW NO. 2123 A Bylaw to exempt from taxation certain Island Corridor Foundation lands and buildings for the years of 2023-2032 The Council of the Town of Ladysmith in open meeting assembled enacts as follows: 1. Charitable, Not-for Profit and Recreational Tax Exemption The Island Corridor Foundation Nonprofit lands and buildings listed on attached Schedule “A” and forming part of this bylaw shall be exempt from taxation for the calendar years 2023 to 2032, inclusive. 2. Citation This Bylaw may be cited for all purposes as "10 Year Island Corridor Foundation Permissive Tax Exemptions Bylaw 2022, No. 2123”.
