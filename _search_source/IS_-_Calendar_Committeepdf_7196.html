NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: May 27, 2020 Board of Education Tim Davie, Deputy Superintendent Local School Calendar Committee Background At the December 18, 2019, Regular Board of Education Meeting, the following motion was adopted: Folio: R19/12/18-09 That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) immediately strike an advisory ad hoc local school calendar committee with appropriate representation; and That the local school calendar committee develop and recommend a local school calendar, to the Education Committee and Board of Education. Information The Department of Learning Services subsequently established a Local School Calendar Committee with appropriate stakeholder representation. To date, the Committee has met on two occasions (March 5, 2020 and May 4, 2020). The following topics were discussed: 1. 2. 3. 4. Three Year Local School Calendar Instructional Time Consistent School Schedules Professional Learning Communities (PLCs) The Calendar Committee recommends that the bell schedule for elementary schools be changed to reflect a consistent dismissal schedule Monday to Friday. This means that there will no longer be early dismissal on Mondays, resulting in equal instructional time each day. Next steps include a review of the draft bell schedule by the Manager of Transportation to confirm that bussing schedules can be adjusted accordingly, followed by a consultation at the school level to change the bell schedule. The Calendar Committee will meet again in early fall to discuss community feedback received during the 2020/21 Local School Calendar Consultation and to make recommendations for a Local School Calendar for the years 2021/22 and 2022/23.
