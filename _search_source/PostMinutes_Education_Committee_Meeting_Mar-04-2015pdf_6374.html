CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Education Committee Meeting held March 4, 2015 APPROVAL OF THE MINUTES Minutes - February 4, 2015 Folio E15/03/04-02 That the minutes of the Education Committee Meeting held February 4, 2015 be adopted. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS Ms. Janet Mansbridge Registered Speech and Language Pathologist RE: The role of Speech and Language Pathologists in Schools Ms. Janet Mansbridge presented to the Education Committee the important work our speech and language pathologists perform in our schools. Her presentation was on behalf of the district's speech and language pathologists, which included information on student challenges, classroom opportunities and success stories. We have approximately 1,300 to 2,000 students in the district with communication difficulties that are significant enough to affect learning success. Ms. Sherry Wade/Diane Goodman/Geoff Hall RCMP Liaison RE: The Role of Liaison Officers in Schools Acting Corporal Sherri Wade, of the Nanaimo RCMP Youth Unit presented to the Board the role of the RCMP Liaison Officers in our schools. The Youth Unit currently consists of four constables and one Acting Corporal and responds to calls for service at 34 schools in Nanaimo. In the community of Ladysmith, there are currently no dedicated liaison officers, however, they do have officers that work closely with the schools. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae That the Education Committee move the Role of the School Liaison Officer to New Business, item 11.1.
