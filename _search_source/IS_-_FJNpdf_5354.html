Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BUSINESS COMMITTTE INFORMATION SHEET DATE: March 9, 2016 TO: Business Committee FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary Treasurer SUBJECT: Frank J. Ney Elementary Addition Update Background: The Board passed the following school closure bylaw at its meeting on December 2, 2015: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Rutherford Elementary School, 5840 Hammond Bay Road, Nanaimo, BC, V9T 5M6; Facility No. 068077; on June 30, 2017 or later, subject to the completion of a new wing at Frank J. Ney School. In the event capital funding is not approved for the Frank J. Ney construction, or construction is delayed such that it is not expected to be completed by September 2018, the issue of the closure of Rutherford Elementary School will be revisited by the Board. A Project Identification Report (PIR) for the new wing at Frank J. Ney Elementary has been submitted to the Ministry of Education along with a revised 5 Year Capital Plan. The Ministry is in the process of reviewing capital plans from all districts, and as of today’s date, no decisions have been made yet, by the Ministry. The PIR and Five Year Capital Plan Summary are attached for your information.
