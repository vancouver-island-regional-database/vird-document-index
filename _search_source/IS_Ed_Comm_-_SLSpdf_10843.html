NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS EDUCATION COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: March 1, 2023 Education Committee Laura Tait, Deputy Superintendent Student Learning Survey 2021/22 Background The Student Learning Survey (SLS), formerly known as the Student Satisfaction Survey is a Ministry data tool that is administered across the province. It has been administered every year since 2001. It surveys students in grades 4, 7, 10 and 12 about their school experience. It is also open to their parents and to all staff in BC public schools. The survey covers a range of topics, providing a comprehensive picture of the educational experience. Questions were developed with input from partner groups, teachers and experts in educational measurement and special education. The survey is now open each year from January to April. To help address local issues, schools and districts can add custom questions. NLPS does not have custom questions at this time. # of Questions: Grade 4 – 57 Grade 7 – 72 Grade 10 and 12 – 81 Staff – 14 Parent - 47 Questions cover a range of topics such as: assessment, belonging, choice, communication, curriculum, demographics (grade, gender, Indigenous, designation), drugs, engagement in learning, feedback, food security, future goals, human development, mental, mental health, nutrition, participation, physical, safety, satisfaction, self-efficacy, social development, support.
