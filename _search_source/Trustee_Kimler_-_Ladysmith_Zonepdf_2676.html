Trustee Zone Report 15-Mar-2017 Ladysmith Zone Ladysmith Intermediate School (LIS)  Night of Celebrating Learning – On March 9th from 5:30 to 6:30, the school extended an invitation to parents to visit their child’s class to look through their portfolios. It was a great opportunity for parents to hear about their child’s learning.  Drug Abuse Resistance Education (DARE) – This program with Constable Knott will begin after Spring break. Ladysmith Primary School (LPS)  Happy Camper Book Fair – Thanks to PAC, parents, students & staff for making the fair a success. Over $3,150 in sales helped to exceed the goal. This resulted in over $1k-worth of books added to the school library.  Peter Pan – On Thursday, March 16th from 9:15 to 10:00 am Dufflebag Theatre will be visiting in the morning (and then travelling to LIS for an afternoon performance). Ecole North Oyster  Family Literacy Week – Students read for a total of 46,480 minutes (774.6 hours or 32.2 days) during the Read-a-Thon! Congrats to the 33 students winning book prizes & to 4 lucky grand prize winners winning $75 gift cards to Chapters Books.  Jump Rope for Heart Event – Students turn in their pledges & participate in rope jumping all to help the Canadian Heart & Stroke Foundation raise awareness & research these common diseases. Ladysmith Secondary School (LSS)  Red Cross Babysitting Course – is being offered at half-price over Spring Break at LSS for ages 11-15. This 7-8 hour course has an emphasis on First Aid, but covers everything from managing difficult behaviours to content on leadership & professional conduct as a babysitter. Trustee Kimler
