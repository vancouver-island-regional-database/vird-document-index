THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF ANNUAL BOARD MEETING HELD DECEMBER 4, 2013 Trustees D. Neary, in the Chair D. Allen B. Bard J. Brennan N. Curley (via telephone) K. Howland B. Robinson T. Saunders S. Welch Call to Order Trustee Curley - Participation in Tonight's Meeting via Telephone Folio A13/12/04-01 Election of Board Chair Staff D. Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO G. Roberts, Asst. Secretary-Treasurer J. Blain, Deputy Superintendent D. Reimer, Director of Communications C. Kelt, Executive Assistant Meeting No. A08 The Assistant Secretary-Treasurer called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Allen That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves that in accordance with Board Procedure No. 2.5 that Trustee Curley be allowed to provide her personal vote this evening, where required by secret ballot, in confidence to the Executive Assistant, Ms. Cathy Kelt. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Assistant Secretary-Treasurer Roberts said he will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board and will confirm that each Trustee nominated confirms their acceptance of the nomination by asking “do you accept? Mr. Roberts said that in the event there was an election, Mr. John Blain and Ms. Donna Reimer would act as scrutineers. Trustee Welch nominated Trustee Neary for the position of Board Chair. Trustee Howland seconded this nomination. Mr. Roberts called for nominations a second time. Mr. Roberts called for nominations a third Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 4, 2013 time. Mr. Roberts asked Trustee Neary if she accepted the nomination for the position of Board Chair. Trustee Neary stated that she did. Mr. Roberts declared Trustee Neary Board Chair. Asst. Secretary-Treasurer vacates the Chair Mr. Roberts vacated the Chair and passed the gavel to Chair Neary. Election of Vice-Chair Chair Neary called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Howland nominated Trustee Allen for the position of Board Vice-Chair. Trustee Saunders seconded this nomination. Chair Neary called for nominations a second time. Chair Neary called for nominations a third time. Chair Neary asked Trustee Allen if she accepted the nomination for the position of Board ViceChair. Trustee Allen stated that she did. Chair Neary declared Trustee Allen Board ViceChair. Election of Business Committee Chair Chair Neary called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Saunders nominated Trustee Brennan for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Howland seconded this nomination. Chair Neary called for nominations a second time. Chair Neary called for nominations a third time. Chair Neary asked Trustee Brennan if he accepted the nomination for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Brennan stated that he did. Chair Neary declared Trustee Brennan Chair of the Business Committee. Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 4, 2013 Election of Education Committee Chair Chair Neary called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Brennan nominated Trustee Howland for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Bard seconded this nomination. Chair Neary called for nominations a second time. Chair Neary called for nominations a third time. Chair Neary asked Trustee Howland if she accepted the nomination for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Howland stated that she did. Chair Neary declared Trustee Howland Chair of the Education Committee. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Representative Chair Neary called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Allen nominated Trustee Saunders for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Robinson seconded this nomination. Chair Neary called for nominations a second time. Chair Neary called for nominations a third time. Chair Neary asked Trustee Saunders if She accepted the nomination for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Saunders stated that she did. Chair Neary declared Trustee Saunders BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Alternate Representative Chair Neary called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Alternate Provincial Councillor. Trustee Saunders nominated Trustee Robinson for the position of BCSTA Alternate Provincial Councillor. Trustee Howland seconded this nomination. Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 4, 2013 Chair Neary called for nominations a second time. Chair Neary called for nominations a third time. Chair Neary asked Trustee Robinson if he accepted the nomination for the position of BCSTA Alternate Provincial Councillor. Trustee Robinson stated that he did. Chair Neary declared Trustee Robinson BCSTA Alternate Provincial Councillor. For Information CUPE/Board Collective Bargaining Chair Neary announced that the Canadian Union of Public Employees, Local 606, and the SD68 Board of Education have both ratified a new collective agreement. ADJOURNMENT IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Welch Folio A13/12/04-02 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:11 pm. Dot Neary, Board Chair G. Roberts, Asst. Secretary-Treasurer
