THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 (NANAIMO-LADYSMITH) CHASE RIVER ELEMENTARY SCHOOL WATERWORKS RIGHT-OF-WAY AND COVENANT BYLAW, 2023 WHEREAS Section 65(5) of the School Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 412, provides that a board of education may exercise a power with respect to the acquisition or disposal of property owned or administered by the board of education only by bylaw; AND WHEREAS: (a) The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (the “Board”) owns the Chase River Elementary School site (the “Property”); (b) the Property is facility number 6868022; (c) the address of the Property is 1503 Cranberry Avenue, Nanaimo, British Columbia V9R 6R7, and the legal description of the Property is: Parcel Identifier: 002-972-379 Lot 1, Sections 18 and 19, Cranberry District, Plan 24118 Except that Part in Plan VIP77486; (d) (e) the City of Nanaimo (the “City”) has asked the Board to grant the City: (i) a blanket statutory right of way over the Property (the “Right-of-Way”) pursuant to Section 218 of the Land Title Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 250, in respect of water meters, meter reading devices, electrical connections and other works (the “Works”) for measuring the volume of water supplied to the building on the Property, including the initial Works shown on the plan attached hereto as Schedule A; and (ii) a covenant (the “Covenant”) pursuant to Section 219 of the Land Title Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 250, with respect to the use of the Property and the building thereon; and the Board is satisfied that the Works will benefit the Board and that the granting of the Right-of-Way and Covenant will not interfere with the Board’s use of the Property for educational purposes. NOW THEREFORE be it resolved as a Bylaw that the Board grant the Right-of-Way and Covenant; and BE IT FURTHER resolved as a Bylaw that the Secretary-Treasurer be and is hereby authorized, on behalf of the Board, to execute and deliver all documents required to complete the granting of the Right-ofWay and the Covenant on such terms and conditions as the Secretary-Treasurer may consider advisable as witnessed by the signature of the Secretary-Treasurer.
