TOWN OF LADYSMITH PROCLAMATION WHEREAS: National AccessAbility Week is a week for Canadians to promote inclusion and accessibility in our communities and workplaces, to celebrate our progress and to be inspired to further break down accessibility barriers; and WHEREAS: Individuals with disabilities are impacted daily by barriers to information, facilities, employment, transportation and communication supports, therefore negatively challenging an individual’s ability to fully participate in community life; and WHEREAS: Accessibility and inclusion are essential for ensuring that all community members have equity in opportunities and the ability to fully participate in community life. THEREFORE, I, Aaron Stone, Mayor of the Town of Ladysmith, do hereby proclaim May 26 – June 1, 2024 as “National AccessAbility Week” in the Town of Ladysmith, British Columbia. May 16, 2024 ___________________________________ Mayor A. Stone
