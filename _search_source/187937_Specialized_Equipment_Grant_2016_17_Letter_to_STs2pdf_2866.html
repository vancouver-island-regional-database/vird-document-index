Our Ref: 187937 May 30, 2016 Dear Secretary Treasurer: Re: Specialized Equipment Grants Specialized Equipment Grants (SEGs) are distributed to school districts when funds are available within the Ministry of Education’s Provincial Resource Program (PRP) Budget. Due to increasing cost pressures within the PRPs for the 2016/17 school year, there are no additional funds available for SEGs. PRPs are funded through a Special Purpose Grant under Section 115 of the School Act. School districts who host a PRP are allocated operational funding through an annual budget review process. When there are unallocated PRP funds or recoveries, funds are redistributed to all school districts by way of SEGs. In the past three years, school districts have received SEGs as follows: • $700K 2013/14 • $1.2M 2014/15 • $215K 2015/16 In March, 2016, Ministry staff identified $2M of unspent funds held by school districts in their Special Purpose Accounts for SEGs. We will continue to review all SEGs and PRPs to ensure they continue to support districts in meeting the needs of special needs students across British Columbia.
