February 17, 2017 To: Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) Re: IB Society to support the International Baccalaureate program in SD68 Dear Trustees: Thank you for receiving our presentations at the last Education Committee meeting February 1st, 2017. We listened to and appreciated your comments and concerns regarding the implementation of the IB Primary Years Programme at Fairview Community school. Since the meeting, we have continued to explore ways to address your concerns. It is our opinion that forming a registered society to fundraise money to cover the cost of the IB fees would be a positive solution. We are hopeful that the board will recognize the value that comes with the additional expense of having this programme at Fairview Community School for the benefit of all public students district wide. However, if the board feels that receiving additional funds to offset the annual IB fees is a critical aspect to the implementation of IB in SD 68, then we will definitely form the society to raise those funds once the draft implementation budget has been approved. To be clear, this society would be a non-profit charitable society with no affiliation to any PAC group. We look forward to working with the Nanaimo-Ladysmith school district for the successful implementation of an IB programme. Sincerely, Alia Acton (parent & PAC Chair Fairview Community school) Dr. Johnathan Lam (parent, optometrist)
