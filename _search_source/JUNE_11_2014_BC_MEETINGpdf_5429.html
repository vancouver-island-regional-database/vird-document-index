CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Cedar Conversion to Elementary  Staff reviewed the motion amendment and referral and concluded that the Business Committee wished staff to: • Consider how the “playfield” (as indicated on the plans) could be upgraded as part of the project or AFG • To ensure the cost of Cedar Elementary was effective as possible maximizing possible savings which could be reinvested in the “playfield” Cedar Conversion to Elementary • The project design development is complete and the most recent estimate is for $2.0M • As the project was previously budgeted for $2.0M no savings were identified by staff over the provisional budget • The site work, playground, and playfield design are complete and out to tender • Tender scheduled to close June 13th • The playfield portion of the tendered work is estimated at $500,000 • The site work and playground portion of the site tender are included in project budget Cedar Conversion to Elementary • As staff and Trustees are aware the AFG envelope has been and continues to be challenged by district needs, the inclusion of focused 10 year plan initiatives has further increased the challenge. Given the above, staff are not recommending funding Cedar playfield with AFG. Staff recommend that if the Trustees would like to add the Cedar playfield, that local capital is considered.
