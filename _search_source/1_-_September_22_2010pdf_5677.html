CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY There were no presentations this evening. Mr. Green gave the Business Committee an update on the following: The Finance Department is in the process of preparing a three-year financial forecast, as requested by the Board of Education. It is expected that the forecast will be presented at the October Business Committee meeting. When the Board decided to implement a twoweek spring break in March 2011, it also decided to allocate some funding to help lessen the impact of the two-week school closure on support staff in schools. District staff have worked with the CUPE executive to develop a plan that will allocate additional professional development time to education assistants.
