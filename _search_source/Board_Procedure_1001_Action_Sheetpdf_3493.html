THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: December 5, 2011 TO: The Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Board Procedure 1001(C)P Board Meetings and Standing Committee Meetings Board Procedure No. 1030P Section IV – Inaugural Meetings – Rules of Order BACKGROUND The Board at its October 26th meeting received the report from Mike McAvoy regarding the Governance Structures and Processes and referred it to the Board Development Plan. (R11/10/26-06) The Board will meet on Thursday, December 15th and begin the Board Development Process. Past practice of the Board has been to appoint Chairpersons for its three Standing Committees; Education, Business and Human Resources; at the Inaugural meeting in December. Due to the Governance Structures and Processes Report it is recommended that the Board consider waiving Procedure 1001(C)P for one year (December 5, 2012) with respect to its Standing Committees and Procedure 1030P Section IV (last paragraph) with respect to an election of a Human Resources Committee Chairperson while it reviews its governance structures and processes and proceed with a Standing Committee for both Education and Business at this time. These two standing committees will meet on a monthly basis and all nine Trustees will be members of the committee including the regular partner group representatives. RECOMMENDATIONS The Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) waives Board Procedure 1001(C)P Board Meetings with respect to its three Standing Committee meetings for Education, Business and Human Resources and Procedure 1030P Section IV (last paragraph) for a period of one year. The Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) proceeds with an Education Standing Committee and a Business Standing Committee that includes all nine Trustees and the regular partner group representatives for a one year period (December 5, 2012).
