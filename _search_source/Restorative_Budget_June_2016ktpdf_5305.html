Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION ACTION SHEET DATE: June 8, 2016 TO: Business Committee FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Restorative Budget Background: The Business Committee passed the following motion at its April 13 Special Business Committee meeting: Folio BC16/04/13-03 The Business Committee recommends to the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) that upon completion of the 2016-17 budget, staff prepare a "needs based" budget using staffing levels from the 2002-2003 year to be submitted with the actual budget. The attached analysis compares the 2016-17 budget to a more restorative budget based on the noted assumptions included in the analysis. This analysis take into account the efficiencies the Board has realized over the years on school closures and calculates an underfunding of $4.8 million dollars. Recommendation: The Business Committee recommends to the Board Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) that the Board Chair write a letter to the Minister of Education and the Minister of Finance expressing the Board’s concern in its ability to improve educational outcomes while receiving provincial funding which has not kept up with the costs downloaded to districts.
