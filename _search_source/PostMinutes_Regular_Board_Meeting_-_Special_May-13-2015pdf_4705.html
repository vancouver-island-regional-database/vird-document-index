THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 MINUTES OF SPECIAL BOARD MEETING HELD May 13, 2015 Trustees S. Rae, Chair S. Higginson, Vice-Chair N. Bob J. Brennan T. Brzovic S. Kimler B. Robinson N. Routley J. Solomon Staff J. Blain, Superintendent/CEO G. Roberts, A/Secretary-Treasurer P. Sabo, Director of Planning/Operations D. Burgos, Director of Communications K. Toye, Executive Assistant Meeting No. 17 Absent P. Trick, Associate Superintendent/HR CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio SB15/05/13-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY FROM THE BUSINESS COMMITTEE 2015-16 Annual Budget IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Solomon Folio SB15/05/13-02 The Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approves the 2015-16 budget as presented at the May 6, 2015 Special Business Committee meeting with the following amendment: To move $200,000 earmarked for the NDSS project (year two (2) and three (3)) and that this money be invested as additional classroom support for students. Opposed by Trustee(s) Brennan, Routley, Brzovic CARRIED Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held May 13, 2015 NEW BUSINESS 2015-2016 Annual Budget Bylaw IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Solomon Folio SB15/05/13-03 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $133,769,760 for fiscal year 2015-2016 and that it be given first reading. Opposed by Trustee(s) Brzovic, Routley, Brennan CARRIED IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Solomon Folio SB15/05/13-04 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $133,769,760 for fiscal year 2015-2016 and that it be given second reading. Opposed by Trustee(s) Brzovic, Routley, Brennan CARRIED IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Kimler IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Rae Folio SB15/05/13-05 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $133,769,760 for fiscal year 2015-2016 at its Special Board Meeting of May 13, 2015. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Solomon Folio SB15/05/13-06 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $133,769,760 for fiscal year 2015-2016 and that it be given a third and final reading. Opposed by Trustee(s) Brzovic, Routley, Brennan CARRIED Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held May 13, 2015 QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions this evening. ADJOURNMENT IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Routley Folio SB15/05/13-07 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:07 pm.
