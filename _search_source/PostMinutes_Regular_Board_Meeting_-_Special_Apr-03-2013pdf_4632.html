THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 688 MINUTES OF SPECIAL BOARD MEETING HELD APRIL 3, 2013 Trustees J. Brennan, in the Chair D. Allen B. Bard N. Curley K. Howland D. Neary B. Robinson T. Saunders S. Welch Staff D. Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO P. Turin, Secretary-Treasurer Meeting No. S12 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:00 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio S13/04/03-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS Repeal of Facilities Plan Change Bylaw IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Bard Folio S13/04/03-02 That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw is repealed effective April 3, 2013, and that it is given third and final reading. Opposed by Trustee Allen CARRIED QUESTION PERIOD Mike Ball Did this change completely repeal the school closure bylaw? Chair Brennan replied that the District Facilities Change Process Bylaw is repealed. Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held April 3, 2013 If so, do you have plans for a replacement bylaw? Chair Brennan replied no, not at this time. ADJOURNMENT Folio S13/04/03-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 6:06 pm. Jamie Brennan, Board Chair Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer
