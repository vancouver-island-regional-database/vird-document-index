THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: April 27, 2011 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Capital Project Bylaw 126310 2011-12 Annual Facilities BACKGROUND: On March 30, 2011 school districts were advised of their Annual Facility Grant funding. For 2011-12 school districts will receive an operating portion and a capital portion of AFG funding. The capital portion received by SD68 was $1,662,872 and requires the passage of a bylaw. Although the school district has not submitted its AFG plan to the Ministry for approval, we want to avoid any timing issues with access to funding and the Ministry has recommended that Board’s adopt the capital portion of their AFG funding as soon as possible. FIRST READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw 126310 2011-12 Annual Facilities, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the Bylaw’s title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 126085 2011-12 Annual Facilities Grant and that it be given first reading. SECOND READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: UNANIMOUS CONSENT ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw 126310 2011-12 Annual Facilities Grant, the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 126085 2011-12 Annual Facilities and gives it a second reading. In order for the third and final reading of the Capital Project Bylaw 126310 2011-12 Annual Facilities Grant to take place at the same meeting as the first and second readings, unanimous approval of the board is required. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 126310 - 2011-12 Annual Facilities Grant and that it be given third reading. THIRD & FINAL READING ACTION REQUIRED: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw 126310 - 2011-12 Annual Facilities Grant the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 126085 2011-12 Annual Facilities and that it be given third and final reading. BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 CAPITAL PROJECT BYLAW 126310 A BYLAW by the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (hereinafter called the "Board") to adopt Capital Project Bylaw 126310 2011-12 Annual Facilities. WHEREAS in accordance with provisions of the School Act the Minister of Education (hereinafter called the "Minister") has approved a capital plan of the Board. NOW THEREFORE the Board agrees to the following: (a) upon approval to proceed, commence the Project(s) and proceed diligently and use its best efforts to complete the project substantially in accordance with the Project Agreement (where required); (b) observe and comply with any rule, policy or regulation of the Minister as may be applicable to the Board or the Project; and, (c) maintain proper books of account, and other information and documents with respect to the affairs of the Project, as may be prescribed by the Minister. NOW THEREFORE the Board enacts as follows: 1. The capital project of the Board approved by the Minister and specifying a maximum expenditure of $1,662,872 is hereby adopted. 2. This Bylaw may be cited as “School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 201112 Annual Facilities Grant - Capital Project Bylaw No. 126310. READ A FIRST TIME THE 27th day of April 2011. READ A SECOND TIME THE 27th day of April 2011.
