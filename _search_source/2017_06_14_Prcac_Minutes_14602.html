Minutes of the Parks, Recreation & Culture Advisory Committee Wednesday, Jun 14, 2017 COMMITTEE MEMBERS PRESENT: Tim Richards, Chair Roger Kimmerly Jim Allair Councillor Duck Paterson Bruce Mason Tara Pollock Tim Godau STAFF PRESENT: Clayton Postings REGRETS: Gord Horth AGENDA Moved and seconded: 2017-13: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee approve the agenda for the meeting as presented. Motion carried. MINUTES Minutes not provided. TOUR Kathy Holmes provided a tour of the Art Gallery. REFERRED BY COUNCIL Parks Bylaw, Hours of Use – Commissioners made the following recommendation after a review of the proposed time changes and discussion regarding access and enforcement regulations if required: Moved and seconded: 2017-14: That Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee recommend that all parks remain open to the public from 5:00am to 10:00pm with the exception of Transfer Beach Park, which will remain open 24-hours per day and the Ladysmith Skate Park which will remain open from 5:00am to 9:00pm. UNFINISHED BUSINESS Permanent Event Signage Station at Aggie Hall – tabled. NEW BUSINESS Nil. UPDATES Department Update: PRC Report: No updates FJCC Lease Space Update: C. Postings informed the Committee that a proposal from a local health and wellness service provider was accepted. Construction will take place in the following months. It is anticipated that the clinic will open in September. Waterfront Area Plan: No update, however there will be an open house to review plans on July 18.
