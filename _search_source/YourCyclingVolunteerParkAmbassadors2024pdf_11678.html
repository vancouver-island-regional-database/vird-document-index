Your Cycling Volunteer Park Ambassadors Your cycling, City of Nanaimo Volunteer Park Ambassadors (VPAs), are the co-leaders of GoByBikeBC Team Copenhagen. Your ambassadors were drafted into the program when Melissa Griswold and Joel Canfield were spotted helping the VIU Eco Club doing a litter and garbage clean up at the Colliery Dam Park. As the pair of seniors use e-bikes and trailers as their primary form of transportation, every mundane shopping trip is also an opportunity to pass through parks cleaning up litter and illegal dumping. Your VPAs clean up minor graffiti, and report tougher graffiti and vandalism to the City of Nanaimo. The city then assigns paid staff to deal with the reported issues. Normal Volunteer Park Ambassadors are expected to frequent a specific park, to answer questions from the public, do light litter pickup, and report problems such as vandalism. Although officially assigned to the E&N Trail, your cycling VPAs rove City Parks from Cinnabar to Woodgrove Mall. Your friendly cycling VPAs do NOT enforce by-laws, but may politely remind park users of by-laws that should be respected. If you are interested in becoming a “normal” volunteer you can apply at https://www.nanaimo.ca/get-involved/volunteer/partners-inparks/volunteer-park-ambassador-program . The cycling Volunteer Park Ambassadors are available to assist you with your volunteer community litter clean up events. The City of Nanaimo loans pickers, pails, and safety vests to groups that apply; https://www.nanaimo.ca/get-involved/volunteer/volunteer-work-parties . The city also supplies garbage bags, and will arrange for the bagged garbage to be picked up at the conclusion of your volunteer work event. Your cycling VPAs can dramatically increase the productivity of your event, by taking full bags from your team mates who are working on foot, and giving out fresh bags. Your team does not have to walk back the collection point with heavy bags. We also have sharps containers on our bikes, so we can safely handle any needles you call in for us to pick up. If you would like Joel and Melissa to assist with your event, please email TeamCopenhagen@shaw.ca .
