NEW BUSINESS Amended Budget for 2011-2012 Mr. Turin said that every year at this time, school districts are required to submit an amended budget to the Ministry of Education. This reflects the changes that have been made to the budget since it was adopted last spring. Mr. Turin reviewed the district’s amended budget for 2011-12 with committee members. He said that because the district qualifies for the Ministry’s funding protection program, the government revenue for the district has not changed significantly. To emphasize this commitment, the following beliefs and values and budget principles will be employed by the District in the development of its annual operating budget. Budget Beliefs & Values 1. We believe that schools exist for learners. 2. We believe in providing a balanced curriculum to the whole learner. 3. We believe that learning is a process that individuals can and should pursue throughout their lifetimes. 4. We believe that safe and healthy environments contribute to student learning. 5. We value the pursuit of excellence in teaching and learning. 6. We believe that education is a shared responsibility among school, home and community. 7. We believe educational leadership is important in sustaining educational programs. 8. We value the dedication of our educators and support staff and the accomplishments of our learners. Budget Principles 1. The allocation of human and material resources should be directed first toward improving student learning. 2. The allocation of resources should respond to the diversity of student needs and the vulnerability of our learners 3. Resources should be provided to all learners at equitable and sustainable levels. 4. Program choices and adjustments should be made in the best interests of students and should be guided by credible research, successful professional past practice and thoughtful implementation of new or emerging practices. 5. Student learning is best served when adequate staffing exists at all levels of the organization and the necessary infrastructure supports are in place.
