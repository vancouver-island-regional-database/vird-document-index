Level 1: Building the Foundation What type of leadership do we value in School District #68? This fundamental piece is central to the development of a sustainable leadership succession plan for our district as it will greatly influence the ways that we are going to build up leadership capacity, manage the dynamics of leadership transitions, and the nature of developmental and training activities that would be aimed at facilitating such capacity building and transitions.
