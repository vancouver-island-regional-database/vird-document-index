THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 INFORMATION SHEET DATE: May 13, 2015 TO: Business Committee FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations Brent Spohn, Facilities Planning Supervisor SUBJECT: Cedar Elementary - Conversion to a Secondary School BACKGROUND: At the Special Board Meeting on January 14, 2015, the Board of Education unanimously carried the following motion: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) to suspend the completion of Cedar Elementary for the 2014/15 school year . . . At the Regular Board Meeting on April 22, 2015, the Board of Education carried the following motion: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) reopen Cedar Secondary/Elementary as a high school with a grade 8-12 configuration for the start of the 2016 school year. Work on the conversion of Cedar Secondary to an elementary school concluded in January 2015 about halfway through the process with renovations incomplete. District forces working on the conversion were re-assigned to other projects. Completion of renovations and/or adjustments to the scope of work in specific areas is required to re-configure the facility as a secondary school. SUMMARY STEPS TO ADVANCE AND IMPLEMENT CONVERSION: 1. Restart the re-design, planning and consultation process for the Cedar conversion a. In accordance with Administrative Procedure 500 and Procedure No. 4005P, re-establish the School Construction Committee (SCC) i. To review and make recommendations respecting school construction, design, construction schedule and arrange for consultation with school staff and school community ii.
