APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Saunders Folio S14/01/0-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS Report on Bussing to John Barsby & Ladysmith Secondary Chair Neary stated that tonight’s Agenda is a one item agenda and that it is to discuss bus service for secondary students from Cedar. The Board received a report from staff about the costs and the logistics of providing bus service for Cedar students who have chosen to attend Ladysmith Secondary instead of John Barsby Secondary. The report said that currently 197 students have indicated they will be attending the catchment school, John Barsby, and 187 students have indicated that they will attend Ladysmith Secondary. The report said that the additional annual operating cost of busing students from the Cedar Draft Minutes of the Special Board Meeting held January 8, 2014 area to John Barsby Secondary is expected to be $175,878. There would also be a one-time capital cost of $280,000 for two new buses. The report said that if bus service was also provided to Ladysmith Secondary, the annual operating cost would be $308,680 plus a onetime capital cost of $560,000 to acquire four new buses. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Saunders IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio S14/01/08-02 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) refers the issue of bussing in the Cedar/Ladysmith area be referred to the Transportation Committee. DEFEATED UNANIMOUSLY IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio S14/01/08-03 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receives the staff report on bussing to John Barsby Secondary and Ladysmith Secondary. Opposed by Trustees Welch, Howland and Robinson opposed.
