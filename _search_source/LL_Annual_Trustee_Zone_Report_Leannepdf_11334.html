ANNUAL TRUSTEE ZONE REPORT Trustee Leanne Lee School Year: 2022/2023 School Names/Zone: NDSS, Brechin Elementary, Forest Park Elementary, Gabriola Elementary, Hammond Bay Elementary Annual Highlights: Gabriola Elementary: Excited to be planning out the indigenous garden, New fence has been installed around the garden and students will be cleaning up the area and planting native species. Worm bed, flower garden and herb garden under way. Fantastic breakfast program, Community partnerships and highly active PAC. High focus on literacy and numeracy and acknowledging students efforts. Hammond Bay Elementary: Begins each school day with a minute of breath work – magic. Embracing their access to outdoor learning spaces with a focus on truth and reconciliation, inclusion and land based learning. Active school with school sports and cross country. Excited for the opening of the new addition and access to more learning spaces. Proud to have had grade seven students play Wonderwall on National Day for Truth and Reconciliation. Forest Park: High focus on numeracy and literacy, creating fun learning environments. Excited to have childcare on site. Principal connects with each classroom every morning to create relationships with students, highly successful for connections. Brechin Elementary: Thrilling Thursdays, creating an hour of stations to engage learning in small group activities that students enjoy, creativity focused. Recess zones to create calm and build relationships. High needs creates a very focused environment for engaging learners. Exciting breakfast in class Proud of meaningful partnerships with community partners. NDSS: Dynamic school, with lots of moving pieces. Highly active and effective student council, which is providing lots of learning opportunities for students, but also for staff through presentations. Lots of leadership opportunities to help with the large student population. A very full and busy school, but very happy student population.
