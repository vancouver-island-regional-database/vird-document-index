CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY PRESENTATIONS Dr. John Lam & Sophia La Bounty RE: International Baccalaureate (IB) Program Dr. John Lam and teacher, Sophia La Bounty presented to the Business Committee a proposal for a Primary Years Programme (PYP) and Middle Years Programme (MYP) International Baccalaureate (IB) program for the district. In their presentation, they indicated that there are many families in the district that are looking for other options and provided a petition in favour of the PYP and MYP. They feel that should the district provide and IB program, it would draw students from neighbouring districts, private and international schools. School District 68 would also be the only district on Vancouver Island to offer PYP and MYP in the public system.
