Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION SPECIAL BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: May 13, 2015 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, A/Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Annual Budget Bylaw for Fiscal Year 2015-2016 Background: Section 113 of the School Act, “Adoption of the Budget” states: 113. (1) A board, by bylaw, (a) must adopt an annual budget on or before June 30 of each year for the next fiscal year, and (b) may amend the annual budget adopted under paragraph (a) (2) If an operating grant to a board is amended under section 10.63 (6) or a grant is withheld or reduced under section 117(1), the minister may order that (a) the board, by bylaw, must amend its annual budget, and (b) the board must send a certified copy of the amended annual budget to the minister within 60 days of the order of the minister (3) At the request of the minister, the board must send to the minister a certified copy of the annual budget. The Annual Budget Bylaw for School District No. 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) for fiscal year 2015-2016 is attached and is referred to as the “Annual Budget Bylaw for fiscal year 2015-2016”. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $ 133,769,760 for fiscal year 2015-2016 and that it be given first reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $ 133,769,760 for fiscal year 2015-2016 and that it be given second reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts all three readings of the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $ 133,769,760 for fiscal year 2015-2016 at its Special Board Meeting of May 13, 2015. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) adopts the Annual Budget Bylaw in the amount of $ 133,769,760 for fiscal year 2015-2016 and that it be given a third and final reading.
