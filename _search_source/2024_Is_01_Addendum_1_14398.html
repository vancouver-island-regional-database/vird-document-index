Infrastructure Services Request for Proposals No. 2024-IS-01 2nd AVENUE SLOPE STABILITY Addendum #1 – Answers to Proponents’ Questions For further information please contact: Ryan Bouma Director of Infrastructure Services 250-245-6440 rbouma@ladysmith.ca Addendum Issue Date:March 18, 2024 RFP Issue Date: March 8, 2024 RFP Closing Date: April 4, 2024 2:00 pm, RFP Opening: April 4, 2024 2:15 pm, Location of Bid Opening: Ladysmith City Hall Request for Proposals – 2nd Avenue Slope Stability – Addendum #1 RFP #2024-IS-01 Page 2 Question 1 – What prompted the Town to conduct this study? Answer: The study has been on the Town’s capital list for several years. This last winter, a relatively small slough of the soil was observed, so the RFP was initiated. Question 2 – Are there any utilities on 2nd Avenue and have they had any performance issues? Answer: Yes there are utilities, see map below. There have NOT been any performance issues. Question 3 – Are there any budget limitations for this project? Answer: The Town’s budget is $35,000. Question 4 – What timelines need to be met for the design and/or construction of this project? Answer: There is not a deadline for this work, although a reasonable length of time should be included in the proposal. Construction needs are not yet identified. If the results lead staff to recommend a capital project, it will be in the 2025 budget at the earliest, unless the findings were urgent.
