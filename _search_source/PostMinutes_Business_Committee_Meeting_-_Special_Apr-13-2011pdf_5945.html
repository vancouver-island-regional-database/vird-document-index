CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS Budget 2011 - 2012 The Special Business Committee continued with its discussions about the proposals for balancing the 2011-12 budget. At the conclusion of the April 6th Special Business Committee the committee asked staff to develop an additional $500,000 in proposed budget reductions. Some of the proposals below are further Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 13, 2011 2 reductions to items tabled last week, while others are entirely new proposals. • A further reduction of school-based administration time – 0.55 FTE administrators $50,533 • A further reduction of the secondary staffing supplement for courses offered outside of the standard timetable – 0.64 FTE teachers – $58,802 • Further reduction of the Early Years Coordinator, for elimination of the position 0.60 FTE teacher and the addition of 0.20 FTE administrative officer for a net reduction of $44,806 • A reduction of school clerical positions – 3.00 FTE - $100,648 • Not replacing certain staff on the first day of absence - $100,000 • Further increase to the square footage assigned to custodians – 1.00 FTE - $52,906 • Further reductions to Information Systems capital equipment and other expenses - $70,000 • Reduction in the Maintenance Department’s replacement costs - $20,000 • Reduction in contracts for equipment repair $30,000 The committee asked staff questions related to the above proposals. Trustee McNamee left the meeting at 7:15 pm.
