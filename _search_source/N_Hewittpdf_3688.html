Nicole Hewitt 604 Nova Street, Nanaimo BC V9R 5S7 Phone (250)667-1269 or email: nickpushnic@gmail.com Mother of Bradley, Maverick & Mackenzie Burak April 15, 2015 School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 395 Wakesiah Avenue Nanaimo, British Columbia V9R 3K6 Re: Three-Year Local School Calendar At the SD68 meeting on March 18, 2015, the Board of Education directed staff to begin a 30 day consultation on the five recommendations created by the Nanaimo Ladysmith School Calendar Committee. As a parent of three young children at Park Ave School, I decided, as parents, we need to have a clear voice stating our opposition to your proposals. Majority of parents are not in support of a 2 week spring break. Most parents strongly oppose early dismissals. In fact, most parents want to see our kids spend more quality time in school then have it reduced. I created this petition at Change.org and it was live for 18 days and within that time frame it had 81 parents sign this petition – it has been attached to this letter. We, the parents of School District 68, recommend the following motion: That the Board of Education of Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools direct staff NOT to amend the Three-Year Local School Calendar. Thank you for reading this petition and acknowledging that the Parents of School District 68 are NOT in favor of such drastic changes to their children’s education.
