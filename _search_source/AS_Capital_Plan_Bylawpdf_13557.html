Background: As the Board is aware, a capital bylaw is required under the School Act (S. 143). It is non-ﬁnancial in nature and, only one bylaw is required for all capital projects approved by the Ministry. The bylaw once adopted by the Board, authorizes the Secretary-Treasurer to execute project and funding agreements related to the expenditures contemplated by the underlying capital plan.
