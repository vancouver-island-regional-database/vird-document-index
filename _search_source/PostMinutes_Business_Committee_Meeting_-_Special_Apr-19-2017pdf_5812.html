Graham, DASG Rep Meeting No. BC09 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 5:02 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio BC17/04/19-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS UNFINISHED BUSINESS Superintendent Blain & Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh RE: 2017-18 Proposed Preliminary Budget Mr Blain & Ms McVeigh presented the 2017-18 Proposed Preliminary Budget to the Business Committee. This Action Sheet is attached to the Agenda. Chair Kimler explained that this is a 'roll-over' budget as it would be without the SCC Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 19, 2017 implementations. There will be a second, separate budget that focuses on the SCC outcome and the total budget will be presented as a bylaw. DPAC President McKay brought forward a strong community parent desire of having 3 FTE for the Sexual Health program. General discussion and recognition of staff's work. Folio BC17/04/19-02 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Rae IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Solomon That the Business Committee recommends the Board of Education of School District No.68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) to adopt the 2017-18 budget as presented on April 12, 2017 which includes a 1.5M restricted contingency from accumulated surplus funds, as outlined in the presentation April 5, 2017. Opposed by Trustee(s) Routley, Brennan CARRIED Superintendent Blain & Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh RE: SCC Information Mr Blain provided a brief update as to where staff are at with the SCC work and what deadlines are coming up. QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions tonight. ADJOURNMENT Folio BC17/04/19-03 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 5:59 pm.
