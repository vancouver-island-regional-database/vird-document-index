THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: June 27, 2012 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Capital Project Bylaw 126475 – Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) BACKGROUND: FIRST READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: SECOND READING ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: The Ministry announced in April that they had initiated a capital funding program for school districts in an amount equal to, or greater than, the annual cost of carbon offsets for school districts. The cost of offsets for school districts in 2011 was $4.7 million and the Ministry has allocated $5 million for the CNCP in the 2012/13 government fiscal year. School District 68’s allocation is $25,068. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126475 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP), the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the Bylaw’s title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw 126475 in the amount of $25,068 and that it be given first reading. As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126475 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP), the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the Bylaw’s title and giving a summary of its contents. That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts Capital Project Bylaw No. 126475 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) in the amount of $25,068 and that it be given second reading. UNANIMOUS CONSENT ACTION REQUIRED: RECOMMENDATION: In order for the third and final reading of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126475 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) to take place at the same meeting as the first and second readings, unanimous approval of the Board is required. That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) gives unanimous consent to the Secretary-Treasurer to read the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126475 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) for a third and final time. THIRD & FINAL READING ACTION REQUIRED: As Trustees and members of the public are in possession of a copy of the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126475 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) the Secretary-Treasurer must present the Bylaw by reading the title and giving a summary of its contents. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts the Capital Project Bylaw No. 126475 Carbon Neutral Capital Program (CNCP) in the amount of $25,068 and that it be given third and final reading.
