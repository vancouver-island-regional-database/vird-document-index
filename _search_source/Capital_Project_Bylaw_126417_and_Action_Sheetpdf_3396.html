THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: April 27, 2012 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Capital Project Bylaw 126417 2012-13 Annual Facilities Grant BACKGROUND: At the March Board Meeting the Board adopted the AFG Plan for 2012-13 in the amount of $1,947,936. It is necessary for the Board to adopt a Bylaw in order for the Certificate of Approval to be issued. The Business Committee at its March 7th meeting reviewed the AFG Plan and the anticipated expenditures. AFG funding has been developed considering the following: - High priority District wide annual programs, including re-roofing, flooring and energy management. - Completion of selected high priority AFG projects, including those supporting educational program initiatives. - Funding in support of initiatives to reduce vandalism. - Contingency funding allowing for unforeseen emergent projects and program changes. BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 CAPITAL PROJECT BYLAW 126417 A BYLAW by the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) (hereinafter called the "Board") to adopt Capital Project Bylaw 126417 2012-13 Annual Facilities. WHEREAS in accordance with provisions of the School Act the Minister of Education (hereinafter called the "Minister") has approved a capital plan of the Board. NOW THEREFORE the Board agrees to the following: (a) upon approval to proceed, commence the Project(s) and proceed diligently and use its best efforts to complete the project substantially in accordance with the Project Agreement (where required); (b) observe and comply with any rule, policy or regulation of the Minister as may be applicable to the Board or the Project; and, (c) maintain proper books of account, and other information and documents with respect to the affairs of the Project, as may be prescribed by the Minister. NOW THEREFORE the Board enacts as follows: 1. The capital project of the Board approved by the Minister and specifying a maximum expenditure of $1,947,936 is hereby adopted. 2. This Bylaw may be cited as “School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) 201213 Annual Facilities Grant - Capital Project Bylaw No. 126417. READ A FIRST TIME THE 25th day of April 2012.
