THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: November 23, 2011 TO: Board of Education FROM: Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations SUBJECT: Pioneer Park License Reference:  School District 68/City of Nanaimo Joint Use – License to Occupy Pioneer Park Action Sheet Background: At the June 22, 2010 Facilities Planning Committee meeting the City’s request to license the Pioneer Park site was discussed and staff were requested to proceed with drafting a license and place the item on a future joint use agenda. At the SD68/City of Nanaimo Joint Use Meeting of October 15, 2010, the Committee was informed that City and District staff were working together on determining if the District could license its Dickenson Road property to the City while it remains vacant. Staff reviewed the license terms with the Asset Management Plan consultant to ensure that it was not at cross purposes with the current planning. At the September 6, 2011 Facilities Planning Committee meeting, the draft license was reviewed and staff was requested to finalize the license. The license was placed on the October 25, 2011 SD68/City of Nanaimo Joint Use Meeting. At the meeting, the Joint Use Committee discussed the license drafted for the purpose of the land being used as a public park (which off-leash dog walking is permitted) and it was unanimously agreed upon. Once the license has been signed, City and District staff will issue a joint press release with respect to communicating the intent of the agreement. It was discussed at the November 8th Facilities Planning Committee meeting. Therefore, staff proposes the following recommendation.
