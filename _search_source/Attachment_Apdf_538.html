We note that the Board can institute the charges (subject to the dispute mechanism in legislation) on developments of 4 doors or more but the local jurisdiction has the discretion to apply to charge to the lower number. Given that infill creating at least two doors but less than four is likely to have single family dwellings, a housing type that creates a high number of school age children, the Board feels that the charge is appropriate in the circumstances. We look forward to discussing this further and appreciate the work you do in growing our communities to welcome so many new families in the next decade.
