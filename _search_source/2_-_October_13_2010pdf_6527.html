CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY EDUCATIONAL UPDATE Report from Ms. Frisson and Ms. Southwick Learning Alternatives - Update Mr. Scott Saywell and Mr. Tim Davie Mr. Scott Saywell, District Principal of Learning Alternatives and Vice-Principals Mr. Tim Davie and Mr. Patrick Young told the committee about the recent growth in the Learning Alternative programs and described the programs and the numbers of students each program serves. The programs include alternate pathways for students, including the Learn@Home K-7 and 812 programs, the Career-Technical Centre, the Secondary School Apprenticeship Program and Summer School for Grades 8 and 9 students. Opportunity programs include the Junior and Senior Learning Alternative programs, the Aboriginal Outreach program, the program offered at the Nanaimo Correctional Centre and a new program, the Snuneymuxw House of Learning for adult learners. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan Folio EC10/10/13-04 That the Education Committee thanks the presenters from the Learning Alternatives Program and requests that they extend the Education Committee's appreciation of the work done by their staff.
