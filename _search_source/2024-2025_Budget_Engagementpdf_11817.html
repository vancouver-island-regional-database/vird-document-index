NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: March 27, 2024 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Consultation Feedback – 2024-2025 Budget Background On February 22, 2024, the district began the 2024-25 budget consultation process using the ThoughtExchange engagement tool and email feedback. Feedback on these two platforms completed on March 15, 2024. The community engagement webpage and background information with instructions on how to participate was posted on: https://www.sd68.bc.ca/board/community-engagement/ Information on the community engagement period was: • Posted in district news • Shared on social media (Facebook, X – formerly Twitter and Instagram) Consultation Timeline • • • • Thursday, February 22, 2024 o District news for staff o Social media posts o Posted on district website Monday, February 26, 2024 o District news for staff o Social media posts o Remained on home page of district website Thursday, March 7, 2024 o District news for staff o Social media posts o Remained on hoe page of district website Thursday, March 14, 2024 o Social media posts ThoughtExchange survey: As we continue to plan the upcoming budget, we asked some specific questions. What are your three top priorities? How important is it to invest in sustainable and environmentally friendly initiatives? How important are facilities compared to resources and supports for students? How important is technology in education? Results We did not receive email feedback. We did receive a message via social media about making playgrounds a priority, if possible. ThoughtExchange was the platform of preference during this consultation.
