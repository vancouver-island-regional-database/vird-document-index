Sproston, Manager IS Department Meeting No. A09 Call to Order The Superintendent called the meeting to order at 5:30 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. A/Secretary-Treasurer IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brzovic Folio A15/12/02-01 That Superintendent Blain act as SecretaryTreasurer. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Election of Board Chair A/Secretary-Treasurer Blain said he will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board and will confirm that each Trustee nominated confirms their acceptance of the nomination by asking "do you accept?" Mr. Blain said that in the event there was an election, Mr. Blain and Mr. Burgos would act as scrutineers. A/Secretary-Treasurer Blain said he will call for nominations for the position of Chairperson of the Board and will confirm that each Trustee nominated confirms their acceptance of the nomination by asking "do you accept?" Mr. Blain said that in the event there was an election, himself and Mr. Burgos would act as scrutineers. Trustee Robinson nominated Trustee Rae for the position of Board Chair. Trustee Routley nominated Trustee Kimler for the position of Board Chair. Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 2, 2015 Trustee Brzovic nominated Trustee Higginson for the position of Board Chair. Mr. Blain called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Mr. Blain asked Trustees Kimler and Higginson if they would accept the nomination. Both Trustees Kimler and Higginson said they decline. Mr. Blain asked Trustee Rae if he would accept the nomination. Trustee Rae stated that he did. Mr. Blain declared Trustee Rae Board Chair. A/Secretary-Treasurer vacates the Chair and the gavel is turned over to the Incoming Chair Mr. Blain vacated the Chair and passed the gavel to Chair Rae. Election of Vice-Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chairperson of the Board. Trustee Solomon nominated Trustee Higginson. Trustee Brennan nominated Trustee Brzovic. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and for a third time. Chair Rae asked Trustees Higginson and Brzovic if they would accept the nomination. Both Trustee Higginson and Trustee Brzovic indicated they do. Chair Rae declared there would be an election. The ballots were distributed. The ballots were collected and counted. Chair Rae advised that Trustee Higginson is the Vice-Chair of the Board. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Kimler Folio A15/12/02-02 That the ballots be destroyed. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 2, 2015 Election of Business Committee Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Bob nominated Trustee Kimler for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Kimler if he accepted the nomination for the position of Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Kimler stated he did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Kimler Chair of the Business Committee. Election of Business Committee Vice-Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Kimler nominated Trustee Solomon for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Solomon if he accepted the nomination for the position of Vice-Chair of the Business Committee. Trustee Solomon stated that he did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Solomon the Vice-Chair of the Business Committee Election of Education Committee Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Bob nominated Trustee Higginson for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Routley nominated Trustee Brennan for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Higginson and Trustee Brennan if they accepted the nomination for the position of Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Brennan declined and Trustee Higginson accepted. Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 2, 2015 Chair Rae declared Trustee Higginson the Chair of the Education Committee. Election of Education Committee Vice-Chair Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Bob for the position of Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Bob if she accepted the nomination for the position of ViceChair of the Education Committee. Trustee Bob stated that she did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Bob the Vice-Chair of the Education Committee. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Representative Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Brzovic for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Bob nominated Trustee Brennan for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Brzovic and Trustee Brennan if they accepted the position nomination for the position of BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Brennan declined. Trustee Brzovic stated that she did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Brzovic the BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Election of BCSTA Provincial Councillor Alternate Representative Chair Rae called for nominations for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Higginson nominated Trustee Brennan for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Draft Minutes of the Regular Board Meeting held December 2, 2015 Chair Rae called for nominations a second time and third time. Chair Rae asked Trustee Brennan if he accepted the nomination for the position of Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Trustee Brennan stated that he did. Chair Rae declared Trustee Brennan the Alternate BCSTA Provincial Councillor. Adjournment IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Kimler IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Solomon Folio A15/12/02-03 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 5:42 pm.
