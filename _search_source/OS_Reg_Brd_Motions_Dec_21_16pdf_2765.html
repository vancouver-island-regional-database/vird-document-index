SB16/08/31-14 That the Board of Education of School District 68 In Progress (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve adjustments to trustee remuneration, effective September 1, 2015 and September 1, 2016; and That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to provide the Board, at the October Regular meeting, with trustee remuneration comparisons; and R16/05-25-18 That staff develop and present a draft administrative procedure, at the October Regular meeting, for the annual review of trustee remuneration based on comparative Boards. The Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoIn Progress Ladysmith) requests that staff make the Administrative Review report public to our stakeholders with personal information on individuals from other districts redacted and that an addendum is included with the most relevant information. Carrie McVeigh No 19 Oct 2016 Carrie McVeigh No 27 Jul 2016 R16/04/27-11 The Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) requests staff to provide the Board with a report regarding any follow up that was made as a result of the last letter received. Carrie McVeigh No R16/03/30-13 The Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoIn Progress Ladysmith) directs staff to investigate and report back on the following: (a) The necessary steps & ministry response to the possibility of reducing the current SD68 Board from nine members to seven; (b) What are the estimated savings from reducing the number of trustees from nine to seven?; (c) SD68 trustee stipends (how do they compare; stipends of board chair, vice chair & committee chairs versus trustees at large; should stipends increase or be tied to COLA (cost of living allowances)?; (d) options for a ward system (versus the current "at large" system). Report should be delivered for consideration on or before October 26, 2016. Carrie McVeigh R16/03/30-16 The Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoIn Progress Ladysmith) directs staff to investigate and report back on the cost and benefits of a BCSTA membership (i.e., what is the total cost of BCSTA membership - dues, winter conference registration fees, travel, hotel & meal expenses, AGM registration fees, travel, hotel & meal expenses, etc; cost of membership alone; cost of conferences & AGM's; comparison of non-member conference/AGM fees; list of benefits derived from BCSTA membership). Staff should also provide a list of alternate options for Trustees. Report should be delivered for consideration on or before October 26, 2016. Carrie McVeigh No 26 Oct 2016 No 26 Oct 2016 R16/03/30-17 The Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) directs staff to investigate the cost of a timing system that provides for an egalitarian and accurate measurement for 10 (or other) minute presentations. Further, the committee recommends that the existing policy/procedures include language that provides for documentation of the questions (and answers) be included in the public record (i.e., the meeting minutes).
