Page 46 of 143 9.2 Staff reviewed the announcement and considered the following points:            The current district transportation policy has been in place for decades and reflected previous Ministry of Education policy dating back to a the time when school districts were received specific transportation funding, designed to fulfill Ministry policy, and was based on a 3 year rolling average. The current policy provides for a robust transportation service which has essentially met the needs of the school district. Over the years, other non-policy transportation services have been added (by Board directions), resulting in the provision of service that is above current policy requirements. Many of these decisions were made to address issues related to safety and school closures. Ministry of Education funding for transportation was eliminated many years ago.
