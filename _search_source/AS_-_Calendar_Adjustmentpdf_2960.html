Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION REGULAR BOARD MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: February 24, 2016 TO: Board of Education FROM: John Blain, Superintendent SUBJECT: Adjustment to Local School Calendar Background: On January 27, 2016, the Board of Education approved the amended Local School Calendar to go to a 30day consultation period, as required. The amendment to the calendar included: adding one noninstructional day per year, for the purpose of Curriculum In-service. This date is to be announced at a later time and is to be added to the Local School Calendar for the 2016-17 and 2017-18 local school years. Rationale: The Ministry of Education has recently mandated a curriculum redesign and is also providing two days of curriculum in-service, during the 2015-16 school year, for teachers to prepare for the changes. The Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools (NLPS) local school calendar for 2015-16, therefore, requires an adjustment. After staff discussion with the Nanaimo District Teachers’ Association, the following has been agreed as the two days designated as non-instructional days and to be devoted to curriculum in-service:   Friday, January 29, 2016 and Monday, May 16, 2016 In addition, the Ministry has provided for one more non-instructional Curriculum In-service day in each of the following two school years. As a result, the revised Local School Calendars for 2016-17 and for 2017-18 will also need to be amended to reflect the Ministry’s adjustment in the number of instructional hours (5 hours less for each school year), and its adjustment to the minimum hours of instruction. Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) approve the current Local School Calendar and that it be amended by adding non-instructional days for the purpose of Curriculum In-Service. These non-instructional days (1 day per year on a date to be announced) are to be added to the Local School Calendar for the 2016-17 and 2017-18 school years.
