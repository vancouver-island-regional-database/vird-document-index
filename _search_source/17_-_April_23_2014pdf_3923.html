NEW BUSINESS Criminal Record Checks Folio R14/04/23-17 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Allen IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) requests the Superintendent/CEO to develop a draft policy and procedure for Board approval by June 2014, that would require candidates for Trustee, once elected, to provide a Criminal Record Check to the District. Trustee Curley challenged the Chair stating that this motion had been before the Board earlier and that it should not be permitted. Chair Neary stated that she would allow the motion. Trustee Curley challenged the Chair's ruling to allow the motion to be debated. Chair Neary passed the gavel to Vice-Chair Allen who asked the question "Is the Chair sustained?" Trustees Robinson, Brennan, Curley, Welch, Saunders and Bard voted nay. Vice-Chair Allen declared the motion out of order.
