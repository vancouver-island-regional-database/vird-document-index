Transportation Department Sub-Committee Meeting Minutes February 5, 2014 Attendees: Chair - TerryLynn Saunders, Phil Turin, Scott Saywell, Patrick Young, Dave Prevost, Gabriela Cartwright Absent: Alana Cameron, Pete Sabo Meeting started at 2:05 PM - Phil, Gabriela and Dave gave an overview of the purchasing process followed to potentially purchase bus routing software. Through this process, it was determined that purchasing bus routing software might not be in the best interest for our school district at this time. Routing software and alternatives were discussed. - As a possible budget reduction initiative, a proposal from School Bus Consultants (SBC) was discussed. The proposal included assessments on courtesy riders, bell schedules and fee for service. - Transportation Sub-Committee recommendations were discussed (see below). - Dave gave an update on transportation for Protection Island Students. o The structure of transportation was changed from a contract with the ferry provider to a Transportation Assistance type of payment to families. o It was estimated that this change of structure would produce $15,850 in annual savings. At this time, the savings is estimated to be $20,832 for the 2013/14 school year. This savings will fluctuate as it is dependent upon the number of students requiring transportation from Protection Island. - Dave provided an update on the Transportation Review low and medium priority recommendations. The meeting was adjourned at 2:50 PM. Transportation Sub-Committee Recommendation: “That the Transportation Sub-Committee recommends to the Business Committee that the school district not purchase transportation routing software and that approval be given to proceed with a transportation routing assessment pertaining to courtesy riders as per the School Bus Consultants (formerly Management Partnership Services, Inc. – MPS) proposal dated January 27, 2014 at a cost of $9,800.” Note: There is a budget of $58,000 dedicated to the purchase of transportation routing software which would cover the cost of the proposed transportation routing assessment.
