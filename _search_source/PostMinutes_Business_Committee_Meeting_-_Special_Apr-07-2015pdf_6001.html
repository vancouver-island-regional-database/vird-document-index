APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA Folio BC15/04/07-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY NEW BUSINESS Meeting times for Special Business Committee Meetings Trustee Brzovic stated that she would like the meeting time for the Special Business Committee meeting to be changed to 6:00 pm as the 4:30 pm meeting time is too difficult for the public to attend as they're too early and as a Board we need to appear transparent. Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 7, 2015 Trustee Rae stated that staff time is important too. This time (4:30 pm) was agreed upon by everyone when the budget schedules were created and believes the time should be kept at 4:30 pm. It was agreed to keep the 4:30 pm start time for the Special Business Committee Meetings. Staffing - Mr. Roberts Mr. Roberts presented to the Business Committee information focusing on staffing costs. His presentation is attached to the Agenda. He compared Nanaimo-Ladysmith Public Schools to other school districts in the province in areas such as: total wages; benefits; wages by job function; and estimated students per teacher. He also provided elementary and secondary schools staffing formulas to the Committee. UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was no Unfinished Business this evening. QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions this evening. ADJOURNMENT Folio BC15/04/07-02 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 5:44 pm.
