Meeting No. SB09 CALL TO ORDER The Chair called the meeting to order at 6:01 pm and recognized that tonight’s meeting is being held on the traditional territory of the Snuneymuxw people. APPROVAL OF THE AGENDA IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio SB14/04/16-01 That the Agenda be approved. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY UNFINISHED BUSINESS 2014-2015 Draft Budget Staff reviewed the proposed budget reduction strategies presented at last week's meeting. Staff Draft Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 16, 2014 responded to the questions that were raised last week when the budget strategies were presented. The Committee was provided with additional documentation clarifying several of the proposals. Proposal 1.08, the reduction of education assistant time in the Skills for Life Programs was withdrawn. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Neary IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Curley Folio SB14/04/16-02 That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) submits to the Minister of Education, alongside the finalized 2014-2015 budget, a draft shadow budget that reflects the resources the district would need in order to be in compliance with the working conditions provisions of the pre 2002 Teachers' Collective Agreement. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY QUESTION PERIOD There were no questions this evening. ADJOURNMENT IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Howland Folio SB14/04/16-03 That the meeting be adjourned. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY The meeting adjourned at 8:45 pm.
