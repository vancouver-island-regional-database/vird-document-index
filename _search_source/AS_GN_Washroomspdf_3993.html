BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: March 20, 2014 TO: Board of Education FROM: Dave Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO SUBJECT: Gender Neutral Washrooms Administrative Procedure (DRAFT) Background: The Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity (SOGI) committee of the Board of Education recommended that consideration be given to creating gender neutral washrooms in system facilities. Rationale: A number of public institutions are developing policy and practice with respect to Transgendered and Transsexual individuals. The following “Human Rights Rationale” was developed by the Queen’s University Transgender/Transsexual Policy Group and given its relevance to the Board of Education’s SOGI recommendation, is worth quoting in full: Gender specific washrooms designated for use by a single gender only, fail to accommodate gender variant* members of the Queens University Community. Both the Ontario Human Rights Commission policy and human rights decisions are clear that people in the process of transitioning have a right to use the facilities designed for their felt gender; gender specific washrooms can result in instances of harassment and violence towards those perceived by others as not being of the specified gender. Without the existence of gender neutral facilities, these individuals have no choice but to use gender specific washrooms – an experience that can be uncomfortable, embarrassing, hurtful, frightening, and dangerous. Further, gender specific facilities reflect a gender binary with which many Individuals do not identify. *This terms refers to expressions of gender that do not conform to the dominant gender norms of Western culture and encompasses: transgender, transsexual, transitioning, intersexed, twospirited, and cross-dressing individuals. The District’s commitment to upholding the rights of gender variant individuals is reflected in Board Policy 2.10: Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity (attached). To address the SOGI committee’s recommendation with respect to Gender Neutral washrooms however, it is evident that an administrative procedure is needed in order to provide direction to staff. Given this, the following draft administrative procedure has been developed: 1 Administrative Procedure _____: Gender Neutral Washrooms (DRAFT) 1. District and school administration shall ensure the existence of at least one gender neutral washroom in every newly constructed or significantly renovated building. 2. In all other cases involving the accommodation of gender variant individuals, District and school administration shall ensure that an existing washroom is designated as gender neutral. 3. All such facilities must include signage that makes it absolutely clear that they are not restricted by gender. Legal Reference: Board Policy 2.10 Recommendation: That the draft Administrative Procedure: Gender Neutral Washrooms be distributed to the SOGI Committee, District senior staff and school administrators for feedback, with the intent that the procedure is finalized by the April 23, 2014 regular Board meeting. 2
