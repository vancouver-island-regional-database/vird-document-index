NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: June 14, 2023 Business Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Transportation Administration Fee Background: At the Business Committee Meeting on May 10, 2023, the following motion was moved and seconded: The Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) institute a yearly administration fee beginning with bussing registrations submitted after October 1, 2023 for all students who register for the bus who are inside of the walk limits as set out in Administrative Procedure 512 including students who are bussed to specialized programming with the exception of Special Needs associated bussing and BCTEA related riders and that staff include an update in the yearly Transportation Update to the Board. The Board then referred the motion to the Business Committee Meeting of June 14, 2023. Discussion: The original action sheet dated May 10, 2023, is attached. No additional information has been added.
