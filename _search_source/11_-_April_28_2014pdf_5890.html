CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Minutes of the Special Business Committee Meeting held April 28, 2014 UNFINISHED BUSINESS Mr. Hutchinson presented the committee with a new Proposal 7.01 for $390,959 in administrative reductions in the operating budget. These include the elimination of an executive assistant position in the Human Resources Department and moving the cost of the facilities planning supervisor to the annual facilities grant budget and the cost of the capital project coordinator to the Wellington seismic project. This proposed reduction also includes the elimination of the positions of self-regulated learning coordinator and one instructional coordinator. Mr. Turin provided the committee with a calculation of the budget that would be required if the district were to implement the provisions of the 2002 teachers’ collective agreement. He said that the district would require 62.49 FTE additional classroom teachers, and 17.64 FTE fewer non-enrolling teachers for a total of 44.85 FTE additional teachers. When facilities costs are added, the additional budget required is estimated to be $6.5 million. 2014-2015 - Draft Budget Folio SB14/04/28-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Curley IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Welch The Special Business Committee recommends that budget proposal 1.02 "Restructuring of Secondary Libraries" be removed from the list of Budget Proposals.
