ADJOURNMENT 2 INFORMATION REPORT TO COUNCIL Report Prepared By: Meeting Date: File No: Re: Erin Anderson, Director of Financial Services November 10, 2020 Water and Sewer Rates for 2021 RECOMMENDATION: That Council receive for information the report from the Director of Financial Services, dated November 10, 2020, regarding Water and Sewer Rates for 2021. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: The Water and Sewer utility expenses are funded by user rates and parcel taxes. The user fees are billed each quarter, with the first quarter starting January. PREVIOUS COUNCIL DIRECTION: N/A DISCUSSION: The Town operates our own Water and Sewer (also known as waste water) utilities. The expenses in the respective utilities are funded through user fees and parcel taxes. This budget continues with the past practice of using the parcel taxes to fund the debt payments and the capital projects whereas the user fees cover most of the day-to-day operations. The Water Fund is proposing a 3% utility increase which will result in a $3.66 increase per quarter for an average single family dwelling using 1 cubic meter of water a day. There is no proposed increase to the Water Parcel Tax. The increase is due to a rise in inflationary operating costs as well as factoring in a decrease in annual consumption. The Sewer Fund is proposing to keep the rates at the current levels for both the user fees and the parcel tax. I approve the report and recommendation(s).
