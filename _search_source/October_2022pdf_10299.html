B22/10/12-03 IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Stanley The Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) rescind the following motion “That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to draft proposed boundary changes for Cilaire, Rock City and Departure Bay Elementary schools (with the potential of other minor boundary shifts) that would shift students away from Departure Bay, for review and approval in principle, followed by community consultation and implementation for September 2023.
