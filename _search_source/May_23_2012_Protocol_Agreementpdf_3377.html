THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: May 23, 2012 TO: The Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Board of Education & City of Nanaimo Protocol Agreement Background: At the Thursday, May 10, 2012 school district meeting with the City of Nanaimo a recommendation was made to establish a Protocol Agreement between the parties to focus on working together on matters of common interest. Recommended Motion: “That the Board of Education of School District 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to develop a protocol agreement between the Board of Education and the City of Nanaimo to govern relations on matters of common interest".
