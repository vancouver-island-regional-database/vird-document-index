Background Information NLPS has an overall budget of $132.5 million of which $118.2 million is the operating budget. NLPS operations are dependent on funding from the Ministry of Education (MoE). The majority of MoE funding is determined by student enrolment, specifically per student funding based on full-time equivalent (FTE). An additional contributing factor for budget reductions is declining enrolment. In 1999-2000 NLPS had 17,087 students. The budget plan which was developed last year, ‚ÄúBecoming Sustainable,‚ÄĚ was initially a two-year plan to end the continuous budget cycle of making cuts each year.
