Palmetto Study • Spot screens subject from 3 feet away, capture time of 1 second and delivers immediate, comprehensive and objective results on an easy to read touchscreen. Palmetto Study • 537 students screened, 148 referred for follow-up care, just over the one in four national average.2 • 55 already owned glasses, leaving 93 students recommended for follow-up care. • Myopia, (nearsightedness) 48% + of the referrals, Astigmatism (blurred vision) 37% of the referrals . • Spot was able to identify students missed in prior years or not obtained follow-up care from previous screenings.
