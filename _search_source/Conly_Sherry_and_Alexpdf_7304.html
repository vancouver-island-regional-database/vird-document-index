From: Sherry Conly Sent: Monday, June 8, 2020 9:31 PM Subject: Fwd: Concerning South Wellington School Property Good evening, I understand that the future of the South Wellington School property is not yet decided, and before it is, I would like to appeal to you to consider how beneficial and valuable this property would be as a recreation hub. I don't just mean valuable to South Wellington, but to the south Nanaimo community, spanning from Chase River to Cassidy. I've lived here for 6 years and am seeing more and more residents coming in, buying land and building their dream homes. The demographic is also diverse, from young families to retirees. It's an active and close-knit community, we're just lacking the space to really connect and build on the foundation. Currently we have no sports courts, no real space for community events or farmers markets, or even a playground suitable for younger children. We pay taxes for parks and recreation, so it only makes sense that we have these amenities in our own community. In closing, SW is an absolute gem of an area, and I know that a gathering place would be of enormous value to our community, able to offer something for everyone, from tai chi and yoga lessons, to basketball, soccer, community meetings, markets, and much, much more. Thank you for your consideration, Sherry and Alex Conly
