HAZARDOUS MATERIALS DECLARATION (To be used in support of a Building Permit Application) This form is to be completed and submitted with an application for a Building Permit where the proposed works includes demolition, alterations or renovations within an existing building or structure, or a moved-on building or structure. The information provided here is intended to assist the Building Inspector in determining if the worksite, building or structure will be safe to enter to carry out field inspections. Additional information may be required by the TOL to confirm that hazardous materials will not be disturbed or removed. If deemed necessary, the Building Inspector may require a Written Confirmation (Clearance Document or a post Abatement Inspection Report) prepared by a qualified person in accordance with WorkSafeBC Occupational Health and Safety Regulation. Written confirmation may also be required at various stages of construction if further disturbance of hazardous materials occurs.
