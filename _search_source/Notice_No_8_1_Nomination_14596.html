NOMINATION PERIOD Public Notice is given to the electors of the Town of Ladysmith that nominations for the office of Mayor – One (1) to be elected for a term ending October 2026, will be received by the Chief or Deputy Chief Election Officer from 9:00 am on Tuesday, January 14, 2025 until 4:00 pm on Friday, January 24, 2025, as follows: By hand, mail or other delivery service: By email: By fax: Ladysmith City Hall (410 Esplanade) PO Box 220, Ladysmith, BC V9G 1A2 vote@ladysmith.ca** 250.245.6411** **Originals of emailed or faxed nomination documents must be received by the Chief Election Officer no later than 4:00 pm on Friday, January 31, 2025. Nomination forms are now available at www.ladysmith.ca/election or at City Hall until the close of the nomination period (4:00 pm on January 24, 2025). QUALIFICATIONS FOR OFFICE A person is qualified to be nominated, elected, and to hold office as a member of local government if they meet the following criteria: • Canadian citizen; • 18 years of age or older on general voting day March 1, 2025; • resident of British Columbia for at least 6 months (July 23, 2024) immediately before the day nomination papers are filed; and • not disqualified under the Local Government Act or any other enactment from voting in an election in British Columbia, or being nominated for, being elected to or holding the office, or be otherwise disqualified by law.
