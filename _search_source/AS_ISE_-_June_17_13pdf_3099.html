BOARD OF EDUCATION Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools ACTION SHEET DATE: June 26, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Dave Hutchinson, Superintendent/CEO RE: International Student Education: Fees Background: During the Facilities Review process, Dr. Doug Player recommended that we increase the return on investment (ROI) for the International Student Education Program (ISE). The recommendation included the possibility of increasing fees for the 2014-15 school year. Given this, it is recommended that we increase our tuition from its current $11,500 to $12,000 which would allow us to still be as competitive as our neighbouring districts’ programs. Both the Qualicum and Cowichan School Districts are currently charging $12,000. Their number of FTEs is lower than ours: they have approximately 100125 FTEs, whereas we currently have 225 FTEs. Please note that we must pay our agents a 15 percent commission on tuition, so although we will be increasing the amount by $500, the agents will receive an additional $75. Agents will receive a total of $1,800 commission, based on a tuition increase to $12,000 per ISE student. I would also like to recommend increasing our homestay application fee from $150 to $250 which will increase our fees by a further $100.
