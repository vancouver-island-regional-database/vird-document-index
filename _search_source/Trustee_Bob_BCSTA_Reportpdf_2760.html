Report from BCSTA Provincial Meeting At the October 29th Provincial Council meeting, the Finance Committee reviewed a number of factors that are expected to affect BCSTA’s 2017/2018 operating budget and we will be discussing these at the next Provincial meeting in February.  Student FTE enrollment  Declining interest rates  Reinstatement of BCSTA’s membership in the National School Boards Association (NSBA)  Capital asset replacement  An increase in lease costs in 2017/2018  Staffing changes  Movement on the staff salary grid  A one-half of one percent salary grid increase effective July 1, 2017  A one percent salary grid increase effective May 1, 2018  Rising benefit costs
