NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BUSINESS COMMITTEE PUBLIC MEETING INFORMATION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: January 13, 2021 Business Committee Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Administrative Procedure 507 - Fencing Background As part of our ongoing review of our Administrative Procedures, this memo is to inform the Board of the removal of Administrative Procedure 507 – Fencing (the “AP”). Rationale The AP is not followed by the District and we have not been able to determine how long it has been since we have enforced it. Specifically, it requires that prior to the installation of a fence at a school site that a neighbour is obligated to pay 50% of the costs of the fence. Further, it envisions wooden fences in urban areas and three pronged barbwire fencing for rural areas. Giving that fencing is ubiquitous on our properties and we have no intention of not installing fencing and/or other appropriate barriers between our sites and neighbouring properties, or the road, the AP is unnecessary. Further, if a neighbour is interested in assisting with the cost of fencing to install a product that differs from the District standard we may consider it, but would not require an AP to do so. Further, we do not have a standard fence that differs between urban and rural areas. For these reasons, AP 507 - Fencing is unnecessary and will be deleted.
