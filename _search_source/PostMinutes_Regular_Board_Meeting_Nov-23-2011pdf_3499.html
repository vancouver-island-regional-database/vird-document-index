BC Hydro Energy Ambassadors - Dover Bay, Ladysmith and Woodlands Secondary Schools a at brief 5:50 recess. pm and Ms. Becky Longe, a BC Hydro rep presented secondary students with BC Hydro Energy Ambassador grants of $1,000 per school. The students outlined the projects they have undertaken as Energy Ambassadors and their plans to use the grant. • Woodlands Secondary Energy Ambassadors plan to use the grant money to increase the energy efficiency of their school building with improvements in such things as weatherstripping. • The Ladysmith Secondary Green Team students have helped establish a garden in front of the school. They plan to use the grant money to support recycling efforts. • The Dover Bay Eco Club will use the grant money for an exercise bicycle that, when in use, will create energy that will go back into the electrical grid at the school.
