The dinner is formal and is one of the major fundraisers during the Tour de Rock’s timeline, usually including the service of some alcoholic beverages which are served by the members. It is for this reason that we are requesting consideration from the Board to be able to serve this type of dinner/drinks within LSS. Preparation of food items in the kitchen is likely to involve students, however, food leaving the prep area and drinks served would all be handled by the RCMP/Fire Department and Tour de Rock Ladysmith committee members with their Serving-It-Right license(s). The committee is thrilled that LSS is willing to open its doors to host this event, as it became evident last year that the fundraiser had outgrown its venue. It is our sincere desire to involve the students to enhance their engagement in this particular community initiative, so that they can see the dedication required of the Tour participants and breathe the passion of the riders. Thank you for your consideration. I will be happy to answer any questions or concerns that the Board may have with regards to the event and/or service therein.
