THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: March 27, 2013 TO: Board of Education FROM: Phil Turin, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Repeal of District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw BACKGROUND: The Board of Education is developing an Enhanced Facilities – 10 Year Plan as part of its Strategic Plan. In order to facilitate the Enhanced Facilities Plan, the District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw should be repealed as it originated from the previous district Facilities Plan and is outdated by referencing the Facilities Planning Committee which no longer exists. RECOMMENDATION: The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) District Facilities Plan Bylaw is repealed effective March 27, 2013 and that it is given first reading. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) District Facilities Plan Bylaw is repealed effective March 27, 2013 and that it is given second reading. RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings to repeal the District Facilities Plan Bylaw at its Regular Board Meeting of March 27, 2013. RECOMMENDATION: That The Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) District Facilities Plan Bylaw is repealed effective March 27, 2013 and that it is given third and final reading. ATTACHMENT: District Facilities Plan Change Process Bylaw adopted December 15, 2010
