Appendix A Administrative Procedure 318 – Travel Assistance to National Competitions AP 318 – Travel Assistance to National Competitions Purpose To encourage excellence in all fields of student extra-curricular endeavour the Board shall, in the event a student group qualifies and competes in a national competition, assist in the travel cost incurred within the confines of the budget by a grant of up to one thousand dollars ($1,000), on a matching basis in terms of student/parent/school raised funding.
