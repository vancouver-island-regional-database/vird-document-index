Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION INFORMATION SHEET DATE: June 3, 2015 TO: Education Committee FROM: John Blain, Superintendent/CEO SUBJECT: Scholarship Summary Over the next two weeks, Nanaimo-Ladysmith Schools Foundation (NLSF) will be co-hosting 7 Award Ceremonies for graduating students. A total of over $325,000 of scholarships, bursaries and awards will be presented to deserving students due to the generous donations from community individuals, businesses and organizations. In addition, 151 Ministry Dogwoods valued at $151,000 will be presented to students who have shown excellence in their chosen field. In total, graduating students will receive over $482,000 to help pursue their post-secondary education. Awards $500 and more will be presented at the Port Theatre Honours Night on Tuesday June 16 @ 7:00 pm. School based award ceremonies will take place at each of the six (6) high schools where students will be presented school based awards, as well as Ministry Dogwoods and those under $500.00. The breakdown of awards not including the Ministry Dogwoods: Woodlands Secondary Awards Ceremony - Wednesday June 3 @ 7:00 pm $37,000 Awards John Barsby Secondary Awards Ceremony – Thursday June 4 @ 1:50 pm $44,400 Awards Dover Bay Secondary Awards Ceremony – Thursday June 4 @ 7:00 pm $56,800 Awards Ladysmith Secondary Awards & Commencement – Friday June 5 @ 5:30 pm $100,200 Awards Wellington Secondary Awards Ceremony – Thursday June 11 @ 7:00 pm $44,300 Awards Nanaimo District Secondary (including Alt) Awards Ceremony – Thursday June 11 @ 7:00 pm $42,700 Awards The 151 Ministry Dogwoods breakdown was based on the number of registered graduating students from each school at September 2014.
