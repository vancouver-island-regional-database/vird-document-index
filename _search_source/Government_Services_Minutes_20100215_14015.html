TOWN OF LADYSMITH MINUTES OF A REGULAR SESSION OF THE GOVERNMENT SERVICES COMMITTEE MONDAY, FEBRUARY 21, 2011 – 5:30 P.M. LADYSMITH COUNCIL MEMBERS PRESENT: Councillor Steve Arnett (Chair) Councillor Jillian Dashwood Councillor Bruce Whittington STAFF PRESENT: Ruth Malli Patrick Durban Joanna Winter Mayor Rob Hutchins Councillor Lori Evans Councillor Scott Bastian Councillor Duck Paterson Sandy Bowden Joe Friesenhan Erin Anderson Chris Trumpy CALL TO ORDER Councillor Arnett called the meeting to order at 5:30 p.m. AGENDA APPROVAL Councillor Arnett requested Council’s consideration of the following addition to the agenda: 6.2 Correspondence regarding a Scout Event on February 24, 2011 2011-013 It was moved, seconded and carried that the agenda be adopted as amended. MINUTES 2011-014 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Government Services Committee minutes for the meeting held January 17, 2010 be adopted as amended to correct the reference to the salt mix the Town uses in winter weather to ‘sodium’ chloride (page 3). Councillor Bastian entered the meeting. DELEGATIONS Dan Little, Cara Light -- Hayes, Stewart, Little Mr. Little and Ms Light provided Council with an overview of the audit plan for fiscal year 2010, which is scheduled to begin in March 2011, and responded to questions. CITY MANAGER’S REPORT The City Manager provided Council with a summary of staff activities and priorities for the preceding month. Government Services Committee February 21, 2011 Page 1 REPORTS 2011-015 Public Input Period Prior to Regular Council Meetings It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that support in principle be given to the establishment of a public input period prior to Council meetings, and that a committee be struck to work with staff to establish guidelines and terms of reference for the public input period. Opposed: Councillor Evans, Councillor Dashwood and Councillor Whittington. 2011-016 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Committee recommend to Council that Councillors Bastian and Arnett be appointed to form a committee that will draft the Terms of Reference for the Public Input Period. 2011-017 Building Inspector’s Report for January 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Building Inspector’s Report for January 2011 be received. 2011-018 Trolley Report for January 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Trolley Report for January 2011 be received. 2011-019 Fire Chief’s Report for January 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Fire Chief’s Report for January 2011 be received. 2011-020 Animal Control / Pound Report for January 2011 It was moved, seconded and carried that the Animal Control / Pound Report for January 2011 be received. MEMBER SUBMISSIONS 2011-021 Registration for the Association of Vancouver Island & Coastal Communities 2011 Convention It was moved, seconded and carried that Town of Ladysmith Policy No. 05-1790 B, Travel Expenses, be amended to increase the number of Council members authorized to attend the annual Association of Vancouver Island Coastal Communities (formerly Association of Vancouver Island Municipalities) and the Union of BC Municipalities conferences from two to four, in addition to the Mayor. Scouting Event, February 24, 2011 Councillor Whittington will attend the Ladysmith Division of Scouts Canada event on February 24, 2011.
