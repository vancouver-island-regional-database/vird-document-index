November 4, 2014 Ref: 177408 To: All Superintendents of Schools All Secretary-Treasurers RE: Service Delivery Project Phase 2 In his letter of October 29, 2014, Dave Byng, Deputy Minister of Education shared with Board Chairs that we would be working together on Phase 2 of the Service Delivery Project. This initiative focuses on implementation of the recommendations of the working groups on Procurement, Legal Services and Attendance Support and Wellness, and further evaluation of the opportunities in Facilities, Transportation, Business Systems, and IT and Communications. Government has directed the Ministry to be more ambitious in the implementation of changes and assessment of further shared services opportunities. It is no longer feasible for districts to consider these initiatives as optional; rather, imperative next steps. To inform this process, we will utilize the project work to date and the updated Deloitte report, which is on the Ministry website at: http://www.bced.gov.bc.ca/deloitte_report_july_2014.pdf We have also posted a summary report of the work of Phase 1 of the Service Delivery Project on the Ministry website at: http://www.bced.gov.bc.ca/pubs.htm I am pleased to share with you that the Ministry will be supporting this project in three important ways: 1. A new Shared Services Branch that will work in collaboration with school districts. 2. Continued support for the Service Delivery Project by bringing key representatives of the sector together to evaluate and provide workable strategies for these initiatives. 3. Hosting of two sessions to bring district officials together to share information and engage in work on these opportunities, the first session prior to the joint BCSSA and BCASBO conference on November 12, 2014, and another prior to the BCSTA Trustee Academy on December 4, 2014. Stay tuned for more detailed information on these sessions. Our project plan continues with the structure of district representatives on committees and working groups and will now include an Education Partners Advisory Committee to ensure we hear the perspectives of trustees. We will continue to rely on your participation as senior district leaders and appreciate your support for the project to ensure that workable solutions are recommended. … /2 Ministry of Education Services and Technology Division Mailing Address: PO Box 9151 Stn Prov Govt Victoria BC V8W 9H1 Location: 620 Superior St Victoria BC V8V 1V2 -2- Thank you to all who participated in Phase 1.
