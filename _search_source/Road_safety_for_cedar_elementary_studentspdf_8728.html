This has been an ongoing area of concern starting before woodbank school was closed. The amount of traffic we have going in and out of the school area creates complete chaos at drop off and pick up in the morning. The lack of parking only makes this worse as cars park in the roadways making an already dangerous situation even worse. We do not have sidewalks or safe crossing areas . We are a rural area where we would like to encourage our youth to walk and ride bikes to school, unfortunately many parents feel it is too unsafe to allow their children to do so. This means more cars on the road. I encourage you all to come by our school and see for yourself - especially after school. It is a nightmare quite honestly. We do not want to see a child hit and I see daily close calls. Thank you for your time Jalene Dick Cedar Elem PAC chair
