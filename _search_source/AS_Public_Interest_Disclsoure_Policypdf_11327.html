NANAIMO LADYSMITH PUBLIC SCHOOLS BOARD OF EDUCATION PUBLIC MEETING ACTION SHEET DATE: TO: FROM: SUBJECT: November 22, 2023 Board of Education Mark Walsh, Secretary-Treasurer Policy 2.22 - Public Interest Disclosure Recommendation: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopt Policy 2.22 – Public Interest Disclosure. Background: On September 27, 2023, the Board of Education passed the following motion: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) receive the Draft Policy – Public Interest Disclosure and circulate as a notice of motion for 30 days, utilizing the consultation process as per Board Governance, Section 1, 2.7 – Policy Development. This Action Sheet will show that there was only one response from the community on the Policy. Discussion: Community Engagement Plan Utilized the community engagement webpage and included background information with instructions on how to participate during the consultation period: https://www.sd68.bc.ca/board/communityengagement/ Information on the community engagement period was: • Shared with all district staff via NLPS News; • Emailed to all families via School Messenger platform; and • Shared on social media (Facebook and Twitter) The consultation period began on September 27, 2023. The community had until October 27, 2023, to provide feedback by emailing consultation@sd68.bc.ca.
