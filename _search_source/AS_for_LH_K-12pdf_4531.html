THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF SCHOOL DISTRICT NO. 68 ACTION SHEET DATE: December 2, 2015 TO: Board of Education FROM: Graham Roberts, Secretary-Treasurer SUBJECT: Learn@Home K-12 School FIRST READING OF BYLAW NO. 2015-03 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Learn@Home K-7 School; 3004 Departure Bay Road, Nanaimo, BC, V9T 1B4; Facility No. 06899139; on June 30, 2016, and that it be given first reading. SECOND READING OF BYLAW NO. 2015-03 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Learn@Home K-7 School, 3004 Departure Bay Road, Nanaimo, BC, V9T 1B4; Facility No. 06899139 on June 30, 2016, and that it be given second reading. UNANIMOUS CONSENT REQUIRED TO READ THIRD AND FINAL BYLAW RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Learn@Home K-7 School Closure Bylaw No. 2015-03 at its Special Board Meeting of December 2, 2015. THIRD AND FINAL READING OF BYLAW NO. 2015-03 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Learn@Home K-7 School, 3004 Departure Bay Road, Nanaimo, BC, V9T 1B4; Facility No. 06899139 on June 30, 2016, and that it be given third and final reading. FIRST READING OF BYLAW NO. 2015-04 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Learn@Home 8-12 School, 355 Wakesiah Ave, Nanaimo, BC, V9R 3K5; Facility No. 06899165 on June 30, 2016, and that it be given first reading. SECOND READING OF BYLAW NO. 2015-04 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Learn@Home 8-12 School, 355 Wakesiah Ave, Nanaimo, BC, V9R 3K5; Facility No. 06899165on June 30, 2016, and that it be given second reading. UNANIMOUS CONSENT REQUIRED TO READ THIRD AND FINAL BYLAW RECOMMENDATION: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) adopts all three readings of the Learn@Home 8-12 School Closure Bylaw No. 2015-04 at its Special Board Meeting of December 2, 2015. THIRD AND FINAL READING OF BYLAW NO. 2015-04 RECOMMENDATION: Be it resolved that the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) permanently closes Learn@Home 8-12 School, 355 Wakesiah Ave, Nanaimo, BC, V9R 3K5; Facility No, 06899165 on June 30, 2016, and that it be given third and final reading. RECOMMENDED MOTION: That the Board of Education of School District 68 (NanaimoLadysmith) opens the school formerly known as Mt. Benson School as a new distributed learning K-12 school (school name yet to be determined) effective September 2016.
