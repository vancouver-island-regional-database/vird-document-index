Department of Learning Services Office of the Assistant Superintendent 395 Wakesiah Avenue Nanaimo, BC V9R 3K6 Phone: 250-741-5266 Fax: 250-741-5225 May 5, 2015 Dear Cedar and Cinnabar Valley area Students and Parents: The school district is currently developing the bussing provisions available for students planning to attend both John Barsby Community Secondary and Ladysmith Secondary schools during the 2015-16 school year. In addition to providing busing to John Barsby Community Secondary there will also be bussing available (for the 2015-16 school year only) for students currently attending or wishing to attend Ladysmith Secondary School. The next step in the transition planning is to identify which Cedar and Cinnabar Valley area students wish to attend Ladysmith Secondary School in September 2015. Beginning September 2016, all Cedar Community Secondary catchment students who choose to attend out of catchment will be required to obtain their own transportation to attend any other SD68 school. Similarly, beginning September 2016, any Cinnabar Valley area student will be required to obtain their own transportation to attend a secondary school other than Cedar Community Secondary. Attached you will find a secondary school attendance form. Please complete and return to your child’s school by May 15, 2015 at 12 noon. This will help in the development of a transportation plan to and from your child’s chosen school. If we do not receive the form by the aforementioned deadline, we will assume you wish to attend John Barsby Community Secondary in September 2015. Please contact your school principal, vice-principal or counselor if you have questions. Sincerely, Scott Saywell Assistant Superintendent Attachment
