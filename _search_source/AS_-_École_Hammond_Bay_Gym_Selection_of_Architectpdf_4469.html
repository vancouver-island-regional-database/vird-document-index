Nanaimo Ladysmith Public Schools BOARD OF EDUCATION ACTION SHEET DATE: February 21, 2018 TO: The Board of Education FROM: Carrie McVeigh, Secretary - Treasurer Pete Sabo, Director of Planning and Operations SUBJECT: Capital Project Development Appointment of Architect – École Hammond Bay Gym Expansion Recommendation: That the Board of Education direct staff to award the contract to KMBR Architects Planners Inc. for the final design and construction supervision for the expansion of the École Hammond Bay gymnasium subject to the successful negotiation of a Client/Architect Agreement. Background: KMBR were retained as consultants to develop a Project Definition Report (PDR) for the expansion of the Gymnasium, which included a thorough site/building analysis along with a proposed project budget. This report was completed January 2017. At the meeting of February 21, 2018, the Board is considering the following motion as recommended by the Business Committee during its meeting on February 14, 2018: That the Board of Education of School District No. 68 (Nanaimo-Ladysmith) direct staff to move forward with plans to complete the new gymnasium at École Hammond Bay as Phase 1 of the Facility Plan. Should this motion pass, staff recommend the appointment of KMBR as the Architect firm. Funding for the new gymnasium is to come from local capital as discussed in the Business Committee meeting of February 14, 2018. Discussion: In respect of the Board’s urgency to commence this project and in consideration of District Administrative Procedure 513 (Purchasing) and AP501 (Selection of Architect), staff recommends a variance to the tendering requirements for the sole sourcing of an Architect by engaging the services of KMBR to provide f u l l design services in a prime consulting role. Given that KMBR were the consultants who prepared the initial PDR, the District will realize savings in costs and project efficiencies by continuing with this firm through the final design and construction. KMBR would assume the role of Prime consultant and lead a team of qualified sub-consultants in preparing the report. KMBR have substantial experience in elementary school planning and construction including an addition to École Hammond Bay, Randerson Ridge and Cinnabar Valley. Staff recommends that KMBR be engaged immediately to permit the appropriate level of District input/involvement and to ensure that planning and design are completed in the most efficient manner.
