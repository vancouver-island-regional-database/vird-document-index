UNFINISHED BUSINESS Budget 2012-2013 Mr. Turin reviewed the draft budget for 2012-13 with the Committee. He gave an overview of how the Ministry of Education's funding protection affects the district’s projected revenue for next year and subsequent years. For 201213, the funding protection grant has been reduced by $1.7 million. For 2013-14, 2014-15 and 2015-16, it is projected the district’s funding protection revenue will decline by more than $1.6 million in each of those years. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brennan IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Neary Folio BC12/04/18-03 NEW BUSINESS The Business Committee recommends that staff prepare the draft budget in the new format under Public Sector Accounting Standards as required by the Ministry of Education for presentation at the next Business Committee meeting.
