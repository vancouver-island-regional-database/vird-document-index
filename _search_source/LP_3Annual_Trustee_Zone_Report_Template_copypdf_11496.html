ANNUAL TRUSTEE ZONE REPORT Trustee Pellegrin School Year: 2022/2023 School Names/Zone: Wellington Secondary School Annual Highlights: Wellington, home of the Wildcats! Andrea Davidson is the excellent principal of this big beautiful high school. Vice principals are Nicole Suhr and Stacy Marusic. Wellington had over 1000 students last year and the number went down a bit for this year. They have a beautiful new basketball court. This is the only high school in our district that does not have a working cafeteria, this changing soon! Wahoo! Stacy runs the breakfast program in the multipurpose room, and there is also the indigenous room for breakfast and snacks. There is a hul’q’umi’num class taught by Gina Steward for grade 9 to 12 students. There is also an island connected embedded classroom with a teacher at all times this provides outreach and inreach. There is an active GSA club, robotics club, arts club and many more. Carmella Luviosotto who won the educator of the year award! What a huge accomplishment. The band room still isn’t quite ready since the fire of last January they are hoping to get back in there very soon. Andrea conducts interviews with the grade 12s each year and ask them a series of questions changes have come from these such as extend it breaks that have benefited all students from the feedback. Wild cat chats on social behavior and safety are very enriching and make a real difference. Basketball, rugby and volleyball are big sports a this school. The library commons and breakout rooms are a great design and very beneficial for the students. What a great learning community at Wellington.
