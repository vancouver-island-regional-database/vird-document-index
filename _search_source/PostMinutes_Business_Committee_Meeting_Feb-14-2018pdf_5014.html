SENIOR STAFF REPORTS Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh and Director of Finance Sutton Re: 2017-2018 Amended Budget (30 minutes) Folio BC18/02/14-04 Superintendent Blain • 2018-2019 Projections (10 minutes) • 2018-2019 Local School Calendar Folio BC18/02/14-05 Director of Finance Sutton presented the 20172018 Amended Budget, the presentation is attached to the Agenda. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Higginson IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Robinson That the Business Committee recommends that the Board of Education adopt the 2017/2018 Amended Budget Bylaw during the February 21, 2018, Special Open Board meeting. Opposed by Trustee Routley Superintendent Blain presented the Estimated Enrolments for 2018/2019 school year. The Estimated Enrolment document is attached to the Agenda. Superintendent Blain presented the draft 2018-2019 Local School Calendar, which is attached to the Agenda. It was noted that CUPE representatives were not included in the Calendar Committee, Superintendent Blain will meet directly with CUPE representatives to review the draft calendar. IT WAS MOVED BY Trustee Brzovic IT WAS SECONDED BY Trustee Brennan That the Business Committee recommend that the staff make the 2018/2019 local calendar Minutes of the Business Committee Meeting held February 14, 2018 available to the public immediately and to report back to the March Business Committee meeting with final recommendations. CARRIED UNANIMOUSLY Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh Re: Financial Governance and Accountability Secretary-Treasurer McVeigh presented an overview on financial governance and accountability, Ministry expectations and a staff recommended timeline to meet Ministry expectations. This Information Sheet is attached to the Agenda. Ney Expansion Project and the NDSS Turf Field. The presentations are attached to the Agenda. CORRESPONDENCE REFERRED FROM REGULAR BOARD MEETING There were none. UNFINISHED BUSINESS There was none.
