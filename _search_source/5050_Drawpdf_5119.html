50/50 Payroll Contribution Draw Purpose The purpose of the 50/50 Payroll Contribution Draw is to raise funds for selected programs operated by the Nanaimo-Ladysmith Schools Foundation (NLSF) to support students in the District. We also hope that this brings a spirit of “camaraderie” amongst the staff in the School District. We are proposing to implement this Draw in September 2013. Projected Revenue and Proposed Uses Projected annual revenue from this draw is $15,000 as detailed below. The NLSF will restrict the use of the funds for the District Initiative Grant (DIG) program. Criteria for this grant will be based on 3 main areas: Social Responsibility, Community Development and Student Leadership and will be developed with the objective to encourage, support and teach young people to be involved in their community. In addition, based on student need, funds may be used to increase the number of bursaries awarded to vulnerable grade 12 students continuing on with their post-secondary education. # of District Employees Projected % of participation - Year 1 2,000 15% Projected # of employees per draw Estimated # of entries per participant 300 2 Total entries per draw Entry fee Total Estimated Monthly Draw proceeds 600 $ 5.00 $3,000.00 Monthly draw winner #1 proceeds(25%) $750.00 Monthly draw winner #2 proceeds(25%) Residual 50% retained for Programs of NLSF $750.00 # of draws per school year Estimated Annual Program Funding 1,500.00 10 $15,000.00 Overview of Process The NLSF will be responsible for the majority of the draw administration including all reporting and advertising for the Draw. Promotion We propose to inform the School District (SD) staff about the Draw at the May 14/15 “Success for All” days. We will then promote the draw at the end of June with the distribution of posters throughout the schools. The Executive Director (ED) will make arrangements for the Foundation President and ED to address the school Administrators at the end of August. At that time, we will have an information letter and a “sign up” sheet to be distributed to all staff member’s boxes. We will also be available to attend an ‘all secretaries’ meeting in August if one takes place. If available, we would promote the Draw program in the SD News through Communications. Sign-up Staff interested in participating will complete a sign-up sheet (sample attached) and return it to the NLSF office located at John Barsby Secondary. A copy of this form will then be sent to the SD #68 Payroll Department for processing. Below are some points regarding the process:             The NLSF is responsible for applying for the Class A Gaming License. Once an employee signs up, they must commit to the draw for the year ending June 30 th. Each “chance” will be $5.00. Employees can sign up for as many “chances” as they wish. The NLSF Accountant will maintain a spreadsheet of all employees who sign up based on the sign-up forms received directly from District employees. The NLSF Accountant will reconcile the compiled Foundation entry list compiled to the payroll contribution list processed by SD68 payroll staff to ensure all entries are paid and that the list is complete (may change due to new employees, employees who have left etc.). The Payroll Department will email the list of payroll contributors and their number of entries to the ED. All of the employee names/numbers will be entered into a locked drum that will be used for the draw on a monthly basis. The drum will be housed at the Treasurer’s office at MNP or other secure location offsite from the District. Since there are different pay-periods in the SD, the proposal is to conduct the draw on a monthly basis on a consistent date i.e. the 1st Monday of every month. Since SD employees have 2 pay-periods in a month, the proposal is to do 2 draws on the draw date. There will always be a NLSF Director present at each draw. Once the draw is conducted, it will be the responsibility of the ED to inform the recipient and send an email to all employees via Master Email informing who the draw winners are. The NLSF Accountant will write the 2 cheques for the recipients and arrange with the recipients to complete payment. It is important to be noted that the 50/50 Payroll Contribution Draw is not income tax deductible. Summary of S.D. #68 Resource Requirements The SD Payroll Department will be responsible for coding each participating SD employee for the payroll deduction into their system. As each pay-period occurs, the Payroll department will email a list of all employees (and employee numbers) to Erin van Steen.
